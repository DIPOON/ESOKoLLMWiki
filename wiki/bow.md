# Bow (활)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Bow is one of the six weapon skills available in Elder Scrolls Online. You can advance through this skill line by equipping and using a bow.

The Bow skill line is granted the first time you kill an enemy with a bow equipped. You can increase your skill by engaging in combat with a bow equipped, or by having Bow skills slotted. The more skills you have slotted, the faster your skill will increase. Wood Elves are naturally gifted with bows, and will learn the ability more quickly than other races. In addition, you can earn free levels in the skill line by reading Skill Books, which may be found randomly on bookshelves anywhere. The skill books which increase Bow are:
*An Archer's Archive
*Bliss
*Father of the Niben, Fragment One
*The Source of the Bone
*Sporting Chance
