# Marbruk (마브룩)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The city is built on the ruins of an older city that was built by Ayleids fleeing Alessia's army after the fall of White-Gold Tower.

The city was built relatively quickly, and stands out in contrast to the more wild settlements of the Bosmer in the rest of the region, a fact that is brought up often.
