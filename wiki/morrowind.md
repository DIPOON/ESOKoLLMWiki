# Morrowind (모로윈드)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
__FORCETOC__
Morrowind is the first Chapter of The Elder Scrolls Online. It is an expansion set on the island of Vvardenfell. Morrowind includes a large new zone, the Warden class, a trial, and a new storyline centered around saving Vvardenfell from Baar Dau's descent. The DLC was made free to all players on April 4, 2022, but did not include the Warden class.
