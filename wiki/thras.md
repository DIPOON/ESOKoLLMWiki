# Thras (쓰라스)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Category:Lore-Places-Islands__NOTOC__
Thras, also known as the Coral Kingdoms, the Sloadlands and the Thrassian Archipelago, is an archipelago located southwest of the Chain and northwest of the Summerset Isles, within either the Sea of Pearls or the Abecean Sea. It is the little-known home of the Sload, and was the source of the deadly Thrassian Plague that swept Tamriel in . In response to the Plague, the people of Tamriel assembled the All Flags Navy, which sunk the islands of Thras, though the surviving Sload managed to bring the islands back to the surface. The Pillar of Thras, a thousand-foot-tall spiral of coral said to be a portal to Oblivion, stands on the largest island.
