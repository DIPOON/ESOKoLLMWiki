# Forgotten Wastes (잊혀진 황무지)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
A large force of cultists from the forsaken Sixth House inhabit this location, where they are in the process of slowly building up their forces, by luring Dunmer from across the island to the ruins via corrupted statuettes.

The dungeon is divided into several sections: a large overland area containing the stronghold of Kogoruhn, the Drinith Ancestral Tomb, the cave of Kora Dur, the Forgotten Depths, and the Caverns of Kogoruhn.
