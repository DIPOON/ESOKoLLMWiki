# Crosswych (크로스위시)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The town has been taken over by the Red Rook Bandits, but a number of residents are mounting a resistance. Crosswych Citizens roam the streets. Resistance Fighters can be found in several locations while the town is being liberated.

The mines are located up the hill north from the center of town. The settlement's skyshard is located on the wooden platforms above the mine's lower entrance. Several dogs and cats roam the town. A small herd of sheep grazes near the local Fighters Guild.
