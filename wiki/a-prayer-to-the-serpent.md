# A Prayer to the Serpent — Celestial Serpent 기도

## 본문 요약 (전문)

[[celestial-serpent|Celestial Serpent]]에게 기도. **[[celestials|Celestial]] 중 하나**.

> *"Celestial Serpent, hear my prayer!*
>
> *Oh, Serpent, who descends from the sky, ... listen to my unworthy voice and raise me up.*
>
> *Oh, Serpent, who sheds the **Worldskin** to return us to a *simpler, better time*, ... make a place for me in your nest.*
>
> *Oh, Serpent, who undulates with power and glory, ... grant me your **venomous blessing**.*
>
> *Celestial Serpent, hear my prayer!"*

## 핵심
- **[[celestial-serpent|Celestial Serpent]] = ESO Craglorn 챕터의 *적대적 *Celestial***. *Mage·Warrior·Thief 외 *13번째 별자리* (= [[the-serpent|the Serpent]]).
- *"Worldskin shed" = *Akatosh의 *kalpa 종말 비유* + *Yokuda Satakal의 *세계 삼킴* 신학과 *유사*.
- *venomous blessing* = *변형/저주의 *상징*.
