# Barbas (발바스)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Category:Online-SkaafinCategory:Online-Skaafin-Male
Barbas is the servant of Clavicus Vile, the Daedric Prince of Wishes. As a shapeshifting Daedra, he usually manifests as a Whiterun Wolfhound, and sometimes a Skaafin.

Barbas can be found in Willowgrove Cavern. When Vile granted the wish of Pircalmo, a mage visiting Willowgrove, Barbas became bound to Vile's altar in the Cavern.
