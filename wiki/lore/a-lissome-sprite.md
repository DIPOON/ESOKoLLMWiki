# A Lissome Sprite — Galen 시인 송 (전문)

[[lore/nynelle-dumaris|Nynelle Dumaris]] (Galen 시인)의 *sprite* 송. *[[lore/galen|Galen]] (Systres 군도)의 *druid 신비 풍경*.

> *The lilting laughter of a lissome [[lore/sprite|sprite]]*
> *Goes meandering through the watchful trees*
> *To kiss stone and brook in secret delight*
> *A loving caress that no other sees.*
>
> *Across fair green hills where [[lore/the-telling-stone|the Telling Stone]]*
> *Remembers holy [[lore/druids|druid]] dreams of yore*
> *Unseen this playful sprite flits to the throne*
> *Of [[lore/ivyhame|Ivyhame]], then lingers by the shore.*
>
> *The laughter of this playful sprite calls me*
> *Drawn from the day's cares I do not resist*
> *I cast aside my chores to go dance free*
> *In sun-drenched glens amid the sacred mists.*
>
> *I walk in [[lore/galen|Galen]]; there is none but me*
> *And my longing for woods, and hills, and sea.*

## 핵심
- *Galen (Systres) druid + sprite 풍경 시*. ESO Firesong DLC.
