# 36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 23 — Scripture of the Sword

## 본문 요약

**Scripture of the Sword** 8개:

1. *Sword = Symbolic Collage*. 인생 첫 절반에 잘 작용. 모든 dynasty가 앎.

2. *immobile warrior가 통일을 이해*. *True eyes 획득*. **[[lore/vivec-city|City of Swords]] 건설** — *laws that cut the people who live there into better shapes*.

3. *내가 무장하고 오면 girls가 옷을 태움*. *minor spirits 사라짐*. **"AE ALTADOON, the third law of weaponry."**

4. *immobile warrior는 결코 피로하지 않음*. *전투 중 *sleep holes 만들어* 회복*.

5. *Instinct = mini-miracles in reserve*. **"Serve me to win."**

6. *apparently inactivated의 span = 절대성에 대한 사랑*. **"The birth of God from the netchiman's wife is the abortion of kindness from love."**

7. *진짜 sword는 *세대의 chains 끊음* (= *적의 창조 신화*)*. **"Look on me as the exiled garden. All else is uncut weed."**

8. *ancient road tempered by [[lore/walking-ways|second walking way]]*. *Your hands must be huge to wield any sword the size of an ancient road, and yet he who is of right stature may irritate the sun with only a stick.*

끝: **ALMSIVI**.

## 핵심
- *Vivec City = City of Swords*. *법 = 사람을 잘라 더 나은 형태로*.
- *AE ALTADOON* — 세 번째 weaponry law (주문).
- *Second Walking Way* 언급 — Vivec/Numidium 미래 복선.
