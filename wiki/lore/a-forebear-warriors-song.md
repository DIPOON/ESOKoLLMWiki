# A Forebear Warrior's Song — Forebears 전사가

## 본문 요약 (전문)

[[lore/forebears|Forebears]] (Imperial 신앙 수용한 [[termbase/redguard|Redguard]] 분파)의 *전통 전사가*. *Crowns의 *Yokudan 순혈주의*와 대조 — *[[termbase/akatosh|Akatosh]]·[[termbase/stendarr|Stendarr]]* 등 *8 Divines 호명*.

### 1절 (Akatosh)
> *Akatosh, my strength comes from your hands, / My enemies fall before my sword. / ... Death, they cry. / Death, I sing. / But death is theirs, not mine.*

### 후렴
> *Wipe the blade of their color. / Wipe the shield of their color. / Victory is ours!*

### 2절 (Stendarr)
> *Stendarr, grant my weapon fall only thus: / For justice, for the right ways. / ... Justice is mine, and also theirs.*

### 3절 (자비)
> *For the way of the righteous is mercy, / And compassion for the weak. / Can I wield your [[lore/one-hand-and-shield|sword and shield]]? / Mercy, they beg. / Mercy, I give.*

### 결말
> *Swift death to the unjust! / Slow death to the merciless! ... **Victory is ours!***

## 핵심
- **Forebears의 *Imperial 신앙 통합* — *Akatosh + Stendarr* 호명**.
- *Crowns의 [[lore/a-betrayal-of-our-heritage|A Betrayal of Our Heritage]] (Yokudan 순혈)과 *대조 텍스트*.

## 톤 가이드
