# A Threnody to Lost Love — 잃은 사랑의 비탄가

## 본문 요약 (전문)

### 1절 (마음)
> *Deep within my mind*
> *The storms, they gather inside me*
> ...
> *Fear is so unkind*
> *Shouting through my tears*

### 후렴
> ***Please don't find me / I can't be free***
> ***Please come find me / Your death haunts me***
> ***Your death haunts me***

### 2절 (영혼)
> *Deep within my soul*
> *The fires burn inside of me*
> ...
> *I'm no longer whole*
> *Screaming through my tears*

### 종결 (강화된 후렴)
*"Your death haunts me"* 3회 반복.

## 핵심
- *ESO Greymoor 챕터 *비탄가*.
- *모순적 호소*: *"Don't find me" + "Come find me"* — *애도의 *내적 분열*.
- *상실 사랑의 *유령적 *집착*.
