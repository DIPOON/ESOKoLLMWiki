# Imperial City Prison (임페리얼 시티 감옥)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It has been overrun by Daedra, who have turned it into a place of torment and slaughter. A large Daedra carried on black wings has declared itself the new "Lord Warden" of the prison, and he has decreed that the prisoners must be tortured for the glory of Molag Bal. Mortals who die in captivity have their flesh taken to be used in the creation of biological weapons crafted by the vile Flesh Sculptor.
