# Clockwork City — *Sotha Sil의 도시*

## 정의

별칭: *"Brass-Throat"*, *"Oil-Slick Tower of Seamless Assembly"* ([[truth-in-sequence|The Truth in Sequence]] v6).

> *"Obsidian towers stretch ever skyward, festooned with polished brass and godly filigrees. Great turbines drive memory through a thousand thousand pipes that stretch out like tangled veins, or the golden roots of an ageless tree."*  
> — [[truth-in-sequence|The Truth in Sequence]]

*[[divayth-fyr|Divayth Fyr]]*: *"an incomprehensibly powerful world-shaping device."*  
*[[mephala|Mephala]]의 종 *Earl Leythen*: *"a place of power similar to the [[crystal-tower|Crystal Tower]], that can change the rules of reality and remake the world."*
