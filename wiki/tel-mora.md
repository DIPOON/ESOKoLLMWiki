# Tel Mora (텔 모라)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is the home of Mistress Dratha, who rules over both the small settlement at the base of her tower and the nearby town of Vos. She has recently fallen ill, leading to a crisis of leadership.
