# Barrow Trench (무덤 참호)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
A skyshard is located east of the delve, along the base of the cliff. They are mining ore throughout and are led by Overseer Basri. There is a camp with a cooking fire outside the delve. There is a Delivery Contract at the delve's entrance that grants the related quest. A note lies on the picnic table in the center of the northern chamber.
