# Sheogorath's Tongue (쉐오고라스의 혀)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The shrine inside is dedicated to Sheogorath.

The Lover can be found on the hill above the delve, to the east. Shivering Shrine is also near the delve.

''Kennixa's List'' can be found on the floor in the first hallway. The skyshard and the potions needed for Darkvale Brews can be found in the northern chamber. A chest can be found between two bookshelves in the western part of the same room. There is a chest up on a ledge in the western portion of the first large chamber. It can be accessed by traversing the tunnels and taking the path east when you reach the crossroads in the center of the delve.
