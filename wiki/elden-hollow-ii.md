# Elden Hollow II (엘든 할로우 II)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The story continues from the events of Elden Hollow I. In this version, Kargand needs your help rescuing his sister Bakkhara from a Daedric realm. She has been trapped there because of the ritual performed by Canonreeve Oraneth earlier.

You can learn about its location from Venomous Fauridil outside Elden Root, Hel Ra'Lala outside Wayrest, or Thongar the Stout outside Mournhold.
