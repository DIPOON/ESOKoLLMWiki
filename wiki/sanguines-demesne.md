# Sanguine's Demesne (생귄의 영지)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is the place of an eternal party held to honor Sanguine. Some of the participants seem to have trouble leaving when they have joined the party. It is advertised on a poster by the road from Mournhold to Stormhold. A copy of the lore book Modern Heretics can be found west of Sanguine's Demesne. Frogs, rats and snakes are found in numbers within. Alchemic nodes can be harvested throughout the dungeon.
