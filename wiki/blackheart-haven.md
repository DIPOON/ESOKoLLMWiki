# Blackheart Haven (검은심장 안식처)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The island is the hideout of the infamous Captain Blackheart and his crew. A pirate, Shifty Tom, wants you to help him turn over a new leaf by defeating his former captain and crewmates. You can learn about its location from Tiny Talisse in Rawl'kha, Mighty Mordra in Evermore, or Ferocious Jakidi in Riften.
