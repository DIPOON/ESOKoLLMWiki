# Khartag Point (카르타그 지점)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Home to Ropefish leader Khartag and his faction, it has been attacked by the Camonna Tong, a ruthless criminal organization.

The following letters can be found here: A One-Time Offer, Blood-Soaked Letter and My Dearest Love. Omaren Trial Transcript can be found in a boat at the entrance to the delve. Dragonflies and shroom beetles are found in numbers within.
