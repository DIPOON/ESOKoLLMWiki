# Hla Oad (흘라 오아드)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
__NOTOC__

Hla Oad was a tiny fishing village at the southern end of the Bitter Coast region, built along Odai River southwest of Balmora on Vvardenfell.

Circa , Hla Oad was not yet a town, though the humble fishing shack of Sarandas' family stood at the site of the future settlement.

Circa , it was an active settlement and it offered few services. The Camonna Tong criminal organization wielded considerable influence within the settlement. Among the few services available were independent operators such as Perien Aurelie the pawnbroker and Pallia Ceno, as well as Camonna Tong affiliated businesses that offered their services only to their own people. At the time, Fatleg's Drop-Off was the only notable tradehouse in the settlement, and it was owned by the Redguard Trasteve.
