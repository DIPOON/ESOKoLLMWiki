# Nchuleftingth (누슈레프팅쓰)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Apart from various Dwemer constructs, the dungeon has lately been occupied by treasure hunter Lenarmen and his hired Bronzefist mercenaries, searching for a powerful artifact. Butterflies, Centipedes, Dragonflies and Skavengers are found in numbers within.

The seven fragments for the Dwarven Theodolite pet and achievement are randomly dropped from enemies in this zone.
