# Cathnoquey (카스노퀘이)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Cathnoquey is an archipelago that lies somewhere between Tamriel and Akavir. It was conquered by Uriel Septim V in , and was still an inhabited Imperial territory and trade hub circa . According to dubious Imperial records, its main inhabitants are the Chimeri-quey, a tribal remnant of Chimer composed of fisherfolk who live on the coastlines in grand flotilla-townships of advanced design, and the Keptu-quey, men with tan brown skin said to have a strange obsession with drawing bulls and living in leaderless societies in military fortresses. One of its major exports is wasabi, a substance that has been popular in Skyrim since ancient times.
