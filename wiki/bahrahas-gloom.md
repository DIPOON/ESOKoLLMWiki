# Bahraha's Gloom (바라하의 우울)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The tomb served as the headquarters of a local cult of necromancers known as the Bleak Veil, which was led by Prince Hew's uncle. After discovering what they were doing inside, Prince Hew decided to act to save face by sealing them inside. However, rather than dying, they fully committed to their cause and turned themselves into undead.
