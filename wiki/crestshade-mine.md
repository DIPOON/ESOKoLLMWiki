# Crestshade Mine (마루그늘 광산)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The mine was responsible for most of Crestshade's prosperity, but was closed down when trouble came to the village. It has since been overrun by trolls. Bitterhand Brutes, Bitterhand Menders, Bitterhand Rogues, Bitterhand Scouts and Skeevers are found near the entrance. Fleshflies and rats are found in numbers within.
