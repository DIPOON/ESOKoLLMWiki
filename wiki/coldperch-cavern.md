# Coldperch Cavern (콜드퍼치 동굴)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is the lair of the hagraven Kraala Birdsong and her flocks of birds. After she entered into a relationship with a Winterborn leader, they have also moved into the cave, much to her displeasure.

The book, Birds of Wrothgar, and the note Letter to Mairrna can be found here. Birds of Wrothgar is sitting next to the corpse of its author, Haenelisse. ''Kraala's Journal'' is located upon Kraala Birdsong's altar in the southeastern chamber.
