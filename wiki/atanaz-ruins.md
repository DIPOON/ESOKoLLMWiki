# Atanaz Ruins (아타나즈 유적)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The tribe raids other villages, steals their valuables and kills the inhabitants. They also worship a wamasu, which resides deep within the ruins.

A guide to caring for this wamasu can be found near the beast. An old Orc's journal can be found next to his corpse. The ruins are home to two species of unique plants with healing properties.
