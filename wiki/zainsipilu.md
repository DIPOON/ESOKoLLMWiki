# Zainsipilu (자인시필루)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Owing to the immense lushness and fertility of the cavern's soil, Halinjirr chose it as his secret location to grow moon sugar. However, his operation was recently seized by the Black Darts.

The notes Journal of a Fallen Officer, ''Halinjirr's Notes, and Bandit's Letter can be found here.

The cave also contains the Ascendant Order Hideout, an area only accessible during Ascending Doubt.
