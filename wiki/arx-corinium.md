# Arx Corinium (악스 코리늄)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
While previously constructed and held by the Imperials, the fort has fallen into disrepair and is overrun with Lamias. A group of nereids now occupy the flooded fort and focus on restoring balance to the land. Rumors say the fort is home to a great source of power, although it is perverse, corrupted. You can learn about its location from Mindil the Untested in Marbruk, Abzag the Monster in Shornhelm, or Maela the Crone in Stormhold.
