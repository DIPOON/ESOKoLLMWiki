# Vos (보스)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is ruled over from Tel Mora by Telvanni wizard Mistress Dratha, who has recently fallen ill. Savarak Fels now rules over the town in the meantime as magistrate, though his leadership has quickly led to a decrease in quality of life.

A petition is posted on a board in town. A copy of Dark Elves, Dark Hearts can be found on the floor of a shed out in the saltrice paddies.
