# Thukhozod's Sanctum (쓰쿠조드의 성역)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Gorlar the Dark, a powerful Orc necromancer, resides deep within the ruins.

The notes Letter from Thukhozod and ''Gorlar's Journal, Part One and Part Two can be found here, and the book, The Legend of Thukhozod can be found here too.

The skyshard is located at the west end of the long hallway across the gap from the entrance. The fastest way is around the right corner as you enter, but beware the Bone Colossus standing between you and the hallway.
