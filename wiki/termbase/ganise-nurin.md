# Ganise Nurin (가니스 누린)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Ganise Nurin is a Breton found at Nurin Farm. Her father is William and her mother is Rosalie.

*
*

When you start the quest through one of the farmhands, you can speak to Rosalie:
:''"There has to be something, some trace ….<br>I'm not leaving until I know what happened."

After speaking with Rosalie, she will ask you to find Ganise to tell her it is time to give up searching for her missing father. Ganise can be found pacing in one of the wrecked buildings; when approached she will be thinking out loud:
:"This doesn't make sense. There has to be something … anything … some sign of what happened to my father. <br>Can you help me? Can you help me sift through these ashes?" 
:'You mother says it's time to give up.'
:"How can she say that? How could she leave without him? <br>She can give up if…
