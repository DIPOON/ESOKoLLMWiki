# Thibaut's Cairn (티오부트의 돌무덤)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The Cairn is named for its most noted "guest," the famed Thibault of Kvatch. Thibault was a general of Colovia, and in his time some hundreds of years ago he was one of the anchor points for the Hastrel Cohort. The details of Thibault's death have been lost.

Fleshflies and rats are found in numbers inside.
