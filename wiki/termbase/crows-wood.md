# Crow's Wood (까마귀 숲)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is inhabited by wolves, wisps, giant bats, ghosts, and various talking crows that collectively call themselves the Blackfeather Court. To reach it, look for a small cave on the shore just to the east of Davon's Watch. Inside, there's what appears to be a treasure chest. Touch it, and a portal will open that transports you into Crow's Wood. It seems the portal's creator didn't think too carefully about his choice of a non-tempting camouflage for his portal.
