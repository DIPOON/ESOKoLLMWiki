# Ashlands (애쉬랜즈)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Most of the region is inaccessible, aside from a narrow strip of land on Vvardenfell's northern coast, stretching from the West Gash to the Grazelands. The Forgotten Wastes, a wasteland on the slopes of Red Mountain, is situated in this part of the region. In the east of the region sits the Valley of Wind, housing the Cavern of the Incarnate.
