# Rkindaleft (르킨다레프트)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The ruins are inhabited by its dwarven guardians, as well as the crazed husks of Orcs adventurers that were slain and brought back to life via mysterious means.

The overland sets Briarheart, Mark of the Pariah, and Trinimac's Valor can drop here.

* Rkindaleft Objective: Thwart an ancient evil inside the thawing ruin of Rkindaleft. / You stopped an ancient mage from completing his dark plans.
