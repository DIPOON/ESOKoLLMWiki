# Yldzuun (이드준)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The Imperials were hired by Quintus Verres to stop his ex-wife, Tharayya. Rats and scorpions are found in numbers within.

Imperials and Dwarven constructs can be found fighting against each other in the western room, which is near the entrance. The western chamber has three sections: the northern section, the center, and the southern section. The north and south lead out to tunnels into other sections of the ruin. A heavy sack is in the southeastern corner of the northern section of the room. A chest lies along the western wall in the north. The center contains a Dwarven Centurion and a heavy sack. Tharayya's Journal, Entry 19 can be found on a table in the western room, surrounded by empty bottles.
