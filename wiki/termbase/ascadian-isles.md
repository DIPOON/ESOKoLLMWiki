# Ascadian Isles (에스케디언 제도)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The region is dotted with agricultural plantations under the jurisdiction of Redoran-controlled Suran, located in the northeast of the area. The Tribunal-controlled Vivec City, capital of Vvardenfell and home of Lord Vivec, is situated in the south of the region.

Amaya Lake Lodge is found here.
