# Brindle Home (브린들 홈)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Brindle Home, formerly Castle Brindle, is a settlement located far north of Skingrad, deep in the Great Forest of Cyrodiil.

During the Three Banners War, Castle Brindle was an expansive and strategically important fortress. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was considered to be in the Aldmeri Dominion's territory.
