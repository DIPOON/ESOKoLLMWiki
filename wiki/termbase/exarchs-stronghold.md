# Exarch's Stronghold (엑사치의 요새)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is located in the Valley of Scars.

It contains two boss fights, which you need to complete to clear the location: the troll Grothuska and Ordooth the Corruptor.

Two quest-related boss fights also take place here. Agganor is found here and must be killed during the quest Iron and Scales. As part of the quest The Serpent's Fang, you will gain access to a locked throne room containing Exarch Braadoth, the leader of the Iron Orcs, and his nirncrux-infused troll Kurzoth.
