# Hrota Cave (흐로타 동굴)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Wisps can be found throughout the cave, and will animate nearby corpses if not killed quickly enough. The cave contains several named wispmothers, but only Exulus needs to be killed to clear the delve. The vampire lord Zalar-do also lairs here. Torchbugs are found in great numbers within.

Exulus has a chance to drop the following chapters of the Assassins League style: Boots, Bows, Chests, Maces, and Swords.
