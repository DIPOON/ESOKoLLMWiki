# Mines of Khuras (쿠라스 광산)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Peasants have been enslaved by the cult and forced to mine. Rats are found in ample numbers within the tunnels.

A heavy sack is located near the end of the tunnel connecting the central chamber to the entrance. Bloodthorn Orders are located in this same tunnel, closer to the delve's entrance. The delve's boss is located in the north central chamber at the end of a dock where there is a pick embedded in stone.
