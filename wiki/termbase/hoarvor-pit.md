# Hoarvor Pit (호어보르 웅덩이)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
An ogrim named Oghezai is bound deep within the cave, being fed on by the blood-sucking hoarvors that inhabit it. The hoarvors also feed on dead senche, crocodiles, and Bosmer. A small valley is found in the northern part, infested with crocodiles. Centipedes, lizards, spiders and torchbugs are found in numbers within.
