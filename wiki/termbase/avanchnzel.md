# Avanchnzel (아반츤젤)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
There are several bookshelves in the ruin, as well as a lorebook . A Skyshard can be found in the southwestern chamber, while Thzallek Eft occupies the south hall. Centipedes and spiders are found in numbers within.

There is a dead Dunmer named Solvar in front of the ruin. If you head through the crevice north of his corpse, you'll find another skyshard, which is guarded by a sleeping bear. A chest can spawn just east of this skyshard.
