# Apocrypha (아포크리파)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is the larger of the two zones in the Necrom Chapter, the other being the Telvanni Peninsula.

It is inhabited by Hushed, Lurkers, Seekers and Tomeshells.

*

Two of the Delves required for Zone Completion are found on the Telvanni Peninsula.
