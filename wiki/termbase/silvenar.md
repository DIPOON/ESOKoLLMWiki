# Silvenar (실베나)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Upon first arriving to the city, it has been taken over by Ulthorn the Hound and his Houndsmen, intent on putting a stop to the marriage of the Silvenar and the Green Lady.

The merchant Rithros wanders around south of the city, near Sleepy Senche Overlook.

The Khajiit Embassy can be reached by climbing up the path leading out south of Silvenar Supplies. The Chancel of Divine Entreaty is located west of the city. Werewolves run amok below the city.
