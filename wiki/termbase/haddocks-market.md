# Haddock's Market (하독의 시장)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
They are operating their illegitimate business inside the delve. Sandroaches are found in numbers within.

Numerous beasts can be found in cages, though no nameplates appear when viewing them (excluding one caged monkey and rat). These include a bear, two bantam guars, a pony guar, a senche, and a spider.
