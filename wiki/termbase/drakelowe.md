# Drakelowe (드레이크로위)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Drakelowe is a settlement located along the Reed River in the Nibenay Basin of Cyrodiil.

During the Three Banners War of , Drakelowe Keep was an expansive and strategically important fortress located on the western bank of the river. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was initially located within the territory held by the Ebonheart Pact, although the keep changed hands many times over the course of the war.
