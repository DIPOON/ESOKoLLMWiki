# Kynesgrove (카인즈그로브)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The Keepers of the Grove live here and serve as priests of Kyne. Before going out in war, the local Nords used to go to Kynesgrove and pray to Kyne for good fortune. Unfortunately, the grove has recently become inhospitable, occupied with hostile mages and enthralled soldiers.

Gulfa Red-Beard the traveling merchant wanders the road between here and Thulvald's Logging Camp.
