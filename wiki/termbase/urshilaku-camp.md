# Urshilaku Camp (우르실라쿠 캠프)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It serves as the home of a group of Urshilaku Ashlanders.

Breynshad Alasien the traveling merchant stops near the camp on his route between here and the Forgotten Wastes.

*
*

The Urshilaku Camp Wayshrine is located just over a ridge to the southwest.

*Urshilaku Camp also appears in Morrowind, but in a different location. They are evidently a nomadic tribe.
