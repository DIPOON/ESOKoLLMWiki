# Loth'Na Caverns (로스'나 동굴)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The Argonian Visskar can be found at the top of the hill, and must be killed to clear the delve. The entrance to Skyreach Temple can also be found within. Snakes can be found in numbers within.

The interior of the delve consists of a valley with a central hill. Cross the stream from the entrance and take a right to crest the hill and reach Visskar, the boss of the delve. The skyshard can be found by instead taking a left at the stream; it can be found hidden behind some boulders. The Pledge of Obedience for the quest The Gray Passage can be reached from the top of the hill, as can the Bridge Triggers you need to activate to reach Skyreach Temple during the quest Dawn of the Exalted Viper.
