# Dragonstar (드래곤스타)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The townspeople are currently busy mining nirncrux for Regent Cassipia. The Dragonstar Arena can be found nearby. The Dragonstar Caravan Company was originally founded here, and later moved their main headquarters to Ska'vyn, though they still hold a regional office in town.

The town is patrolled by Caravan Guards. Horses, goats and skavengers can be found in the surrounding farms, and gravelclaws can be seen outside the mines.
