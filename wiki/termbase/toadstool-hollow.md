# Toadstool Hollow (독버섯 동굴)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Skeletons and giant bats can be found outside around the cave entrance. Inside, you'll find yourself on the top level of a frozen cavern. This part of the cave is infested with giant spiders. Head into the main chamber and proceed straight forward, and you'll have the opportunity to fight Dreadfang, the first of the delve's bosses. If you head east, you'll start your descent further into the cave. A tomb will start to poke out of the ice. A heavy sack is in this first hallway.
