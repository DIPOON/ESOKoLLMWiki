# Tribulation Crypt (시련의 묘실)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is the preferred cemetery of the Rivenspire nobility. The Legend of Dame Marcelle can be found in the last room of the crypt. The spirit of Marcelle Stenric can be encountered inside, where she will guide you. Centipedes and fleshflies are found in numbers within.
