# Shardrock (샤드록)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It has been overrun by Great Bears.

A treasure chest and a heavy sack may spawn in the farmyard. The house cannot be entered.

* Shardrock first appeared in Oblivion, where it was similarly plagued by bears.
* This location is not actually named in-game, but it is mentioned in the ''Traveler's Guide to West Weald''.
