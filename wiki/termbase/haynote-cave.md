# Haynote Cave (헤이노트 동굴)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
There is a cooking fire in the camp outside of the cave. Ore, jewelry seams, runestones and mushrooms can be harvested inside the cave.

Theurgist Thelas is standing on a wooden platform within the first large chamber. A chest can be found in the hallway leading to the next chamber, at the eastern bend where the road turns north. The chest lies beyond the scaffolding, and you can pick the lock from behind the scaffolding.
