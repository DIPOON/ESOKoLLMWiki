# Broken Tusk (부러진 엄니)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The shrine contains a number of dead Molag Bal Cultists, who were summoning Daedra to control and release upon the world in the name of Molag Bal, but were quickly overwhelmed.

A dying priest of Arkay and a cursed urn can be found deep within the shrine. A book of rituals can be found in the hallway leading to Naeraizozan's chamber. A chest can be found in this hallway as well. Another chest can be found in the northwest corner of Naeraizozan's room. Four banners depicting the emblem of Sheogorath can be found in the hallway beyond the entrance, and a single banner depicting Hircine can be found in the first large chamber.
