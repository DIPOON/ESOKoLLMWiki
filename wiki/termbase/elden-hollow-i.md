# Elden Hollow I (엘든 할로우 I)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
In this version, the Mages Guild is looking into reports of a strange ritual happening within Elden Hollow and need protection from the hostile Wood Orcs of the Darkfern Clan.

You can learn about its location from Venomous Fauridil outside Elden Root, Hel Ra'Lala outside Wayrest, or Thongar the Stout outside Mournhold.
