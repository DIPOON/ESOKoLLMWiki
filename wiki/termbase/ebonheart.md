# Ebonheart (에본하트)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is ruled by House Dres, but has a significant presence of House Redoran and House Hlaalu. The northern district of the city is inhabited by former Argonian slaves, who even have their own Hist tree, while Ashlander traders and Nord sawyers camp outside the southern gate.

The city is led by Drathus Othral. Its citizens are experiencing some racial tensions.
