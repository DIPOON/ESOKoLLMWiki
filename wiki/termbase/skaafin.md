# Skaafin (스카핀)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Skaafin are a race of horned, yellow-skinned, red-eyed humanoid Daedra associated with Clavicus Vile. They vary significantly in size, with some being quite small in stature to others being as tall as Altmer. They can be found in Vvardenfell, Cold-Blood Cavern, the Clockwork City Vault, and the Ventral Terminus.
