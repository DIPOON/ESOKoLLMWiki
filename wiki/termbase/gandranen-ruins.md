# Gandranen Ruins (간드라넨 유적)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The Ayleid Fenlords, practitioners of necromancy, made the halls the place where they would go on to live in undeath.

The bodies of some Blackguards can also be found here, along with their map piece. The exterior of the delve is surrounded by wasps and swarming wasps. Inside the delve you will meet Zahra, a Khajiit treasure hunter, who needs your help retrieving an Ayleid artifact.
