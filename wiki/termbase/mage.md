# Mage (메이지, 마법사, 마도사 등)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Mages are members of the Mages Guild that appear throughout the Hollow City after rescuing Vanus Galerion and unlocking the Mages Guildhall. They can also be encountered in the wilderness of Coldharbour fighting Daedra. They cannot be interacted with and have no unique dialogue.

*
*
*
*
*

As you exit the Reaver Citadel Mages Guild hall, you'll see a Mage fleeing in terror from a Daedric Titan, which flies in from the fortress ahead and lands on top of a building northeast of the guild hall and roars at the invasion force to intimidate them.
