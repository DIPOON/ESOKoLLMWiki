# Lainlyn (레인린)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Lainlyn (formerly known as '''Tava's Blessing''') is the capital of the eponymous barony in south-central Iliac Bay, in the province of Hammerfell. It is a minor port city occupied by the Crowns, who normally hold territories in the north and east. Lainlyn is most well-known for its shipyards, which have supplied vessels for the Kingdom of Sentinel's royal navy and Daggerfall Covenant in its heyday.
