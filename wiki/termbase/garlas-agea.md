# Garlas Agea (갈라스 아제아)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Captain Jena Apinia and her gang of deserters holed up in the ruins following their recent massacre in Bravil.

The delve boss, Ironfang, has a chance to drop the following chapters of the Assassins League style: Shoulders, Legs, Shields, Shoulders, and Staves.

Rats and Torchbugs are found in numbers within. Hazards include Swinging Blades
