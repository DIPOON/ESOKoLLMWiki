# Ashalmawia (아샬마위아)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Galur Rithari, a Dunmer vampire and former Buoyant Armiger, can be found in the sunken vaults, feeding on the Worm cultists. Cultists are performing rituals in multiple locations throughout the ruins, which will summon additional Daedra to attack you. Ash Hoppers, Fetcherflies and Shroom Beetles are found in numbers within. Fiendroth are found outside.
