# Nisin Cave (니신 동굴)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The cave contains Shadowed Path necromancers, Dremora, skeletons, and wisps. A chest can be found around the exterior of the cave, to the east, behind the mountain. Mushrooms grow within the cave.

The first room contains an altar where a Shadowed Path Defiler performs a ritual on a skeletal corpse. A heavy sack can be found at the end of the entrance tunnel, beside a crate. Across from the entrance tunnel, near a cooking station sits Tamrielic Artifacts, Part Two. A chest can be found in the tunnel leading to the next chamber, in front of where Volgo the Harrower stands.
