# March of Sacrifices (희생의 행군)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It is part of the Wolfhunter DLC, along with Moon Hunter Keep.

The entrance can be found at a Hircine statue southwest of Serpent's Grotto in Greenshade. Spectral animals such as goats, herons and springboks can be found within.

The following item sets will drop in the dungeon: Hanu's Compassion, Blood Moon, Haven of Ursus, and Balorgh.
