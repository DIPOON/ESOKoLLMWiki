# Hag Fen (마녀의 습지)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The Bloodthorn Cult have set up operations in the south of the marsh around Tangle Rock. They have struck up a deal with the hag coven in the northern fens to launch joint assaults on the Beldama Wyrd.

The Exorcised Coven Cottage player house is located in central-eastern Hag Fen. A tower located south of the cottage is inhabited by Bloodthorn cultists. A backpack and a water skin are located in the ruined tower, and a chest can spawn west of the tower. Crocodiles, as well as smaller passive snakes, lurk near the water.
