# Bitter Coast (비터 코스트)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It stretches from Khartag Point in the north, past the Redoran town of Balmora on the edge of the West Gash, to the Gold Coast Trading Company/House Hlaalu outpost of Seyda Neen in the south. The Ahemmusa Ashlander Camp can be found in the region.
