# Bleaker's Way (블리커의 길)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
According to ''Ulf's Torn Journal and Rayno's Scorched Journal'', which can be found among the ruins, the village was home to the feuding families of the Bleakers and the Dalvilu, who eventually killed each other and left the place deserted. Now the ruins are occupied by giants.

Bleaker's Way does not have its own map marker.
