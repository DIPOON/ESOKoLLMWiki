# Fungal Grotto II (곰팡이 동굴 II)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The storyline continues from the events of Fungal Grotto I. In this version, Defender Two-Blades needs you to defeat the Spider Cultists who have taken control of a dangerous artifact called the Obsidian Husk.

You can learn about its location from Mindil the Untested in Vulkhel Guard, Abzag the Monster in Daggerfall, or Kailstig the Axe in Davon's Watch.
