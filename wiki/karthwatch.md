# Karthwatch (카스워치)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
It has recently been struck by a harrowstorm, which killed most of the residents, or turned them into mindless harrowfiends. The remaining inhabitants have been either been transformed into hollow harrowed husks, or have been abducted by Reachmen.

Lodestone, one of the musical instruments collected for the Bards College in Solitude, can be found behind the Karthwatch windmill.
