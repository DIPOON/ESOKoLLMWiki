# Bad Man's Hallows (나쁜 남자의 성지)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Mobs have a chance to drop the antiquity lead for the Ring of the Pale Order. Bosses have a chance to drop the antiquity lead for the Ring of the Wild Hunt.

Bad Man Followers live inside the clearing, where they are currently about to celebrate the Harvest Festival, dedicated to the Bad Man. Multiple ressource nodes are found in both areas. You can find a lake fishing hole on the surface and a foul fishing hole in the caverns. Butterflies, frogs, rabbits, and squirrels are found in numbers on the surface while rats and snakes are found in the caverns.
