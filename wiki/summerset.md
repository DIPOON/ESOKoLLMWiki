# Summerset (서머셋)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
The zone includes the entirety of Summerset Isle, the largest island of the Summerset archipelago. It is included with the Summerset Chapter of the game. Summerset also contains an entrance to the Coral Aerie group dungeon, but the Ascending Tide DLC is needed in order to access it.

One of the Delves required for Zone Completion is found on Artaeum.
