# Verrik (베릭)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
Verrik is a Reachman assassin with the Bloodthorn cult who is part of the plot to assassinate King Casimir of Daggerfall.

He was approached by Captain gro-Mak, a Covenant spy secretly working for a cabal of war profiteers, to recruit his men; but his offer did not work out quite as planned.
