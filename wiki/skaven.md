# Skaven (스카벤)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
__NOTOC__
Skaven (also known as '''Ska'vyn) is one of the eight major cities in the province of Hammerfell, situated in the region of Sunforge in central Hammerfell. In the Third Era, the city was the seat of power for the eponymous Kingdom of Skaven'''.

Skaven is found in north-central Hammerfell near the Dragontail Mountains, where old Yokudan traditions continue to flourish by the Crowns. On the northern outskirts of Ska'vyn lies the Caverns of Wry, which contains herds of Skav Bats that are bred to produce dairy by the locals. Their signature Bat Cheese is supposedly paired well with Pomegranate wine. Northwest of the kingdom, across from the Bjoulsae River is the town of Candlemass.
