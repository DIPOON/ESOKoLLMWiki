# Imperial Sewers (임페리얼  하수도)

## 채택 근거 (UESP 자동 보강)
For those who own the Imperial City DLC or are members of ESO Plus, the sewers serve as the means of entry to the city and players will be ported straight to their own Alliance's base of operations when joining an Imperial City Campaign. The Imperial Sewers plays similarly to a Public Dungeon, but with PvP in all areas except a few small alliance safe zones. The alliance safe zones contain ladders that go to all the city districts, leading to small rally points in the city.
