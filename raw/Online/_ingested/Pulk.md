Pulk's cave network is currently being used as a hideout by a bandit gang led by [[ON:Bralsa Inlador|Bralsa Inlador]]. These bandits have targeted the nearby settlements of [[ON:Molag Mar|Molag Mar]], [[ON:Sadrith Mora|Sadrith Mora]], and [[ON:Vos|Vos]], and you may be sent here on a [[ON:Hall of Justice|Hall of Justice]] to [[ON:Tax Deduction|recover stolen taxes]] and prevent the bandits from pulling off even more heists.

==Notes==
* Pulk also [[MW:Pulk|appears]] in [[MW:Morrowind|''TES III: Morrowind'']].
* Prior to Update 16, you could be sent to Pulk to track down [[ON:Bulag Idolus|Bulag Idolus]] (found hiding on the lower levels) as part of the deprecated quest [[ON:A Friend In Need|A Friend In Need]].
* The bandits have attempted and partially succeeded at taming the resident nix-hounds of the cavern. However as a result, several of them are found in a wounded state, holding their ribs, which makes them slower to draw their weapons when initiating combat.
