The ruins are inhabited by its [[ON:Dwarven Automatons (creatures)|dwarven guardians]], as well as the crazed husks of [[ON:Orc|Orcs]] adventurers that were slain and brought back to life via mysterious means.

The overland sets [[Online:Briarheart|Briarheart]], [[Online:Mark of the Pariah|Mark of the Pariah]], and [[Online:Trinimac's Valor|Trinimac's Valor]] can drop here.{{Vn|section=Drops}}

==Notes==
*The [[Online:Eye of Zthenganaz|Eye of Zthenganaz]] relic for the [[ON:House of Orsimer Glories|House of Orsimer Glories]] can be found buried in a snowdrift to the right of the cave entrance. {{Map Link|16022}}
*Half of the [[Online:Ancient Orc Style|Ancient Orc style]] can be obtained in Rkindaleft, with the other half found in [[Online:Old Orsinium|Old Orsinium]].
*The [[ON:Rkindaleft Dwarven Style|Rkindaleft Dwarven style]] is themed after this location. Despite the name, it is actually available from the [[ON:New Life Festival|New Life Festival]] event.
