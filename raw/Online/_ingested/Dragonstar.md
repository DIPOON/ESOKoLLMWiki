}} The townspeople are currently busy mining nirncrux for [[Online:Regent Cassipia|Regent Cassipia]]. The [[Online:Dragonstar Arena|Dragonstar Arena]] can be found nearby. The [[ON:Dragonstar Caravan Company|Dragonstar Caravan Company]] was originally founded here, and later moved their main headquarters to [[Lore:Ska'vyn|Ska'vyn]], though they still hold a regional office in town.
[[File:ON-place-Dragonstar 02.jpg|thumb|right|Outer walls of Dragonstar]]
The town is patrolled by [[Online:Caravan Guard|Caravan Guards]]. [[Online:Horse|Horses]], [[Online:Goat|goats]] and [[Online:Skavenger|skavengers]] can be found in the surrounding farms, and [[Online:Gravelclaw|gravelclaws]] can be seen outside the mines.

A [[Online:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can spawn in the shed in front of the lowest entrance to the nirncrux mine, near the book ''[[Online:In Praise of Regent Cassipia|In Praise of Regent Cassipia]]''. Another shed can be found at the highest level, in front of a barricaded door. A heavy sack can spawn inside this shed, and a [[Online:chest|chest]] can spawn behind it.
