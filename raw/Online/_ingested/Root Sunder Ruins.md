Sitting now long-abandoned, the ruins have now become inhabited by all manners of local wildlife who have come to reclaim it for the forest. [[ON:Nodes|Resource Nodes]] are present inside. [[ON:Beetle|Beetles]], [[ON:Lizard|Lizards]] and [[ON:Torchbug|Torchbugs]] are found in numbers within.

The skyshard is located in the chamber second-left from the entry point (southwest), near one of the quest markers for "If the Dead Could Talk".[[File:ON-interior-Root Sunder Ruins.jpg|thumb|right|Root Sunder Ruins Interior]][[File:ON-interior-Root Sunder Ruins 02.jpg|thumb|right|Lower level]]

Bosses have a chance to drop the {{Item Link|Coral Clasp|quality=5|antiquityid=343}} [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity lead]] for the [[ON:Pearls of Ehlnofey|Pearls of Ehlnofey]].

There's a cooking fire in the camp outside the ruins, northwest of the entrance. [[Online:Tandare (Root Sunder Ruins)|Tandare]] stands beside a campfire next to the ruin's entrance. Sirdor, Tancaano the Elder, and Uryaamo's journals can be found next to her fire after the objective quest is complete.
