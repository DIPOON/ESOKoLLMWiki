Thibault was a general of Colovia, and in his time some hundreds of years ago he was one of the anchor points for the Hastrel Cohort. The details of Thibault's death have been lost.

[[ON:Fleshflies|Fleshflies]] and [[ON:Rat|rats]] are found in numbers inside.

[[Online:Rithlen|Rithlen]] the [[Online:Traveling Merchants|traveling merchant]] can be found walking along the road east of the cairn.
