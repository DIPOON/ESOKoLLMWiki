[[Online:Tellanil|Tellanil]] the [[Online:Traveling Merchants|traveling merchant]] walks along the road between [[Online:Valeguard|Valeguard]] and the tradepost.

== Notes ==
*The wayshrine was previously labeled "Baandari Post Wayshrine" before discovery, and "Baandari Tradepost Wayshrine" afterwards. Around June 2023, it was changed to "Baandari Market Wayshrine". The location itself is called "Baandari Trading Post".
*This location is referred to as "Baandari Tradepost" in several bits of dialogue in Malabal Tor. That name is also displayed while exiting buildings in town.
