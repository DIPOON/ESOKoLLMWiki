The ruins have recently become occupied by the [[ON:Duneraider Bandits|Duneraider bandits]], who are scavenging them for relics they can sell outside. [[ON:Beetle|Beetles]], [[ON:Lizard|lizards]], [[ON:Scorpion|scorpions]] and [[ON:Snake|snakes]] are found in numbers within. Hazards include: [[ON:Traps#Flame Wall|Flame Walls]], [[ON:Traps#Spike Trap|Spike Traps]]
