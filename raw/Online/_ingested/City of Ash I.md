==Notes==
*In [[ON:Patch/1.6.5|Patch 1.6.5]], all [[ON:Imp|Imps]] were replaced with  [[ON:Scamp|Scamps]].
*Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Normal City of Ash was designed for levels 28–31, and Veteran City of Ash was designed for Veteran Ranks 1-4. Until the Veteran Rank increase in Update 7, Veteran City of Ash did not scale like the other dungeons.
* Prior to Update 12, City of Ash I was only available in Normal mode, and [[ON:City of Ash II|City of Ash II]] was only available in Veteran mode. These were named ''City of Ash'' and ''Veteran City of Ash'' respectively.
