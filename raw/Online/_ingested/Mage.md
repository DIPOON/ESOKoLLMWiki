'''Mages''' are members of the [[Online:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] that appear throughout [[Online:the Hollow City|the Hollow City]] after rescuing [[Online:Vanus Galerion|Vanus Galerion]] and unlocking the [[ON:Mages Guildhall (Hollow City)|Mages Guildhall]]. They can also be encountered in the wilderness of Coldharbour fighting Daedra. They cannot be interacted with and have no unique dialogue.

==Quest-Related Events==
===The Citadel Must Fall===
As you exit the [[Online:The Reaver Citadel|Reaver Citadel]] Mages Guild hall, you'll see a Mage fleeing in terror from a [[Online:Daedric Titan|Daedric Titan]], which flies in from the fortress ahead and lands on top of a building northeast of the guild hall and roars at the invasion force to intimidate them.
:'''Mage:''' ''"Tongues of Xarxes! What is that thing?"''
:'''Mage:''' ''"Make it go away! Make it go away!"''

===War Within===
Generic Mages are also encountered in [[ON:Sanity's Edge|Sanity's Edge]] as fictional dream simulacrums. They are found throughout the trial in captive states or being tortured through various means.
