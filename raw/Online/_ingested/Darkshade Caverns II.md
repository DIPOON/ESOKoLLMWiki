The caverns also contain the hidden [[ON:Dwemer|Dwemer]] ruin of Rkugamz. 

The storyline continues from the event of [[ON:Darkshade Caverns I|Darkshade Caverns I]]. In this version, a [[ON:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] researcher named [[ON:Shatasha|Shatasha]] needs your help destroying a dangerous Dwemer mechanism within Rkugamz, before it corrupts Deshaan above.

You can learn about its location from [[ON:Mad Zebba|Mad Zebba]] outside [[ON:Elden Root|Elden Root]], [[ON:Lady Blade|Lady Blade]] outside [[ON:Wayrest|Wayrest]], or [[ON:Hranir Icy-Mane|Hranir Icy-Mane]] outside [[ON:Mournhold|Mournhold]].

Its [[ON:Dungeon Sets|dungeon sets]] are [[ON:Armor of Truth|Armor of Truth]], [[ON:Netch's Touch|Netch's Touch]], and [[ON:Strength of the Automaton|Strength of the Automaton]]. Darkshade Caverns II is accessible starting at level 42.

==Notes==
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Normal Darkshade Caverns was designed for Levels 20–23, and Veteran Darkshade Caverns was designed for Veteran Ranks 6–10.
* Prior to Update 12, [[ON:Darkshade Caverns I|Darkshade Caverns I]] was only available in Normal mode, and Darkshade Caverns II was only available in Veteran mode. These were named ''Darkshade Caverns'' and ''Veteran Darkshade Caverns'' respectively.
