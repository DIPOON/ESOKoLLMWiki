According to a [[ON:Aldmeri Court Transcript|transcript]] found in the ruins, a [[ON:Thaliel the Voracious|person]] found guilty of [[Lore:Necromancy|necromancy]] and other various crimes has been imprisoned there. [[ON:Beetle|Beetles]] are found in numbers within. [[ON:Traps#Lava_Pool|Lava Pools]] scour throughout the delve.

[[ON:Magdra Ibrai|Magdra Ibrai]] can be found in the Flame Lair during the related quest.
