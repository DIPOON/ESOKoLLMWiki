It can be found down a gully between the [[ON:Brass Fortress#Wayshrine|Brass Fortress Wayshrine]] and [[ON:Daram Stables|Daram Stables]], where ramshackle structures have been constructed from scrap metal. [[ON:Razgurug|Razgurug]] is the self-appointed mayor of Slag Town who gives you [[ON:Repeatable Quests|daily quests]] to help the unfortunate denizens of his town.
