It contains two boss fights, which you need to complete to clear the location: the troll [[ON:Grothuska|Grothuska]] and [[ON:Ordooth the Corruptor|Ordooth the Corruptor]]. 

Two quest-related boss fights also take place here. [[ON:Agganor|Agganor]] is found here and must be killed during the quest [[ON:Iron and Scales|Iron and Scales]]. As part of the quest [[ON:The Serpent's Fang|The Serpent's Fang]], you will gain access to a locked throne room containing [[ON:Exarch Braadoth|Exarch Braadoth]], the leader of the Iron Orcs, and his nirncrux-infused troll [[ON:Kurzoth|Kurzoth]].

[[ON:Skavenger|Skavengers]] are found in ample numbers within.

==Notes==
* Prior to Update 12, this location was an instanced 4-player [[File:ON-mapicon-GroupDelve.png|20px]][[ON:Group Delves|Group Delve]]. Notably, there were two Iron Orc Fire Shamans guarding Grothuska when the location was a Group Delve, and all the bosses both outside and inside the delve itself were difficulty 2 or 3. They have now all been reduced to difficulty 1.
*None of the delve bosses here are necessary for the quests in the region, and the quest-related bosses don't count towards completion of this delve.
