==Notes==
*Blackwood previously [[OB:Blackwood|appeared]] in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]'', although it only included the Cyrodilic territory of County Leyawiin. A [[Online:Blackwood Borderlands|portion of the area]] was also visited in the Dark Brotherhood DLC.
*Much of the Black Marsh area was originally planned to be the [[ON:Adventure Zones|Adventure Zone]] version of [[ON:Murkmire|Murkmire]], but was later re-purposed into Blackwood.
