It is the lair of the [[ON:Hagraven|hagraven]] [[ON:Kraala Birdsong|Kraala Birdsong]] and her flocks of birds. After she entered into a relationship with a [[ON:Winterborn|Winterborn]] leader, they have also moved into the cave, much to her displeasure.

The book, ''[[ON:Birds of Wrothgar|Birds of Wrothgar]]'', and the note ''[[ON:Letter to Mairrna|Letter to Mairrna]]'' can be found here. Birds of Wrothgar is sitting next to the corpse of its author, [[ON:Haenelisse|Haenelisse]]. ''[[ON:Kraala's Journal|Kraala's Journal]]'' is located upon [[ON:Kraala Birdsong|Kraala Birdsong]]'s altar in the southeastern chamber.
