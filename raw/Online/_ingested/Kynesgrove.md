The [[Lore:Keepers of the Grove|Keepers of the Grove]] live here and serve as priests of [[Lore:Kyne|Kyne]]. Before going out in war, the local [[Online:Nord|Nords]] used to go to Kynesgrove and pray to Kyne for good fortune. Unfortunately, the grove has recently become inhospitable, occupied with hostile mages and enthralled soldiers.

[[Online:Gulfa Red-Beard|Gulfa Red-Beard]] the [[Online:Traveling Merchants|traveling merchant]] wanders the road between here and [[Online:Thulvald's Logging Camp|Thulvald's Logging Camp]].

[[File:ON-place-Kynesgrove Wayshrine.jpg|thumb|left|Kynesgrove Wayshrine]] '''Kynesgrove Wayshrine''' is located northwest of the woods. The [[ON:horse|horse]] [[ON:Peri|Peri]] will appear here during [[ON:The Dragonguard's Legacy|The Dragonguard's Legacy]], offering permanent one-way [[ON:transport|transport]] to [[ON:The Valley of Blades (Dragonhold Prologue)|the Valley of Blades]]. During the [[ON:New Life Festival|New Life Festival]], "Gateway to the New Life Festival" portals will be opened at the various [[ON:Event Exchange|Event Exchanges]], offering one-way transport to this wayshrine due to its proximity to the [[ON:New Life Tent|New Life Tent]].
