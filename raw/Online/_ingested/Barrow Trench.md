}}A skyshard is located east of the delve, along the base of the cliff. They are mining ore throughout and are led by [[Online:Overseer Basri|Overseer Basri]]. There is a camp with a cooking fire outside the delve. There is a Delivery Contract at the delve's entrance that grants the related quest. A [[Online:Note from Jahla|note]] lies on the picnic table in the center of the northern chamber.

The delve's boss is located atop a wooden platform in the southern side of the southwestern chamber. The delve's skyshard is located outside the one-way exit outside the eastern side of the southwestern chamber. [[ON:Rat|Rats]] are found in number within.
