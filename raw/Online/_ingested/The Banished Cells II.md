==Notes==
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Normal Banished Cells was designed for levels 12-15 and Veteran Banished Cells was designed for Veteran Ranks 1-5.
* Prior to Update 12, [[ON:The Banished Cells I|The Banished Cells I]] was only available in Normal mode, and The Banished Cells II was only available in Veteran mode. These were named ''The Banished Cells'' and ''Veteran Banished Cells'' respectively.
