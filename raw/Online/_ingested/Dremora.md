# Online:Dremora

<!-- UESP / ns 144 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

{{Trail|Races}}[[File:ON-npc-Dremora.jpg|thumb|right|A Dremora]]
'''Dremora''' are an intelligent, humanoid [[Online:Daedra|Daedric]] [[Online:Races|race]] that despises mortals. They refer to themselves as the '''Kyn'''. While Dremora are commonly associated with [[ON:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]], various clans can be found serving most of the Princes, though some of the clans are not aligned with anyone and act as mercenaries in [[Online:Oblivion|Oblivion]]. 

Dremora society operates in a rigid caste system with an emphasis on loyalty and clan ties. On the other end of the scale are the "Clanless", individual Dremora that for any number of reasons are not part of any clan. These individuals can be looked down upon depending on their situation.

When multiple clans are serving the same Prince, intense rivalries can form. An example of this would be the [[ON:Deathbringer Clan|Deathbringer]] and the [[ON:Foolkillers Clan|Foolkillers]] clans that serve [[Online:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]], which resulted in the latter being reduced to a single being.

The [[ON:Xivilai|Xivilai]] and [[ON:Xivkyn|Xivkyn]] are races similar to the Dremora.

The Dremora have a chance to drop the {{Item Link|antiquityid=438|quality=5|Void Alloy Rivets}} [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity lead]] for the [[Online:Spaulder of Ruin|Spaulder of Ruin]].

For more information, see the [[Lore:Dremora|lore]] article.
