}}The city is ruled by [[ON:Count Carolus Aquilarios|Count Carolus Aquilarios]], although the count is locked in a power struggle with [[ON:Primate Artorius|Primate Artorius]] and his [[ON:Order of the Hour|Order of the Hour]]. Kvatch maintains loyalty to the [[Lore:Empire of Cyrodiil (Interregnum)|Empire of Cyrodiil]], and faces hostility from [[ON:Anvil|Anvil]] which claims dominion over the entirety of an independent Gold Coast.

A Monument to Rislav is located in front of the [[ON:Kvatch Arena|arena]].

[[ON:Chicken|Chickens]] and [[ON:Goat|Goats]] are seen throughout the city.
