It has recently been struck by a [[ON:Harrowstorm|harrowstorm]], which killed most of the residents, or turned them into mindless [[ON:Harrowfiend|harrowfiends]]. The remaining inhabitants have been either been transformed into hollow [[ON:Harrowed|harrowed]] husks, or have been abducted by [[ON:Reachmen|Reachmen]].

Lodestone, one of the musical instruments collected for the [[Online:Bards College|Bards College]] in [[Online:Solitude|Solitude]], can be found behind the Karthwatch windmill.

The entrance to [[ON:Bleakridge Barrow|Bleakridge Barrow]] is located within the town's outer edge, which in turn leads to Red Eagle Ridge in the mountain above the town.
