This delve contains a [[ON:Skyshard|Skyshard]], three copies of ''[[ON:Skooma Runner Logs|Skooma Runner Logs]]'', and a ''[[ON:Note to Marianas|Note to Marianas]]''. The [[ON:Shrine of the Golden Eye|Shrine of the Golden Eye]] is located through one of two hidden doors here and must be visited to complete the quest ''Eye of Fate.''

Due to being a [[Lore:Skooma|skooma]]-manufacturing den, a large number of lootable alchemy bottles are found inside, offering a good source of potions concentrated in one location. [[ON:Rat|Rats]] and [[ON:Torchbug|torchbugs]] are found in numbers within.

==Layout==
===Ruined Reliquary===
You will come here during A Moonlit Shadow. A trapdoor in the eastern chamber leads down into the ruined reliquary. Generic [[Online:Twilight Cantor|Twilight Cantors]] lie dead on the ground within. 

===Abandoned Altar===
You'll fight [[Online:Ta'khida-morna|Ta'khida-morna]] here during A Moonlit Shadow.
