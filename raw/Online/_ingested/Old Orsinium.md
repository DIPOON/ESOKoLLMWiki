It consists of the ravaged and sieged remnants of one of the previous [[ON:Orsinium|Orsinium]]. Now, only wildlife and [[ON:Winterborn|Winterborn]] [[ON:Reachmen|Reachmen]] inhabit it.

On the eastern side of the forges is a door leading to Old Orsinium Shrine, which you need to visit when doing [[Online:Flames of Forge and Fallen|Flames of Forge and Fallen]]. A southern exit from the shrine will lead you back to the central hub room.

Mobs have a chance to drop {{Item Link|Anvil of Old Orsinium|quality=5|antiquityid=249}} [[Online:Antiquity Leads|antiquity lead]] for the [[Online:Anvil of Old Orsinium|furnishing of the same name]].

Bosses have a chance to drop {{Item Link|Malacath's Brutal Might Loop|quality=5|antiquityid=132}} antiquity lead for the [[Online:Malacath's Band of Brutality|Malacath's Band of Brutality]].

The overland sets [[Online:Briarheart|Briarheart]], [[Online:Mark of the Pariah|Mark of the Pariah]], and [[Online:Trinimac's Valor|Trinimac's Valor]] can drop here.{{Vn|section=Drops}}

==Notes==
*Half of the [[Online:Ancient Orc Style|Ancient Orc style]] can be obtained in Old Orsinium, with the other half found in [[Online:Rkindaleft|Rkindaleft]].
*Two relics for the [[ON:House of Orsimer Glories|House of Orsimer Glories]] can be found near and in Old Orsinium:
**The Horn of Beasts can be found in a small cave on the shoreline just southeast of the entrance, guarded by a troll.
**The Hammer of Glass can be found near some beds on the way out of the shrine within the ruins.
