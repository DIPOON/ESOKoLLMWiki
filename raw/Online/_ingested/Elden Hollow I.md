==Layout==
[[File:ON-interior-Elden Hollow I.jpg|thumb|right|Elden Hollow]]
When you arrive, you'll find yourself in a flooded chamber. [[ON:Justiciar Elanar|Justiciar Elanar]] is guarding the tunnel leading further into the Hollow alongside two [[ON:Thalmor Adept|Thalmor Adepts]] and three [[ON:Thalmor Justiciar|Thalmor Justiciars]]. Two copies of a [[ON:Time is of the Essence|letter]] are located on large bricks near the mouth of the tunnel. You'll need to fall down a waterfall to progress. A chest can spawn at the base of the falls.

The first group of enemies consists of one Justiciar, two Darkfern Diggers, one healer and two maulers. A ''[[ON:Note from Akash|Note from Akash]]'' is located atop a crate on the east side of this first room. A chest can spawn next to the crate. 

There are more diggers in the next room. The first strangler is located along the wall on the left-hand side. There is a Darkfern Archer on the eastern scaffolding, and a Thalmor Justiciar patrolling the room. A [[ON:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can spawn on the western corner on the southern end of the raised platform. Another Justiciar and an archer are standing on the southern scaffolding. There are three diggers and two healers in the north half of this room. Another strangler lies to the south, beneath the scaffolding. Two diggers are also working beneath the scaffolding. In the hallway leading to the next area, you'll find several Darkfern Maulers, a healer and an archer.

A large group of Darkfern Orcs and a few Thalmor agents are in the large room beyond. Another copy of the ''Note from Akash'' is located atop a crate on the north side of this room. The door to the next half of the dungeon opens after Akash gra-Mal is defeated. A fully functional blacksmithing station is located near the flaming bone altar Akash is guarding. A ''[[ON:Letter to Akash|Letter to Akash]]'' is sitting on top of a bench next to that blacksmithing station. A copy of the lore book, ''[[ON:Civility and Etiquette: Wood Orcs I|Civility and Etiquette: Wood Orcs I]]'', is located atop a large stone in front of the burning altar.

If you head south from Akash's room, you'll enter a short corridor with [[ON:Spriggan|spriggans]] in it. The [[ON:Ancient Spriggan|Ancient Spriggan]],  mini boss, resides here. Heading north brings you into a corridor filled with Orcs, which you need to pass through in order to progress. This passage opens up to a bridge above the lower section of the dungeon, which leads down into a tunnel with several [[ON:Alit|alits]] and stranglers in it. You can jump down from the bridge to skip Chokethorn, though this isn't advised if you're running Elden Hollow I for a daily pledge.

The next boss is Chokethorn, a giant strangler located in a partially flooded chamber. The tunnel beyond leads to another large chamber. A book titled ''[[ON:Savior of the Altmer, Part 1|Savior of the Altmer, Part 1]]'' is sitting on top of a large, partially broken crate to the left as you exit the tunnel. ''[[ON:Savior of the Altmer, Part 2|Savior of the Altmer, Part 2]]'' is located a little further out from the tunnel, atop a crate to the right, closer to the flaming altar. A chest can spawn in the northwest corner of this room, near Nenesh gro-Mal. The bone altar in this room is alight with blue fire. A scroll titled ''[[ON:The Realm of Shadows|The Realm of Shadows]]'' is sat upon the altar. A heavy sack and a chest can spawn near it. When you clear a path to the bone altar, the mages appear to destroy it. The tunnels leading west and north out of this chamber both lead you to Canonreeve Oraneth's dwelling. A heavy sack can spawn to the right of the western tunnel. ''[[ON:Savior of the Altmer, Part 3|Savior of the Altmer, Part 3]]'' is located on top of a crate to the left of the northern tunnel. A heavy sack can spawn next to this crate, and a chest can spawn to the right of the northern tunnel.

A giant alit mini boss named Leafseether resides within the northern tunnel. Canonreeve Oraneth lies in the next room, and must be defeated in order to clear the dungeon. When the canonreeve dies, the mages enter the room. On Veteran, the Scroll of Glorious Battle is located just to the right of the door as you enter. If you go behind the dark rift Oraneth has opened up and peer beyond the vines, you'll see a massive [[ON:Bogdan the Nightflame|Daedric creature]] pacing beyond.

==Notes==
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Normal Elden Hollow was designed for levels 20–23, and Veteran Elden Hollow was designed for  Veteran Ranks 6–10.
* Prior to Update 12, Elden Hollow I was only available in Normal mode, and [[ON:Elden Hollow II|Elden Hollow II]] was only available in Veteran mode. These were named ''Elden Hollow'' and ''Veteran Elden Hollow'' respectively.
* Several books can be found inside the dungeon, mostly featuring the thoughts of Canonreeve Oraneth or the communication between Oraneth and Darkfern Orcs: ''[[ON:Letter to Akash|Letter to Akash]]'', ''[[ON:Note from Akash|Note from Akash]]'', ''[[ON:Savior of the Altmer|Savior of the Altmer]]'', and ''[[ON:Time is of the Essence|Time is of the Essence]]''.
