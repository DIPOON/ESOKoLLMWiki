According to ''[[Online:Ulf's Torn Journal|Ulf's Torn Journal]]'' and ''[[Online:Rayno's Scorched Journal|Rayno's Scorched Journal]]'', which can be found among the ruins, the village was home to the feuding families of the Bleakers and the Dalvilu, who eventually killed each other and left the place deserted. Now the ruins are occupied by [[Online:Giant|giants]].

Bleaker's Way does not have its own map marker.
