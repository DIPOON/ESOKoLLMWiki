They are operating their illegitimate business inside the delve. [[ON:Sandroach|Sandroaches]] are found in numbers within.

Numerous beasts can be found in cages, though no nameplates appear when viewing them (excluding one caged [[ON:Monkey|monkey]] and [[ON:Rat|rat]]). These include a [[ON:Bear|bear]], two [[ON:Bantam Guar|bantam guars]], a [[ON:Pony Guar|pony guar]], a [[ON:Senche|senche]], and a [[ON:Spider|spider]].

==Notes==
* A [[ON:Group Bosses|group boss]] named [[ON:Packleader Stigthar|Packleader Stigthar]] can be found outside Haddock's Market during the [[Online:Zeal of Zenithar|Zeal of Zenithar]] event. He is related to the event quest [[ON:The Unrefusable Offer|The Unrefusable Offer]].
* When Haddock's Market was released, it was littered with "Missing" texture error boxes.
* Prior to Update 12, this location was an instanced 4-player [[File:ON-mapicon-GroupDelve.png|20px]][[ON:Group Delves|Group Delve]]. Bosses were Boss rank. [[ON:Ariana's Guard|Ariana's Guards]], [[ON:Greycloak Necromancer|Greycloak Necromancers]] and [[ON:Mudcrab|Mudcrabs]] were also present.
