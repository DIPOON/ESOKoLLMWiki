}} The town has been taken over by the [[Online:Red Rook Bandits|Red Rook Bandits]], but a number of residents are mounting a resistance. [[Online:Crosswych Citizen|Crosswych Citizens]] roam the streets. [[Online:Resistance Fighter|Resistance Fighters]] can be found in several locations while the town is being liberated.

The mines are located up the hill north from the center of town. The settlement's [[Online:skyshards|skyshard]] is located on the wooden platforms above the mine's lower entrance. Several [[Online:Dog|dogs]] and [[Online:Cat|cats]] roam the town. A small herd of [[Online:Sheep|sheep]] grazes near the local Fighters Guild.

[[Online:Red Petal Bastion|Red Petal Bastion]] lies south of town.
