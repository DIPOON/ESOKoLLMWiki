It is the stronghold of the [[ON:The Serpent (Celestial)|Celestial Serpent]] and his [[ON:Scaled Court|Scaled Court]]. It has both a Normal and a Veteran mode, as well as a "Hard Mode" which can be triggered only in Veteran mode by desecrating the sacred banners of the Scaled Court. Hard Mode only affects the Celestial Serpent, making this boss fight far more challenging.

You will receive {{Item Link|The Serpent's Egg-Tooth|quality=5|id=81180}} for defeating the Celestial Serpent with hard mode active.

==Hard Mode==
[[File:ON-item-Sacred Banner.jpg|thumb|left|Sacred Banner]]
Hard Mode is only available in the [[ON:Veteran|Veteran]] version of Sanctum Ophidia. When you get to the staircase on the approach to the final boss fight against the Serpent, you have the option to burn two Sacred Banners found on either side of the staircase. If all banners are set aflame, Hard Mode will be activated, making the boss fight much more difficult.
