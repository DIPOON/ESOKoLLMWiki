It resembles one of the ziggurats of [[Online:Vivec City|Vivec City]], and is a pilgrimage site for the [[ON:Tribunal Temple (faction)|Tribunal Temple]]. Many pilgrims have [[Online:An Armiger's Duty|gone missing]] of late, and the [[Online:Buoyant Armiger|Buoyant Armiger]]s warn travelers to stay off the nearby roads at night.
