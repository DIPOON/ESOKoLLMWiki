The interior of the delve consists of a valley with a central hill. Cross the stream from the entrance and take a right to crest the hill and reach Visskar, the boss of the delve. The skyshard can be found by instead taking a left at the stream; it can be found hidden behind some boulders. The Pledge of Obedience for the quest [[Online:The Gray Passage|The Gray Passage]] can be reached from the top of the hill, as can the Bridge Triggers you need to activate to reach Skyreach Temple during the quest [[Online:Dawn of the Exalted Viper|Dawn of the Exalted Viper]].
