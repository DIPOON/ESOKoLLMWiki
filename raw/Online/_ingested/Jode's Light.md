A ''[[Online:Khajiit's Lost Journal Page|Khajiit's Lost Journal Page]]'' is located on top of a crate at the entrance. [[ON:Beetle|Beetles]] and [[ON:Torchbug|torchbugs]] are found in numbers within.

The skyshard is located in the northwestern chamber, near the northern wall. [[Online:Giant Bat|Giant Bats]] inhabit this room. The tunnel to the south leads to the delve's boss. The ''[[Online:Second Khajiiti Journal Page|Second Khajiiti Journal Page]]'' is located in the passage leading to the boss. A ''[[Online:Third Khajiiti Journal Page|Third Khajiiti Journal Page]]'' is located near the boss room's northern one-way exit.
