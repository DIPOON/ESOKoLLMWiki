It has two buildings: a chapel and the [[Online:Weynon Priory Monastic Lodge|monastic lodge]]. Several acolytes can be found both inside the buildings and around the yard, and a skyshard can be found near a wall outside the monastic lodge.

A copy of ''[[ON:Lucius' Note|Lucius' Note]]'' can be found within the chapel after [[ON:Overdue Supplies|Overdue Supplies]]. A hostile [[ON:Peasant (Weynon Priory)|peasant]] can be found living in a burned building to the west of the priory.
