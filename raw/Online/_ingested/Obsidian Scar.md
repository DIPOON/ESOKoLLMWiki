The cave and ruins system are inhabited by the [[ON:Oathbound Clan|Oathbound]] Orc clan and their [[ON:Daedra|Daedra]] allies. [[ON:Beetle|Beetles]], [[ON:Rat|rats]], [[ON:Scorpion|scorpions]] and [[ON:Spider|spiders]] are found in numbers within.

An [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity]] [[ON:Excavation|dig site]] can be found in the ruins southeast of the cave's entrance. A chest is nestled against a ruined tower on the side of the road southeast of the Obsidian Scar.
