A pirate, [[ON:Shifty Tom|Shifty Tom]], wants you to help him turn over a new leaf by defeating his former captain and crewmates.  You can learn about its location from [[ON:Tiny Talisse|Tiny Talisse]] in [[ON:Rawl'kha|Rawl'kha]], [[ON:Mighty Mordra|Mighty Mordra]] in [[ON:Evermore|Evermore]], or [[ON:Ferocious Jakidi|Ferocious Jakidi]] in [[ON:Riften|Riften]].

Its final boss, [[ON:Captain Blackheart|Captain Blackheart]], has a chance to drop the {{Item Link|Torc Strand of Song|quality=5|antiquityid=127}} [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity lead]] for the [[ON:Torc of Tonal Constancy|Torc of Tonal Constancy]].

==Notes==
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Blackheart Haven was designed for levels 40–43.
* In the early part of this dungeon (before you go inside), you can find [[ON:Pirate Disguise|Pirate Disguises]] in baskets which you can put on and avoid much of the fighting. Note that the dogs can still detect that you're not one of them. 
* A [[ON:Brewers|brewer]] vendor named [[ON:Martha the Wench|Martha the Wench]] can be found in the starting area of the dungeon.
