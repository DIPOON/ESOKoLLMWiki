It was known as the "Temple of Seven Riddles" prior to its occupation by the [[ON:Dro-m'Athra|Dro-m'Athra]].

Just outside the entrance to the temple is a small hub with two merchants, crafting stations and a cooking fire. There are also some members of the [[Online:Twilight Cantors|Twilight Cantors]] nearby, and a few monks that have been afflicted by the Bent Dance. [[Online:Adara'hai|Adara'hai]] can be found in front of the entrance to the temple; she will request your help in dealing with the invading dro'm-Athra.

You will receive {{Item Link|The Rid-Thar's Moon Pearls|id=74453|quality=5}} along with your other rewards after defeating the Fang of Lorkhaj with Hard Mode engaged.{{TOCright}}

==Notes==
* This trial is commonly [[Online:Abbreviations and Terms|abbreviated]] as "MoL".
* The reward chests for defeating bosses here have a chance to contain [[ON:Defiled Whiskers|Defiled Whiskers]].
** To celebrate the [[ON:Thieves Guild (DLC)|Thieves Guild DLC]]'s one year anniversary, Defiled Whisker drops were temporarily doubled as part of the [[Online:Events/2017#Thieves Guild Anniversary Event|Thieves Guild Anniversary Event]].
* Even though this trial is in an Alliance Zone region, access to the [[ON:Thieves Guild (DLC)|Thieves Guild DLC]] is required to run it.
* You will receive {{Item Link|The Rid-Thar's Moon Pearls|quality=5|id=74453}} for defeating Rakkhat with hard mode active.
