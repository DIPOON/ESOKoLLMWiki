}} It is the capital of the [[Online:Ebonheart Pact|Ebonheart Pact]], and is traditionally controlled by [[Online:House Indoril|House Indoril]]. It is the seat of the [[Lore:Great Moot|Great Moot]], the ruling council of the Pact. It is home to the [[Online:Tribunal Temple|Tribunal Temple]], where the [[Online:Dark Elf|Dunmer]] goddess [[Online:Almalexia|Almalexia]] resides.

The city is divided into several districts. The southwestern part of the city is occupied by the Guild Plaza, which is home to the halls of the Fighters Guild and the Mages Guild. In the west lies the [[Online:Royal Bazaar|Royal Bazaar]], filled with numerous merchant stalls. The northern part of the city is dominated by the towering [[Online:Tribunal Temple|Tribunal Temple]]. In the east lies the Residential District, which contains several houses, [[Online:The Flaming Nix|The Flaming Nix]] inn, and the docks  where students of [[Online:Shad Astula|Shad Astula]] can take a boat to their academy. 

In the southeastern part of the city lies the Plaza of the Gods, which houses the [[Online:Artisans Craftworks|Artisans Craftworks]]&mdash;a smithy containing all the relevant crafting stations&mdash;as well as an [[Online:Hlaalu Alchemical Goods|alchemy store]] with a dye station and an [[Online:The Kind Enchanter|enchanting store]]. It also contains the city's [[Online:Mournhold Wayshrine|wayshrine]] and seven stalls of [[Online:Guild Traders|guild traders]]. In the very center of the city lies the Banking District, which is home to the city's main administrative building, called simply [[Online:City Center|City Center]]. It contains the Bank of Mournhold and the rededication shrines.

The city has two docks: One is located east of the Tribunal Temple, in the northeastern corner of the city. This dock lies north of the Plaza of the Gods. You can gain passage to academy of [[Online:Shad Astula|Shad Astula]] from here. A [[Online:Thieves Trove|thieves trove]] can spawn in the hooded boat anchored at the center of the pier.

A thieves trove can spawn outside the southern gate, amid a cluster of rocks west of the ash gardens where [[Online:Farona Telvanni|Farona Telvanni]] stands after Mournhold's objective quest is complete.

==Notes==
*Mournhold also appeared in ''[[Arena:Mournhold|Arena]]'', as well as in the ''[[Tribunal:Mournhold|Tribunal]]'' expansion for ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''.
*[[ON:Wretched Spire|Wretched Spire]] in [[Online:The Deadlands|the Deadlands]] was originally a part of Old Mournhold. It was ripped from Tamriel and transported to Oblivion during [[ON:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]]'s attack on the city 600 years ago. Due to the strange passage of time in the Deadlands, some of the surviving inhabitants can still be encountered there.
