}}{{TOCleft}}This dungeon takes place in a vast [[ON:Daedric Ruins|daedric ruin]] repurposed as a gladiatorial arena. In it, you must help [[ON:Talres Voren|Talres Voren]] pay off his debts by winning the tournament and becoming the Champion of the Crucible.  You can learn about its location from [[ON:Vlesyl the Skin-Stealer|Vlesyl the Skin-Stealer]] in [[ON:Rawl'kha|Rawl'kha]], [[ON:Sluz the Beryl-Chested|Sluz the Beryl-Chested]] in [[ON:Evermore|Evermore]], or [[ON:Kailstig the Axe|Kailstig the Axe]] in [[ON:Riften|Riften]].

[[ON:Traps#Bottomless Pit|Bottomless Pits]] and [[ON:Traps#Lava Pool|Lava Pools]] serve as environmental hazards in the dungeon.

A [[ON:Wish Me Good Fortune|letter]] is located on the top bunk of one of the beds to the right of the entrance to the Gladiators' Quarters. There is a chest to the left of this room's exit.

Several books are located on the plaques of statues in the chamber preceding the Lava Queen's arena, which tell of the Crucible's ten champions: ''[[ON:Aleris the Shroud|Aleris the Shroud]]'', ''[[ON:Crow Bringer|Crow Bringer]]'', ''[[ON:Hagrof the Righteous|Hagrof the Righteous]]'', ''[[ON:The Thousand Arrows|The Thousand Arrows]]'', ''[[ON:Lucius the Stalwart|Lucius the Stalwart]]'', ''[[ON:Whitebear|Whitebear]]'', ''[[ON:Ferian Darkstorm|Ferian Darkstorm]]'', ''[[ON:Felhorn|Felhorn]]'', ''[[ON:Sanarel the Great|Sanarel the Great]]'' and ''[[ON:The Lava Queen (book)|The Lava Queen]]''. The tale of ''[[ON:Josef the Intolerant|Josef the Intolerant]]'' is located in the previous room.

==Notes==
* Despite being dungeon bosses, the members of the Pack are not immune to crowd control. Use this to your advantage during the fight.
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Blessed Crucible was designed for levels 40–43.
* When you exit this dungeon at the end, you'll wind up in [[ON:Stonefalls|Stonefalls]] instead of [[ON:The Rift|The Rift]].
