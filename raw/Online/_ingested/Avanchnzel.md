==Clearing the Dungeon==

==Books==
*{{Book Link|Dwarven Ruin Explorer's Journal}}

==Layout==
A Steam Pipe full of [[Online:Swarming Dwarven Spider|Swarming Dwarven Spiders]] is located in the middle of the first hallway. A ''[[Online:Dwarven Ruin Explorer's Journal|Dwarven Ruin Explorer's Journal]]'' is located in the nook just north of the entrance. If you take the path leading east in the first hallway, you'll come across the northern chamber. A chest can spawn in the northeastern corner of this room, behind a Dwarven Centurion and some rubble in a patch where sunlight is filtering in through the ceiling. There's a Steam Pipe  south of this chest, in the southeast corner of the room. Another Steam Pipe is located in the hallway leading east from this chamber. This hall leads to the southern chamber.

The boss resides along the northern wall the southern chamber. A Steam Pipe lies on the west side of this room, and a Dwarven Centurion patrols the southern end of the room. The hallway leading west of here brings you to the southwestern chamber. A chest can spawn behind a pillar near the Steam Pipe in the middle of the room, between the east and west sections. The interior skyshard is guarded by a Dwarven Centurion. There are four bookshelves near the skyshard. Heading through the northern hallway brings you back towards the entrance.
