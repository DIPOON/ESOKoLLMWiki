}}{{TOCleft}} According to [[ON:Fights-With-Tail|Fights-With-Tail]], it is the oldest city in Craglorn. Elinhir is home to the [[Online:Blackcaster Mages Guild|Blackcaster Mages Guild]], who serve as its protectors. Under their guidance, the city has flourished and become a prominent center of civilization in the wilderness of Craglorn... until recently. The [[Online:Elinhir Sewerworks|Elinhir Sewerworks]] run beneath town. [[ON:Skavenger|Skavengers]] are in numbers within.

The entrance to [[Online:Falkreath Hold|Falkreath Hold]] is located at the end of the road leading east of the city, beyond [[Online:Domus Phrasticus|Domus Phrasticus]]

==Layout==
The city features a mix of Nede and Imperial architecture. Due to the havoc now it is littered with ruined houses, hostile blackcaster mages, atronachs and spellfiends. The atmosphere is very eerie due to the purple sky, the purple fog, the purple particles raining and the near-silence the city is in. The Sewerworks are accessible from the city. The West Tower and East Tower must be accessed as part of the area's quest and the Apex Tower, located in the south, is quest-locked. A unique storm atronach is being assaulted by spellfiends and blackcaster mages in the south part of the city.
===West Tower===
The tower is filled with zombies on the first floor, prisoners in the second floor and Leta Doniticus awaits at the third floor with many weak zombies. You must use portals to navigate between the floors.
