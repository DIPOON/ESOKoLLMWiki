A chest can be found on an altar in the westernmost chamber, in the southeastern section of the room. The skyhard can be found in the southwestern chamber. Many Heritance members lay dead in the southern chambers. ''[[ON:Mercano's Journal|Mercano's Journal]]'' can be found in the southeastern chamber, next to Archmage Camaano. [[ON:Fleshflies|Fleshflies]] and [[ON:Spider|Spiders]] are found in numbers within.
