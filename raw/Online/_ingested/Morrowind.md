'''''Morrowind''''' is the first [[Online:Chapters|Chapter]] of [[ON:Online|''The Elder Scrolls Online'']]. It is an expansion set on the island of [[ON:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]]. ''Morrowind'' includes a large new zone, the [[ON:Warden|Warden]] class, a [[ON:Trials|trial]], and a new storyline centered around saving Vvardenfell from [[Lore:Baar Dau|Baar Dau]]'s descent. The DLC was made free to all players on April 4, 2022, but did not include the Warden class. 

''Morrowind'' entered invite-only closed [[ON:Beta|beta]] on the [[ON:PTS|PTS]] on April 17, 2017, although all content from the Chapter was under a [[wikipedia:Non-disclosure agreement|non-disclosure agreement]]. The Chapter launched in early access on May 22, 2017 for PC/Mac, and officially launched on all platforms on June 6, 2017.

As of [[Online:Update 18|Update 18]] and the release of ''[[Online:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]'', the Chapter is no longer available for retail purchase and its contents have been divided between the base game and the [[Online:Crown Store|Crown Store]]. [[Online:Battlegrounds|Battlegrounds]] have moved to the base game. Certain promotional bonus content which was originally available in the [[Online:Discovery Pack|Discovery Pack]] and ''[[Online:Morrowind Collector's Edition|Morrowind Collector's Edition]]'' is available in the Crown Store as part of the [[ON:Morrowind Collector's Pack|Morrowind Collector's Pack]].

On June 5, 2018, ''Morrowind'' became available in the [[ON:Crown Store|Crown Store]] as a [[Online:Morrowind (DLC)|DLC purchase]], and the [[Online:Warden|Warden]] class became a separate Crown Store Upgrade available for {{ESO Crowns|1,500}}. The ''Morrowind'' DLC was also included with [[ON:ESO Plus|ESO Plus]]. Since April 6, 2022, the ''Morrowind'' DLC is available for free and is automatically given to all players upon logging in. It was removed from the Crown Store on March 9, 2026 after the ''Morrowind'' DLC was fully integrated into the base game.

== Features ==
<section begin=features/>
* '''[[ON:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]]''', a large new [[ON:Zones|zone]].
* The '''[[ON:Warden|Warden]]''', an ice-based nature [[ON:Classes|class]].
* A new [[ON:Story Quests|'''story''']], in which you must aid [[ON:Vivec|Vivec]] in regaining his lost powers as part of an emerging Daedric plot.
* [[ON:Battlegrounds|'''Battlegrounds''']], a new 4v4v4 arena-style PvP mode.
* A new 12-player [[ON:Trials|'''trial''']], the [[ON:Halls of Fabrication|Halls of Fabrication]].
* A new [[ON:Broken Bonds|'''tutorial''']] and a [[ON:Saint Delyn Penthouse|'''free player house''']] for new characters.
* Aid the [[ON:Morag Tong|Morag Tong]] in [[ON:Balmora|'''Balmora''']] or help a slave ascend the ranks of the [[ON:House Telvanni|Telvanni]] in [[ON:Sadrith Mora|'''Sadrith Mora''']], among other [[ON:Vvardenfell#Quests|side quests]].
* Six new [[ON:Delves|delves]], six new [[ON:Group Bosses|group bosses]], and two new [[ON:Public Dungeons|public dungeons]].
* Daily '''[[ON:Repeatable Quests|repeatable quests]]''' from the [[ON:Ordinators|Ordinators]] of [[ON:Vivec City|Vivec City]] and the [[Online:Ashlanders|Ashlanders]] of [[ON:Ald'ruhn|Ald'ruhn]].
* [[ON:Collections|'''Collectibles''']] to earn, including [[Online:Non-Combat Pets|pets]], tattoos, and a [[ON:Personalities|personality]].
* New [[ON:Dyes|'''dyes''']], [[ON:Styles| '''styles''']], and [[ON:Sets|'''sets''']], including three new [[ON:Crafting Sites|crafting sites]].
* A [[ON:Ancestral Tombs Hunter|tomb hunting]] [[Online:Museums|'''collection task''']] that will take you all over Vvardenfell.

== Versions ==
'''''Morrowind''''' is available in five different versions:
*'''Morrowind Digital Upgrade''': For existing players, this version includes the standard ''Morrowind'' Chapter.
*'''Morrowind Collector's Edition Digital Upgrade''': For existing players, this version includes the ''Morrowind'' Chapter and Collector's Edition bonuses.
*'''Morrowind Standard Edition''': For new players, this version includes the base game and the ''Morrowind'' Chapter, and is available both digitally and in stores.
*'''Morrowind Digital Collector's Edition''': For new players, this version includes the base game, the ''Morrowind'' Chapter and Collector's Edition bonuses.
*'''Morrowind Collector's Edition''': This version includes the base game, the ''Morrowind'' Chapter, Collector's Edition bonuses, and unique collectibles. The included game code for ''Morrowind'' can also be applied to an existing account.
