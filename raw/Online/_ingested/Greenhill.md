}} Despite its [[Lore:Colovia|Colovia]]n origins and [[Lore:Second Empire|Second Empire]] architecture, it is now firmly part of [[Online:Valenwood|Valenwood]] and ruled by [[ON:Treethane Bowenas|Treethane Bowenas]]. Although the [[Online:Greenhill Church|Church]] has been abandoned since the fall of the Second Empire, religious tolerance is the norm in Greenhill and the [[Online:Green Pact|Green Pact]] is enforced less strictly than elsewhere in Valenwood. As a result, most of the townspeople, including [[Online:Wood Elf|Bosmer]], work in the large vegetable fields and apple orchards which lie between the town and the river.

The villagers have recently become angry and rude towards visitors to the town, and sightings of [[ON:Stonefire Cult|mysterious and dark robed men]] have made the atmosphere within the town very tense.

[[Online:Haldwyn (merchant)|Haldwyn]] the [[Online:Traveling Merchants|traveling merchant]] walks up and down the road between Greenhill and [[Online:Fort Grimwatch|Fort Grimwatch]].

==Layout==
The local market, [[Online:Goods of Greenhill|Goods of Greenhill]], is located just west of the road leading into Greenhill from the north. There's a tall tree growing in the middle of the square, south of the stone housing east of the market. [[Online:Gonlas's House|Gonlas's House]] is adjacent to the market, and [[Online:Tashpir's House|Tashpir's House]] is across the street east from it. [[Online:Ganniel and Lothiel's House|Ganniel and Lothiel's House]] is next door to Tashpir's House. [[Online:Bowenas and Halindor's House|Bowenas and Halindor's House]] is next to that. The Greenhill Church is south of the housing block and the main square. There's a skyshard behind the temple.

The farm is located west of the market, down a flight of stone stairs. Ezreba's House lies next to the stairs, between the market and an apple orchard. The rest of the farm consists of corn fields. There's a farm house and a windmill on the farm.
{{NewLeft}}

===Greenhill Catacombs===
The entrance to the catacombs lies in the back of the ruined church, between two braziers. The containers inside the catacombs can be looted freely. A letter entitled ''[[Online:To Anchorite Gaius|To Anchorite Gaius]]'' can be found at the western end of the first hallway.
