==Layout==
A chest can spawn in the southeast corner of the first chamber. 

Three chests can spawn in the next main chamber, which lies through the tunnel to the south: one of them spawns near the flowers in the northeast corner of the room, and two spawn in the southwest corner of the room, beneath a stone structure. One spawns beneath the structure, and another can spawn between the structure and the southern wall. Light filters in from the ceiling, and an inaccessible mud hut lies along the southern wall, between two stone structures.

The tunnel leading to the delve's boss has an open ceiling. A chest can spawn to the right of the entrance of Stormscale's cave. Yet another chest spawns on the north side of the cave, between the wall and a pillar of stone. Two dead Atanaz Warriors lay on the ground in this chamber. A door leading back to the entrance lies beyond the boss.
