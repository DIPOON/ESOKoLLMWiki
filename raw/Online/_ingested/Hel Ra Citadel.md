Hel Ra Citadel Hard Mode can be triggered by entering the Veteran version of the trial and destroying the [[Online:Hel Ra Statue|statues]] in the room before the Warrior's chamber.

Completing a full run of Veteran Hel Ra Citadel will reward 12 {{Item Link|Undaunted Plunder|id=114427|summary=1|quality=4}} worth 12000 gold total (2 from Ra Kotu, 2 from Yokeda Kai or Yokeda Rok'dun, and 8 from The Warrior). You will receive a {{Item Link|Star-Studded Champion's Baldric|quality=5|id=87701}} for defeating The Warrior with hard mode active.

==Layout==
===The Bridge===
When you exit the door leading into the trial proper, the speedrun timer (if you're on Veteran) begins. When you exit the door, you'll find yourself on a sandy ridge overlooking a canyon. A few corpses and a backpack are not too far from the door, and a ''[[Online:Torn Page (letter)|Torn Page]]'' lies near an abandoned sword. Many groups will get on their mounts and race to the bridge, which is located to the northeast. You'll run along the northern ridge, heading east. [[ON:Talirinde|Talirinde]] is slumped against a crate in front of the bridge. She warns you of the citadel's war horn before falling unconscious.
