}}It's built on a bridge leading over the river between [[ON:Leyawiin Castle|Leyawiin Castle]] and the rest of the city.

Each timber framed building has one room on each floor with a wooden staircase joining the rooms along the back wall. The second building contains a large fireplace on the lowest floor and a stone fireplace on the middle floor. The outdoor space is a two level wooden walkway and terrace with window boxes on your house and views of Leyawiin's walls, buildings and river.

To purchase this house, you need to have access to the [[ON:Blackwood|Blackwood]] Chapter and to have completed the [[ON:Blackwood Grand Adventurer|Blackwood Grand Adventurer]] achievement.

==Notes==
*The Leyawiin seen from the porches is part of the house's worldspace, meaning no [[ON:NPCs|NPCs]] or Players will be visible. An invisible wall prohibits any attempt to jump into the river or onto the bridge. 
*This house should not be confused with [[OB:Water's Edge|Water's Edge]], a village seen in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''.
