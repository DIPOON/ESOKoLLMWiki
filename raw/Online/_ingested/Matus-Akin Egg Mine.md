This [[ON:House Hlaalu|House Hlaalu]] mine was usurped by [[ON:Drovos Nelvayn|Drovos Nelvayn]], a [[ON:House Telvanni|Telvanni]] contractor that was working for them, when his ambitions for his legacy grew bolder.

The following notes can be found here, ''[[Online:Drovos Research Notes 1|Drovos Research Notes 1]]'', ''[[Online:Drovos Research Notes 2|Drovos Research Notes 2]]'', ''[[Online:Drovos Research Notes 3|Drovos Research Notes 3]]'', ''[[Online:Ridena's Letter to Drovos|Ridena's Letter to Drovos]]'' and ''[[ON:Kwama Breeding Research Notes (Flora)|Kwama Breeding Research Notes (Flora)]]''.

[[ON:Ash Hopper|Ash Hoppers]], [[ON:Fetcherfly|fetcherflies]] and [[ON:Shroom Beetle|Shroom Beetles]] are found in numbers within. Hazards include: [[ON:Traps#Lava Pool|Lava Pools]].
