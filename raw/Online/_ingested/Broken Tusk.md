The shrine contains a number of dead [[ON:Molag Bal Cultist|Molag Bal Cultists]], who were summoning Daedra to control and release upon the world in the name of [[ON:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]], but were quickly overwhelmed.

A dying [[ON:Mallius Falto|priest of Arkay]] and a cursed urn can be found deep within the shrine. A [[ON:Stonefire Ritual Tome|book]] of rituals can be found in the hallway leading to [[ON:Naeraizozan|Naeraizozan]]'s chamber. A chest can be found in this hallway as well. Another chest can be found in the northwest corner of Naeraizozan's room. Four banners depicting the emblem of Sheogorath can be found in the hallway beyond the entrance, and a single banner depicting Hircine can be found in the first large chamber.

A copy of ''[[ON:Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls|Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls]]'' can be found south from the ruins, across the river. A [[ON:Thieves Trove|Thieves Trove]] is hiding on the other side of the river, beneath a fern just up the slope towards [[ON:Camp Merciful Reduction|Camp Merciful Reduction]]. Two [[ON:Crocodile|crocodiles]], a [[ON:Giant Wasp|giant wasp]] and a [[ON:Guar|guar]] loiter just outside the entrance to the ruins. [[ON:Rat|Rats]] and [[ON:Spider|Spiders]] are found in numbers within.
