==Layout==
The ''[[Online:Supplementary Orders|Supplementary Orders]]'' are laying on the ground next to a dead [[Online:Argonian|Argonian]] at the entrance. When you enter the first large chamber, you'll find that some of the Argonian, [[Online:Dunmer|Dunmer]], and [[Online:Nord|Nord]] soldiers scattered across the floor of the ruin aren't sleeping quietly: they'll rise as [[Online:Zombie|zombies]] and attack you. Another copy of the orders can be found atop a crate near the stairs descending into this chamber, and a chest can spawn in the nook with the campfire to the right as you approach this stairwell.

Another chest can spawn against the northernmost wall in the first chamber, behind a stone altar with a skeleton on top of it. Zombies fill the central and southern halls, accessible via the staircase on the west side of the room. If you loop back around to the east side of the map while traversing this hallway (just keep going straight instead of turning right to enter the delve boss' room), you'll find the remaining members of the [[Online:Ebonheart Pact|Ebonheart Pact]]'s expedition to Wansalen holed up in a room.

The Skyshard can be found off to the side in the corridor with water.

The southernmost chamber contains a garden as well as the delve boss: a necromancer named [[Online:Nolonir|Nolonir]]. A chest can spawn beside a pillar immediately to the right of his ritual site.
