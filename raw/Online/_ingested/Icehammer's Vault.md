A [[ON:Letter to Idirfa|letter]] from [[ON:Uggissar|Uggissar]] to his cousin can be found not far from the entrance. Farther in lies a [[ON:Legend of Thane Icehammer|book]] about Thane Icehammer. In the northwestern room there is a [[ON:Nine Commands of the Eight …|torn book]] shedding some more light into the fate of Uggissar and his family who had been hiding in the tomb. In the northern tunnel you can find another [[ON:Uggissar's Lament|note]] from Uggissar.
