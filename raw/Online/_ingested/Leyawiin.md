==Layout==
The crafting writ turn-in station lies down the road south from the [[Online:Mages Guild (Leyawiin)|Mages Guild]], heading towards the [[Online:Guild Traders (Leyawiin)|guild traders]]. The crafting stations are located at a [[#Crafting Stations|plaza]] in the southwest corner of the city, south of the wayshrine. You can pick up Equipment and Consumables crafting writs in this plaza. The plaza has no formal name, but all of the crafting stations and related merchants are located here. [[Online:Danel Telleno|Danel Telleno]] is standing next to the fountain between the outfit, clothing and enchanting stations. [[Online:Millenith|Millenith]] is standing by the blacksmithing station. [[Online:Guild Herald Amsaad|Guild Herald Amsaad]] lies east of the woodworking stations.

There's a statue of [[Online:Sai Sahan|Sai Sahan]] in front of the [[Online:Fighters Guild (Leyawiin)|Fighters Guildhall]].

Leyawiin's [[Online:Event Exchange (Blackwood)|Event Exchange]] is located outside the city's northeastern gate. [[Online:Leyawiin Castle|Leyawiin Castle]] lies within the northeastern gate (across from the [[Online:Guard Tower (Leyawiin)|Guard Tower]]), south of a garden containing a statue of [[Lore:Kynareth|Kynareth]]. Further south, you'll find the Ivory Brigade's training yard.
