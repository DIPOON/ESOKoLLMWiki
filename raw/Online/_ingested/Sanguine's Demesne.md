It is the place of an eternal party held to honor [[Lore:Sanguine|Sanguine]]. Some of the participants seem to have trouble leaving when they have joined the party. It is advertised on a [[Online:The Sanguine Cult|poster]] by the road from [[Online:Mournhold|Mournhold]] to [[Online:Stormhold|Stormhold]]. A copy of the lore book ''[[ON:Modern Heretics|Modern Heretics]]'' can be found west of Sanguine's Demesne. [[ON:Frog|Frogs]], [[ON:Rat|rats]] and [[ON:Snake|snakes]] are found in numbers within. Alchemic nodes can be harvested throughout the dungeon.

Bosses have a chance to drop the {{Item Link|Fanged Cuirass|quality=5|antiquityid=139}} [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity lead]] for the [[Online:Bloodlord's Embrace|Bloodlord's Embrace]].
