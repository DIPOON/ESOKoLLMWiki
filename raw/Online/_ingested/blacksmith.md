The '''Blacksmith''' costume was originally sold in the [[Online:Crown Store|Crown Store]] for {{ESO Crowns|400}} from March 17 to May 26, 2015. This costume was on sale for {{ESO Crowns|200}} from May 21 to May 26, 2015 before being removed from the Crown Store. When it returned in February of 2024, the price was raised to {{ESO Crowns|500}}. The Blacksmith costume also dropped from {{Item Link|Undaunted Reward Boxes|itemid=171267|quality=e|summary=1}} during 2018's [[Online:Undaunted Celebration Event#2018|Undaunted Celebration]], and is now available as a Superior-level reward in [[ON:Gloomspore Crate|Gloomspore]] [[ON:Crown Crates|Crates]].
