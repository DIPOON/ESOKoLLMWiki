==Hard Mode==
[[File:ON-interior-Jarl's Hall Warhorn.jpg|thumb|right|The Warhorn]]
Hard Mode is only available in the [[ON:Veteran|Veteran]] version of Falkreath Hold. When you get to the final boss fight against Domihaus, you have the option to activate a '''Warhorn''' to the right of the door after you enter the Jarl's Hall. Activating the Warhorn will destroy two of the wooden pillars prior to the fight beginning and will make the boss fight more difficult. If your group dies, the horn will need to be sounded again to reenable Hard Mode.

==Notes==
*Falkreath appeared in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]'' as [[Arena:Falcrenth|Falcrenth]], and also appears in ''[[SR:Falkreath|Skyrim]]'' and [[LG:Falkreath|Legends]].
**Locations from ''Skyrim'' reappear, such as the [[SR:Falkreath Watchtower|Falkreath Watchtower]] and [[SR:Shriekwind Bastion|South Shriekwind Bastion]]; they can be seen in the distance but cannot be traveled to.
*[[ON:Hakkvild's High Hall|Hakkvild's High Hall]] has a view of the city.
*Dialogue with [[ON:Denskar Earth-Turner|Denskar Earth-Turner]] reveals that he lived here in his youth.
*Falkreath Hold was seen on a pre-beta map of the game as a planned full-sized zone, extending across the northern edge of [[ON:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] all the way from [[ON:Craglorn|Craglorn]] to [[ON:The Rift|The Rift]], much as it was seen in ''Skyrim''.
