Mobs have a chance to drop the {{Item Link|Order-Etched Gallery Rail|quality=5|antiquityid=336}} [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity lead]] for the [[Online:Ring of the Pale Order|Ring of the Pale Order]]. Bosses have a chance to drop the {{Item Link|Band of Water|quality=5|antiquityid=123}} antiquity lead for the [[Online:Ring of the Wild Hunt|Ring of the Wild Hunt]].

[[Online:Bad Man Follower|Bad Man Followers]] live inside the clearing, where they are currently about to celebrate the Harvest Festival, dedicated to the [[ON:Bad Man|Bad Man]]. Multiple ressource nodes are found in both areas. You can find a lake fishing hole on the surface and a foul fishing hole in the caverns. [[ON:Butterfly|Butterflies]], [[ON:Frog|frogs]], [[ON:Rabbit|rabbits]], and [[ON:Squirrel|squirrels]] are found in numbers on the surface while [[ON:Rat|rats]] and [[ON:Snake|snakes]] are found in the caverns.
