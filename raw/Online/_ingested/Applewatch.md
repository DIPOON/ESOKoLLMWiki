The settlement consists of only one building: the '''Applewatch Inn''', owned by [[Online:Leono Draconis|Leono]] and [[Online:Rasala Draconis|Rasala Draconis]]. The bard [[Online:Javid|Javid]] also lives there. ''[[Online:The Argonian Maid—An Oral Tradition|The Argonian Maid—An Oral Tradition]]'' can be found on a table. Another book, ''[[Online:Dealing with Werewolves|Dealing with Werewolves]]'', can be found in the graveyard behind the inn. A copy of ''[[Online:The Homilies of Blessed Almalexia|The Homilies of Blessed Almalexia]]'' can be found on the nightstand next to the bed, closest to the door.
