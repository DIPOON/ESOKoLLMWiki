==Notes==
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Volenfell was designed for levels 34–37.
* This dungeon contains curious references to the movie "Tremors" (Underwood, 1990). The boss [[ON:Tremorscale|Tremorscale]], whose name already suggests this, is a big [[ON:Duneripper|Duneripper]], whose shape is very similar to the creatures from the movie. Also, Tremorscale's Rupture skill, through which he burrows, moves under the terrain, and launches himself out of it, is the typical "attack" of the original creatures.
