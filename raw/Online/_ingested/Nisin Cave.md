}}The cave contains [[ON:Shadowed Path|Shadowed Path]] necromancers, [[Online:Dremora|Dremora]], [[Online:Skeleton|skeletons]], and [[Online:Wisp|wisps]].  A chest can be found around the exterior of the cave, to the east, behind the mountain. [[ON:Alchemy #Reagents|Mushrooms]] grow within the cave.

The first room contains an altar where a Shadowed Path Defiler performs a ritual on a skeletal corpse. A [[ON:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can be found at the end of the entrance tunnel, beside a crate. Across from the entrance tunnel, near a cooking station sits ''[[ON:Tamrielic Artifacts, Part Two|Tamrielic Artifacts, Part Two]]''. A chest can be found in the tunnel leading to the next chamber, in front of where [[ON:Volgo the Harrower|Volgo the Harrower]] stands. 

A shrine to [[ON:Boethiah|Boethiah]] can be found in the westernmost chamber. Here, you can fight [[ON:Barasatii|Barasatii]], a Dremora fire mage who is associated with the related quest. The delve's skyshard lies atop a large boulder in the southern portion of the chamber. A chest is located behind the western pillar in this room, near the tunnel where Volgo lies.
