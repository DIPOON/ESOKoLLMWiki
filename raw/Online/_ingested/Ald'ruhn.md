It serves as a site of pilgrimage for the nomads, and is the legendary site where the last [[Lore:Emperor Crab|emperor crab]], [[Online:Skar|Skar]], was killed by a combined force of all the tribes. The corpse now serves as a meeting hall.

There's a [[Online:Thieves Trove|thieves trove]] behind Skar's second leg on the east side.
