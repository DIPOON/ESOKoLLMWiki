==Notes==
* The Daedric text written around the zone on [[Online:The Aurbis|the Aurbis]] map translates to ''THE REALM OF APOCRYPHA ETERNAL LIBRARY OF HERMAEUS''.
* Apocrypha was first mentioned in ''[[Online:The Doors of Oblivion|The Doors of Oblivion]]'' and first appeared in ''[[Skyrim:Apocrypha|Dragonborn]]''.
* The cut quest "Devotees of Knowledge" for [[Online:Apocrypha's Gate|Apocrypha's Gate]] was intended to involve a gate to Apocrypha, where you would throw a Diadem of Memories filled with knowledge taken from Dagon's Dremora into Apocrypha for Mora to analyze.
* The word "Apocryphal" means: A story or statement of doubtful authenticity, although widely circulated as being true.
