}} For those who own the [[Online:Imperial City (DLC)|Imperial City DLC]] or are members of [[Online:ESO Plus|ESO Plus]], the sewers serve as the means of entry to the city and players will be ported straight to their own Alliance's base of operations when joining an Imperial City [[Online:Campaigns|Campaign]]. The Imperial Sewers plays similarly to a [[Online:Public Dungeons|Public Dungeon]], but with PvP in all areas except a few small alliance safe zones. The alliance safe zones contain ladders that go to all the [[ON:Imperial Districts|city districts]], leading to small rally points in the city. The only ways to get back to the sewer safe zone if one has left the rally point is to use one of the ladders on the edges of the district, which connect to deeper parts of the sewers, and then walk back to the alliance base, or to use a {{Item Link|Sigil of Imperial Retreat|id=68347|summary=1|quality=5}}. These sigils can be purchased from Tel Var General Merchants or found in chests in the Imperial Sewers.

Within the sewers are three different alliance-based branches that all converge to the central [[ON:Barathrum Centrata|Barathrum Centrata]]. Each branch contains [[ON:Sewer Subjugator|four group boss champions]], [[ON:Imperial Sewers Pathfinder|two striking locales]], two district ladders, and a total of [[ON:Imperial City Skyshard Hunter|seven skyshards]] spread out among them. The [[ON:Coldharbour Elite Guard|Coldharbour Elite Guard]], a powerful group of [[ON:Xivkyn|Xivkyn]] group bosses, patrol paths through the sewers, and [[ON:Cunning Scamp|Cunning]] and [[ON:Trove Scamp|Trove Scamp]]s also spawn at rare times. The sewers contain three entrances to the [[Online:White-Gold Tower|White-Gold Tower]], and three entrances to the [[Online:Ancient Underground|Ancient Underground]], all in close proximity to alliance bases.

The group boss champions have a chance to drop the {{Item Link|Hecatomb Tassets|quality=5|antiquityid=140}} [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity lead]] for the [[Online:Bloodlord's Embrace|Bloodlord's Embrace]].

==Notes==
[[File:ON-mapicon-ImperialCity-gate.png|right]]
*Exiting the sewers will transport you back to the location you were at prior to entering the Imperial City Campaign, rather than returning you to the [[Online:Lake Rumare|Lake Rumare]] shoreline.
*Many [[Online:Imperial City Portals|portals]] can be found scattered throughout the sewers. Closing all three portals in the center of the [[Online:Barathrum Centrata|Barathrum Centrata]] will summon the [[Online:Simulacrum of Molag Bal|Simulacrum of Molag Bal]].
*Prior to Update 22, the Imperial City was accessed through the Sewers from a [[Online:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] campaign. Access was indicated by a dragon-shaped gate icon on the map.
