}} The town can be captured by gaining control of the three objective flags for your alliance. It is connected via the [[ON:Transitus Network|Transitus Network]] to [[ON:Drakelowe Keep|Drakelowe Keep]]. The controlling alliance of Drakelowe also sends their guards through the town, which can result in conflict with the controlling alliance of Cropsford. It functions as a convenient spawn point for the defense of [[ON:Culotte Bridge|Culotte Bridge]] and [[ON:Niben Bridge|Niben Bridge]].

There is an Imperial Legion camp southeast of Cropsford, across the river. It contains a [[ON:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] and two random legionaries. An [[Online:Abandoned Homestead|Abandoned Homestead]] and a watchtower known as [[Online:Crown Point (place)|Crown Point]] lie south of the village. The ruins of [[Online:Culotte|Culotte]] are located down the hill to the west.

==[[File:ON-mapicon-BorderKeep.png|text-bottom|32px|link=ON:Alliance War#Safe Zones|Protected]] Safe House==
[[File:ON-place-Cropsford 11.jpg|thumb|left|Cropsford Safe House]] The Cropsford Safe House is located on the southeastern side of the town, above the waterfall. The alliance which controls the town can use the Safe House to access the [[ON:Transitus Network|Transitus Network]]. It is linked to Drakelowe Keep. There is a cooking fire outside the safe house.
{{NewLeft}}

==[[File:ON-mapicon-AvA Flag.png|text-bottom|32px]] Capture Points==
There are three capture points in Cropsford, all of which must be controlled by the same alliance in order to gain control of the town:
* '''Central flag:''' In the center of the town (three guards)
* '''Merchant flag:''' South of the main road (two guards, and the quartermaster)
* '''Outlier flag:''' Northwest of the farm fields (four guards)

==Notes==
* Prior to Update 11, Cropsford was an [[ON:Explorable Locations|Explorable Location]] and used a [[File:ON-mapicon-Town.png|20px]][[ON:Towns|Town]] map marker. It originally provided experience on discovery, but this Discovery XP was removed when the town became an Alliance War objective.
* Prior to Update 20, the Safe House could be used as a respawn location for the controlling alliance, but was not connected to the Transitus Network.
* The Outlier Flag was reinforced in Update 20, adding additional guards, as an attempt to bolster its defenses due to being in close proximity to the Niben River.
* Cropsford also [[OB:Cropsford|appeared]] in [[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]].
