'''Barbas''' is the servant of [[Online:Clavicus Vile|Clavicus Vile]], the Daedric Prince of Wishes. As a shapeshifting [[ON:Daedra|Daedra]], he usually manifests as a [[ON:Whiterun Wolfhound|Whiterun Wolfhound]], and sometimes a [[Online:Skaafin|Skaafin]].

Barbas can be found in [[Online:Willowgrove Cavern|Willowgrove Cavern]]. When Vile granted the wish of [[Online:Pircalmo|Pircalmo]], a mage visiting [[Online:Willowgrove|Willowgrove]], Barbas became bound to Vile's altar in the Cavern.

Barbas can also be encountered on [[Online:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]] in the form of a [[Online:Skaafin|Skaafin]], assisting Clavicus Vile with his plot to discover the location of [[Online:Sotha Sil|Sotha Sil's]] [[Online:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]], as well as on [[Online:Summerset|Summerset]]. He serves as a guide up to the [[ON:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]] in the [[Lore:Fields of Regret|Fields of Regret]].

==Skills and Abilities==
You will fight Barbas during Divine Restoration.
;Lunge: Barbas rushes the target, swinging his blades when he arrives. He prefers to lunge at rooted targets, so if you stay close to Barbas, then he won't lunge. This ability serves as a gap-closer, and if you're hit, you'll be knocked back. <!--It deals moderate damage, and affects a cone-shaped area in front of him.-->
;Spinning Blades: Barbas leaps into the air, slicing nearby targets with both of his blades. This ability appears as a circular AoE, deals high damage, and will knock you back if you get hit. Stay out of the circle.
;Divine Hijack: Barbas channels Vivec's stolen energy to unleash many circular AoEs that deal moderate damage. You'll experience knockback if you get hit.
;Barbs: Barbas hurls a bolt of flame at a distant target, rooting them in place. It deals moderate flame damage. Use of this ability triggers lunge. He won't use this ability if you stay close to him.
