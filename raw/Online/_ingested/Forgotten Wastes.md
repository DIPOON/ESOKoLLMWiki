A large force of cultists from the forsaken [[ON:Sixth House|Sixth House]] inhabit this location, where they are in the process of slowly building up their forces, by luring [[ON:Dunmer|Dunmer]] from across the island to the ruins via corrupted statuettes.

The dungeon is divided into several sections: a large overland area containing the stronghold of [[Lore:Kogoruhn|Kogoruhn]], the Drinith Ancestral Tomb, the cave of Kora Dur, the Forgotten Depths, and the Caverns of Kogoruhn.

The seven [[Online:Fragments#Sixth_House_Robe|fragments]] for the [[online:Sixth House Robe|Sixth House Robe]] costume and [[Online:Sixth_House_Robe_(achievement)|achievement]] are randomly dropped from elite enemies in this zone.

[[ON:Ash Hopper|Ash Hoppers]], [[ON:Centipede|Centipedes]], [[ON:Fetcherfly|Fetcherflies]], [[ON:Shroom Beetle|Shroom Beetles]] and [[ON:Spider|Spiders]] are found in numbers within.

Hazards include: [[ON:Traps#Falling Rocks/Deadfall|Falling Rocks]], [[ON:Traps#Flamethrower|Flamethrower]], [[ON:Traps#Lava Pool|Lava Pools]], [[ON:Traps#Meteor|Pyroclasts/Meteors]] and [[ON:Traps#Spinning Blade|Spinning Blades]]

==Notes==
*This location appears in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'' as three separate locations: [[Morrowind:Drinith Ancestral Tomb|Drinith Ancestral Tomb]], [[Morrowind:Kogoruhn|Kogoruhn]], and [[Morrowind:Kora-Dur|Kora-Dur]].
*The Forgotten Wastes are mentioned in ''[[BK:The Official Survival Guide to Tamriel|The Official Survival Guide to Tamriel]]''.
*To solve the Sixth House bell puzzle, you must spell the word "DREAMS" out of the Daedric tiles. From left to right, the letters are as follows: D-R-A-E-S-M.
*In the outdoors section of the public dungeon, groups of cliff striders will often swoop down from nearby cliffs to ambush you after you are done defeating groups of enemies.
