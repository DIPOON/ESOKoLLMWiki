}} It is connected via the [[ON:Transitus Network|Transitus Network]] to [[ON:Fort Dragonclaw|Fort Dragonclaw]]. The city has been conquered by [[Online:Dremora|Dremora]] and [[ON:Shadowed Path|Shadowed Path]] cultists, leaving few survivors, but the area around the manor house can be captured for your alliance by gaining control of the three objective flags.  

As the residents are quick to point out, no reinforcements have been sent from the nearby [[Online:Alliances|alliance]] stronghold of Fort Dragonclaw, and the local [[Online:Bruma Guard|militia]] are hostile towards the alliance hold-outs that can be found around the manor house.

A [[Online:Dark Anchor|Dark Anchor]] and [[Online:Skyshard|Skyshard]] can be found here. Closing the [[ON:Dolmens|dolmen]] here is required to mark the [[File:ON-mapicon-Portal.png|link=Online:Dolmens|32px|middle]]'''Bruma''' marker as complete.

==[[File:ON-mapicon-BorderKeep.png|text-bottom|32px|link=Online:Alliance War#Safe Zones|Protected]] Safe House==
[[File:ON-place-Bruma 10.jpg|thumb|left|Bruma Safe House]] The Bruma Safe House is located at the southern end of the city. The alliance which controls the city can use the Safe House to access the [[Online:Transitus Network|Transitus Network]]. It is linked to Fort Dragonclaw and [[ON:Fort Aleswell|Fort Aleswell]].
{{NewLeft}}

==[[File:ON-mapicon-AvA Flag.png|text-bottom|32px]] Capture Points==
There are three capture points in Bruma, all of which must be controlled by the same alliance in order to gain control of the town:
* '''Central flag:''' East of the manor house (three guards)
* '''Merchant flag:''' South of the manor house (two guards, near the quartermaster)
* '''Outlier flag:''' North of the manor house (no guards)

==Notes==
*When entering the area, the name appears as '''Bruma Anchor''' in the UI.
*Prior to Update 11, Bruma was marked as a [[File:ON-mapicon-City.png|20px]][[Online:Cities|City]] which provided experience on discovery and was marked as complete upon destroying the Dark Anchor. Clearing Bruma was therefore needed in order to obtain several Dark Anchor achievements. The associated Discovery and Completion XP was shifted to the new Bruma Anchor icon when the city became an Alliance War objective.
*Prior to Update 20, the Safe House could be used as a respawn location for the controlling alliance, but was not connected to the Transitus Network.
*The repeatable "Bruma Adventurer" quests will award a [[Online:Bruma Reward Container|Bruma Reward Container]] for completing each one. 
*Bruma also [[OB:Bruma|appeared]] in [[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]].
