'''Skaafin''' are a race of horned, yellow-skinned, red-eyed humanoid [[ON:Daedra|Daedra]] associated with [[ON:Clavicus Vile|Clavicus Vile]]. They vary significantly in size, with some being quite small in stature to others being as tall as [[Online:High Elf|Altmer]]. They can be found in [[ON:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]], [[ON:Cold-Blood Cavern|Cold-Blood Cavern]], the [[ON:Clockwork City Vault|Clockwork City Vault]], and the [[ON:Ventral Terminus|Ventral Terminus]].

==Overheard Dialogue==
Hostile Skaafin have [[ON:Ambient Dialogue|ambient dialogue]] that is uttered as they idle. Tall Skaafin are generally more intelligent than their smaller brethren, and can be seen leading troupes of them wherever they are found. The smaller Skaafin are referred to as "wretches" by their superiors.

==Notes==
* Similar-looking Daedra appeared in Clavicus Vile's realm in ''[[RG:Redguard|Redguard]]'', but they were simply called "[[Redguard:Guardian|Guardian]]s".
* Cut dialogue implies that [[Online:Hircine|Hircine]] and the [[Online:Glenmoril Wyrd|Glenmoril Wyrd]] may have originally planned to be the antagonists in [[Online:Stormhaven|Stormhaven]], instead of [[Online:Vaermina|Vaermina]] and her [[Online:Supernal Dreamers|Supernal Dreamers]]. This story would have involved the Vestige being given a task to slay a Herne, and to offer its heart during a Glenmoril Coven's ritual to anger Hircine, as Hernes were one of his favored daedra. Hernes didn't appear in the base game and would [[Online:Herne_Ghorev|appear]] much later in [[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'s [[Online:The Missing Prophecy|prologue quest]]. For more info, please see the [[Online:Cut Content/Stormhaven|Cut Content article]].
**The art team also looked at Hernes when designing the [[ON:Ruinach|Ruinach]]s.{{Ref|name=BP|{{Cite Book|Blackwood Preview—The Mighty Ruinachs|ns_base=General}}}}
