Owing to the immense lushness and fertility of the cavern's soil, [[ON:Halinjirr|Halinjirr]] chose it as his secret location to grow [[Lore:Moon Sugar|moon sugar]]. However, his operation was recently seized by the Black Darts.

The notes ''[[ON:Journal of a Fallen Officer|Journal of a Fallen Officer]]'', ''[[Online:Halinjirr's Notes|Halinjirr's Notes]]'', and ''[[Online:Bandit's Letter|Bandit's Letter]]'' can be found here.

The cave also contains the Ascendant Order Hideout, an area only accessible during [[ON:Ascending Doubt|Ascending Doubt]].
