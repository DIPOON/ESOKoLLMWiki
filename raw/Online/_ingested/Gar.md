'''Gar''' is a [[ON:Nord|Nord]] and the son of [[ON:Hrogar|Hrogar]], the owner of [[ON:Hrogar's Hold|Hrogar's Hold]]. He is the brother of [[Online:Bjorick|Bjorick]].

==Quest-Related Events==
===Protecting the Hall===
Speaking to him for the first time:
:''"These [[ON:Goblin|Goblin]]s are merciless, stranger. They don't feel pity or remorse or fear, and they won't stop until they're killed."''
:'''Your father sent me to help you find Bjorick.'''
:''"You want to help? Get out there and kill some of those stinking Gobs.<br>Bjorick is nearby. I heard him call my name. If you can distract the Gobs, I think I can get to him. Meet me here when you're done."''

Speaking to him again:
:''"Kill every goblin and spider you meet. If you can distract them, I can slip past and rescue Bjorick.<br>Meet me here when you're finished."''

===Kinsman's Revenge===
After freeing him from the spider cocoon:
:'''Gar:''' ''"We've got to kill the [[ON:Shadowsilk Warlord|Goblin Warlord]] and the spider queen while we have the chance. Follow me."''

Speaking to him further in the cave:
:''"We're in the heart of the cave, friend. Now is the time to strike.''
:''What do you say? Think we're mighty enough to take on the Goblin Warlord and his spider queen?"''
:'''How can we defeat the Goblins and spiders?'''
:''"Crush the eggs. Crush them! That will draw out the ugly spider queen and her Goblin ally.''
:''[[Lore:Sovngarde|Sovngarde]] awaits. We'll kill these beasts and send the Goblin horde to the pits. [[Lore:Ysgramor|Ysgramor]]!"''
:'''I'll crush the eggs, Gar.'''

:'''Gar:''' ''"I'll watch your back while you crush spider eggs."''

Speaking with him before luring in the spider queen:
:''"[[ON:Troll|Troll's]] blood! You've saved my life and the lives of my kind. I'll ask for your help one more time, if you'll let me.<br>I crave revenge. We must make the Goblin Warlord and his spider queen pay for what they've done."''

After crushing spider eggs:
:'''Gar:''' ''"Weapons out. Here they come."''

The spider queen and Goblin Warlord are drawn out:
:'''Gar:''' ''"For Bjorick! For Ysgramor!"''

After the spider queen and Goblin Warlord are dead:
:'''Gar:''' ''"We've won the day! I'll meet you at the hall."''

Speaking with him here:
:''"Without your help, I'd have never defeated the Goblin chief and his spider queen. I'm going to collect the Gob's head as a trophy for my father.<br>Head back to the manor house and give my father the good news. I'll meet you when I'm done."''

After the quest, he will come in to announce that he has the head of the Goblin Warlord:
:'''Gar:''' ''"Father! I've brought the head of the Goblin Warlord!"''

Back at the hall:
:''"You saved my life. I'll never forget the debt I owe you."''
