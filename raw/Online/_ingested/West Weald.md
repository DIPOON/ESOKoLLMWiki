[[File:ON-prerelease-Gold Road Ayleid Ruins.jpg|thumb|right|An Ayleid ruin]]
The West Weald encompasses three sub-regions. In the southeast is [[Online:The Gold Road|the Gold Road]], the seat of the Colovian city of [[Online:Skingrad|Skingrad]]. In the north is the [[Online:Colovian Highlands|Colovian Highlands]], home to the Imperial settlements of [[ON:Sutch|Sutch]] and [[ON:Ontus|Ontus]], and a wealth of Ayleid ruins. In the southwest is the emergent jungle known as the [[Online:Dawnwood|Dawnwood]] and the Wood Elf settlement of [[Online:Vashabar|Vashabar]]. The temperate Gold Road and Colovian Highlands are separated from the encroaching Dawnwood by a boundary area called the [[Online:Wildburn|Wildburn]], where the wildlife and vegetation have been magically twisted.

==Notes==
*The West Weald [[OB:West Weald|previously appeared]] in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''.
*On the [[:File:ON-map-alpha map.jpg|alpha map]], this location was originally called the '''Colovian Highlands''', and was initially going to take up less space as the [[ON:Gold Coast|Gold Coast]] was planned to be a larger zone that went further north. At the [[ON:Varen's Wall Gatehouse|Varen's Wall Gatehouse]], this zone was originally labeled as the '''Colovian Estates''' prior to the release of ''Gold Road''.
