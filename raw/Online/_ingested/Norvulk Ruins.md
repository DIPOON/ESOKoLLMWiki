Several texts dedicated to [[ON:Vaermina|Vaermina]] can be found within the ruins: ''[[ON:In Dreams We Awaken|In Dreams We Awaken]]'' is near the entrance to the ruins and ''[[ON:Dream of a Thousand Dreamers|Dream of a Thousand Dreamers]]'' can be found in the northern part of the large open area. Just before entering that area, you can also read some [[ON:Orders: Norvulk Ruins|orders]] for the followers of Vaermina.  Lastly, ''[[ON:Dreamers Our Time Has Come|Dreamers Our Time Has Come]]'' is found near the delve's boss, [[ON:Nariam|Nariam]].

[[ON:Beetle|Beetles]] and [[ON:Rat|rats]] are found in numbers within.
