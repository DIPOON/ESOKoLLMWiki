'''Daine''' is a [[ON:Breton|Breton]] found with his brother [[Online:Dariel|Dariel]] inside the [[ON:Black Vine Ruins (place)|Black Vine Ruins]].
{{NewLeft}}

==Related Quests==
*{{Quest Link|Brothers and Bandits}}

==Quest-Related Events==
You'll find him lamenting his brother's injuries by the entrance.
:''"Dariel! I was supposed to die, not you!"''

Talk to him.
:''"Who's there? Dariel?<br>No, you're someone else. Please, don't kill me. I've nothing of value to steal."''
:'''I'm not going to kill you.'''
:''"Then you're not a bandit? Thank [[Lore:Divines|the Eight]] for small blessings.<br>My brother Dariel wasn't as lucky. He thought he could outsmart the bandits in the ruins. Now he's gone. He'd do anything to find a cure for me, but all I pray for now is his safety."''
:'''Are you sick?'''
:''"I've been sick most of my life. We came looking for a plant, one that grows by the light of an [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]] well. They say its sap can heal any malady.<br>The bandits stopped us, but Dariel made a deal in exchange for passage. I haven't seen him since."''
:'''I'll see if I can find him.'''

You can speak to him further after accepting the quest.
:''"I'm not accustomed to strangers offering help. My brother and I, we've just had each other for so long.<br>The only thing that matters now is Dariel's safety. If you find him, tell him to forget the plant. I won't see him hurt trying to save me."''

If you chose to help Dariel find the sap and you go Daine before finding the sap:
:''"Dariel's been gone for hours. I don't know what I'll do if…if he doesn't come back."''

===Bringing the Sap===
The brothers will converse as they stagger out of the ruin:
:'''Dariel:''' ''"We've got the sap, brother. One trip to the alchemist, and you'll be right as rain!"''
:'''Daine:''' ''"Dariel? I didn't think I'd see you again!"''
:'''Dariel:''' ''"We're sticking together from now on. I promise."''

===Escorting Dariel Out===
The brothers will converse as they stagger out of the ruin:
:'''Dariel:''' ''"Come on Daine. Your friend saved my life, but apparently didn't have time to save yours."''
:'''Daine:''' ''"Dariel? I didn't think I'd see you again!"''
:'''Dariel:''' ''"We're sticking together from now on. I promise."''
