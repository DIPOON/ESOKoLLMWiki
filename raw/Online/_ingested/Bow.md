[[File:ON-icon-skill-Bow.png|frameless|left]]'''Bow''' is one of the six [[Online:Skills#Weapon|weapon skills]] available in Elder Scrolls Online. You can advance through this skill line by equipping and using a [[Online:Bows|bow]].

{{NewLeft}}
== Learning ==
The Bow skill line is granted the first time you kill an enemy with a bow equipped.  You can increase your skill by engaging in combat with a bow equipped, or by having Bow skills slotted.  The more skills you have slotted, the faster your skill will increase.  [[ON:Wood Elf|Wood Elves]] are naturally gifted with bows, and will learn the ability more quickly than other races.  In addition, you can earn free levels in the skill line by reading [[ON:Skill Books|Skill Books]], which may be found randomly on bookshelves anywhere.  The skill books which increase Bow are:
*[[ON:An Archer's Archive|An Archer's Archive]]
*[[ON:Bliss|Bliss]]
*[[ON:Father of the Niben, Fragment One|Father of the Niben, Fragment One]]
*[[ON:The Source of the Bone|The Source of the Bone]]
*[[ON:Sporting Chance|Sporting Chance]]

===Other Sets===
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Hawk's Eye|Hawk's Eye]] set from [[ON:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] reduces the cost and increases the damage of your bow abilities by 6%.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Marksman's Crest|Marksman's Crest]] set from [[ON:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] reduces the cost of your {{ESO Stamina Link}} abilities by 5% and increases the damage of your Bow abilities against Players by 8%.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Red Eagle's Fury|Red Eagle's Fury]] set from [[ON:Red Eagle Redoubt|Red Eagle Redoubt]] crafting site increases the {{ESO Weapon Damage Link|||y}} and {{ESO Spell Damage Link}} damage of all your weapon abilities, Bow included, but it also increases their cost.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Wilderqueen's Arch|Wilderqueen's Arch]] set from overland [[ON:Greenshade|Greenshade]] makes your Bow attacks reduce the [[Online:Movement Speed|Movement Speed]] of any enemy they hit by 0-15% and increase your Movement Speed by 0-15% for 3 seconds.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Daring Corsair|Daring Corsair]] set from the [[ON:Cat's-Claw Station|Cat's-Claw Station]] crafting site decreases the {{ESO Stamina Link}} and {{ESO Magicka Link}} costs of all your weapon abilities by 10%, and grants you [[ON:Minor Heroism|Minor Heroism]] for 8 seconds after you use a weapon skill.
