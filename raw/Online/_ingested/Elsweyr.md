'''''Elsweyr''''' is the third [[Online:Chapters|Chapter]] of [[ON:Online|''The Elder Scrolls Online'']]. It is an expansion set in the kingdom of [[Lore:Anequina|Anequina]], also known as [[ON:Northern Elsweyr|Northern Elsweyr]]. The Chapter includes a new large and diverse zone, the new [[ON:Necromancer|Necromancer]], [[ON:Dragon Hunt|Dragon Hunt]] events, a complimentary house, a new Trial, and a new storyline. It was officially released on all platforms on June 4, 2019, although early access was launched on PC/Mac on May 20, 2019.

Similar to previous Chapters, ''Elsweyr'' has been demoted to a DLC following the release of the [[ON:Greymoor|Greymoor]] Chapter. The [[Online:Necromancer|Necromancer]] class has been moved to the Crown Store, and the ''Elsweyr'' DLC is now available through [[ON:ESO Plus|ESO Plus]] and the [[ON:Crown Store|Crown Store]]. Certain promotional bonus content was available with ''Elsweyr''; see the ''[[ON:Elsweyr Collector's Edition|Elsweyr Collector's Edition]]'' for details.

==Versions==
'''''Elsweyr''''' is available in four different versions:
* '''Elsweyr Pre-purchase Standard Edition''': For new players, this version includes the base game, the ''Morrowind'', ''Summerset'', and ''Elsweyr'' Chapters, [[Online:Rahd-m'Athra|Rahd-m'Athra]] horse, [[Online:Blue Dragon Imp|Blue Dragon Imp]] pet, one [[ON:Baandari Pedlar Crate|Baandari Pedlar Crate]], the [[ON:Costumes#Noble Clan-Chief|Noble Clan-Chief]] costume and Experience Scrolls & Treasure Maps.
* '''Elsweyr Pre-purchase Digital Upgrade''': For existing players, this version includes the base game, the ''Elsweyr'' Chapter, Rahd-m'Athra Horse, Blue Dragon Imp pet, one Baandari Pedlar Crate, the Noble Clan-Chief costume and Experience Scrolls & Treasure Maps.
* [[ON:Elsweyr Collector's Edition|'''Elsweyr Pre-purchase Digital Collector's Edition''']]: For new players, this version includes the base game, the [[ON:Morrowind Collector's Edition|Morrowind Collector's Edition]], [[ON:Summerset Collector's Edition|Summerset Collector's Edition]] and ''Elsweyr'' Chapter, Rahd-m'Athra Horse, Blue Dragon Imp pet, one Baandari Pedlar Crate, the Noble Clan-Chief costume, Experience Scrolls & Treasure Maps and exclusive [[Online:Senche-raht (mount)|Senche-Raht]] mount, [[Online:Ashen Scar Jerboa|Ashen Scar Jerboa]] pet, the [[Online:Khajiiti Cultural Emote Pack|Khajiiti Cultural Emote Pack]], Dragonbone outfit style conversions and [[Online:Archaic Lore Tablets|Archaic Lore Tablets]] memento.
* [[ON:Elsweyr Collector's Edition|'''Elsweyr Pre-purchase Digital Collector's Edition Upgrade''']]: For existing players, this version includes the ''Elsweyr'' Chapter, Rahd-m'Athra Horse, Blue Dragon Imp pet, one Baandari Pedlar Crate, the Noble Clan-Chief costume, Experience Scrolls & Treasure Maps and exclusive Senche-Raht mount, Ashen Scar Jerboa pet, the Khajiiti Cultural emote pack, Dragonbone outfit style conversions and Archaic Lore Tablets memento.

==Release==
''Elsweyr'' was initially teased on January 9, 2019 after an [[General:ESO Live|ESO Live]] stream, when a preview of the teaser trailer was released showing [[ON:Khamira|Khamira]] and [[ON:Abnur Tharn|Abnur Tharn]] reassembling the Wrathstone tablet. A promotional [[:File:ON-prerelease-2019 Q1 Teaser.jpg|Wrathstone Tablet and letter from Tharn]] were also distributed to community promoters at this time. The Chapter was officially announced on January 15, 2019 with the tagline ''"The Season of the Dragon Approaches"''. Datamined text for the chapter reads ''"ESO reaches new heights of storytelling with a war against the [[ON:Dragon|Dragon]]s that unfolds and builds over four quarterly releases to an unexpected climax."'' ''Elsweyr'' was released in [[ON:Update 22|Update 22]], being on the [[ON:PTS|PTS]] on April 16, 2019, PC early access on May 20, and console release on June 4.
