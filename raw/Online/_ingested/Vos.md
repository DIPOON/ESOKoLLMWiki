It is ruled over from [[Online:Tel Mora|Tel Mora]] by [[Online:House Telvanni|Telvanni]] wizard [[Online:Mistress Dratha|Mistress Dratha]], who has recently fallen ill. [[ON:Savarak Fels|Savarak Fels]] now rules over the town in the meantime as magistrate, though his leadership has quickly led to a decrease in quality of life.

A [[ON:A Petition for the Mighty Nix-Ox|petition]] is posted on a board in town. A copy of ''[[ON:Dark Elves, Dark Hearts|Dark Elves, Dark Hearts]]'' can be found on the floor of a shed out in the saltrice paddies.

[[ON:Nix-Ox|Nix-Oxen]] and [[ON:Pony Guar|pony guar]] cavort in the saltrice paddies where several [[ON:Argonian|Argonians]] are working. A copy of ''[[ON:Blessed Almalexia's Fables for Evening|Blessed Almalexia's Fables for Evening]]'' can be found atop a table on the patio to the east of [[ON:Savarak's Manse|Savarak's Manse]]. A [[ON:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can be found atop a building in western Vos.
