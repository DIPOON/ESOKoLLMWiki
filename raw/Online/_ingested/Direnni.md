The [[Online:Direnni|Direnni]] were a prominent clan of [[Online:High Elf|Altmer]] merchants who became a powerful dynasty that ruled [[Lore:High Rock|High Rock]] during the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]]. They faced the [[ON:Alessian Order (faction)|Alessian Order]] at the [[ON:Glenumbra Moors|Glenumbra Moors]].<noinclude> The [[Lore:Adamantine Tower|Direnni Tower]] on the [[Online:Isle of Balfiera|Isle of Balfiera]] can be sighted from the shores of [[ON:Glenumbra|Glenumbra]] and [[ON:Stormhaven|Stormhaven]], and the reportedly haunted [[ON:Direnni Acropolis|family acropolis]] can be visited on [[ON:Summerset|Summerset Isle]].
