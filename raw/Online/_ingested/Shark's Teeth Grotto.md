The Wake Walkers wrested control from the grotto's previous inhabitants, the [[ON:Shark's Teeth|Shark's Teeth]]. [[ON:Scorpion|Scorpions]] are found in numbers within. Hazards include: [[ON:Traps#Flare Trap|Flare Traps]], [[ON:Traps#Oil Trap|Oil Traps]], [[ON:Slaughterfish|Slaughterfish]]

The boss battle involves attacking [[ON:First Mate Rodros|First Mate Rodros]] and his pet [[ON:Giant Snake|snakes]] [[ON:Laylah|Laylah]] and [[ON:Leelo|Leelo]] in order to summon a [[ON:Haj Mota|Haj Mota]] named [[ON:Krona Keeba|Krona Keeba]].

The book ''[[Online:Legend of Shalug the Shark|Legend of Shalug the Shark]]'' can be found at the top of the second set of stairs.

==Notes==
*Numerous traps can be found throughout the delve, ranging from situational traps such tripwire and flare traps, to more lethal traps like explosive and poison gas traps that can quickly drain your health.
**Most of them will appear highlighted when crouching, however some will not, like the bear traps and the poison jar traps.
