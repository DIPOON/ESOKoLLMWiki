|loadtext=The Second Empire made some advances into the periphery of Black Marsh, but they didn't stay long. Arx Corinium, once an Imperial fort, is now occupied by denizens of the deep swamp—and even stranger things.
}}{{TOCleft}} While previously constructed and held by the [[ON:Imperial|Imperials]], the fort has fallen into disrepair and is overrun with [[ON:Lamia|Lamias]]. A group of [[ON:Nereid|nereids]] now occupy the flooded fort and focus on restoring balance to the land. Rumors say the fort is home to a great source of power, although it is perverse, corrupted.  You can learn about its location from [[ON:Mindil the Untested|Mindil the Untested]] in [[ON:Marbruk|Marbruk]], [[ON:Abzag the Monster|Abzag the Monster]] in [[ON:Shornhelm|Shornhelm]], or [[ON:Maela the Crone|Maela the Crone]] in [[ON:Stormhold|Stormhold]].

Its [[ON:Dungeon Sets|dungeon sets]] are [[ON:Lamia's Song|Lamia's Song]], [[ON:Medusa|Medusa]], and [[ON:Undaunted Infiltrator|Undaunted Infiltrator]].

There are always 4 treasure chests available to find in this dungeon, which may contain weapons, armor or jewelry from the dungeon's sets.

==Notes==
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Arx Corinium was designed for levels 28-31.
* While traveling Shadowfen within the vicinity of Arx Corinium, you may spot a distant, non-hostile nereid in the direction of the dungeon that vanishes into the swamp when approached, only to reappear again in the direction of the dungeon, leading you toward it.
