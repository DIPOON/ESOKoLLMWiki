==Notes==
*The northernmost part of the ruins, which is just outside of the zone boundaries, was probably originally supposed to represent the ruin of [[ON:Silorn|Silorn]] (note the distinct cross-shaped layout of the ruin). However, Silorn later made a proper appearance in the [[ON:West Weald|West Weald]] zone.
