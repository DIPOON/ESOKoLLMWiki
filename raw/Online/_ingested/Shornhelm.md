}} The city is split into two main districts: the Upper City contains the castle and manor houses, while the Lower City contains the stores and other residences. The ruler of Rivenspire holds court in [[Online:Shornhelm Castle|Shornhelm Castle]], but since the events of Ranser's War, the province has had no king or queen; instead, Rivenspire has been ruled by a triumvirate comprising [[Online:House Montclair|House Montclair]], [[Online:House Tamrith|House Tamrith]] and [[Online:House Dorell|House Dorell]]. House Montclair has recently led an attack on the city, resulting in them controlling the upper city.

The front gate is located to the south. A set of stairs leading up to the city crosses through the [[Online:South Shornhelm Stalls|South Shornhelm Stalls]], where [[Online:Guild Traders|guild traders]] gather. When you enter the city, you'll find yourself in the [[Online:Shornhelm Market Plaza|Shornhelm Market Plaza]]. The Navigators are in the northeast corner of the market, in front of [[Online:Sorick House|Sorick House]] and [[Online:Candile Elles' House|Candile Elles' House]]. The [[Online:Dead Wolf Inn|Dead Wolf Inn]] is in the northwest corner of the market.

If you head down the street east from the market, you'll go down a street called [[Online:Tailor's Way|Tailor's Way]]. The [[Online:Leatherworkers|leatherworker]], [[Online:Endless Autumn Outfitters|Endless Autumn Outfitters]], is the first building on the left. [[Online:Westmark Boots and Apparel|Westmark Boots and Apparel]], the [[Online:Tailors|tailor]], is just across the street. A ruined home is next door to the leatherworker on the east side. There's a trapdoor leading to a Hidden Basement inside the building. You'll find the [[Online:Fighters Guild (Shornhelm)|Fighters Guild]] in the southeastern corner of the city. It is connected to an [[Online:Armorers|armory]] called [[Online:The Fighting Edge|The Fighting Edge]].

The street passing the Fighters Guild leads north. On the west side, the first thing you'll pass by is the [[Online:Shornhelm Smithy|Shornhelm Smithy]]. The local [[Online:Shornhelm Outlaws Refuge|Outlaws Refuge]] lies just southwest of the smithy; a [[Online:Cat|cat]] sits next to the grate leading inside. If you head down the road east of the smithy, you'll pass by [[Online:Hammer & Saw Carpentry|Hammer & Saw Carpentry]] and [[Online:Moorside Mounts|Moorside Mounts]]. The city's eastern gate lies beyond the stable. You can turn in crafting writs at the eastern gate; there's also a refugee camp here. The [[Online:Shornhelm Wayshrine|wayshrine]] lies northeast of the smithy, up a flight of stairs. The [[Online:Shornhelm Savings and Loan|bank]] lies down the street heading west, north of the smithy.

If you head west of the market plaza, the first shop you'll pass by is [[Online:Untamed Elixirs|Untamed Elixirs]], which is on the north side of the street. The outfit station is right next to the alchemist's shop, and [[Online:Tildus Falon's House|Tildus Falon's House]] is at the southwest corner of the road. [[Online:Merethrin's House|Merethrin's House]] is the next house over. A copy of the lore book, ''[[Online:Vivec and Mephala|Vivec and Mephala]]'' sits atop a fence behind the house. The road turns north from here. The [[Online:Mages Guild (Shornhelm)|Mages Guild]] is on the west side of the street, and [[Online:Stoneglow Enchantments|Stoneglow Enchantments]] lies across from it. The western gate lies north and west of the Mages Guild. The [[Online:Shornhelm Chapel|Shornhelm Chapel]] lies down the street heading north from the guildhall. An [[Online:Abandoned Cistern|abandoned cistern]] lies across the street west of the chapel, in front of [[Online:Haliyya's House|Haliyya's House]].

[[Online:Brea Knodel's House|Brea Knodel's House]] lies up the stairs north of the market plaza. From in front of this building, you can take the road west to go to the chapel, or you can head east towards the bank and the smithy. [[Online:Davin's Books|Davin's Books]] lies to the west, between the chapel and Brea's house.
