'''Green Pact''' is an [[ON:Overland Sets#Alliance Zones|overland set]] that drops in [[ON:Grahtwood|Grahtwood]]. It cannot be [[ON:Craftable Sets|crafted]]. Pieces are [[ON:Wood Elf Style|Wood Elf Style]] in [[ON:Heavy Armor|Heavy Armor]].

===Bonuses===
<onlyinclude>
'''2 items''': Adds {{ESO Health Link|28-1206 Maximum}}<br>
'''3 items''': Adds {{ESO Health Link|3-129|Recovery}}<br>
'''4 items''': Adds {{ESO Health Link|28-1206 Maximum}}<br>
'''5 items''': While you have a food buff active, your {{ESO Health Link|Max}} is increased by 58-2500 and {{ESO Health Link||Recovery}} by 8-356.
</onlyinclude>
===Pieces===
The set consists of heavy armor and jewelry in all slots, as well as weapons and shields of all types. Unique pieces include:
* {{Item Link|id=34663|Time Buckler of the Green Pact|quality=3|summary=1}}, a unique drop from [[ON:Lieutenant Khari|Lieutenant Khari]].
* {{Item Link|id=34664|Spiderstaff of the Green Pact|quality=3|summary=1}}, a unique drop from [[ON:Spider Queen|Spider Queen]].
* {{Item Link|id=42993|Nindaeril's Tooth of the Green Pact|quality=3|summary=1}}, a unique drop from [[ON:Nindaeril the Monsoon|Nindaeril the Monsoon]].
* {{Item Link|id=42997|Valanir's Lost Hope of the Green Pact|quality=3|summary=1}}, a unique drop from [[ON:Valanir the Restless|Valanir the Restless]].
* {{Item Link|id=1674|Ukaezai's Scalpel|quality=3|summary=1}}, a quest reward for [[ON:Heart of the Matter|Heart of the Matter]].
