}} It is ruled by [[Online:House Dres|House Dres]], but has a significant presence of [[Online:House Redoran|House Redoran]] and [[Online:House Hlaalu|House Hlaalu]]. The northern district of the city is inhabited by former [[Online:Argonian|Argonian]] slaves, who even have their own [[Lore:Hist|Hist]] tree, while [[Lore:Ashlander|Ashlander]] traders and [[Online:Nord|Nord]] sawyers camp outside the southern gate.

The city is led by [[Online:Drathus Othral|Drathus Othral]]. Its citizens are experiencing some racial tensions.

[[Online:Io-Io|Io-Io]] the [[Online:Traveling Merchants|traveling merchant]] walks along the road between Ebonheart and [[ON:Vivec's Antlers|Vivec's Antlers]] with her [[ON:Caravan Guar|caravan guar]] and her companion, [[Online:Caravaneer Iggistill|Caravaneer Iggistill]]. Another traveling merchant, [[Online:Lathisa Heryon|Lathisa Heryon]], can be found between Ebonheart and [[Online:Othrenis|Othrenis]].

==Layout==
The Argonian Quarter is located north of the city proper. There is a small Hist tree in the center of the marshy stretch of land. The city's docks are located west of the Argonian Quarter. A chest can spawn on the northern edge of the dock, near the Argonian [[Online:Smuggler|smuggler]]'s boat.
