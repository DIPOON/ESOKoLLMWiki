'''William Nurin''' is a [[Online:Breton|Breton]] farmer at his [[Online:Nurin Farm|farm]] north of [[Online:Koeglin Village|Koeglin Village]]. After a recent Daedra attack on his farm, he has gone missing and his daughter [[ON:Ganise Nurin|Ganise]] wants some helping looking for him, despite his wife [[ON:Rosalie Nurin|Rosalie]]'s insistence that he is likely dead.

==Quest-Related Events==
===A Family Affair===
[[File:ON-quest-A Family Affair.jpg|thumb|right|William held captive]]
After some investigating you will find out that William was actually abducted to be sacrificed for Rosalie's initiation into the [[ON:Supernal Dreamers|Supernal Dreamers]]. He can be found tied up on an altar while Rosalie and [[ON:Hark Sentulus|Hark Sentulus]] perform the rituals. Once they have been killed, you can free and he immediately run off, desperate to find his daughter:
:'''William Nurin''': ''"Where's Ganise? I have to find her!"''

When you arrive back at the farm he can be found with Ganise.
:''"I just can't believe my Rosalie would … it's just unthinkable.<br>I owe you for my life, and for my daughter's welfare. Though she wants to thank you herself."''

===Scamp Invasion===
After you have spoken with Ganise back at the farmhouse, William will still be shaken by Rosalie's betrayal but will ask for your further assistance:
:''"If you're willing, I might ask one other favor from you?"''

Speaking to him:
:''"To think I shared a bed with that woman and she nearly killed me … I suppose she may have been under the influence of that crazy cultist, Sentulus.<br>That reminds me. I hate to ask anything else of you, but I think there may still be some danger."''
:'''What danger?'''
:''"Well, the [[ON:Scamp|scamp]]s running around here for one thing, but I have reason to believe there could be worse things coming if we don't act fast.<br>That crazy Sentulus was telling Rosalie about these portals he had opened up around the farm."''
:'''We should destroy these portals.'''
:''"My thoughts exactly, and I'd like to go out and do it myself ….<br>I wouldn't want Ganise to lose both her parents. I think it might be smarter if you go. If you're willing, that is."''
:'''I'll destroy the portals.'''

Speaking to him again:
:''"If you can just take care of those portals, I'm sure the rest of the scamps will wander off eventually."''

Once you have destroyed the four portals around the farm, you can return to William.
:''"Were those Daedra death screams I heard? Music to my ears.<br>And you destroyed those damnable portals too. I think now we'll be safe."''
:'''I think you're going to be fine.'''
:''"It's hard to believe that someone I don't even know would go so far out of their way to help us.<br>There's hope now, at least. Thank you."''

==Dialogue==
Surveying the damage to his farm, he'll remark that he wish he had more children to help work the farm. You can ask him about [[Online:Ingride Vanne|Ingride Vanne]], to which he'll remark that he always thought she was pretty, and note that she's a widow.
:''"I guess the next thing is start making plans to rebuild. Times like these I wish we had more children—a larger family, you know?<br>I guess there's always the future."''
:'''You know, there is Ingride Vanne ….'''
:''"My neighbor? Are you saying what I think you're saying? I mean, Ingride's nice, but she's ….<br>Well, I always did think she was rather pretty, and she is a widow. I think maybe I'll just have to pay her a visit. Thanks for the suggestion."''
