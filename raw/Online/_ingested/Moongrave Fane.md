However, they wish to do more than simply destroy the humbled beast, as the clan prepares a ritual to drain the dragon of its blood and gain its power.

==Backstory Description==
;Update 23 Patch Notes
''Not everyone who hunts Dragons does so with noble intentions. Within the ancient ruins of a forgotten Khajiiti faith, the vampires of the Hollowfang Clan hold one of these powerful creatures captive, and their intentions are anything but noble. Join the mysterious Dragonguard, Chevalier Renald, in his crusade against the Hollowfangs before their nefarious plans spell doom for his misguided comrade.''

;[https://www.elderscrollsonline.com/en-us/news/post/56655 Battle Deadly Vampires & Stop a Terrible Ritual in Moongrave Fane]
''Deep within the ruins of an abandoned Khajiiti temple, the Hollowfang Clan has struck down a Dragon from the sky and now holds it captive. However, instead of destroying the massive beast, the clan, joined by a former member of the Dragonguard, has other, more nefarious plans for the monster … This Dragonguard vampire Grundwulf is convinced that he can drink the blood of the Dragon Sahrotnax and absorb his power … Hoping to save his brother in arms before he dooms himself, Chevalier Renald has recruited you and your team to enter Moongrave Fane, confront the vampires, and put a stop to the terrible ritual before it's too late.''

;Scalebreaker Bundle Details
''A clan of vampires have captured a Dragon at Moongrave Fane and plan to drain its blood to gain its power. You must venture deep within the jungle temple to stop these vampires and the lost Dragonguard that leads them.''

==Layout==
===Moongrave Fane Grounds===
A note titled ''[[ON:Enough Kitten Play!|Enough Kitten Play!]]'' is located just before the area with the first [[ON:Sliding Stone|sliding stone]] puzzle, beside a wall in the southeast corner. The [[ON:Risen Ruins|Risen Ruins]] guards the door at the northernmost end of the path.
{{NewLine}}

===Fane of Waning Moons===
[[File:ON-place-Fane of Waning Moons.jpg|thumb|right|The Fane of Waning Moons]]
This area lies beyond the Risen Ruins. [[ON:Dro'zakar|Dro'zakar]] is the boss of this section.
{{NewLine}}

===Penumbral Path===
[[File:ON-place-Penumbral Path.jpg|thumb|right|The Penumbral Path]]
[[ON:Kujo Kethba|Kujo Kethba]] lies at the end of the path, and guards the door leading to Moonlight's Mausoleum.
{{NewLine}}

===Moonlight's Mausoleum===
[[File:ON-place-Moonlight's Mausoleum.jpg|Moonlight's Mausoleum|thumb|right]]
[[ON:Sahrotnax|Sahrotnax]] is being held prisoner at the crash site in the deepest recesses of the mausoleum. You will fight [[ON:Nisaazda|Nisaazda]] and [[ON:Grundwulf|Grundwulf]] on the platform overlooking him.
{{NewLine}}
