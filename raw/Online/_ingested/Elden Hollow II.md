==Notes==
* Before dungeon scaling was introduced in Update 5, Normal Elden Hollow was designed for levels 20–23, and Veteran Elden Hollow was designed for  Veteran Ranks 6–10.
* Prior to Update 12, [[ON:Elden Hollow I|Elden Hollow I]] was only available in Normal mode, and Elden Hollow II was only available in Veteran mode. These were named ''Elden Hollow'' and ''Veteran Elden Hollow'' respectively.
* The part of Oblivion where [[ON:Murklight|Murklight]] and [[ON:Bogdan the Nightflame|Bogdan]] are found was initially called "Nightflame", before being changed to "Daedric Ruins" a few updates later, and again to "Daedric Realm" after that.
