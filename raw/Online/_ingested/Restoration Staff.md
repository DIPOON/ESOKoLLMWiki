'''Restoration Staff''' is one of the six [[Online:Skills#Weapon|weapon skills]] available in Elder Scrolls Online. Most active and passive abilities enabled by '''Restoration Staves''' focus on protecting and healing allies, while others help recharge the user's magicka. In addition, dealing a fully-charged heavy attack to an enemy will also recharge the user's magicka based on their level. The use of these abilities, and progression in the skill line, require equipping a Restoration type staff.
{{NewLeft}}
== Learning ==
The Restoration Staff skill line is granted the first time you kill an enemy with a restoration staff equipped.  You can increase your skill by engaging in combat with a restoration staff equipped, or by having Restoration Staff skills slotted.  The more skills you have slotted, the faster your skill will increase.  [[ON:Argonian|Argonian]]s are naturally gifted with Restoration Staves, and will learn the ability more quickly than other races.  In addition, you can earn free levels in the skill line by reading [[ON:Skill Books|Skill Books]], which may be found randomly on bookshelves anywhere.

===Other Sets===
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Light Speaker|Light Speaker]] set from the [[ON:Elden Hollow I|Elden Hollow I]] and [[ON:Elden Hollow II|II]] group dungeons increases the {{ESO Weapon Damage Link|||y}} and {{ESO Spell Damage Link}} of your Restoration Staff abilities.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Red Eagle's Fury|Red Eagle's Fury]] set from the [[ON:Red Eagle Redoubt|Red Eagle Redoubt]] crafting site increases the {{ESO Weapon Damage Link|||y}} and {{ESO Spell Damage Link}} damage of all your weapon abilities, Restoration Staff included, but it also increases their cost.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Daring Corsair|Daring Corsair]] set from the [[ON:Cat's-Claw Station|Cat's-Claw Station]] crafting site decreases the {{ESO Stamina Link}} and {{ESO Magicka Link}} costs of all your weapon abilities by 10%, and grants you [[ON:Minor Heroism|Minor Heroism]] for 8 seconds after you use a weapon skill.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Eyes of Mara|Eyes of Mara]] set from the [[ON:Mara's Eye Manufactory|Mara's Eye Manufactory]] crafting site decreases the cost of your Restoration Staff abilities by 15%.
* Wearing five pieces of the [[ON:Wisdom of Vanus|Wisdom of Vanus]] set from the [[ON:Summerset|Summerset]] overland grants [[ON:Major Mending|Major Mending]] for 5 seconds. This duration increases by 1 second for each rank of the Restoration Staff passive [[ON:Essence Drain|Essence Drain]], if you are using a Restoration Staff.
