}} The city is the main seat of power for [[Online:House Telvanni|House Telvanni]] on Vvardenfell, and contains the wizard's tower of [[Online:Tel Naga|Tel Naga]]. ''[[ON:Testimonials on Mushroom Towers|Testimonials on Mushroom Towers]]'' can be found sitting on a barrel near the wayshrine. Sadryon Ancestral Tomb is located north of the city.

A muckspunge farm is located to the west of the main island. [[ON:Kaushtarari|Kaushtarari]] can be found south of Sadrith Mora. [[ON:Hlervi Ancestral Tomb|Hlervi Ancestral Tomb]] lies to the northeast of Sadrith Mora, while [[ON:Hanud Tower|Hanud Tower]] can be found to the northwest. [[ON:Anudnabia|Anudnabia]] is located east of Sadrith Mora, on the neighboring islands.
