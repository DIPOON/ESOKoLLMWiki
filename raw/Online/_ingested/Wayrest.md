}} It is a prosperous trading city situated on the northern coast of [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]].
[[File:ON-place-Wayrest Castle.jpg|thumb||Wayrest Castle]]
The city is split into seven districts. In the northern part of the city, running from the north gate to the east gate, lies the Residential District where many of the homes are situated. The [[Online:Foal's Rest Stables|city stables]] and [[Online:Cloudy Dregs Inn|an inn]] can be found by the north gate. West of the river and south of the Residential District lie the Merchant District and Market Square, which contain many of the city's traders and merchants, as well as the [[Online:Fighters Guild Hall (Wayrest)|Fighters Guild]]. In the west, the [[Online:Temple of the Divines (Wayrest)|Temple of the Divines]] and [[Online:Wayrest Castle|Wayrest Castle]] dominate the Temple and Palace Districts respectively, while in the east, the [[Online:Wayrest Treasury|Wayrest Treasury]] building marks the Banking District. The west gate, leading towards [[Online:Farangel's Landing|Farangel's Landing]] and the [[Online:At-Tura Estate|At-Tura Estate]], can be found in the Palace District, just past Wayrest Wayshrine. Just inside the south gate, the Mages District houses the [[Online:Mages Guild Hall (Wayrest)|Mages Guild]] and the [[Online:Chironasium (Wayrest)|Chironasium]]. Outside the south gate, on the banks of Iliac Bay, sit Wayrest Docks, from which it is possible to buy passage to [[Online:Alik'r Desert|Alik'r]].

Outside the city walls to the north lies the shanty town of [[Online:Dreughside|Dreughside]]. This district has recently been besieged by the [[Online:dreugh|dreugh]] from which it gets its name.
