The bodies of some [[ON:Blackguards|Blackguards]] can also be found here, along with their map piece. The exterior of the delve is surrounded by [[ON:Wasp|wasp]]s and [[ON:Swarming Wasp|swarming wasps]]. Inside the delve you will meet [[ON:Zahra|Zahra]], a Khajiit treasure hunter, who needs your help retrieving an Ayleid artifact.

[[ON:Ayleid Library?|A fragment of a note]] can be found west from the delve's entrance. [[ON:Argonian Journal Pages|Parts of an Argonian journal]] can be found in the northern part of the last room.

There is a [[ON:Skyshard|skyshard]] located rather close to the entrance, just outside the one-way exit from the delve's last room.
