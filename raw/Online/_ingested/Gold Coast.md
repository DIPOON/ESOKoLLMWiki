}} During {{Lore Link|Varen's Rebellion}}, a rough stone wall was built along the region's border with the {{Lore Link|Colovian Highlands}} to protect the cities of [[Online:Kvatch|Kvatch]] and [[Online:Anvil|Anvil]] from retaliation by the [[Lore:Longhouse Emperors|Longhouse Emperors]]. The wall has since played a major part in keeping the region protected from the fallout of the [[Online:Alliance War|Alliance War]] which is raging across the rest of [[Online:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]].

The Gold Coast is a [[Online:DLC|DLC]] zone added by the [[ON:Dark Brotherhood (DLC)|Dark Brotherhood]] DLC. The zone also contains an entrance to the [[ON:Depths of Malatar|Depths of Malatar]] and [[ON:Black Drake Villa|Black Drake Villa]] [[ON:Group Dungeons|group dungeon]]s, but the respective [[ON:Wrathstone|Wrathstone]] and [[ON:Flames of Ambition|Flames of Ambition]] DLCs are needed in order to access them.

==Notes==
* The Gold Coast previously [[OB:Gold Coast|appeared]] in [[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]].
* [[ON:Nodes|Nodes]] in the zone have a chance of containing a [[ON:Grain of Pearl Sand|Grain of Pearl Sand]], while [[ON:Minotaur|minotaur]]s killed in the region may drop [[ON:Oxblood Fungus Spores|Oxblood Fungus Spores]].
* This zone is called "The Gold Coast" in your journal.
* Up until the release of [[ON:Gold Road|Gold Road]], at Varen's Wall Gatehouse was a permanently closed gate leading to "The Colovian Estates" - an area that did not exist as a playable location in the game. Now this gate and the unmarked one past the Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary both lead to [[ON:West Weald|West Weald]].
