}} A magma-filled fissure opened up through the center of town almost four years ago as a result of the [[Lore:Soulburst|Soulburst]], destroying buildings and plaguing the town with [[Online:Flame Atronach|flame atronachs]]. Despite this, survivors and adventurers continue to live there.

A [[ON:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can be found upstairs in the ruined house across the street from [[ON:Castorius' House|Castorius' House]]. A copy of ''[[ON:The Chorrol Crier|The Chorrol Crier]]'' can be found in [[ON:Orneldas Caudex|Orneldas Caudex]]'s sinking house, across from Chorrol's chapel. A copy of ''[[ON:The Legendary Sancre Tor, 1st Ed.|The Legendary Sancre Tor, 1st Ed.]]'' can be found  in front of the statue within the chapel.

The alliance who controls Fort Rayles also controls Chorrol. [[ON:Lliae the Quick|Lliae the Quick]] is handing out repeatable quests behind the storehouse.

A [[Online:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can spawn beside [[Online:Senadon|Senadon]]'s campfire.
