It was the original founding place of the [[ON:Silver Dawn|Order of the Silver Dawn]], but recently has been taken over by the [[ON:Magnar Pack|Magnar Pack]], a band of [[Online:Werewolf (NPC)|werewolves]]. The ''[[ON:Silver Linings|Silver Linings]]'' quest involves recovering the sacred banner of the order from the mine.

==Notes==
* A relic for the [[Online:House of Orsimer Glories|House of Orsimer Glories]], Liquid Silver, is located inside a tent in the room at the center-right of the map.
* The boulder fall traps inside mentioned by [[ON:Dirdre|Dirdre]] pose little threat, as even when triggered, the boulders take a long time to actually drop, giving plenty of time to continue moving forward. Even if hit, the damage they inflict is minimal. The bear traps however remain a lot more dangerous.
