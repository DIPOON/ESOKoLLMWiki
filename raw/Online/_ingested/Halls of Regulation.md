==Notes==
*Halls of Regulation also [[Legends:The Halls of Regulation|appears]] in ''[[Legends:Legends|Legends]]''.
*Some of the Firepot Spiders found inside are already overcharged when encountered.
**After killing Dancing and Firepot Spiders near tunnels in the halls of the delve, the nearby tunnel will emit a noise and release an overcharged Firepot Spider that will charge and explode at you.
*Unlike in most other locations, the skeevatons inside the delve actively flee from you if you get close.
