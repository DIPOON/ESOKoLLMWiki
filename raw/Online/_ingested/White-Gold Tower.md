==Hard Mode==
Hard Mode is only available in the [[ON:Veteran|Veteran]] version of White-Gold Tower. Hard mode is initiated by completing the ritual in the Obelisk Tome.
:''<The book contains a ritual which imbues Molag Bal's agents with great power. Speaking the following words should bolster the Daedric Prince's champions with his ire.><br>"God of Schemes, inflict your brutality upon us."''
:'''God of Schemes, inflict your brutality upon us.'''

Speaking the words written in the tome will cause a blinding flash of light, making the boss fight more difficult. Molag Kena deals more damage while Hard Mode is active. If your group dies, the spell will need to be cast again to re-enable Hard Mode.

==Layout==
===Palace Sewers===
The entrance. [[ON:Empress Regent Clivia Tharn|Empress Regent Clivia Tharn]], [[ON:Sister Terran Arminus|Sister Terran Arminus]], the Empress' guards, and several Imperial [[ON:Imperial Archer|archers]], [[ON:Imperial Mender|healers]], and [[ON:Imperial Infantry|soldiers]] can be found here. The Daedric Embers Trophy Vault is located through a tunnel on the west side of the room.
{{NewLine}}

===Green Emperor Way===
[[File:ON-place-Green Emperor Way.jpg|Green Emperor Way|thumb|right]]

*First pull: three zombies, two skeletal foot soldiers, one Xivkyn Lacerator.
*Second pull: three zombies, one skeletal foot soldiers, one skeletal assassin, one Dremora Morikyn, one Xivkyn Flagellant.
*Third pull: four zombies, one skeletal assassin, one Xivkyn Lacerator and one Xivkyn Bulwark.
*There are Daedra on the stairs west of the third pull: A Xivkyn Flagellant, a Dremora Morikyn, a Dremora Ferylkyn, and a Zombie.
*Above the second pull (on the ledge east of it; north of the Iron-Swathed Glutton), there's a group of Daedra watching a brawl between a Dremora Sunderer and a Skeletal Assassin. A Dremora Morikyn, a Dremora Ferylkyn, and a Xivkyn Flagellant are watching, laughing and cheering while the two square up. A heavy sack can be found beside a headstone on this ledge.

{{NewLine}}

===Throne Room===
[[File:ON-place-White-Gold Tower (Throne Room).jpg|thumb|right|Throne Room]]
[[ON:The Adjudicator|The Adjudicator]] resides here. When you enter the throne room, you'll see her execute three Imperial [[ON:Prisoner|Prisoners]].

A chest and a [[ON:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can spawn by the Ruby Throne, behind the boss.
{{NewLine}}

===Imperial Guard Quarters===
The first pull consists of one Xivkyn Flagellant, one Dremora Morikyn, three Skeletal Foot Soldiers. There's a snake near the door to the next pull. A chest can spawn in the nook located across from the door to the next room. In the next room, you'll encounter two Planemeld Rifts.

The third pull is just out in the hallway and to the left. It consists of three Skeletal Foot Soldiers, a Dremora Morikyn, a Xivkyn Bulwark,a Xivkyn Lacerator, and a Skeletal Assassin. A passive snake slithers among their boots, and there's a fiendroth ahead and to the right. The door at the end of this hallway brings you to the Lower Elder Scrolls Library.
{{NewLine}}

===Lower Elder Scroll Library===
[[File:ON-place-Lower Elder Scroll Library.jpg|thumb|right|Lower Elder Scroll Library]]
When you pass through the door, you'll end up in a hallway cluttered with rubble, almost to a claustrophobic extent. The first group of enemies in this hallway consists of four Banekin, two Skeletal Assassins, a Skeletal Foot Soldier, and a Dremora Morikyn. A [[ON:Crematorial Guard|Crematorial Guard]] blocks the way through the door at the end of the hall.

The door brings you to the next section of the library, where you'll fight the Imperial Dragonguard. Sister Terran reveals the Elder Scroll, and Clivia Tharn grabs it, causing the ceiling to explode. She then commands her Dragonguard to kill everyone present as she flees through the side door.
{{NewLine}}

==Notes==
*The dungeon originally had a minimum enemy level of 25. This was reduced to 10 in one of the updates.
*Completing the dungeon will remove the Dark Anchor in the instance, but not in Cyrodiil or the Imperial City.
*The tower can be seen from many places outside the dungeon; each district of the Imperial City has its own version of the tower in the distance, the tower and city can be seen from Cyrodiil, and the tower is also present in the skybox for [[ON:Blackwood|Blackwood]] and the [[ON:West Weald|West Weald]].
*White-Gold Tower previously appeared in [[Arena:The Imperial Palace|''Arena'']] and [[OB:Imperial Palace|''Oblivion'']] as the Imperial Palace.
**Green Emperor Way also [[OB:Green Emperor Way|appears]] in [[OB:Oblivion|''Oblivion'']].
