The frozen undead wander the wilderness nearby, around Morthal. Frozen zombies are found in the marsh north of the city, while skeletal remains lie closer to [[Online:Kjenstag Ruins|Kjenstag Ruins]], to the east. 

One of the instruments needed for [[ON:An Instrumental Triumph|An Instrumental Triumph]]—Shriek of Silk—can be found on the top floor of [[ON:Jarl Reddharn's Hall|Jarl Reddharn's Hall]].

[[Online:Marcelle Melie|Marcelle Melie]] the [[Online:Traveling Merchants|traveling merchant]] travels the road south of Morthal.
