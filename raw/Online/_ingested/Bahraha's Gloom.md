The tomb served as the headquarters of a local cult of necromancers known as the [[ON:Bleak Veil|Bleak Veil]], which was led by [[Lore:Hubalajad|Prince Hew]]'s uncle. After discovering what they were doing inside, Prince Hew decided to act to save face by sealing them inside. However, rather than dying, they fully committed to their cause and turned themselves into undead.

Their confinement has recently been broken by treasure-hunting pirates known as the [[ON:Salt Catchers|Salt Catchers]], who entered the tomb and disturbed the seal. The pirates have since then been either turned into skeletons to serve the necromancers or their [[ON:Salt Catcher Spirit|spirits]] being tormented instead.

The boss battle of the delve involves killing a [[ON:Mournful Aegis|mournful aegis]] named [[ON:The First|the First]] in order to awaken [[ON:Magnifico Bahraha|Magnifico Bahraha]], a [[ON:Lich|lich]]. The delve also has an optional boss, a [[ON:Flesh Atronach|flesh atronach]] named [[ON:The Covetous Damned|the Covetous Damned]].

Although visible in the first room, the [[ON:Skyshards|skyshard]] can only be reached by means of a hidden passage just off the final chamber. A [[ON:The Bleak Veil Endures|book]] can be found on a table in the central chamber.
