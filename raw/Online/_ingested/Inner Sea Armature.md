The delve consists of circular Dwarven corridors connecting two main chambers, and breaking through into a natural cavern to the south.  A [[Online:Skyshard|Skyshard]] can be found in the large northernmost chamber. A ''[[ON:Tattered Note|Tattered Note]]'' can be found at a four-way intersection near the entrance to the natural caves. These caves contain the {{Item Link|id=129907|q=0|Construct's Left Hand}}, found laying on the ground near some gears; this is needed for the quest [[ON:The Precursor|The Precursor]]. The unique Dwarven Sphere [[Online:Zozuzetharus|Zozuzetharus]] can be found in a shallow pool in the center of the natural cavern. 

During the quest [[ON:A Mother's Obsession|A Mother's Obsession]], [[ON:Irenni Elendis|Irenni Elendis]] can be found here, imprisoned by the bandits in their pantry.
