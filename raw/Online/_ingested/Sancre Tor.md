==Sancre Tor==
===Sancre Tor Antechamber===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor.jpg|thumb|left|Sancre Tor Antechamber]]
This is the first major area within Sancre Tor. [[Online:Mannimarco|Mannimarco]] will interrupt your delve when you reach the largest chamber. There are no enemies here.
{{NewLeft}}

===Sancre Tor Interior===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor 09.jpg|thumb|left|Sancre Tor Interior]]
The Crypt of Heroes lies to the north. The Inner Courtyard can be reached by continuing east along the path instead of diverting towards the crypt. There are no enemies here, but you will find hostiles in the crypt.
{{NewLeft}}

===Crypt of Heroes===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor 02.jpg|thumb|left|Crypt of Heroes]]
The Crypt of Heroes is an optional section of the dungeon, and houses a mausoleum in the center. Inside, you will find an unlocked treasure chest, but looting it will cause Mannimarco to summon the [[ON:Lord of Iron and Woe|Lord of Iron and Woe]]. There is an unopenable chest behind a sarcophagus on the south side of the river, all the way to the east. Several [[Online:Wraith|wraiths]], [[Online:Skeletal Soldier|Skeletal Soldiers]], and a [[Online:Skeletal Pyromancer|Skeletal Pyromancer]] reside within this courtyard.
{{NewLeft}}

===Sancre Tor Inner Chamber===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor 03.jpg|thumb|left|Sancre Tor Inner Chamber]]
You'll encounter Mannimarco once again in the first room. Mannimarco summons four [[Online:Worm Cult Necromancer|Worm Cult Necromancers]] and a rather large [[Online:Bone Colossus|Bone Colossus]] after his speech is finished. A portal to the Harborage lies in the northeast corner. The Inner Courtyard lies east of here.
{{NewLeft}}

===Inner Courtyard===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor 04.jpg|thumb|left|Inner Courtyard]]
Sai Sahan will need to take a breather here. The Dragonguard Tomb lies to the east, the Reman Vault to the west, and the Vault of Kings to the north.
{{NewLeft}}

===Dragonguard Tomb===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor 05.jpg|thumb|left|Dragonguard Tomb]]
Mannimarco will raise the honored dead in this tomb as you pass through it to reach the shrine. The shrine must be consecrated, and is on the far west side of the room. [[Online:Dragonguard Soldier (Sancre Tor)|Dragonguard soldiers]], [[Online:Dragonguard Archer (Sancre Tor)|archers]], [[Online:Dragonguard Assassin|assassins]] and [[Online:Dragonguard Fire Mage|fire mages]] will oppose you as you cross.
{{NewLeft}}

===Reman Vault===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor 06.jpg|thumb|left|Reman Vault]]
There is a puzzle involving levers in this chamber. Mannimarco summons a large Bone Colossus after you consecrate the shrine, and two wraiths to guard the bridge on the shrine's side of the chasm. Two wraiths will be by the levers, and two more wraiths guard the exit. If you fall into the river, a slope on the west side of the northern hill will lead you back up. A sack and a barrel can be looted by the exit.
{{NewLeft}}

===Vault Antechamber===
[[File:ON-interior-Sancre Tor 07.jpg|thumb|left|Vault Antechamber]]
The Vault of Kings lies to the north. Images of [[Online:Clivia Tharn|Clivia Tharn]] (absent if White-Gold Tower is completed), [[Online:Eyes-of-Steel|Eyes-of-Steel]], [[Online:Jorunn the Skald King|Jorunn the Skald King]], [[Online:General Noramil|General Noramil]], [[Online:Queen Ayrenn|Queen Ayrenn]], [[Online:The Silvenar|The Silvenar]] (Appears as [[ON:Indaenir|Indaenir]] even if [[Online:Edhelorn|Edhelorn]] has not died yet), The Mane ([[ON:Akkhuz-ri|Akkhuz-ri]] by default),{{vn|Is he replaced by Khali or Shazah, and if so after what quest?}} [[Online:King Emeric|King Emeric]], [[Online:King Fahara'jad|King Fahara'jad]], and [[ON:King Kurog|King Kurog]] (replaced by [[ON:King Bazrag|King Bazrag]] after [[ON:Long Live the King|Long Live the King]]) appear while Mannimarco monologues. The specters kneel.

When the vision dissipates, Mannimarco summons a [[Online:Skeletal Archer|Skeletal Archer]] and [[Online:Skeletal Assassin|Skeletal Assassin]], then three Skeletal Soldiers, two Skeletal Pyromancers, and finally two Skeletal Assassins and one Bone Colossus.
{{NewLeft}}

===Vault of Kings===
[[File:ON-place-Vault of Kings.jpg|thumb|left|Vault of Kings]]
The Vault of Kings is an open-air garden with no ceiling. It is walled in. A cemetery surrounds the central courtyard, and the Amulet of Kings lies to the far west. Mannimarco appears in the center of the room; he must be defeated.
{{NewLeft}}

}}The exterior of Sancre Tor can be found in [[Online:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]], west of [[Online:Fort Glademist|Fort Glademist]] in the Amber Woodland. It is overrun with skeletons and wraiths.

The lorebook ''[[ON:With Regards to the Ebony Blade|With Regards to the Ebony Blade]]'' can be found in the west tower, while the lorebook ''[[ON:Triumphs of a Monarch, Ch. 3|Triumphs of a Monarch, Ch. 3]]'' can be found in the east one. A graveyard is also found in the west, while a heavy sack can be found beside a statue at the center of the site.

The tower closest to the center contains ''[[ON:A Death Desired|A Death Desired]]''. Beneath the stairs lies ''[[ON:The Remanada, Chapter One|The Remanada, Chapter One]]'' and a Book Stack containing a random book. On top of the tower is a chair with a goblet and a bottle next to it. The chair is in front of a broken portion of the tower, by the ledge. A heavy sack lies across this broken section, and you must jump over the gap to reach it.

In front of the central tower, a [[ON:Heavy Sack|heavy sack]] can be found beside an intact statue of a hooded warrior holding a sword.
