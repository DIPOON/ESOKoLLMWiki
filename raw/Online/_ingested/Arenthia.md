}} The city has been seized by [[ON:Legion of the West Weald|Colovian invaders]] led by [[Online:General Lavinia|General Lavinia]]. The majority of the city is only accessible during the two major quests here: you cannot enter the Residential District, Temple District, or Tower District before or after these quests.

The city consists of two well differentiated areas: the [[Online:Imperial|Imperial]]-style citadel, to the south, and the [[Online:Khajiit|Khajiit]]-style shantytown, to the north and by the river. At the same time, the citadel has three districts: the Residential District, the Temple District, and the Tower District. Due to the position of Arenthia, the [[Lore:Second Empire|Second Empire]] constructed Arenthia over an older [[Online:Wood Elf|Bosmer]] settlement to serve as a center of trade between [[Lore:Colovia|Colovia]], [[Lore:Anequina|Anequina]] and [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]]. Arenthia docks are also very busy and ships loaded with wood sail down the [[Lore:Strid River|Strid River]]. [[Lore:Skooma|Skooma]] smugglers are [[Online:Cohort Briefing: Arenthia|present]] in the city and they operate out of the city's disused [[Online:Temple to the Divines|Temple to the Divines]]. Arenthia was deemed to be militarily strategic too, for which reason the citadel was heavily fortified with tall walls and defensive towers, and two big stone bridges were constructed across the Strid to ease troop movements between [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]], Valenwood and [[Lore:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]].

Although nominally part of Valenwood, there is a heavy Khajiit presence as it has a Clan Mother, [[ON:Kazirra|Kazirra]], as its leader.  After the city is retaken, the Imperials flee the city and the citadel becomes inaccessible, but life in the rest of the town normalizes and Resistance soldiers patrol the streets.

The [[Online:Dro-m'Athra|dro-m'Athra]] are also conspiring to take the city, but its [[Online:Marasadra|menace]] is contained to one dissident for now.

[[Online:Sakaneth|Sakaneth]] the [[Online:Traveling Merchants|traveling merchant]] walks along the road between Arenthia and the [[Online:Northern Woods Dolmen|Northern Woods Dolmen]].
