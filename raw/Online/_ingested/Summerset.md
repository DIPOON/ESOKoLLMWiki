The zone includes the entirety of [[Lore:Summerset Isle|Summerset Isle]], the largest island of the Summerset archipelago. It is included with the [[Online:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]] Chapter of the game. Summerset also contains an entrance to the [[ON:Coral Aerie|Coral Aerie]] [[ON:Group Dungeons|group dungeon]], but the [[ON:Ascending Tide|Ascending Tide]] DLC is needed in order to access it.

==Notes==
* Summerset's coastline features [[Online:Abyssal Geysers|Abyssal Geysers]], a Summerset counterpart to the mainland's [[Online:Dark Anchors|Dark Anchors]].
* Fishing in this zone has a chance to provide {{Item Link|Pyandonean Bottles|id=140443|quality=3}} containing a motif chapter in the [[ON:Pyandonean Style|Pyandonean style]].
* The island and some locations on it, like [[ON:Shimmerene|Shimmerene]] and the [[ON:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]], can be seen from the western shores of [[Online:Auridon|Auridon]].
* The southern portion of the island was originally planned as the starter zone for the [[ON:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]] players. The [https://www.imperial-library.info/content/eso-alinor-and-ayarene storyline] involved a joint Maormer and Sload invasion, and Queen Ayrenn's name was initially "Ayarynne".
