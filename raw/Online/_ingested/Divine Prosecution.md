The [[Online:Divine Prosecution|Office of Divine Prosecution]] is a section of the [[Online:Thalmor|Thalmor]] whose members serve as lawkeepers that uphold both secular and religious law on [[ON:Summerset|Summerset Isle]], along with enforcing the complex social rules that govern [[Online:High Elf|Altmer]] society. <noinclude>

They have recently taking to deputizing new arrivals to the island so contract work can be carried out on their behalf. On top of their social duties, they are the first sent to investigate crises wherever they occur on the island, such as disasters or murder cases.
