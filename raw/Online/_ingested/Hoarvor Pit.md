An [[ON:Ogrim|ogrim]] named [[Online:Oghezai|Oghezai]] is bound deep within the cave, being fed on by the blood-sucking [[ON:Hoarvor|hoarvors]] that inhabit it. The hoarvors also feed on dead senche, crocodiles, and [[Online:Wood Elf|Bosmer]]. A small valley is found in the northern part, infested with crocodiles. [[ON:Centipede|Centipedes]], [[ON:Lizard|lizards]], [[ON:Spider|spiders]] and [[ON:Torchbug|torchbugs]] are found in numbers within.

A copy of ''[[ON:Horrors of the Strid Basin|Horrors of the Strid Basin]]'' can be found in a tunnel leading to the second floor in the southern portion of the delve. A copy of the [[ON:Lorebook|Lorebook]] ''[[ON:A Nereid Stole My Husband|A Nereid Stole My Husband]]'' can be found on the second floor, in the tunnel west of Oghezai's chamber.

A [[ON:The Hoarvor Pit|note]] can be found in the round chamber at the western end of this tunnel, where it turns north towards the northernmost chamber. A chest can also be found in this portion of the tunnel. The skyshard can be found in the northernmost chamber. A chest can be found east of the skyshard. Another chest can be found further east, near the mouth of the cave leading into the tunnel heading back towards the first floor.
