'''Oblivion''' is a realm within [[Online:The Aurbis|the Aurbis]], which lies away from [[Online:Tamriel|Tamriel]] and beyond the [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]]. Several [[Online:Zones|zones]] are located in Oblivion: [[Online:Apocrypha|Apocrypha]], [[Online:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]], [[Online:The Deadlands|The Deadlands]], and [[Online:Fargrave|Fargrave]]. On the map of the Aurbis, [[Online:Artaeum|Artaeum]] and the [[Online:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]] are represented alongside these Oblivion realms as being places outside the confines of Tamriel.

For historical information, see the [[Lore:Oblivion|lore article]].
