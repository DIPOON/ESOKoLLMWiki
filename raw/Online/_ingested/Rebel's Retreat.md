}}[[ON:Arana|Arana]] and her [[ON:Wayward Guardians|rebels]] are camped out here, the ruins serving as the staging grounds of both their rebellion against [[ON:Ard Caddach|Ard Caddach]], as well as their skirmishes against the [[ON:Gray Host|Gray Host]].

The [[Online:Cinder-Heart Clan Camp|Cinder-Heart Clan Camp]] is due west, and the [[Online:Harrowed Haunt Ritual Site|Harrowed Haunt Ritual Site]] lies to the north. [[Online:Four Skull Lookout|Four Skull Lookout]] is located to the northwest, and [[Online:Red Eagle Redoubt|Red Eagle Redoubt]] lies up the road north. The containers you'll find here are owned. The [[Online:Wayward Guardians|Wayward Guardians]] make their base here.
