Home to [[ON:Ropefish|Ropefish]] leader [[ON:Khartag|Khartag]] and his faction, it has been attacked by the Camonna Tong, a ruthless criminal organization. 

The following letters can be found here: ''[[ON:A One-Time Offer|A One-Time Offer]]'', ''[[ON:Blood-Soaked Letter|Blood-Soaked Letter]]'' and ''[[On:My Dearest Love|My Dearest Love]]''. ''[[ON:Omaren Trial Transcript|Omaren Trial Transcript]]'' can be found in a boat at the entrance to the delve. [[ON:Dragonfly|Dragonflies]] and [[ON:Shroom Beetle|shroom beetles]] are found in numbers within.
