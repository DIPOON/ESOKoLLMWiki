'''Martine Lerineaux''' is a [[ON:Breton|Breton]] acting as the [[ON:Bloodthorn Cult|Bloodthorn]] agent in [[ON:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]. He was [[Online:Lerineaux's Concerns|recruited]] by [[Online:Captain gro-Mak|Captain gro-Mak]] as a covert agent to begin to smuggle goods between Daggerfall, [[Online:Wayrest|Wayrest]] and [[Online:Sentinel|Sentinel]].
