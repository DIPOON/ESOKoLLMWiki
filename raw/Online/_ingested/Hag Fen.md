}} The [[Online:Bloodthorn Cult|Bloodthorn Cult]] have set up operations in the south of the marsh around [[Online:Tangle Rock|Tangle Rock]]. They have struck up a deal with the hag coven in the northern fens to launch joint assaults on the [[Online:Beldama Wyrd|Beldama Wyrd]].

The [[Online:Exorcised Coven Cottage|Exorcised Coven Cottage]] player house is located in central-eastern Hag Fen. A tower located south of the cottage is inhabited by Bloodthorn cultists. A backpack and a water skin are located in the ruined tower, and a chest can spawn west of the tower. [[Online:Crocodile|Crocodiles]], as well as smaller passive [[ON:Snake|snakes]], lurk near the water. 

[[Online:Mother Murk|Mother Murk]]'s hut is located atop a hill east of [[Online:Spindleclutch|Spindleclutch]]. There are two cooking fires here: one is in the hearth inside the hut, and another is a pot suspended over a campfire in front of the building. A copy of ''[[Online:The Witches of Hag Fen|The Witches of Hag Fen]]'' lies on the floor inside the hut.

==[[File:ON-mapicon-Wayshrine.png|text-bottom|32px]]Wayshrine==
[[File:ON-place-Hag Fen Wayshrine.jpg|thumb|left|Hag Fen Wayshrine]] [[File:ON-place-North Hag Fen Wayshrine.jpg|thumb|right|North Hag Fen Wayshrine]] Due to the large area covered by Hag Fen, the area includes two wayshrines. '''Hag Fen Wayshrine''' is located on the eastern coast of the fens, just west of [[Online:Mesanthano's Tower (place)|Mesanthano's Tower]]. The Beldama Wyrd have a camp east of the Hag Fen Wayshrine. '''North Hag Fen Wayshrine''' is located at the northern end of the marshes, west of [[Online:Spindleclutch|Spindleclutch]] and below [[Online:Dwynnarth Ruins|Dwynnarth Ruins]]. A copy of the lore book, ''[[Online:History of the Fighters Guild Pt. 1|History of the Fighters Guild Pt. 1]]'', is located beside a wheelbarrow southwest of North Hag Fen Wayshrine.
