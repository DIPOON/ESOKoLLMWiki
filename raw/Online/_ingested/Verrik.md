'''Verrik''' is a [[ON:Reachman|Reachman]] assassin with the [[ON:Bloodthorn Cult|Bloodthorn cult]] who is part of the plot to assassinate [[ON:King Casimir|King Casimir]] of [[ON:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]. 

He was [[Online:Crumpled Note in the Desk|approached]] by [[Online:Captain gro-Mak|Captain gro-Mak]], a [[Online:Daggerfall Covenant|Covenant]] spy secretly working for a cabal of war profiteers, to recruit his men; but his offer did not work out quite as planned.

==Skills and Abilities==
;Quick Strike: A basic melee attack dealing low [[ON:Physical Damage|physical damage]].
;Whirlwind: Verrik spins around, dealing moderate physical damage to all targets as indicated by a red circle.
;Assassinate: Verrik strikes at the target's back for moderate physical damage. This attack can only be performed if Verrik is behind his target, and he will often use "Passing Through" to get there. Face toward Verrik to prevent him from using this ability.
;Passing Through:Verrik jumps above the target to get behind them.
;Roll Dodge: Verrik can perform a [[Online:Combat#Roll_Dodging|roll dodge]] to avoid incoming damage.
;Backstabber:Verrik has a passive ability that causes him to deal 20% more damage when attacking the target's back.

==Quest-Related Events==
When you follow King Casimir upstairs, Verrik will appear.

You must kill Verrik and save the king.
