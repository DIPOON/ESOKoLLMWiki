==Notes==
* The large southernmost room of the dungeon was added in [[Online:Patch/3.2.5|Update 16]] to be used in the quest [[Online:Through a Veil Darkly|Through a Veil Darkly]]. In Update 17, a few Maormer corpses and passive spiders were added.
* Despite the number of quests requiring this dungeon, it is possible to actually do them all in one visit, even for Aldmeri Dominion characters, if one juggles one's prologue quests carefully.
** This includes finding the Dusk Mushrooms for [[ON:The Demon Weapon|The Demon Weapon]] even as a Covenant or Pact character, since they are available in all three delves, so even though the quest has pointed you to a faction-appropriate one, they can be collected here instead if you're at the right stage of the quest.
