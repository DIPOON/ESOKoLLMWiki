[[ON:Gorlar the Dark|Gorlar the Dark]], a powerful [[ON:Orc|Orc]] necromancer, resides deep within the ruins. 

The notes ''[[Online:Letter from Thukhozod|Letter from Thukhozod]]'' and ''[[Online:Gorlar's Journal, Part One|Gorlar's Journal, Part One]]'' and ''[[Online:Gorlar's Journal, Part Two|Part Two]]'' can be found here, and the book, ''[[Online:The Legend of Thukhozod|The Legend of Thukhozod]]'' can be found here too. 

The [[Online:Wrothgar Skyshard Hunter|skyshard]] is located at the west end of the long hallway across the gap from the entrance. The fastest way is around the right corner as you enter, but beware the Bone Colossus standing between you and the hallway.
