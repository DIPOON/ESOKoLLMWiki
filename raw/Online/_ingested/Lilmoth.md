==Notes==
* Lilmoth previously [[Arena:Lilmoth|appeared]] in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''.
* When Murkmire was originally planned to be a zone bordering [[Online:Blackwood|Blackwood]] and [[ON:Shadowfen|Shadowfen]], the main city would have been [[Online:Gideon|Gideon]], but it was replaced in favor of the coastal southern city of Lilmoth after the plans for Murkmire being an [[ON:Adventure Zones|Adventure Zone]] were scrapped.
