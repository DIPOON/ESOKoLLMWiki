==Notes==
*The quests [[Online:Breaking Through the Fog|Breaking Through the Fog]] and [[Online:Broken Bonds|Broken Bonds]] are mutually exclusive. Which one you receive depends on your character: new characters created after the release of ''[[Online:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'' will start off with Broken Bonds as their very first quest if they choose to play the tutorial, while existing characters who travel to [[Online:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]], and new characters who skip the tutorial, can accept Breaking Through the Fog to complete the town's objective.
*Seyda Neen also [[MW:Seyda Neen|appears]] in ''[[MW:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''.
