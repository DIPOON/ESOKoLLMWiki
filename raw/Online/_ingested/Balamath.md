==Notes==
* Prior to Update 12, this location was an instanced 4-player [[File:ON-mapicon-GroupDelve.png|20px]][[ON:Group Delves|Group Delve]].
* There is an optional boss, the [[ON:Corrupted Scion of Balamath|Corrupted Scion of Balamath]], that can be summoned by activating three switches on a pillar inside the ruins, north from the entrance.
* Balamath Hall, located in the western section of the delve, can only be accessed during [[Online:Elemental Army|Elemental Army]]. The Air Monarch within Balamath Hall, [[Online:Parel Nirus|Parel Nirus]], is not required for delve completion.
