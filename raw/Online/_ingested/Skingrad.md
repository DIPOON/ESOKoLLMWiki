==Notes==
*The city was first named in ''[[Redguard:Maps|Redguard]]'' and made its first full appearance in ''[[Oblivion:Skingrad|Oblivion]]''.
*Skingrad has been a planned city since the beta, originally meant to be the hub for the [[:File:ON-map-alpha map.jpg|Colovian Highlands]] zone, and a [[Online:Out-of-Bounds Locations in the Aldmeri Dominion#Skingrad|rough placeholder version]] was located outside the map in [[ON:Malabal Tor|Malabal Tor]] before being removed in 2020.
