|loadtext=Ancestral home of the Direfrost clan, this frozen castle has long been sealed to the outside world, its owners neither seen nor heard from in generations. Mountain storms rage above the castle walls, conjured up by some unknown malignant power.
}}{{TOCleft}} Once the ancestral home of the witch hunting Direfrost clan, the castle has been abandoned following an attack by the ice witch [[ON:Drodda of Icereach|Drodda]]. A surviving member of the clan, [[ON:Eboric Direfrost|Eboric Direfrost]], needs your help to reclaim his home.  You can learn about its location from [[ON:Untouchable Naim|Untouchable Naim]] outside [[ON:Velyn Harbor|Velyn Harbor]], [[ON:Twice-Lucky|Twice-Lucky]] in [[ON:Sentinel|Sentinel]], or [[ON:Felvyn the Swordarm|Felvyn the Swordarm]] in [[ON:Windhelm|Windhelm]].

Its [[ON:Dungeon Sets|dungeon sets]] are [[ON:Draugr Hulk|Draugr Hulk]], [[ON:Magicka Furnace|Magicka Furnace]], and [[ON:The Ice Furnace|The Ice Furnace]].

A chest can spawn under the bridge leading to the keep's main gate, and on top of the sewer grate that leads into the dungeon.

Its final boss, [[ON:Drodda of Icereach|Drodda of Icereach]], has a chance to drop the {{Item Link|antiquityid=116|quality=5|Snowy Sabre Cat Fur Strip}} [[ON:Antiquities|antiquity lead]] for the [[Online:Snow Treaders|Snow Treaders]].
