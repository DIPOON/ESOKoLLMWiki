[[Lore:Caldera|Caldera]] was an [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial]] charter town just north of [[Lore:Balmora|Balmora]]. It was built to service the nearby [[Lore:Ebony|Ebony]] mine, providing the miners with homes, entertainment, and other services. The town was ruled largely from the Caldera Mining Company's fortress to the northeast. 

The buildings, which had [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] and [[Lore:Nord|Nordic]] architectural influences, formed an inverse "L" shape between two large guard towers. While [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]] presence in Caldera was almost nonexistent, a large contingent of [[Lore:Orc|Orcs]] lived in the dilapidated Ghorak Manor (along with an unusual [[Lore:Creeper|Scamp]] who was a merchant and claimed the house was his). Notable institutions in Caldera included an [[Lore:Imperial Cult|Imperial Cult]] altar, a [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild branch]], two general merchants, a blacksmith, a tavern, and one of the few Imperial-style clothiers in [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]].<noinclude>

A '''Redoran Garrison''' was present at the town's future location in {{Year|2E 582}}, where many meetings between house councilors were held. The ebony mine, during the late Third Era, was operated on land claimed by [[Lore:House Redoran|House Redoran]], while the mine was administered by [[Lore:House Hlaalu|House Hlaalu]], who had a stranglehold on all of the [[Lore:East Empire Company|East Empire Company]]'s business interests on Vvardenfell. This was the cause of a great deal of friction between these two great houses.
