# Lore:2E 582

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

{{Trail|History|Second Era}}{{Lore History}}[[File:ON-concept-Dark Anchor.jpg|thumb|right|The [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]], one of the worst catacylsms of this year]]
[[Lore:2E 582|2E 582]] was a year of the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]]. The year was characterized by extraordinary strife, one of the most destructive known years of an era remembered for its "days and nights of blood and venom."{{ref|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 1}}}}

==Events==
====The Five Companions are reformed====
The Five Companions are reformed to stop Molag Bal's Planemeld. Their members include [[Lore:Varen Aquilarios|Varen Aquilarios]], [[Lore:Sai Sahan|Sai Sahan]], [[Lore:Abnur Tharn|Abnur Tharn]], [[Lore:Lyris Titanborn|Lyris Titanborn]], and the prophesied Vestige.{{ref|name=ESO}}
====The [[Lore:Mortuum Vivicus|Mortuum Vivicus]] is destroyed====
The [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]] successfully destroys the Mortuum Vivicus with the help of their newest Champion.{{ref|name=ESO}}
====The Mages Guild sanctuary of [[Lore:Eyevea|Eyevea]] returns to Tamriel====
After nearly a thousand years of being in the [[Lore:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]], the sanctuary of Eyevea is returned to [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] by a Mages Guild Adept with the help of [[Lore:Shalidor|Arch-Mage Shalidor]].{{ref|name=ESO}}
====The [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]] ends====
The three alliance leaders meet on the island of [[Lore:Stirk|Stirk]] in neutral territory to discuss an invasion of Coldharbour. After they fail to come to an agreement, they are attacked by the forces of Molag Bal, after which they give their blessings to the Mages and Fighters Guilds spearheading an assault into the Daedric realm. The combined forces of the Guilds charge into Coldharbour led by the Vestige. There, they destroy the Great Shackle, and seal the Planar Vortex, thus ending the Planemeld.{{ref|name=ESO|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
====Daedric plots in the Imperial City foiled====
In a final attempt to restart the Planemeld,{{ref|group=UOL|[[Online:Temple District|Temple District]] background in the [[General:Future of The Elder Scrolls Online Panel from QuakeCon 2014#Temple District|Future of The Elder Scrolls Online Panel from QuakeCon 2014]]}} Molag Bal focused on the occupation of the Imperial City. A Dragonguard agent called the [[Lore:Drake of Blades|Drake of Blades]], with the help of the Vestige, stopped Molag Bal's plot to destroy the [[Lore:Sublime Brazier|Sublime Brazier]], the key to rekindling the Dragonfires.{{ref|name=IC|Events of [[ON:Imperial City (DLC)|Imperial City]]}} The attempt at a second Planemeld, the "Planemeld Obverse", is also stopped with the slaying of [[Lore:Molag Kena|Molag Kena]].
====Molag Bal is banished====
[[Lore:The Vestige|The Vestige]] banishes Molag Bal within his own realm, thus finally stopping his bid for merging [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]] and [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] into one nightmarish hellscape. With it, the souls stolen by him are released from his clutches.{{ref|name=ESO}}
====[[Lore:Orsinium|Orsinium]] considers withdrawing from the Daggerfall Covenant====
King [[Lore:Kurog|Kurog]] of Orsinium is slain in late autumn after a failed attempt to unite the Orc clans of [[Lore:Wrothgar|Wrothgar]] under his rule by assassinating clan chiefs who refused to follow [[Lore:Trinimac|Trinimac]]. Chief [[Lore:Bazrag gro-Fharun|Bazrag]] of [[Lore:Fharun Clan|Clan Fharun]] is crowned the new king, restoring worship of [[Lore:Malacath|Malacath]]. Since Orsinium's membership of the Daggerfall Covenant was based on an agreement between Kurog and High King Emeric, the chiefs of Orsinium consider leaving the Covenant.<!--{{ref|group=nb|name=nb5|{{Lore Link|Birds of Wrothgar}} previously suggested that these events take place in 2E 583.}}-->{{ref|name=Orsinium|Events of [[ON:Orsinium (DLC)|Orsinium]]}}
====The [[Lore:Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]] of [[Lore:Abah's Landing|Abah's Landing]] is restored to its former glory====
After a failed heist and the resulting persecution from [[Lore:Taneth|Taneth]], the Thieves Guild of Abah's Landing is restored to its prime and begins to spread its influence throughout Tamriel.{{ref|name=TG|Events of [[ON:Thieves Guild (DLC)|Thieves Guild]]}}
====Count [[Lore:Carolus Aquilarios|Carolus Aquilarios]] of [[Lore:Kvatch|Kvatch]] emerges as the dominant power on the [[Lore:Gold Coast|Gold Coast]]====
Shielded from the ongoing Alliance War by [[Lore:Varen's Wall|Varen's Wall]], a ''de facto'' independent Gold Coast sees the end of a power struggle between [[Lore:Anvil|Anvil]], Kvatch, and the [[Lore:Order of the Hour|Order of the Hour]]. Aquilarios has Governor [[Lore:Fortunata ap Dugal|Fortunata ap Dugal]] of Anvil assassinated by the [[Lore:Dark Brotherhood|Dark Brotherhood]]; facing persecution from the Order of the Hour, the Brotherhood subsequently kills Primate [[Lore:Artorius Ponticus|Artorius Ponticus]], stopping him from raising an army against Aquilarios.{{ref|name=DB|Events of [[ON:Dark Brotherhood (DLC)|Dark Brotherhood]]}}
====[[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]] is saved from the machinations of [[Lore:Clavicus Vile|Clavicus Vile]] and [[Lore:Barbas|Barbas]]====
Seeking to gain entry to [[Lore:Sotha Sil|Sotha Sil]]'s [[Lore:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]], the Daedric Prince and his minion steal the staff Sunna'rah, a tool Sotha Sil had created for experimenting on his power. The staff ends up in the hands of the [[Lore:Ashlander|Ashlander]] [[Lore:Conoon Chodala|Conoon Chodala]], who aspired to the title of [[Lore:Nerevarine|Nerevarine]]. Chodala's use of the staff drains away the power of [[Lore:Vivec (god)|Vivec]], which weakens the Warrior-Poet's hold over the moonlet [[Lore:Baar Dau|Baar Dau]] and endangers all of Vvardenfell. Fortunately, the [[Lore:Seryn|Champion of Azura]], a [[Lore:Barilzar|clockwork mage]], and an [[Lore:The Vestige|Outlander]] come to Vivec's aid. Chodala is discredited and defeated, Clavicus Vile's plot is thwarted, and Vivec's power is restored.{{ref|name=ESOMW|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}
====A plot by [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]] to take over the [[Lore:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]] is foiled====
[[Lore:Sotha Sil|Sotha Sil]]'s Shadow, serving Nocturnal, is summoned and replaces him as ruler of the Clockwork City. At the same time, the [[Lore:Blackfeather Court|Blackfeather Court]] enters the city and steals the [[Lore:Skeleton Key|Skeleton Key]], which has been in the Clockwork God's possession. [[Lore:The Vestige|The Vestige]], assisted by [[Lore:Divayth Fyr|Divayth Fyr]] and the Clockwork Apostles, unveils the plot; but is unable to retake the Skeleton Key before it is taken by Sotha Sil's Shadow. Using the key, Nocturnal and the Shadow attempt to take over the Clockwork City; but are stopped by the Vestige, Divayth Fyr and a Clockwork Apostle, who save Sotha Sil by defeating his Shadow. Sotha Sil gives the Skeleton Key to Divayth Fyr for safekeeping.{{ref|name=ESOCWC|Events of [[Online:DLC#Clockwork City|ESO: Clockwork City]]}}
====The [[Lore:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]] is saved from a triad of [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Princes]]====
The three Daedric Princes [[Lore:Clavicus Vile|Clavicus Vile]], [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]] and [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]] attempt to execute their plan to take control of the [[Lore:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]] on [[Lore:Summerset Isle|Summerset Isle]] using the Heart of Transparent Law, which they had made [[Lore:Iachesis|Ritemaster Iachesis]] of the [[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic Order]] steal around {{Year|2E 230}}—who had also hidden it from them. After they trick Iachesis to reveal the Heart's location, that being within himself, they kill him; but Nocturnal betrays Clavicus Vile and Mephala by taking the Heart for herself. With it she takes control of the Crystal Tower, hoping to use its properties to achieve omnipresence in the entire [[Lore:Aurbis|Aurbis]]. However, her plans are foiled by [[Lore:The Vestige|the Vestige]] and the Psijic Order, assisted by the two betrayed Princes and a champion of [[Lore:Meridia|Meridia]], and the Heart is returned to the Crystal Tower.{{ref|name=ESOSummerset|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}
====The [[Lore:Remnant of Argon|Remnant of Argon]] is recovered and the Root-Whisper tribe is restored====
[[Lore:The Vestige|The Vestige]] aids Cyrodilic Collections in recovering a powerful artifact that contains the spirits of a lost Argonian tribe in [[Lore:Murkmire|Murkmire]].{{ref|name=ESOMurkmire|Events of [[Online:DLC#Murkmire|ESO: Murkmire]]}}
====[[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]] unleashes various Daedric artifacts onto Cyrodiil====
In order to relieve his own boredom at the Three Banners War, the Daedric Prince Sheogorath temporarily claims several artifacts belonging to the other Daedric Princes. He then offers them to the combatants in Cyrodiil to bring upheaval to the ongoing conflict.{{ref|name=ESOChaosMagic|[[Online:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]]'s dialogue in the quest [[Online:Chaos Magic|Chaos Magic]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
====A [[Lore:Dragon|Dragon]] threat is averted in [[Lore:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]]====
The Imperial battlemage [[Lore:Abnur Tharn|Abnur Tharn]] accidentally unseals the legendary [[Lore:Halls of Colossus|Halls of Colossus]] using the [[Lore:Wrathstone|Wrathstone]], believing it to contain a powerful weapon. This results in a rage of Dragons—sealed away by the Khajiiti hero [[Lore:Khunzar-ri|Khunzar-ri]] in the Merethic Era—being unleashed upon Elsweyr. 
*The Dragons form an alliance with [[Lore:Euraxia Tharn|Euraxia Tharn]], the ruler of [[Lore:Rimmen|Rimmen]]. The alliance is short-lived, as [[Lore:The Vestige|the Vestige]] slays Euraxia, bringing Rimmen back under the control of the Khajiit.
*The Dragons plot to obtain lunar power of [[Lore:Jone|Jone]] and [[Lore:Jode|Jode]] using the {{Lore Link|Moon Gate}} of [[Lore:Anequina|Anequina]]. They are stopped after the Vestige steps into the Plane of Jode, banishing the Dragon leader, [[Lore:Kaalgrontiid|Kaalgrontiid]].
*[[Lore:Khamira|Khamira]], the daughter of King Hemakar, is crowned queen of Rimmen.{{ref|name=ESOEW|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]]''}}
====The [[Lore:Dragonguard|Dragonguard]] is reformed in [[Lore:Pelletine|Southern Elsweyr]]====
The ex-members of the [[Lore:Five Companions|Five Companions]], [[Lore:Sai Sahan|Sai Sahan]] and [[Lore:The Vestige|the Vestige]], assemble a new Dragonguard—largely consisting of ex-pirates and [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] soldiers—to defend [[Lore:Pelletine|Pelletine]] from the Dragon threat. With the help of the Dragon [[Lore:Nahfahlaar|Nahfahlaar]], they manage to acquire the powerful artifact [[Lore:Mask of Alkosh|Mask of Alkosh]] and defeat the Dragon [[Lore:Laatvulon|Laatvulon]].
*[[Lore:Kaalgrontiid|Kaalgrontiid]] returns to Elsweyr after having been banished, seeking to use the [[Lore:Aeonstone|Aeonstone]] found on the island [[Lore:Dragonhold|Dragonhold]] to gain the power of a god, becoming the prophesized [[Lore:Dark Aeon|Dark Aeon]]. The new-founded Dragonguard are aided by Nahfahlaar, [[Lore:Abnur Tharn|Abnur Tharn]] and Queen [[Lore:Khamira|Khamira]] in destroying the Aeonstone and defeating Kaalgrontiid. However, Kaalgrontiid is able to absorb a great amount of power before being killed, which risks destroying all of Elsweyr if not contained. Nahfahlaar and Abnur Tharn use their combined power to contain it, resulting in the disappearance and possible death of the latter.{{ref|name=ESODH|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Dragonhold|Dragonhold]]''}}
====Death of High King Svargrim====
[[Lore:House Ravenwatch|House Ravenwatch]] agent Fennorian, representative of King Jorunn Lyris Titanborn, Princess of Western Skyrim Svana and the Vestige join forces to unveil the vampiric threat looming over Skyrim.
*[[Lore:Rada al-Saran|Rada al-Saran]] personally approaches High King [[Lore:Svargrim|Svargrim]] of [[Lore:Solitude|Solitude]], offering him incredible power in exchange for his servitude. The High King agrees and becomes a Vampire Lord. The [[Lore:Gray Host|Gray Host]], together with the [[Lore:Icereach Coven|Icereach Coven]], create [[Lore:Harrowstorm|harrowstorms]], magical weather anomalies to pull their comrades out of Coldharbour, and then using the Stone Husks, to fully restore their corporeal forms allowing them to live once more, killing hundreds of inhabitants of Skyrim in the process.
* The Gray Host takes control over Castle Dour and unleashes a powerful harrowstorm inside the city. After a great battle, High King Svargrim is slain and the Gray Host defeated and retreating from Western Skyrim.
*Relations between Western and Eastern Skyrim improve as King Jorunn offers aid to the victims of the vampire attack. Princess Svana becomes Jarl of Solitude.{{ref|name=ESOGM|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Greymoor|Greymoor]]''}}
====The Gray Host is destroyed in The Reach====
[[Lore:Reachfolk|Reachfolk]] Ard [[Lore:Caddach|Caddach]] consolidates more power over the Reachfolk clans. A faction of [[Lore:Wayward Guardians|rebels]] led by [[Lore:Arana|Arana]] is formed in opposition to the power of the Ard.
*The Gray Host unleashes an attack on the citizens of [[Lore:Markarth|Markarth]] as a sacrifice for the [[Lore:Dark Heart|Dark Heart]].
*[[Lore:Verandis Ravenwatch|Verandis Ravenwatch]] assisted by the Vestige, defeats Rada al-Saran, releases the souls harnessed by the Gray Host and resurrects the Reachfolk who died during the Gray Host assault on Markarth. The rebels and loyalists of the Ard join forces.
*Peace treaty between the Reach, Western Skyrim and Eastern Skyrim is signed.{{ref|name=ESOMAR|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Markarth (DLC)|Markarth]]''}}
====Search for the Four Ambitions====
Sombren, Calia, and Destron, three out of the [[Lore:Four Ambitions|Four Ambitions]], have been released.
*Assassins have been targeting members of the Elder Council in [[Lore:Blackwood|Blackwood]]. Councilors Velan Sophus, Jirich, and Abor have been successfully assassinated.
*It is revealed that Councilor [[Lore:Ertus Vandacia|Ertus Vandacia]] was the High Priest of Mehrunes Dagon. His forces surround Gideon, and even Dagon himself makes an appearance, but they are ultimately defeated at Fort Redmane.{{ref|name=ESOBW|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Blackwood (Chapter)|Blackwood]]''}}
====Mehrunes Dagon banished to Oblivion====
The Fourth Ambition, who is actually [[Lore:Mairead|the Anchorite]] of [[Lore:Fargrave|Fargrave]], is revealed as the illegitimate daughter of Emperor Moricar and Sister [[Lore:Celdina|Celdina]].
*Mehrunes Dagon materializes within [[Lore:Sundercliff|Fort Sundercliff]], but his egonymic is employed to banish him back.
*[[Lore:Lyranth|Lyranth]] absconds with the powers of the Four Ambitions, vanishing to employ them for undisclosed purposes.{{ref|name=ESODeadlands|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:The Deadlands (DLC)|The Deadlands]]''}}
====Machinations of the Ascendant Order unveils on High Isle====
Lord [[Lore:Bacaro Volorus|Bacaro Volorus]], heading the [[Lore:Society of the Steadfast|Society of the Steadfast]], takes charge of organizing peace negotiations on the High Isle, aiming to halt the ongoing Three Banners War.
*The Ascendant Order, a clandestine pan-Tamrielic society opposed to monarchies, grows in power and gains prominence.
*Prince Irnskar, Queen Ayrenn, and High King Emeric face assassination attempts.
*A fierce battle unfolds on All-Flags islet between the forces of the Ascendant Order and opposing factions, resulting in the demise of the Ascendant Magus.{{ref|name=ESOHI|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:High Isle (Chapter)|High Isle]]''}}
====Prophecy of [[Lore:Kasorayn|Kasorayn]] comes to fruition====
*[[Lore:Frii|Frii]] is awakened
*Archdruid [[Lore:Orlaith|Orlaith]] of the [[Lore:Firesong Circle|Firesong Circle]] collaborates with the [[Lore:Dreadsails|Dreadsails]] and lays siege to [[Lore:Vastyr|Vastyr]], aiming to proclaim herself as the new Druid King. However, her attempt proves unsuccessful. Count Leonard Mornard tragically passes away during the siege, and his son Stefan Mornard assumes leadership over Galen.
*Lord Bacarro Volorus, is unveiled as the leader of the Ascendant Order and the descendant of [[Lore:House Merchad|House Merchad]], the lineage of the Druid King Kasorayn. He steals the regalia of the Druid King and endeavors to declare himself as the new Druid King. This reckless act triggers the eruption of the [[Lore:Mount Firesong|Mount Firesong]] volcano. In a heroic act, Frii sacrifices themselves to protect others.
*Peace talks resume following these tumultuous events, but the ultimate outcome remains uncertain and unknown.{{ref|name=ESOFS|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Firesong|Firesong]]''}}
