# Lore:Septim Dynasty

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

{{Featured Article}}{{Trail|Appendices/History}}
The [[Lore:Septim Dynasty|Septim Dynasty]] was the founding dynasty and the first ruling dynasty of the [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]] of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] from {{Year|2E 896}} until the dynasty's extinction with the death of [[Lore:Martin Septim|Martin Septim]] in {{Year|3E 433}}. The dynasty and the Empire were founded by the legendary [[Lore:Tiber Septim|Tiber Septim]] in 2E 896 after [[Lore:Tiber War|decades of conquest]], thus marking the end of the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]] and the beginning of the [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]].

Under the Septims, Tamriel prospered and its provinces enjoyed peace and stability that had not been seen since before the chaotic centuries of the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]]. New conflicts still arose, however, which repeatedly stretched the Empire to its breaking point throughout the Third Era: a battle over the imperial succession ensued during the [[Lore:War of the Red Diamond|War of the Red Diamond]] between {{Year|3E 120}} and {{Year|3E 127}}; the [[Lore:Camoran Usurper|Camoran Usurper]] seized control of [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]] and [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]] from {{Year|3E 249}} to {{Year|3E 267}}; Emperor [[Lore:Uriel Septim V|Uriel Septim V]] spearheaded an ill-fated invasion of [[Lore:Akavir|Akavir]] in {{Year|3E 288}}, resulting in the emperor's death and the loss of a considerable portion of the Empire's military strength; and Imperial Battlemage [[Lore:Jagar Tharn|Jagar Tharn]] overthrew Emperor [[Lore:Uriel Septim VII|Uriel Septim VII]] and ruled for a decade beginning in {{Year|3E 389}}, a period that would become known as the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]].

Through all of these challenges, the Empire and the Septim line survived. Their end came in 3E 433 with the onset of the [[Lore:Oblivion Crisis|Oblivion Crisis]]. Emperor [[Lore:Uriel VII|Uriel Septim VII]] and his heirs were assassinated by the [[Lore:Mythic Dawn|Mythic Dawn]] in a single night, and the [[Lore:Dragonfires|Dragonfires]] ceased to burn. The [[Lore:Daedric Prince|Daedric Prince]] [[Lore:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]] launched a massive invasion of Tamriel. Although one last Septim heir, Martin Septim, was located, he sacrificed himself to repel the Daedra and seal [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]] off from [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]] forever. With the Septims gone and the Empire's fate uncertain, the Third Era concluded and the [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]] began.
