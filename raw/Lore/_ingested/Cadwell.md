</noinclude>[[Lore:Cadwell|Sir Cadwell]], also known as '''Cadwell the Betrayer''', '''Hero of Cyrod''', '''The Villain of Elsweyr''', and '''Champion of the Third Nedic Massacre''', is an ancient [[Lore:Nede|Nedic]] knight turned [[Lore:Soul Shriven|Soul Shriven]]. In life, he was a cruel, power-hungry man who claimed the horrors of [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] pale in comparison to his heinous deeds.{{Ref|name=ONCadwellB|[[ON:Cadwell the Betrayer (person)|Cadwell the Betrayer]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} He helped  [[Lore:Khunzar-ri|Khunzar-ri]] and his kra'jun seal the dragons away in the [[Lore:Halls of Colossus|Halls of Colossus]], only to betray him in an attempt to take the power sealed away in [[Lore:Jode's Core|Jode's Core]]. He died at a last stand in the Shadow Dance Temple during the Merethic Era. According to '''Cadwell the Soul Shriven''' his head was "quite unceremoniously" separated from his body via decapitation.{{Ref|name=ONCadwell|Dialogue with [[ON:Cadwell|Cadwell]]}} His remains were scattered across the land and his name wiped from all records.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Khunzar-ri and the Demons|ns_base=Lore}}}}{{ref|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]]''}} His soul was thereafter sent to [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] and he became a [[Lore:Soul Shriven|Soul Shriven]], now being the oldest among the Soul Shriven to have not gone feral.<noinclude>
==Cadwell the Soul Shriven==
[[File:ON-npc-Cadwell.jpg|thumb|left|Cadwell the Soul Shriven]]
{{Quote Box|style=float:none; max-width:none; margin-left:0|''Wherever the forces of evil prey upon the innocent, wherever souls are stolen and bodies shriven, wherever a people cry out for justice, there goest I! I mean, this whole eternal servitude business is rather nasty, don't you agree?''|''Cadwell the Soul Shriven''}}
Upon death, his soul was sent to Coldharbour, where he ended up as a [[Lore:Soul Shriven|soul shriven]]. Being an insane man in life before arriving in Coldharbour is credited as being the reason for him not ending up a feral soul shriven.{{Ref|name=ONLyris|Dialogue with [[ON:Lyris Titanborn|Lyris Titanborn]]}} Cadwell the Soul Shriven's personality is the opposite of the one he had in life and even Cadwell was unsure of why that is. He theorized it had to do with either the transition to life to death, becoming a soul shriven, or his many years spent in Coldharbour.{{Ref|name=ONCadwell}}

In his many years living in Coldharbour, Cadwell has since discovered all the nooks, crannies, secret paths, and hidden locations the realm has to offer. His extensive knowledge of Coldharbour, along with his unique ability with portals, makes him nigh impossible to hold captive, and the [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]] gave up on trying to keep him in chains long ago. During the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]], he assisted the [[Lore:The Vestige|Vestige]] and [[Lore:Lyris Titanborn|Lyris Titanborn]] in escaping from the {{Lore Link|Wailing Prison}} and once again helping them rescue [[Lore:Abnur Tharn|Abnur Tharn]] from the [[Lore:Castle of the Worm|Castle of the Worm]], perhaps even coming back with them to the Harborage in Tamriel. Throughout the joint [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] and [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]] coalition's invasion of Coldharbour, Cadwell assisted in guiding rescued persons back to the [[Lore:Hollow City|Hollow City]]. He also helped [[Lore:Vanus Galerion|Vanus Galerion]] destroy the Great Shackle and joined in on [[Lore:Meridia|Meridia]]'s confrontation against Molag Bal in the {{Lore Link|Planar Vortex}}. Following Molag Bal's defeat, Cadwell became a servant of Meridia and granted the Vestige the opportunity to visit the territories of the [[Lore:Alliance War|Alliances]] they weren't a part of.{{Ref|name=ESO|Events of [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

During the rage of Dragons in [[Lore:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]], [[Lore:Euraxia Tharn|Euraxia Tharn]]'s chief necromancer Zumog Phoom resurrected the original body of Cadwell to serve as his familiar and confidant, although initially only as a floating head. This caused the identity of Cadwell to be temporarily split between his two incarnations, Cadwell the Soul Shriven and Cadwell the Betrayer. Once his mortal body was fully restored and the energy from the Vestige of Cadwell was siphoned into it, Cadwell the Betrayer manifested fully and remained as the only Cadwell. Only after Cadwell the Betrayer was slain, Cadwell the Soul Shriven managed to reform from [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]].{{ref|name=CtB|Events of [[ON:Cadwell the Betrayer (quest)|Cadwell the Betrayer]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

Cadwell is able open up portals to anywhere he was needed or he wanted to go.{{Ref|name=ONCadwell}}{{Ref|name=ESO}} When Cadwell the Betrayer realigned the [[Lore:Moons|moons]] he had to bind himself to the Lunar Lattice. {{ref|name=CtB}} It was theorized by Khamira that Cadwell got this unique ability from this [[Lore:Shadow Dance|connection]] to the Lunar Lattice.{{Ref|name=ONKhamira|[[ON:Khamira|Khamira]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}  This theory was later proven to be correct, as Khamira being bound to the Lattice like Cadwell was able to do it herself.{{ref|name=DE|Events of [[ON:The Dark Aeon|The Dark Aeon]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Cadwell was able to master the ability of portal creation after thousands of years of existence within Coldharbour. Something Khamira herself said she might need in order to master it herself. {{Ref|name=ONKhamira}}

Many prominent figures active in the decade following the onset of the Planemeld were immortalized in a painting commemorating their adventures. Among them was Cadwell, where he could be seen playing a lute.{{ref|[[Online:Painting: 10-Year Anniversary|Painting: 10-Year Anniversary]] furnishing depiction in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
