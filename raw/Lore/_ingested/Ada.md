The [[Lore:Ada|Ada]] (meaning '''spirit'''){{Ref|name=TM|{{Cite Book|The Monomyth}}}}{{ref|group=UOL|[[General:Kurt Kuhlmann's Posts|Kurt Kuhlmann's Posts]]}} are a type of [[Lore:Gods|deity]]. Most known Ada were demi-deities, derived from pairings of mortals and the [[Lore:Et'Ada|Et'Ada]], the original spirits. It is said that the Ada can change things through love,{{ref|name=TSOP5|{{Cite Book|The Song of Pelinal, v 5}}}} with an example of this ability coming in the form of the Ada [[Lore:Morihaus|Morihaus]] creating a new race, the [[Lore:Minotaur|Minotaur]], from his union with [[Lore:Alessia|Alessia]].{{ref|name=OM|{{Cite Book|On Minotaurs}}}} Ada are said to be ambitious beings that can never know death.{{Ref|name=LAFNJTR2|{{Cite Book|Lord Fa-Nuit-Hen and Tutor Riparius Answer Your Questions 2}}}}{{ref|name=TSOP3|}}

==Known Ada==
*[[Lore:Pelinal Whitestrake|Pelinal Whitestrake]], like his "nephew" Morihaus, has been described as a [[Lore:Demigod|Demigod]].{{Ref|name=TFS|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Online|The Footsteps of Shezarr}}}} His parentage is unknown, but it was said that he was thought to be the [[Lore:Shezarrine|Shezarrine]]{{ref|name=TSOP5|}} and the [[Lore:Nord|Nords]] likened him to [[Lore:Shor|Shor]].{{ref|name=TSOP4|{{Cite Book|The Song of Pelinal, v 4}}}} Despite his hatred of god-talk, Pelinal himself spoke of a twin nature he shared with [[Lore:Akatosh|Aka]].{{ref|name=TSOP6|{{Cite Book|The Song of Pelinal, v 6}}}}
*[[Lore:Umaril|Umaril the Unfeathered]] was the son of an [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]] mother with his father being a god of the prior [[Lore:Kalpa|kalpa]] giving him the blood of the Ada.{{ref|name=TSOP7|{{Cite Book|The Song of Pelinal, v 7}}}}{{ref|name=TSOP3|{{Cite Book|The Song of Pelinal, v 3}}}} He also furthered his immortality by binding himself to the realm of his mistress [[Lore:Meridia|Meridia]], giving him a [[Lore:Daedra|Daedric]] nature.{{ref|name=Oblivion|[[Oblivion:The Prophet|The Prophet]]'s dialogue in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]: [[Oblivion:Knights of the Nine|Knights of the Nine]]''}}
*[[Lore:Morihaus|Morihaus]] was the Demigod son of [[Lore:Kyne|Kyne]].{{ref|name=TSOP5|}} His father is unknown but he took on the shape of a man bull.{{ref|name=TSOP5|}}
*[[Lore:Fa-Nuit-Hen|Fa-Nuit-Hen]]{{Ref|name=LAFNJTR2}} was the [[Lore:Demiprince|Demiprince]] scion of [[Lore:Boethiah|Boethiah]].{{Ref|name=LAFNJTR|{{Cite Book|Lord Fa-Nuit-Hen and Tutor Riparius Answer Your Questions}}}} His mortal parent was unknown but he took on the appearance of a demonic [[Lore:Bosmer|Bosmer]].{{ref|name=AMB|{{Cite Book|A Memory Book}}}}
