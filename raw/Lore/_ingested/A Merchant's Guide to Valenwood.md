(An Excerpt)

Any legitimate merchant who's traded in eastern {{Lore Link|Greenshade}} knows to avoid the {{Lore Link|old Merchant Tunnel}}s.

In old times, when we first asked {{Lore Link|the Green}} to create a passage, the tunnels were an important byway, a defense against the wilder things that live in the forest.

Now, it's a haven for all that's illicit, illegal, and disgusting. Backhanded traders moving unreliable product at outrageous costs. Black market trade. {{Lore Link|Skooma}}. It's all there. A sure way to call one's legitimacy as a merchant into question is to pass through those tunnels.

For this reason, though the way is treacherous, it is advised to avoid the tunnels and take the low route over the bridge, beyond the bone pits.

Other good advice for {{Lore Link|Valenwood}} merchants is to travel well-armed, and to keep a watch at night. The {{Lore Link|Bosmer|Wood Elves}} of the area are known for their thieving, and some can be extremely unfriendly to strangers.

In general, it's a good idea to stick close to {{Lore Link|Woodhearth}} and other major settlements, rather than trying to deal with the backwoods {{Lore Link|Green Pact}} Bosmer personally. Let Wood Elves deal with Wood Elves.
