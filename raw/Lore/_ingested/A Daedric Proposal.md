The belief that someone could fully resurrect a dead creature should be disregarded as pointless. Especially when considering what we know about the {{Lore Link|souls}} of {{Lore Link|vampire}}s, such an endeavor may be even considered dangerous. Attempting to thwart the desires of {{Lore Link|Molag Bal}} could bring his wrath down upon you, my {{Lore Link|Lady Thorn|lady}}, which would be ruinous for our clan. I hear word of plans for such a project and I firmly warn you to avoid collaboration.

Instead, I propose an alternative. A certain ritual I learned while studying soulworking deep in some ancient catacombs beneath {{Lore Link|Morrowind}}. Once performed, we can force {{Lore Link|Daedra|Daedric}} {{Lore Link|Animus|spirits}} to reside within the {{Lore Link|Resurrected Vampire|cadavers}} of our fallen brethren. This reanimates the flesh so that they may execute basic martial functions, such as wielding a sword. If bound correctly, they will follow our commands to the letter. These shall be the perfect heavy shock troops for your new legion.

This ritual requires a great deal of soul energy to power it, and a practitioner who could easily communicate with the wild Daedra. I know of just the {{Lore Link|Vaduroth|entity}} (or should I say entities) that could be pressed into such service.

With my lady's permission, I shall begin preparations.

Galdrus Salobar, Sanguimancer Supreme
