[[Lore:Julianos|Julianos]], [[Lore:Aedra|Aedric]] God of Wisdom and Logic, is one of the [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodilic]] [[Lore:Divines|Divines]], governing the realms of literature, law, history, and contradiction. He is usually associated with [[Lore:Magic|magic]], and thus is often revered by wizards. [[Lore:Jhunal|Jhunal]], the [[Lore:Nord|Nord]]ic father of [[Lore:Ehlnofex|language]] and mathematics, fell out of favor in the Nordic pantheon, after which he became Julianos of the Divines. Monastic orders founded by [[Lore:Tiber Septim|Tiber Septim]] and dedicated to Julianos are the [[Lore:Cult of the Ancestor Moth|keepers]] of the [[Lore:Elder Scrolls|Elder Scrolls]].{{ref|name=GerJ|[[Morrowind:Generic Dialogue J|Generic dialogue]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{ref|name=DFUG|{{Cite Book|Daggerfall User's Guide|ns_base=Books}}}} He is particularly venerated among many [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]]s and [[Lore:Breton|Breton]]s,{{ref|name=VOF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith...}}}} but he also sees worship among the [[Lore:Forebears|Forebear]] [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]]s.{{ref|name=VOFTFB|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith, The Forebears}}}} As a Divine, he represents the virtue of learning, scholarship, and justice. His domains include sorcery, [[Lore:Alchemy|alchemy]] and [[Lore:Enchanting|enchantment]].{{ref|name=GerJ}} He is said to "incant the Damned Equation".{{ref|name=PGE1I|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Invocation}}}} His symbol is a triangle.<noinclude>

==Worship==
[[File:BL-misc-Julianos.png|Imperial depiction of Julianos|left|thumb|150px]]
Julianos created the [[Lore:Crusader's Relics#Shield of the Crusader|Shield of the Crusader]] for [[Lore:Pelinal Whitestrake|Pelinal Whitestrake]], to help him defeat [[Lore:Umaril the Unfeathered|Umaril the Unfeathered]].{{ref|name=KOTN|Events of the {{g|kotn|oblivion}}}} A chapel is dedicated to his worship in [[Lore:Skingrad|Skingrad]].{{ref|name=GTS|{{Cite Book|Guide to Skingrad}}}} There is also a Chantry of Julianos in the [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]].{{ref|name=RTBS|{{Cite Book|Response to Bero's Speech}}}}

The ancient Nords worshipped Jhunal, who was associated with the totem of the Owl.{{ref|name=JTOG|{{Cite Book|Jhunal the Rune God}}}}{{Ref|name=Contra|[[Online:Contraband H#Holy Symbol of Jhunal|Holy Symbol of Jhunal]] item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Alessia fused the Nordic and Elven Pantheons together for her new empire, creating the aspect of Julianos.{{Ref|name=SATD|{{Cite Book|Shezarr and the Divines}}}} While certain aspects of the Alessian reforms remain controversial in some parts of Skyrim, there is little controversy regarding the role of Julianos.{{fact}}

In the [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]], temples dedicated to him are known as [[Lore:School of Julianos|Schools of Julianos]].{{ref|name=Daggerfall|Faction information on the [[Daggerfall:School of Julianos|School of Julianos]] in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} As their name implies, these temples are considered educational institutions.{{ref|name=TROZ|{{Cite Book|The Ransom of Zarek}}}}{{Ref|name=PM|[[ON:Pilou Mouriou|Pilou Mouriou]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} They are believed to have spread the term "magicka" to describe the "power associated with performing magic".{{ref|name=KE12|{{Cite Book|King Edward, Part XII}}}} Julianos is the patron deity of the regions of [[Lore:Lainlyn|Lainlyn]], [[Lore:Daenia|Daenia]], [[Lore:Abibon-Gora|Abibon-Gora]], [[Lore:Dwynnen|Dwynnen]], [[Lore:Tulune|Tulune]] and [[Lore:Satakalaam|Satakalaam]].{{ref|Patron deities of [[Daggerfall:Lainlyn|Lainlyn]], [[Daggerfall:Daenia|Daenia]], [[Daggerfall:Abibon-Gora|Abibon-Gora]], [[Daggerfall:Dwynnen|Dwynnen]], [[Daggerfall:Tulune|Tulune]], [[Daggerfall:Satakalaam|Satakalaam]] in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}}

After imperialization and the loss of independence, some [[Lore:Reachmen|Reachmen]] embraced the worship of the Eight Divines, including Julianos, believing that certain aspects of their traditional practices were best left in the past.{{ref|name=Belchimac|[[Skyrim:Belchimac|Belchimac]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}{{ref|name=Cedran|[[Skyrim:Cedran|Cedran]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}{{ref|name=Degaine|[[Skyrim:Degaine|Degaine]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}

==Notes==
* The wine "Julianos Firebelly" can be made with [[Lore:Sacred Lotus Seeds|Sacred Lotus Seeds]] and [[Lore:Lady's Smock Leaves|Lady's Smock Leaves]].{{ref|name=OBWIL|{{Cite Book|Wine Ingredient List|ns_base=Oblivion}}}}
*Grateful mentor idols are carved by followers of Julianos to show their respect to the teacher that made the biggest impression on the student.{{ref|[[Online:Treasures G|Grateful Mentor Idol]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
* Those who visit the Wayshrines of Julianos in Cyrodiil receive blessings enhancing their [[Oblivion:Intelligence|intelligence]] and [[Oblivion:Magicka|magicka]].{{ref|name=Oblivion|Events of {{g|Oblivion}}}}
*Julianos is named after [[General:Julian LeFay|Julian LeFay]]. [[General:Ted Peterson|Ted Peterson]], who is the namesake of [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]], explained that Julian was the more "logical" of the two of them and "always in his own head", while Peterson was the one coming up with crazy ideas.{{Ref|name=LeFay|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Julian LeFay's Posts|ns_base=General}}}}{{ref|name=TedCamel|group=UOL|1=''[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N_YhxCkF09Y Camelworks: Ted Peterson Interview]''}} Julian LeFay passed away on July 22, 2025.
