[[Lore:Velyn Harbor|Velyn Harbor]] (also called '''Port Velyn'''){{Ref|name=ATUQ|{{Cite Book|Ayrenn: The Unforeseen Queen}}}} is a large coastal town near the [[Lore:Strid River|Strid River]] estuary in the province of [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]]. The town is [[Lore:Malabal Tor|Malabal Tor]]'s main port{{Ref|name=ONMaritawen|[[Online:Maritawen|Maritawen]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=FOT|{{Cite Book|Fists of Thalmor (note)|ns_base=ON}}}} and was built by the [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]].{{Ref|name=EGT|{{Cite Book|The Improved Emperor's Guide to Tamriel/Valenwood}}}} Towns such as Velyn Harbor provided an opportunity for a cultural exchange between the Altmer and the local [[Lore:Bosmer|Bosmer]], like how much Bosmeri swordsmanship had been refined from Altmer influence and access to materials.{{Ref|name=CAOV|{{Cite Book|Common Arms of Valenwood}}}}<noinclude>

==Layout and Geography==
[[File:ON-place-Mages Guild (Velyn Harbor).jpg|thumb|left|The Mages Guild of Velyn Harbor]]
[[File:ON-map-Velyn Harbor.jpg|thumb|right|2nd Era map of Velyn Harbor]]
Velyn Harbor is located on the northwesternmost point of Valenwood, on the [[Lore:Broken Coast|Broken Coast]] region where it is adjacent to the [[Lore:Gold Coast|Gold Coast]] in [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]]. The town is built into the eponymous Velyn Bay, a cove riddled with islands sprawled across it to the north. The central marketplace and downtown are on the small western peninsula on the coast, the residential district and town hall are inland to the east, and the docks are north on the rocky islands. Velyn Harbor's southern gate goes straight into the [[Lore:Greenshade|Greenshade]] region while the eastern gate is the gateway into the greater Malabal Tor region.{{Ref|name=ONVelyn|[[Online:Velyn Harbor|Velyn Harbor]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Velyn Harbor's central hub, the marketplace is centered around the smithy. The road to Greenshade goes around one large building to the south and goes uphill through a large gate. The wayshrine is off this beaten path, on a stone foundation while the area in front of the gate provides open spaces for encampments.{{Ref|name=ONVelyn}}

East of the marketplace, across the river, is the residential district, a raised hill area with a fountain in the center. Paved roads run along the base of the hill, going north to the docks and going south toward the eastern gate. The town hall is on the northeast corner of the district. Connected to the residential district via another bridge is the Velyn Harbor docks.{{Ref|name=ONVelyn}} The docks are built on two islands, one where the warehouses are and the other with the sole lighthouse.{{Ref|name=ONVelyn}} The warehouses here contain the settlement's food supply.{{Ref|name=ONLinaarie|[[Online:Sergeant Linaarie|Sergeant Linaarie]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

==History==
[[File:ON-npc-Finoriell.jpg|thumb|left|Finoriell of Velyn Harbor]][[File:ON-place-Town Hall (Velyn Harbor).jpg|thumb|right|Velyn Harbor Town Hall]]
When Velyn Harbor was founded is unknown, however, it was already an established settlement by the time [[Lore:Falinesti|Falinesti]] had disappeared{{Ref|name=ND|{{Cite Book|Nirwaen's Diary}}}} sometime around {{Year|2E 382}} and {{Year|2E 432|432}}.{{Ref|name=ONAndewen|[[Online:Andewen|Andewen]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} At that time, it was a small encampment{{Ref|name=ND}} but the settlement eventually grew into an active port.{{Ref|name=ONVelyn}} Other Altmer colonies on Valenwood, such as [[Lore:Haven|Haven]]{{Ref|name=ONAquifer|[[Online:The Aquifer|The Aquifer]] loading screen text in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} and [[Lore:Marbruk|Marbruk]] were also built sometime in the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]].{{Ref|name=TGATD|{{Cite Book|The Green Pact and the Dominion}}}} The people of Velyn Harbor often traded with [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] merchants and grew to call them [[Lore:Ra Gada|Ra Gada]] as a sign of respect.{{Ref|name=ONLinaarie}} 

Sometime in the mid-Second Era, a local Bosmer named [[Lore:Finoriell|Finoriell]] was revealed to be the [[Lore:Green Lady|Green Lady]] rather later than customary. She performed her handfasting ceremony with the then [[Lore:The Silvenar|Silvenar]], [[Lore:Edhelorn|Edhelorn]].{{Ref|name=TGLA|{{Cite Book|The Green Ladies' Abode|ns_base=ON}}}} After years of traveling around the world, in {{Year|2E 580}}, Princess [[Lore:Ayrenn|Ayrenn]] of the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset Isles]] arrived in Port Velyn and embarked to [[Lore:Firsthold|Firsthold]] where she would reclaim her birthright as Queen.{{Ref|name=ATUQ}} Not long after her arrival, however, the town was attacked by [[Lore:Maormer|Maormer]] pirates.  A contingent of Bosmer scouts accompanied by Treethane Serenarth was able to defeat them and save the town.{{Ref|name=EGT}}

In {{Year|2E 582}}, after the new Silvenar [[Lore:Indaenir|Indaenir]] was announced, preparations for the Handfasting ceremony between him and the new Green Lady, [[Lore:Gwaering|Gwaering]] were put into motion. Velyn Harbor began to make preparations for their arrival while the [[Lore:Fists of Thalmor|Fists of Thalmor]] stationed in town, led by Captain Cularalda, were ordered by the Queen to escort them to their city of [[Lore:Silvenar|Silvenar]] out east as their honor guard. Another contingent of the Fists was supposed to reach the Velyn Harbor by sea but before they arrived, the town was besieged by a combined force of the [[Lore:Drublog Clan|Drublog Clan]] of [[Lore:Wood Orcs|Wood Orcs]], who entered from the wilderness and Redguard pirates called the Ra Gada, who made landfall before them.{{Ref|name=ONLinaarie}}{{Ref|name=ONEhran|[[Online:Lieutenant Ehran|Lieutenant Ehran]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

A retaliation coordinated between Lieutenant Ehran and Sergeant Linaarie of the Fists was set into motion while the Captain hid from battle. An [[Lore:The Vestige|Agent]] of the [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]] assisted Ehran in his plan to create a civilian militia and retake the town. While the Agent rescued civilians from the Trade Hall and [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]], the Lieutenant went out to find the captain in the docks and secure the residential district for a runner to warn the rest of the region. The Agent was later able to clear the way for the runner, but the Lieutenant charged ahead of them and was fatally wounded by Chief Makhug, leader of the Drublog raiders.{{Ref|name=ONHouse|[[Online:House and Home|House and Home]] story quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} 

The Agent killed the chieftain and the Ra Gada captain in the lighthouse, all the while, Sergeant Linaarie found the Captain cowering from the conflict. While Velyn Harbor was saved and its people began to rebuild, the Fists of Thalmor arrested Captain Cularalda for her failure to protect the town.{{Ref|name=ONSwoop|[[Online:One Fell Swoop|One Fell Swoop]] story quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Gwaering, the Green Lady arrived not long afterward and met with the Agent. While she took time to meet with the locals, she sent them to the nearest Wood Orc settlement, [[Lore:Dra'bul|Dra'bul]] to learn more about their current alignments.{{Ref|name=ONDrublog|[[Online:The Drublog of Dra'bul|The Drublog of Dra'bul]] story quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
