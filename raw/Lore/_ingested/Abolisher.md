[[Lore:Abolisher|Abolisher]] is an artifact created by the Daedric Prince Boethiah to aid in [[Lore:Ithelia|Ithelia]]'s imprisonment, described as a pitted blade with an edge that can cut the veil between worlds.{{ref|name=AbolisherQuestItems|[[ON:Gold Road Quest Items|Abolisher's]] item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} It held the power to open [[Lore:Portals|portals]], destroy them, or reopen sealed portals. In addition it could be used to sever the link the [[Lore:Many Paths|Many Paths]] provided to a different reality, rendering it inaccessible.{{ref|name=Ithelia|[[ON:Ithelia|Ithelia]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Mora|[[ON:Hermaeus Mora|Hermaeus Mora]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Leramil|[[ON:Leramil the Wise|Leramil]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Beragon|[[ON:Beragon|Beragon]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Alea|[[ON:Tribune Alea Idolus|Tribune Alea Idolus]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Abolisher was ultimately destroyed when it was used as part of a spell originating from [[Lore:Hermaeus Mora|Hermaeus Mora]], which was provided to [[Lore:The Vestige|the Vestige]] in order to repair the [[Lore:Fate|reality tears]] created by Ithelia's power that were threatening to unravel all existence. After the spell was cast Abolisher became a normal sword, its Daedric power fully expended.{{ref|name=Scruut|[[ON:Scruut|Scruut]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} <noinclude>
