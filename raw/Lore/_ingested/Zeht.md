[[Lore:Zeht|Zeht]], Yokudan God of Farms, is the [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokudan]] god of agriculture,{{ref|name=VOF}} civil law, mathematics,{{ref|name=NJ/HB}} and food,{{ref|name=ZCC|[[ON:Zeht's Cloud Camel|Zeht's Cloud Camel]]'s description in {{g|ESO}}}} but he still finds a place in the pantheon of the [[Lore:Crowns|Crowns]]{{ref|name=VOFTC|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith, Crown Redguards}}}}. He is similar to [[Lore:Zenithar|Zenithar]], and sometimes worshiped in that name by the Forebears.{{ref|name=VOFTF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith, The Forebears}}}} He renounced his father after the world was created, which is why [[Lore:Ruptga|Ruptga]] makes it so hard to grow food.{{ref|name=VOF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith...}}}} He is the father of [[Lore:Zeqqi|Zeqqi]]. He forsakes the land of [[Lore:Hew's Bane|Hew's Bane]], preventing most food from growing there.{{ref|name=LitC|{{Cite Book|Lady in the Cistern: Walks-Softly's Theory|ns_base=Online}}}} Occasionally farming utensils of the Zeht's faithful such as digging spades are decorated with semi-precious stones and blessed by a priests of Zeht.{{ref|name=ContrabandB|[[ON:Contraband B#Blessed Spade|Blessed Spade]] item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The white [[Lore:Camel|camel]] is believed to be sacred to Zeht and pious Redguards tend to bow to passing Cloud Camels.{{ref|name=ZCC}}<noinclude>

Some sources claim that before deceiving the spirits, [[Lore:Sep|Sep]] stole one of the stars [[Lore:Ruptga|Ruptga]] was using to form the [[Lore:Astronomy|Great Map]], and put it in his pocket. When Sep was killed, the same star fell out of his mouth in the form of a pearl. The pearl fell to the land and was covered by sand, and so came to belong to Zeht. This Pearl is claimed to be the "Way and the Guide", the "light to the Far Shores and back again". When it is at last discovered again, [[Lore:Water Magic|water]] will flow over all the previous Worldskins, and the time of Sep's Hunger will be over, the Hungry Stomach of Satakal at last sated. It is also said that the Hunger is what answers the call of the worlds to save them, and that the only real world is that of Satakal, as the stars cannot be devoured.{{ref|[[Online:Hayazzin|Hayyazin]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
