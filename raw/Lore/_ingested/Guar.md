# Lore:Guar

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

<noinclude>{{Lore Bestiary Trail}}[[File:MW-creature-Guar.jpg|thumb|left|A wild guar]]{{TOCright}}[[File:ON-creature-Tessellated Guar 02.jpg|thumb|left|A [[Lore:Necrom|Necrom]] Guard riding a tessellated guar]]
</noinclude>[[Lore:Guar|Guar]] ({{IPA|g|w|a:|r}}){{Ref|name=Archive}} are large bipedal omnivorous [[Lore:Reptile|reptiles]] native to [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]] and [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]].{{ref|name=EDAYQ}} They are the primary domesticated herd animal of these provinces, and are not usually aggressive. Characterized by their endurance and intelligence, guar are used mostly as pack animals and beasts of burden, although certain breeds are used as mounts. Although usually associated with the [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]], guar have a special relationship with [[Lore:Argonian|Argonian]]s and the Dunmer traditionally employ [[Lore:Saxhleel|Saxhleel]] slaves as guar-herds and handlers. A young guar is called a calf or guarling.{{ref|name=Veru|[[Online:Veru Serethran|Veru Serethran]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

<noinclude>The guar is related to the [[Lore:Alit|alit]], [[Lore:Kagouti|kagouti]], and [[Lore:Wormmouth|wormmouth]], and some herders argue that the guar is a distant cousin of the Argonian.{{ref|name=EDAYQ|{{Cite Book|Esqoo of Dhalmora Answers Your Questions}}}} Argonian historians have speculated that guar were originally native to Black Marsh, and were introduced to Morrowind by Dunmer slavers.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive: Murkmire Q&A}}}} However, other sources claim that Morrowind's unique fauna developed in geographic isolation from the rest of Tamriel.{{Ref|name=PoM|{{Cite Book|People of Morrowind|ns_base=GEN}}}}

Despite the name, [[Lore:Bantam Guar|bantam guar]] are actually a member of the [[Lore:Scuttler|scuttler]] family, more closely related to [[Lore:Cliff Racer|cliff racers]] than guar. Domesticated guar are regularly seen outside their native lands, and have been imported into [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] as beasts of burden for hundreds of years.{{ref|name=MF1|{{Cite Book|Morrowind Fauna, Part One}}}} Guar are often raised or hunted for their [[Lore:Guar Hide|hide]], eggs, and meat. Wild guar are usually docile, but may become aggressive and attack travelers. Domesticated guar that are used to carry cargo are known as pack guar.

Guar provide milk,{{ref|name=Archive}}{{ref|name=GavinGavonne|[[Online:Gavin Gavonne|Gavin Gavonne]]'s correspondence in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=BfoB|{{Cite Book|Beverages for the Bereaved}}}} meat, leather, and transport for Vvardenfell's settled Velothi ranchers and Ashland nomads. In its natural state, the aggressive and territorial wild guar is hunted for meat and hides.{{ref|name=Archive}} Guar are known to molt{{ref|name=GuarBrush|[[ON:Contraband D#Deshedding Brush|Deshedding Brush]] item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}, and some are also said to be feathered.{{ref|name=TBS|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 134: Tide-Born Style}}}}

Tiber Septim apparently developed a particular fondness for guar during one of his various tours of Morrowind. Septim struggled with the [[Lore:Dunmer Language|Dunmer language]], and so instead took to calling them "[[Lore:Senche-Tiger|tigers]]"&mdash;another animal he was fond of from the storybooks of his youth. Many Imperial Legionnaires and even [[Lore:Dres|Dres]] slavers still refer to their cattle guar as tigers in honor of this.{{ref|name=MK|group=UOL|[[General:Michael Kirkbride's Posts|Michael Kirkbride's Posts]]}}

==Breeds==
Guar come in many different breeds, listed below:

===Albino Guar===
[[File:MW-creature-White Guar.jpg|thumb|right|An albino guar]]
Albino Guar, also called White Guar,{{ref|[[Morrowind:Dreams of a White Guar|Dreams of a White Guar]] quest in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}} are a rare type of guar. They often feature in [[Lore:Ashlanders|Ashlander]] prophecies, and bezoars taken from their stomachs can be used in rituals.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Drothan's Journal}}}}
{{NewLine}}

===Banded Guar Charger===
[[File:ON-mount-Banded Guar Charger.jpg|thumb|right|Banded Guar Charger]]
The Banded Guar Charger is a breed of striped guar used as a war mount. They are related to the feral [[#Tiger Guar|tiger guar]].{{ref|name=EDAYQ}}
{{NewLine}}

===Common Vvardenfell Guar===
[[File:ON-creature-Guar.jpg|thumb|right|A common guar]]
The Common Vvardenfell Guar is the most common breed of guar. Despite the name, the breed is not limited to the island of [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]].
{{NewLine}}

===Deadlands Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Deadlands_Guar.jpg|thumb|right|A Deadlands guar]]
The Deadlands Guar is a breed of Guar said to have been born and shaped in the [[Lore:Deadlands|Deadlands]]. They have a blazing orange hide that reflects their origin and temperament.{{Ref|name=DGWD|[[ON:Deadlands Guar|Deadlands Guar]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
{{NewLine}}

===Freckled Guar===
[[File:ON-pet-Freckled Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Freckled Guar]]
Freckled Guar are considered to be the most intelligent breed of guar. They can be taught to come when called, and some can even be house-trained. As such, they're regularly kept as pets in [[Lore:Deshaan|Deshaan]].{{ref|name=ESOCSS|[[Lore:Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase|Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase]]}}
{{NewLine}}

===Frostbane Guar===
[[File:ON-pet-Frostbane Pony Guar.jpg|Frostbane Pony Guar|thumb|right]]
Frostbane Guar is a strange breed of guar found in [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]]. It has a shaggy white coat, adorned with symbolic hair-knots and hide strips meant to protect it from the frigid climate. The practice of adorning mounts with these dangling "Frostbane talismans" is an [[Lore:Atmora|Atmora]]n tradition dating back to [[Lore:Ysgramor|Ysgramor]]'s arrival in Skyrim. The ancient [[Lore:Nord|Nord]]s worshipped totem animals as [[Lore:Gods|gods]], and Frostbane talismans continue to be used into modern times. Since the guar is native to warmer climates, the existence of Frostbane Guar suggest that these wards do in fact protect against the cold.

The Frostbane Pony Guar is a smaller breed which has long been popular among the more traditional aristocracy of [[Lore:Riften|Riften]], who enjoy dressing them in Frostbane garb and keeping them as pets.
{{NewLine}}

===Glowgill Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Glowgill Guar.jpg|Glowgill Guar|right|thumb]]
The '''Glowgill Guar''' (also called the '''Lambent Guar'''){{Ref|name=ONLambent|[[Online:Body Markings/Crown Crate Rewards#Lambent Guar Body Markings|Lambent Guar Body Markings]] body markings description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} is an exotic breed of guar that is distinguished by its glowing eyes and stripes. They trace their origins to a coastal Argonian tribe near [[Lore:Archon|Archon]], where they have dark navy skin and bright blue eyes, and bioluminescent stripes. Rumors suggest that they can breathe underwater, but it's unknown if they can.{{Ref|name=ONGlowgill|[[Online:Glowgill Guar|Glowgill Guar]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The glowgill guar from [[Lore:Gideon|Gideon]]{{Ref|name=ONAmber|[[Online:Ambergill Guar|Ambergill Guar]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} and [[Lore:Murkmire|Murkmire]]{{Ref|name=ONTawny|[[Online:Tawny Lambent Guar|Tawny Lambent Guar]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} are distinguished by their golden colors, hence why they are called the Ambergill Guar and not just the Golden Glowgill Guar.{{Ref|name=ONAmber}} Locals that live in Black Marsh's swamps believe that nature's energy feeds the guar's light, but scholars believe that the light is only an [[Lore:Alchemy|alchemical]] reaction.{{Ref|name=ONTawny}} Tattoo artists in Murkmire siphon the guar's blood to create glowing yellow skin markings and say that the blessing of the land keeps them lit.{{Ref|name=ONLambent}}
{{NewLine}}

===Golden Eye Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Golden Eye Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Golden Eye Guar]]
Golden Eye Guar (or "golden-eyes") are a breed of guar used as a mount. It is a cross between a [[#Banded Guar Charger|Banded Guar Charger]] and a [[#Wild Guar|wild guar]], known for its difficulty to control and its distinctive golden eyes. Despite the difficulty involved in training one, Golden Eyes are fearsome, loyal, and intelligent.{{ref|name=ESOCSS}}
{{NewLine}}

===Green Narsis Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Green Narsis Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Green Narsis Guar]]
The Green Narsis Guar is a robust breed, used as the standard mount and dray-beast of central Morrowind.{{Ref|name=ONNarsis|[[Online:Green Narsis Guar|Green Narsis Guar]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} It's named after the city of [[Lore:Narsis|Narsis]].
{{NewLine}}

===Hist Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Hist Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Hist Guar]]
The Hist Guar, known in Black Marsh as Scales-Seem-Daubed-with-Hist-Sap, is a subspecies of guar which appears as if it has splotches of glowing [[Lore:Hist Sap|Hist Sap]] splattered across its green skin. It is unknown if the breed actually has any connection to the [[Lore:Hist|Hist]] or to [[Lore:Amber Plasm|amber plasm]]. The Hist Guar is used as a mount, and is the only subspecies known to enjoy music.{{ref|name=ESOCSS}}
{{NewLine}}

===Hollowjack Rider Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Hollowjack Rider Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Hollowjack Rider Guar]]
Hollowjack Rider Guar are a mysterious breed usually only sighted on the night of the [[Lore:Witches Festival|Witches Festival]]. These sightings are the result of {{Lore Link|Jaque o' the Hollow}}, a malevolent [[Lore:Rivenspire|Rivenspire]] spirit that curses riders all across Tamriel to ride headless through the night. Guar riders stricken by this curse can be sighted riding headless atop jet black guar mounts with red eyes. Hollowjack Rider Guar appear to be native to the realm of [[Lore:Detritus|Detritus]] and can be summoned from that realm.{{ref|name=ESOCSS}}
{{NewLine}}

===Pale Velothi Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Pale Velothi Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Pale Velothi Guar]]
The Pale Velothi Guar is a breed that hails from the ashlands downwind of the volcanic [[Lore:Velothi Mountains|Velothi Mountains]]. They are used as mounts, and are renowned as hardy creatures capable of enduring especially harsh conditions.{{ref|name=ESOCSS}}
{{NewLine}}

===Pony Guar===
[[File:ON-creature-Pony Guar.jpg|thumb|right|A pony guar]]
Pony Guar are a smaller species of guar that are rarely exported since they lack the physical strength of their larger cousins. Not well known outside of southern Morrowind, pony guar are usually bred as a curiosity, although some are raised for their meat and skins.{{ref|name=MF1}} Most guar breeds or variants possess a "pony" equivalent.
{{NewLine}}

===Psijic Guar Exemplar===
[[File:ON-mount-Psijic Guar Exemplar.jpg|thumb|right|A Psijic Guar Exemplar]]
Psijic Guar Exemplars are a breed of guar created by the [[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic Order]]. Like all Psijic Exemplars, this breed theoretically represents the ideal form a guar can assume, at which point the creature transcends the physical and incarnates the mystical. As such, Guar Exemplars take on a yellow ghostly form. They are used as mounts by Psijic monks when traversing the marshy terrain of Black Marsh or southern Morrowind, and are unequaled in this task. The first guar came to [[Lore:Artaeum|Artaeum]] when a Psijic advisor to the [[Lore:Tribunal|Tribunal]] returned from [[Lore:Mournhold|Mournhold]] with a [[#Pony Guar|Pony Guar]] in the middle of the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]],{{ref|[[Online:Psijic Mascot Guar Calf|Psijic Mascot Guar Calf]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}, so the creation of Guar Exemplars presumably began sometime after this.
{{NewLine}}

===Psijic Mascot Guar===
[[File:ON-furnishing-Psijic Mascot Guar Calf.jpg|Psijic Mascot Guar|thumb|right]]
Psijic Mascot Guar are a breed of guar created by the Psijic Order for use as pets. Like all [[Lore:Psijic Mascot|Psijic Mascots]], it is unknown if these guar are real animals with an enchanted appearance, or illusory constructs created from Magicka to be used as beast companions and portable storage batteries of Magicka. The Psijics refuse to clarify the true nature of these creatures, although they have dismissed the theory that Mascots are simply portable batteries. In appearance, the Psijic Mascot Guar is a black guar which glows with Magicka through the mystic blue runes that embellish its possibly-illusory epidermis. Guar have been favorites of the Psijics ever since the first one was brought to Artaeum in the middle of the First Era, and Mascot guar calves are very popular as pets.
{{NewLine}}

==={{Anchor|Shadowghost Guar|Shadowghost Pony Guar}}===
[[File:ON-mount-Shadowghost Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Shadowghost Guar]]
Shadowghost Guar are a mottled black and white breed of guar and are the only known nocturnal breed of the species. Due to their nocturnal habits and the fact that their patterned hides blend in with the shifting gray ash clouds on the slopes of [[Lore:Red Mountain|Red Mountain]], the breed is rarely seen even by the native Dunmer. Nevertheless, Shadowghost Guar are sometimes used as mounts.

Shadowghost Pony Guar are a smaller breed also native to the slopes of Red Mountain. They are sometimes kept as pets.
{{NewLine}}

===Skyfire Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Skyfire_Guar.jpg|thumb|right|A Skyfire Guar]]
Skyfire Guar are often mistaken as being poisonous due to their colorful pattern. Patience is needed to bring this breed to heed.
{{NewLine}}

===Striated Pony Guar===
[[File:ON-pet-Striated Pony Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Striated Pony Guar]]
The Striated Pony Guar is a breed of [[#Pony Guar|pony guar]] kept as household pets in Morrowind.
{{NewLine}}

===Tessellated Guar===
[[File:ON-mount-Tessellated Guar.jpg|thumb|right|Tessellated Guar]]
The Tessellated Guar is a multi-purpose guar mount, at home anywhere from swamps to ashlands.
{{NewLine}}

===Tiger Guar===
Tiger Guar are a legendary breed of large wild guar, and are a highly prized game animal for noble huntsmen.{{ref|name=Archive|[[General:Elderscrolls.com Archive/Screenshots|Screenshot descriptions]] on the official ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'' website}} Found only on the [[Lore:Deshaan Plain|Deshaan Plain]], their stripes have been compared to those of a [[Lore:Tiger|tiger]].{{ref|name=EDAYQ}}{{ref|name=MK|group=UOL}} The [[#Banded Guar Charger|banded guar charger]] is a breed descended from the tiger guar.{{ref|name=EDAYQ}}
{{NewLine}}
