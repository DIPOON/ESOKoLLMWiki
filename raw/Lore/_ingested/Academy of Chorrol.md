The [[Lore:Academy of Chorrol|Academy of Chorrol]] was an [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] academic institution based in [[Lore:Chorrol|Chorrol]], active at some point before the [[Lore:Three Banners War|Three Banners War]] in the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]]. Little is known of the academy, except that one of its scholars, [[Lore:Books by Author#Juno Procillus|Juno Procillus]], interviewed [[Lore:Reachman Shamans|Reachfolk Shaman]] [[Lore:Uraccanach|Uraccanach]] of the [[Lore:Druadach Mountains|Druadach Mountains]] and transcribed his words, publishing a text devoted to the [[Lore:Reachmen|Reachfolk]] understanding of the [[Lore:Daedric Prince|Daedric Prince]] [[Lore:Hircine|Hircine]] named ''[[Lore:Aspects of Lord Hircine|Aspects of Lord Hircine]]'' and one describing the clothing and weaponry associated with his [[Lore:Daedra|Daedric]] servants, the [[Lore:Huntsman|Huntsmen]].{{Ref|name=AOLH|{{Cite Book|Aspects of Lord Hircine}}}}{{Ref|name=CM65|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 65: Huntsman Style}}}}<noinclude>{{NewLeft}}
