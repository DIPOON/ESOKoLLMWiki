The spice island of [[Lore:Esroniet|Esroniet]] is located between [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] and [[Lore:Akavir|Akavir]].{{ref|name=BHOTE4|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 4}}}}{{ref|name=ONHotPepperBantamGuar}} The main port of the island is called {{Lore Link|Black Harbor}}.{{Ref|name=RDaI|{{Cite Book|Report: Disaster at Ionith}}}} The port was captured in {{Year|3E 282}} by [[Lore:Uriel Septim V|Uriel Septim V]], and in {{Year|3E 284}} the island's ruler, [[Lore:Bashomon|Prince Bashomon]], surrendered the whole kingdom to him. Uriel transformed it into a major base for the [[Lore:Imperial Navy|Imperial Navy]] and used it as a launching point for the invasion of Akavir in {{Year|3E 288}}.{{Ref|name=RDaI}}<noinclude>

Esroniet is the source of much of Tamriel's nutmeg, cloves, mace, and hot peppers.{{ref|name=ONHotPepperBantamGuar}} A type of vibrant red dye, extremely popular in [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] around {{Year|2E 53}}, was made from crushed hot peppers imported from Esroniet.{{ref|name=PSR|[[ON:Pottery, Sanguine Repaired|Pottery, Sanguine Repaired]]'s [[ON:West Weald Antiquities#Pottery, Sanguine Repaired|Antiquity Codex entry]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]&#58; ''[[Online:Gold Road|Gold Road]]''}} The flame-eyed Hot Pepper [[Lore:Bantam Guar|Bantam Guar]] is native to the island.{{ref|name=ONHotPepperBantamGuar|[[Online:Hot Pepper Bantam Guar|Hot Pepper Bantam Guar]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
