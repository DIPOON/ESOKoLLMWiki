'''Clannfear''' are ferocious [[Lore:Reptile|reptilian]] [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]] that resembles a bipedal muscular [[Lore:Lizard|lizard]] with a large, bony crest on their head, as well as a curved beak, sharp talons, and clawed [[Lore:Bird|bird]]-like feet.{{ref|name=Athenaeum|{{Cite Book|Battlespire Athenaeum|ns_base=Books}}}}{{rp|57}}{{ref|name=MJDA|group=UOL|[[General:Mark Jones|Jones, M]]. [mjonesgraphics]. (22 March 2016). "''<nowiki>:)</nowiki> The Clannfear in the games has 'porked out' a little over the years. The original design was intended to be more cadaver -like, being as it is a Vulture/Reptile/Demon <nowiki>:)</nowiki>''". Comment on [https://web.archive.org/web/20250904171452/https://www.deviantart.com/mjonesgraphics/art/Clannfear-Polypainted-Zbrush-WIP-598209309 "''Clannfear Polypainted Zbrush - WIP''"]. DeviantArt. }} They have tough scales on their body, and their mouths are lined with razor-sharp teeth for tearing flesh.{{ref|name=TIEGGT|{{Cite Book|The Improved Emperor's Guide to Tamriel/Bangkorai}}}} They are generally thought to be of animal intelligence, though many are intelligent enough to speak.{{ref|name=BSClann|[[Battlespire:Clannfear|Clannfear]] [[Battlespire:Clannfear#Level 6: Havok Wellhead Dialogue|dialogue]] in ''[[Battlespire:Battlespire|Battlespire]]''}} Clannfear are of low status, and are sometimes branded to reveal their loyalty. Clannfear cannot use tools, so they attack their foes through biting, clawing and lashing with their tail spikes. They can be formidable, but they are by no means clever.{{ref|name=TIEGGT}} They are known to serve many Daedric Princes and can be found throughout the various [[Lore:Planes of Oblivion|planes of Oblivion]].{{Ref|name=VoD|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Daedra}}}}{{ref|name=ESOCold|Appearance of Clannfear throughout [[ON:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} In-fact every Daedric Prince is said to possess legions of clannfear among other common [[Lore:Lesser Daedra|Lesser Daedra]].{{Ref|{{Cite Book|Denizens of Apocrypha|ns_base=ON}}}}  Despite this, some attribute them to [[Lore:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]].{{Ref|name=DD|{{Cite Book|Darkest Darkness}}}} They can occasionally be found lingering near [[Lore:Daedric Ruins|Daedric Ruins]] on [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]].{{ref|name=ESOAsh|Abundance of Clannfear at the Daedric Ruins on [[ON:Ash Mountain|Ash Mountain]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

Clannfear walk on two legs and use their clawed arms and sharp beaks as [[Lore:Weapons|weapons]].{{ref|name=MWClann|[[MW:Clannfear|Clannfear]] abilities in ''[[MW:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}} They are capable of ripping mortals to shreds.{{Ref|name=OOR|{{Cite Book|Outsider Observation Report - Log 2}}}} Clannfear claws are made for hooking their prey and pinning them while they are consumed alive.{{ref|name=ESODaedric Dewclaw|{{Item Link|Daedric Dewclaw|itemid=54234|quality=3}} item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} The clannfear can make use of its crest to headbutt or charge at its prey.{{ref|name=FWDGFA|{{Cite Book|Fun with Daedra: Games for Adventurers}}}} Clannfear pounce on their prey to knock it down. They can also slam their spiked tail into their prey to disorient it.{{ref|name=ESOClann|[[ON:Clannfear|Clannfear]] abilities in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Clannfear are inclined to flock together to hunt and may answer to a powerful matriarch, but are still dangerous as individuals.{{ref|name=ESOClannMatron|[[ON:The Screeching Matron|The Screeching Matron]] and [[ON:The Devourer|The Devourer]]'s ability to summon lesser clannfear in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Clannfear vocalize by baying and screeching.{{ref|name=ONClannSound|[[ON:Clannfear|Clannfear]] sounds in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=KCAHC|[[Books:Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion|Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion]]: Hammerfell, Clannfear}} Clannfear are known to call mortals "''pinkskin''" as an insult.{{ref|name=BSClann}}

While they are described as loud-mannered,{{ref|name=Athenaeum}}{{rp|57}} clannfear can be trained by more intelligent Daedra such as [[Lore:Dremora|Dremora]].{{ref|name=ESOTrainer|[[ON:Hzu-Hakan|Hzu-Hakan]] and [[ON:Angata the Clannfear Handler|Angata the Clannfear Handler]]'s abilities, dialogue and story purposes in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Trained clannfear can assist Dremora in hunting mortals for sport,{{ref|name=OnlineGMJ|{{Cite Book|Guild Mage's Journal|ns_base=Online}}}} and have a keen sense of smell that assists them in such tasks.{{ref|name=MTCCC|{{Cite Book|Meet the Character - Captain Caudex}}}}  The [[Lore:Xivilai's Clan|clan]] led by [[Lore:Xivilai Moath|Xivilai Moath]] held many clannfear as retainers during the invasion of the [[Lore:Battlespire|Battlespire]].{{ref|name=BSClann}} Young clannfear purportedly need food to grow, which they often receive in the form of mortal flesh.{{Ref|name=AmbientDialogue|Overheard [[Online:Xivkyn Dialogue|Xivkyn]] and [[Online:Dremora Dialogue|Dremora dialogue]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Imperial City (DLC)|Imperial City]]''}} Their claws and hearts can be used in alchemy.{{ref|name=OBClaw|Alchemical properties of [[OB:Clannfear Claws|Clannfear Claws]] in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}{{ref|name=MWHeart|[[MW:Clannfear|Clannfear]] being a source of [[MW:Daedra's Heart|Daedra Hearts]] in ''[[MW:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{ref|name=KCAHC}}

==Notes==
*The original Clannfear concept art was created by artist [[General:Mark Jones|Mark Jones]], for an early iteration of [[General:Tribunal (canceled game)|The Elder Scrolls III]] that was cancelled. They were originally conceived as Tamrielic fauna rather than Daedra, and would have been called "Rock Chisels" or "Rock Biters".{{Ref|group=UOL|[[General:The Dragon's Eyrie|The Dragon's Eyrie]]}}
*In the ''[[Books:The Art of Morrowind|The Art of Morrowind]]'' the [[Lore:Ogrim|Ogrim]] are referred to as "Clannfear Daddies".{{Ref|name=TAoM|{{Cite Book|The Art of Morrowind|ns_base=Books}}}}{{rp|40}}
