[[Lore:Spider Daedra|Spider Daedra]] (also called '''Perthan'''){{Ref|name=ONCL|{{Cite Book|Caterwaul Logbook}}}}{{intnote|nb1|[nb 1]}} are semi-intelligent [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]]{{Ref|name=VoD|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Daedra}}}} who appear as giant [[Lore:Spider|spiders]] with armored carapaces and humanoid torsos.{{Ref|name=DD|{{Cite Book|Darkest Darkness}}}} They are associated with [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]],{{Ref|name=VoD}} though they can be seen serving other Princes,{{Ref|name=VoD}} such as [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]]{{Ref|name=EGT|{{Cite Book|The Improved Emperor's Guide to Tamriel/Dreams of Cyrodiil}}}}{{ref|name=ESOMalyg|[[ON:Lady Malygda|Lady Malygda]] and the [[ON:Lady of the Depths|Lady of the Depths]]'s allegiance to [[ON:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} and [[Lore:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]].{{ref|name=BSSpider|[[Battlespire:Spider Daedra|Spider Daedra]] dialogue in ''[[Battlespire:Battlespire|Battlespire]]''}} [[Lore:Wonshala Keriayn|Wonshala Keriayn]] commanded of the Spider Daedra during Dagon's invasion of the [[Lore:Battlespire|Battlespire]], and many Spider Daedra served her willingly.{{ref|name=BSSpider}}{{ref|[[Battlespire:Perthan Vorn|Perthan Vorn]]'s dialogue in ''[[Battlespire:Battlespire|Battlespire]]''}} Other Daedra such as [[Lore:Aureal|Aureal]]s may summon them to fight.{{ref|name=KCAC|{{Cite Book|Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion|ns_base=Books}} [[Books:Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion Characters and Summary #Summerset|Summerset: Watcher]]}} Spider Daedra are capable of speech,{{ref|name=ESOAnexiel|[[ON:Anexiel|Anexiel]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} and some are willing to engage in relatively peaceable conversation with mortals.{{ref|name=ESONaluroen|[[ON:Naluroen|Naluroen]]'s dialogue during [[ON:Crawling Chaos|Crawling Chaos]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} 

'''Spiderlings''' are smaller versions of the Spider Daedra.{{Ref|name=Spiderling1|[[ON:Spiderling|Spiderling]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=Spiderling2|[[Oblivion:Daedra#Spiderling|Spiderling]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}{{Ref|name=Spiderling3|[[Legends:Spiderling|Spiderling]] in ''[[Legends:Legends|Legends]]''}} Despite some Spider Daedra considering eggs and [[LO:Sex|reproduction]] "boring mortal stuff",{{ref|name=SpDD|[[Battlespire:Spider Daedra|Spider Daedra]] dialogue in ''[[BS:Battlespire|Battlespire]]''}} it appears that Spider Daedra are capable of copulation{{ref|name=BSPV|[[Battlespire:Perthan_Vorn#Reply_1|Perthan Vorn]]'s dialogue in ''[[Battlespire:Battlespire|Battlespire]]''}} and laying eggs, with Spiderlings being their hatchlings.{{ref|[[ON:Hatchling|Hatchling]] behavior in ''[[ESO:ESO|ESO]]''}}

==Behavior==
[[File:ON-load-Spindleclutch.jpg|Spider Daedra|right|thumb]]{{Quote Box|style=float:none; max-width:none|''Nah. Got to leave the [[Lore:Shrimp|shrimp]] and [[Lore:Potato|potatoes]] alone. Only one thing on the menu tonight, and that's [[Lore:Man|MAN]].''|A Spider Daedra taunting the [[Lore:Apprentice|Apprentice]]{{ref|name=SpDD}} (listen <ab>BS-audio-SpiderDaedra Shrimp and Potatoes.wav</ab>)}}
They are so fierce, unruly and irrational that not even Mephala's worshipers can trust them, as they may not heed the Spinner's commands.{{Ref|name=DD}} Due to their disobedient and willful reputation, many sorcerers in [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]] are unwilling to summon them.{{Ref|name=DD}} They are unstable, oversexed, overstimulated, excitable, high strung, and keeping them focused and on-topic in conversation is a difficult task.{{Ref|name=BSWeb|Spider Daedra description from [[General:Elderscrolls.com Archive/Battlespire: Monsters|Elderscrolls.com Archive/Battlespire: Monsters]]}} Mortals view them as hateful, loathsome, and lacking compassion, though befriending and jovial in conversation, and when lost in their realms of Oblivion find no other option but to come to them for help, with their aid suspected to be part of a larger deception on behalf of their princes.{{Ref|name=EGT}} They are some of the more powerful Daedra, with formidable melee and spellcasting abilities. They can spin webs, spit poison and cast powerful storm spells.{{ref|name=ESOAbility|[[ON:Spider Daedra|Spider Daedra]] abilities in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} They can summon smaller Spider Daedra known as spiderlings to aid them in battle.{{ref|name=ESOAbility}}{{Ref|name=Spiderling3}}

They weave cocoons to ensnare mortals,{{ref|name=ESODepths|The [[ON:Lady of the Depths|Lady of the Depths]]' abilities in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} which are so resilient that they can only be broken from the outside.{{ref|name=ESORuzozuAbility|[[ON:Ruzozuzalpamaz|Ruzozuzalpamaz]]'s abilities in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} They are known to keep mortals as "ornaments" in their collections, ensorcelled into being thralls for their comfort and entertainment, as well as to provide sustenance to their spider "children".{{ref|name=ESONaluroen}}
