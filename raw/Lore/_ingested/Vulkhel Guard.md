'''Vulkhel Guard''', also known as '''Potansa''',{{ref|name=FOA|{{Cite Book|Fragment: On Artaeum}}}}{{intnote|nb1|[nb 1]}} is a large port town located in the southern end of [[Lore:Auridon|Auridon]], in the region [[Lore:Iluvamir|Iluvamir]], southeast of [[Lore:Silsailen|Silsailen]].
{{NewLeft}}
==Layout & Geography==
[[File:ON-place-South Beacon 03.jpg|thumb|right|South Beacon Lighthouse]]

The surrounding nature, like the rest of the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Isles]], is a place of harmony and ''"from the tallest tree, to the shortest blade of grass"'', is in perfect balance by the local [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]].{{ref|name=SIAVG|{{Cite Book|Summerset Isles: A Visitor's Guide}}}} Crystal blue waters surrounds Vulkhel Guard,{{ref|name=AuExI|{{Cite Book|Auridon Explored, Chapter I}}}} and just east out of town, the beaches along the coast are some of the most beautiful and serene places one can find in all of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]].{{ref|name=Oinara|[[ON:Oinara|Oinara]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The seawalls are built on tombolos on the ends of the bay and a barrier island found on the south end. The beaches around Vulkhel Guard have a lot of [[Lore:Mudcrab|mudcrabs]], which can do a great work for making a body disappear, so they are well-appreciated by the [[Lore:Dark Brotherhood|Dark Brotherhood]].{{ref|name=DarkBrotherhood|[[ON:Dark Brotherhood Contracts#Contracts in the Aldmeri Dominion zones|Dark Brotherhood Contracts in Auridon]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} There are a few coves along the coast near Vulkhel, where locals collect clams and other shellfishes.{{ref|name=Smallpaws|[[ON:Smallpaws|Smallpaws]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Akkuz|[[ON:Akkuz the Beachwalker|Akkuz the Beachwalker]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} It was neighbored by several settlements, one of them being [[Lore:Silsailen Point|Silsailen Point]] in the north.{{Ref|name=MapArena|[[:File:AR-map-Summurset Isle towns.png|Map of Summurset Isle – ''The Elder Scrolls: Arena'']]}}

The {{Lore Link|South Beacon}} Lighthouse is located to the north of Vulkhel Guard, a charming structure that guides the {{Lore Link|Summerset Royal Navy}} and other ships safely through the night. The cliff that it is built upon provides a lovely view of the holy [[Lore:Summerset Isle|isle of Summerset]]. On a particularly clear day, one can see the town of [[Lore:Shimmerene|Shimmerene]] from its top. {{ref|name=SIAVG}} Although partially destroyed in {{Year|2E 582}}, it was rebuilt the following year.{{ref|name=SerBea|[[Online:The Serpent's Beacon|The Serpent's Beacon]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=SIAVG}}

The mysterious island of [[Lore:Dranil Kir|Dranil Kir]], once occupied by the [[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic Order]], is located just off the coast of Vulkhel Guard. Unnatural storms are common around the waters of the islands, so sailors avoid it.{{ref|name=Dranil|[[Online:Dranil Kir|Dranil Kir]]'s description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Knives|[[Online:Of Knives and Long Shadows|Of Knives and Long Shadows]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

==History==

[[File:ON-interior-Vulkhel Guard Outlaws Refuge.jpg|thumb|left|Aldmeri ruins under the town]]

Few mainlanders had set foot in the Summerset Isles, and Vulkhel Guard have always been the most cosmopolitan port in the Isles since the early [[Lore:First Era|First Era]], when it was probably founded.{{ref|name=SIAVG}} Even during the few years when Summerset Isle was open to commerce and travel by royal decree of [[Lore:Ayrenn|Queen Ayrenn]] in {{Year|2E 582}}, Vulkhel maintained its status as a prime seaport.{{ref|name=AETI|{{Cite Book|An End to Isolation}}}}

[[File:ON-place-Lluvamir Dolmen.jpg|thumb|left|Iluvamir Dolmen]]

During the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]], just outside the town, a Dark Anchor was summoned at the Iluvamir Dolmen by the [[Lore:Worm Cult|Worm Cult]] but was destroyed by the [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]].

After the formation of the [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|First Aldmeri Dominion]] and with the start of the hostilities in the mainland, the town received even more visitors from [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]] and [[Lore:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]], some of them, immigrants who wanted or needed to establish themselves in Auridon.{{ref|name=Mandor|[[ON:Mandor|Mandor]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Many came to work on the fisheries and to collect clams and other shellfishes along the bountiful coasts of Vulkhel.{{ref|name=Smallpaws}}{{ref|name=Thaeliwaen|[[ON:Thaeliwaen|Thaeliwaen]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Belane|[[ON:Belane|Belane]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} However, not even the {{Lore Link|Three Banners Wars}} restricted civilian travel from Vulkhel Guard to the territories of the [[Lore:Ebonheart Pact|Pact]] or the [[Lore:Daggerfall Covenant|Covenant]].{{ref|name=Selandilwen|[[ON:Selandilwen|Selandilwen]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} And, with new allies, Vulkhel Guard strengthened their trade routes to Valenwood,{{ref|name=Erronfaire|[[Online:Captain Erronfaire|Captain Erronfaire]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} exporting local seafood and fish, their main products, heavily valued by the Bosmer;{{ref|name=Riian|[[ON:Riian (Skywatch)|Riian]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} but also meat from the farms of Greenwater Cove and steel, weapons and armors from [[Lore:Mathiisen|Mathiisen]];{{ref|name=Valiano|[[ON:Valiano|Valiano]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Angren|[[ON:Angren|Angren]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} and importing Bosmer crafts, seen as primitive art by the Altmer, but also mostly valued.{{ref|name=Vesteldore|[[ON:Vesteldore|Vesteldore]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} However, with the Dominion, came the [[Lore:Thalmor|Thalmor]], who began to threaten harsh punishment toward their detractors.{{ref|name=DarkBrotherhood}}

Vulkhel Guard was the first town Queen Ayrenn visited on her tour through Auridon after the formation of the First Aldmeri Dominion. Most of the citizens were excited about the event, their administrators and their servants were very busy, while the atmosphere turned festive.{{ref|name=Ninwin|[[ON:Ninwin|Ninwin]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Canonreeve|[[ON:Canonreeve Nesaranwe|Canonreeve Nesaranwe]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Egelnor|[[ON:Egelnor|Egelnor]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Due to this, security tasked to the {{Lore Link|First Auridon Marines}} was hardened. The Watch Captain of Vulkhel Guard, Astanya, was tasked with most of the new duties. However, she and most of the town watch were plotting with the [[Lore:Veiled Heritance|Veiled Heritance]] to try to murder Queen Ayrenn during her public appearance. The [[Lore:Eyes of the Queen|Eyes of the Queen]], lead by [[Lore:Razum-dar|Razum-dar]], finally foiled Heritance's plans and Astanya was slain in the Temple of Auri-El by the Vestige as she did not surrender. The Vestige became an Eye of the Queen thereafter, a title bestowed by Queen Ayrenn herself.{{ref|name=Security|[[ON:Ensuring Security|Ensuring Security]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Hostile|[[ON:A Hostile Situation|A Hostile Situation]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} When local criminals, organized under the name of the ''Shadow Artisans'', noticed that the Veiled Heritance was engaging in thievery and the fencing of stolen goods in Vulkhel Guard, refrained from any and all dealings with this group, as drawing the attention of the Eyes of The Queen upon their own operations would have been foolish and unnecessarily risky. So, their business was not welcome in the town, and the presence of their operatives were not tolerated.{{ref|name=VHBW|[[Online:Veiled Heritance - Be Warned|Veiled Heritance - Be Warned]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Parallel, agents from the [[Lore:Estre|High Kinlady Estre]], the Veiled Queen, occupied Del's Claim tunnels just off Vulkhel Guard, the richest mine in all of Auridon. However, their agents were eliminated by the Vestige.{{ref|name=Report|[[Online:Del's Claim Report|Del's Claim Report]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

[[File:ON-place-South Beacon 02.jpg|thumb|right|Failed Maormer invasion in 2E 582]]

Although most during the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]] thought that Vulkhel Guard was well defended against attacks from the Maormer,{{ref|name=Erronfaire}} it faced a failed invasion by Admiral Heculoa with the Veiled Heritance's support. They set up a camp in the beaches west of Vulkhel Guard and held First Auridon Marines hostage at South Beacon, a resource, which they were going to use to blackmail Queen Ayrenn when she arrived in town.{{ref|name=Memo|[[Online:Maormer Memo|Maormer Memo]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Invasion|[[Online:Maormer Invasion Camp|Maormer Invasion Camp]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Once they took the lighthouse, they began signaling more ships to come to have the numbers they needed to move on and invade Vulkhel Guard. The Vestige, with Marines' support, rescued the hostages, dismantled the invasion and destroyed the top of the lighthouse to stop signaling.{{ref|name=SerBea}}

After the Daedric invasions of both Mehrunes Dagon and Molag Bal, and the subsequent destruction of most of [[Lore:Firsthold|Firsthold]] in {{Year|2E 582}},{{ref|name=FirstholdMD|[[Online:Sever All Ties|Sever All Ties]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=FirstholdMB|[[Online:Dark Anchors in Tamriel|Dark Anchors in Tamriel]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} certain reference works were lost from the great library, so {{Lore Link|Kinlord Rilis XIII}} asked the leading experts of Auridon to replace them with new accounts, and the ''Armorsmith Peerless'' of Vulkhel Guard was intended with the definitive description of the classical Altmeri [[Lore:Malachite|Glass]] crafting style, proof, of the great skill of its smiths.{{ref|name=Craft16|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 16: Glass Style}}}}

By the time of the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]], Vulkhel Guard was still a vibrant town, being under the rule of Duke Mithra. It had a rivalry with {{Lore Link|West Guard}}.{{ref|name=Arena|Events of ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}} However, by the [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]], it appears that Firsthold had replaced Vulkhel Guard in its role as main trading port with the mainland.{{ref|name=EastEmpireShippingMap|[[:File:SR-icon-misc-EastEmpireShippingMap.png|East Empire Shipping Map]]}}

==Culture==
[[File:ON-place-Vulkhel Docks.jpg|thumb|right|Vulkhel Guard Docks]][[File:ON-place-Temple of Auri-El.jpg|thumb|right|Temple of Auri-El]]

As the least restricted seaport in the Summerset Isles,{{ref|name=MaraKiss|[[Online:Mara's Kiss Inn Room|Mara's Kiss Inn Room]]'s description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} both mainlanders merchants and immigrants commonly enter Auridon through this port town. Vulkhel Guard's townsfolk are well accustomed to dealing with traders, travelers and immigrants. The innkeepers are known to be polite guests and to serve local fare ''"with no equal in Tamriel"''. Despite this hospitality, travelers are recommended not to wander too far from the town limits. Vulkhel Guard also serve as the main link of Summerset Isle with the rest of Tamriel through the port city of Shimmerene, ferrying goods and citizens to and from Auridon. Even in this open port town, any stay longer than five days must be approved by the Auridon authorities or be faced deportation.{{ref|name=SIAVG}} Trade feeds Vulkhel Guard "like rain feeds a tree", so it is regarded as the main economic activity of the town, being the seaport with most loaded cargo in the Summerset Isles.{{ref|name=Scribe Yannod|[[ON:Scribe Yannod|Scribe Yannod]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Vulkhel Guard also serves as a hub for smugglers between Auridon and the mainland, mostly Elsweyr, because of the laxity of their cargo inspectors,{{ref|name=Sugar-Claws|[[ON:Sugar-Claws|Sugar-Claws]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} who are also rumored to be corrupt and steal part of the tariffs.{{ref|name=Riian}}

Although on the surface, the town seems friendly towards foreigners, some local Altmer, mostly of noble descent, still despise other races' presence in Auridon, especially during the formation of the First Aldmeri Dominion, when many Bosmer and Khajiit came to live and trade in Vulkhel Guard.{{ref|name=Ocando|[[ON:Ocando|Ocando]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Yarmondur|[[ON:Yarmondur|Yarmondur]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} They would usually bully and insult the impassive immigrants,{{ref|name=Razigad|[[ON:Razigad|Razigad]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} and were even known to utilize the Dark Brotherhood to kill some of them.{{ref|name=DarkBrotherhood}}

Fishing is the second most important economic activity in Vulkhel Guard and exports many of their catches to Valenwood.{{ref|name=Riian}}{{ref|name=Ode|{{Cite Book|Ode to Auridon}}}} Many Bosmer and Khajiit work as fishers in the docks and ships of the town.{{ref|name=Smallpaws}}{{ref|name=Thaeliwaen}}{{ref|name=Belane}}

Just outside Vulkhel Guard, a local by the name of Del found a huge deposit of lapis lazuli up in the hills, which turned to be one of the most profitable mines on Auridon.{{ref|name=Del|[[ON:Del's Claim|Del's Claim]]'s description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Vulkhel Guard smithers, lead by their ''Armorsmith Peerless'', are experts on the crafting of the traditional Glass armors and weapons of the Altmer, second only in expertise to the ones in Firsthold, where the technique originated.{{ref|name=Craft16}}

Painters of both portraitures and landscapes from Vulkhel Guard are also most appreciated in the Summerset Isles and beyond. They are lead by their ''Master of Portraiture'', a very prestigious position among the wealthy and nobles, who invite him or her to attend all sorts of dinner parties and galas.{{ref|name=Ulymen|[[ON:Ulymen|Ulymen]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Curwe|[[ON:Curwe|Curwe]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Most of the buildings in Vulkhel Guard are made from marble and have tall spires that dominate the skyline of the town.{{ref|name=Ode}}

Before the dissolution of the [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] following the [[Lore:Oblivion Crisis|Oblivion Crisis]], Vulkhel Guard had a large Guildhall, comparable to those in [[Lore:Skywatch|Skywatch]] and Firsthold.{{ref|name=MagesG|[[Online:Mages Guild (Vulkhel Guard)|Mages Guild (Vulkhel Guard)]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The Fighters Guild had an important presence in the town by the mid-Second Era too, however it is unknown if the organization survived after the formation of the Third Aldmeri Dominion.{{ref|name=FightersG|[[Online:Fighters Guild (Vulkhel Guard)|Fighters Guild (Vulkhel Guard)]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

The Temple of [[Lore:Auri-El|Auri-El]] in Vulkhel Guard was the largest and most important of the Summerset Isles by the time of the formation of First Aldmeri Dominion consecrated to the chief-god of the [[Lore:Eight Divines|Altmeri Eight]], and it was the place where his High Priest resided.{{ref|name=Temple|[[Online:Temple of Auri-El|Temple of Auri-El]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Uurkar|[[Online:Uurkar of Auri-El|Uurkar of Auri-El]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Acolyte|[[Online:Temple Acolyte (Vulkhel Guard)|Temple Acolytes]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The temple and its surroundings are regarded as one of the most beautiful spots in all of Auridon.{{ref|name=Curwe}}
