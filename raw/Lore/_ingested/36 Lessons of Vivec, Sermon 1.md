He was born in the ash among the {{Lore Link|Veloth}}i, anon {{Lore Link|Chimer}}, before {{Lore Link|Skyrim Conquests|the war with the northern men}}. {{Lore Link|Almalexia|Ayem}} came first to the village of the netchimen, and her shadow was that of {{Lore Link|Boethiah}}, who was the Prince of Plots, and things unknown and known would fold themselves around her until they were like [[Lore:Stars|stars]] or the messages of stars. {{nst|ON=<br>}}Ayem took a netchiman's wife and said:
:{{nst|ON="|'}}I am the Face-Snaked Queen of the [[Lore:Tribunal|Three in One]]. In you is an image and a seven-syllable spell, [[Lore:Almsivi|AYEM AE SEHTI AE VEHK]], which you will repeat to it until {{Lore Link|Sotha Sil|mystery|MWlink=Tribunal:Sotha Sil}} comes.{{nst|ON="|'}} 

Then Ayem threw the netchiman's wife into the ocean water where {{Lore Link|dreugh}}s took her into castles of {{Lore Link|glass}} and {{Lore Link|coral}}. They gifted the netchiman's wife with gills and milk fingers, changing her sex so that she might give birth to the image as an egg. There she stayed for seven or eight months. 

Then {{Lore Link|Sotha Sil|Seht}} came to the netchiman's wife and said: 
:{{nst|ON="|'}}I am the Clockwork King of the Three in One. In you is an egg of my brother-sister, who possesses invisible knowledge of words and swords, which you shall nurture until the [[Lore:Nerevar|Hortator]] comes.{{nst|ON="|'}} 

And Seht then extended his hands and multitudes of {{Lore Link|Fabricants|homunculi}} came forth, each like a glimmering rope through the water, and they raised the netchiman's wife back to the surface world and set her down on the shoals of {{Lore Link|Azura's Coast|Azura's coast}}. There she lay for seven or eight more months, caring for the egg-knowledge by whispering to it the Codes of {{Lore Link|Mephala}} and the prophecies of Veloth and even the forbidden teachings of {{Lore Link|Trinimac}}. 

Seven {{Lore Link|Daedra}} came to her one night and each one gave to the egg new motions that could be achieved by certain movements of the bones. These are called the {{Lore Link|Barons of Move Like This}}. Then an eighth Daedroth came, and he was a {{Lore Link|Demiprince}}, called {{Lore Link|Fa-Nuit-Hen}}, or the Multiplier of Motions Known. {{nst|ON=<br>}}And Fa-Nuit-Hen said: 
:{{nst|ON="|'}}Whom do you wait for?{{nst|ON="|'}} 

To which the netchiman's wife said the Hortator. 
:{{nst|ON="|'}}Go to the land of the {{Lore Link|House Indoril|Indoril}} in three months' time, for that is when war comes. I return now to haunt the warriors who fell and still wonder why. But first I show you this.{{nst|ON="|'}}

Then the Barons and the Demiprince joined together into a pillar of fighting styles terrible to behold and they danced before the egg and its learning image. 
:{{nst|ON="|'}}Look, little {{Lore Link|Vivec|Vehk|MWlink=Morrowind:Vivec (god)}}, and find the face behind the splendor of my bladed carriage, for in it is delivered the unmixed conflict path, perfect in every way. What is its number?{{nst|ON="|'}} 

It is said the number is the number of birds that can nest in an ancient {{Lore Link|tibrol}} tree, less three grams of honest work, but Vivec in his later years found a better one and so gave this secret to his people. 
:{{nst|ON="|'}}For I have crushed a world with my left hand,{{nst|ON="|'}} he will say, {{nst|ON="|'}}but in my right hand is how it could have won against me. Love is under my will only.{{nst|ON="|'}} 

The ending of the words is [[Lore:Almsivi|ALMSIVI]].
