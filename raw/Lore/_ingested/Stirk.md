[[Lore:Stirk|Stirk]] is an island off the [[Lore:Gold Coast|Gold Coast]] of [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]], in the [[Lore:Abecean Sea|Abecean Sea]].{{ref|[[:File:RG-map-West Tamriel-1024x768.png|Map of West Tamriel]]}} Initially settled by the [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]]s and legendary for being where the [[Lore:All Flags Navy|All Flags Navy]] was founded,{{ref|name=ESOLadyClarisseLaurent|[[ON:Lady Clarisse Laurent|Lady Clarisse Laurent]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} it has since been used by pirates for settling disputes through trial by combat.{{Ref|name=Load|[[Online:Loading Screens|ESO loading screens]]}} Though claimed at times by [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]], Cyrodiil, and [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]], the uninhabited island is really a no-man's-land.{{Ref|name=Load}} Its neutral status during the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]] made [[Lore:Vanus Galerion|Vanus Galerion]] choose it as a meeting ground for the leaders of the [[Lore:Three Banners War|Three Banners War]] to launch their attack on [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] during the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]], as organized by the guilds.{{ref|name=ESOGalerion|[[ON:Vanus Galerion|Vanus Galerion]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

==Geography==
The terrain of Stirk is a mix of shallow beaches on the northwest and southeast and rugged rocky hills in between, surrounded by jagged rocks. The coast is dotted with [[Lore:Palm Tree|palm trees]] and Ayleid docks, while the Stirk Interior{{ref|name=ESOGalerion}} is lightly forested by what appear to be [[Lore:Acacia|acacias]], along with other trees. The Portal Valley is found nestled in the mountains of the north-central part of the island. There are no known permanent settlements on the island.

==History==
The historic island was at one time colonized by the Ayleids, whose white limestone [[Lore:Ayleid Ruins|ruins]] dominate the tropical landscape. There are also dolmen-like [[Lore:Daedra|Daedric]] pillars found on the island,{{Ref|name=Dolmen|[[:File:ON-place-Stirk 02.jpg|Appearance of Daedric Pillars]] in [[Online:Stirk|ESO]]}} but it is unknown whether the Ayleids or some other civilization constructed them. In the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]], it was where the leaders of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] assembled the [[Lore:All Flags Navy|All Flags Navy]] to do battle with the [[Lore:Sload|Sload]].{{ref|name=ESOGalerion}} In the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], although the island was claimed by [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]], the Gold Coast, and [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]], it had little activity besides the pirates in the area, who used the Ayleid ruins as arenas for settling disputes through trial by combat.{{Ref|name=Load}}

In {{Year|2E 582}} a meeting took place here between the three leaders of the Three Banners War, as Stirk was considered neutral territory. The meeting was facilitated by the [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]] and [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]], and aimed to discuss a possible invasion of [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] in response to [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]]'s attempted [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]]. Before an agreement could be reached, Molag Bal sent [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]] to the island to kill the alliance leaders. The attackers were repelled, and the leaders quickly gave their consent for an invasion, having seen the Prince's influence firsthand. A force was then assembled from the ranks of the two guilds, and a portal to Coldharbour was opened on the island.{{ref|name=Online|[[Online:The Weight of Three Crowns|The Weight of Three Crowns]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

The island was considered by the [[Lore:Third Empire|Empire]] to be Cyrodiil's territory circa {{Year|3E 427}},{{Ref|name=MCMap|[[:File:LO-map-Tamriel (Morrowind Codex).png|Map of Tamriel]] – [[General:Elderscrolls.com Archive/Codex|Morrowind Codex]]}} but around {{Year|3E 433}}, the island appeared to be unaffiliated with any province on certain maps.{{Ref|name=OCMap|[[:File:LO-map-Tamriel (Oblivion Codex).gif|Map of Tamriel]] – [[General:Elderscrolls.com Archive/Codex|Oblivion Codex]]}}

==Culture==
Stirk Pork-and-Beets is a gourmet dish named after the island. It is a type of stew containing [[Lore:Pig|pork]], beets, herbs, and bananas.{{ref|[[Online:Stirk Pork-and-Beets|Stirk Pork-and-Beets]] in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}
