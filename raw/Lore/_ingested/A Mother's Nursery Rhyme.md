Do you have five children, {{Lore Link|Night Mother|Mother}}?<br>
I've heard that you do.<br>
Five children? No, tonight I have four!<br>
Four children, sweet and pure.<br>
Four and no more!


Do you have four children, Mother?<br>
I've heard that you do.<br>
Four children? No, tonight I have three!<br>
Three children abed late today.<br>
Three and no more!


Do you have three children, Mother?<br>
I've heard that you do.<br>
Three children? No, tonight I have two!<br>
Two children, quiet and shy.<br>
Two and no more!


Do you have two children, Mother?<br>
I've heard that you do.<br>
Two children? No, tonight I have one!<br>
One child, singing a song.<br>
One and no more!


Do you have one child, Mother?<br>
I've heard that you do.<br>
One child? Please, I have none!<br>
They're with [[Lore:Sithis|their father]] now<br>
And live here no more.
{{Book End}}
