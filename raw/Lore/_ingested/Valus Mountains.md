The [[Lore:Valus Mountains|Valus Mountains]] are a mountain range on the border between [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] and [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]]. They converge with the [[Lore:Jerall Mountains|Jerall]] and [[Lore:Velothi Mountains|Velothi]] ranges at [[Lore:Mount Trolhetta|Mount Trolhetta]].{{Ref|name=OBMap|[[:File:OB-map-Cyrodiil.jpg|Map of Cyrodiil – ''The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion'']]}}{{Ref|name=ONTrolhetta|[[Online:Trolhetta|Trolhetta]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} They are sometimes considered an extension of the [[Lore:Velothi Mountains|Velothi Mountains]].{{ref|name=PGE1M|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Morrowind}}}} [[Lore:Goblin|Goblins]] of the region often travel between the provinces through secret passageways and caves under the mountains. Because of this, cultural exchanges have taken place between tribes ever since.{{Ref|name=ONLoadMuck|[[Online:Muck Valley Cavern|Muck Valley Cavern]] loading screen text in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The [[Lore:Kavas Rim Pass|Kavas Rim Pass]] is a road through the provincial border.{{Ref|name=2920v5|{{Cite Book|2920, Second Seed (v5)}}}}<noinclude>
{{NewLeft}}

==Cyrodiil==
The {{Lore Link|Hero Hill}}, a secret shrine to [[Lore:Boethiah|Boethiah]], and the ruins of {{Lore Link|Fort Scinia}} are some of the many landmarks in the region. The mountains are home to several [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]] ruins, such as [[Lore:Fanacas|Fanacas]] and [[Lore:Kemen|Kemen]]. The city of [[Lore:Cheydinhal|Cheydinhal]] is located on the foothills.{{ref|name=OB|Events of ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

During the [[Lore:Alliance War|Alliance War]] in {{Year|2E 582}}, one of the two bases of the [[Lore:Ebonheart Pact|Ebonheart Pact]] was located in the Valus Mountains, along with the {{Lore Link|Scroll Temple of Ghartok}}.{{ref|name=ONC|Appearance of [[ON:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} In {{Year|3E 433}}, a rogue [[Lore:Telvanni|Telvanni]] wizard [[Lore:Frathen Drothan|Frathen Drothan]] crossed the Valus Mountains from [[Lore:Kragenmoor|Kragenmoor]] in search for the lost Ayleid city of [[Lore:Varsa Baalim|Varsa Baalim]], hidden beneath the ruins of [[Lore:Sundercliff Watch|Sundercliff Watch]].{{ref|name=OBMR|Events of [[Oblivion:Mehrunes' Razor|''Mehrunes' Razor'']] official plug-in for ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} Circa {{Year |4E 48}}, a village called {{Lore Link|Mountain Watch}} existed in the Valus Mountains. It was evacuated when the undead forces of [[Lore:Umbriel|Umbriel]] crossed the mountains and invaded Cyrodiil.{{ref|name=LoS|{{Cite Book|Lord of Souls}}}} Clawhorn Mountain [[Lore:Goat|Goats]] are native to the region.{{ref|name=CMG|[[ON:Clawhorn Mountain Goat|Clawhorn Mountain Goat]] description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

==Morrowind==
The Valus Mountains form the western border of two regions of Morrowind, [[Lore:Deshaan|Deshaan]] and [[Lore:Stonefalls|Stonefalls]]. The cities of Kragenmoor and [[Lore:Narsis|Narsis]] are located on the foothills.{{ref|name=ONDS|Appearance of [[ON:Deshaan|Deshaan]] and [[ON:Stonefalls|Stonefalls]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
