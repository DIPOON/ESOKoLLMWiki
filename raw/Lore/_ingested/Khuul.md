[[Lore:Khuul|Khuul]] was a small fishing village in the [[Lore:West Gash|West Gash]] region of [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]], on the island's northern coast. Aside from a single [[Lore:House Redoran|Redoran]]-style shell-house, which served as a Tradehouse, the buildings were all rustic huts made of logs, rough planks, and thatch. There was a boat to [[Lore:Solstheim|Solstheim]] in the village if one had any reason to go.{{Ref|name=MWDialogue|[[Morrowind:Khuul People|Khuul people]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}

The only other legitimate businesses near Khuul were two egg mines–the Panud Egg Mine, just outside town, and the Asha-Ahhe Egg Mine, far to the southwest. There was also a [[Lore:Silt Strider|silt strider]] port just south of town, by the Panud Egg Mine.

The date of Khuul's founding is uncertain. While an area known as Khuul was a stop for [[Lore:Silt Strider|Silt Strider]] caravaneers around {{Year|2E 582}},{{Ref|{{Cite Book|Silt Strider Caravaner's Log|ns_base=ON}}}} there was no settlement there besides the nearby [[Lore:Urshilaku Camp|Urshilaku Camp]].{{Ref|name=ESO|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}} Khuul had always been rumored to be a smuggling town, but the residents  never confirmed nor denied this. They were suspicious of strangers asking a lot of questions and didn't like people poking around.{{Ref|name=MWDialogue}}<noinclude>
