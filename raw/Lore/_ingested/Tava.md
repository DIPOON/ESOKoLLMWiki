# Lore:Tava

<noinclude>{{Lore Gods Trail}}[[File:ON-place-Tava's Beak.jpg|Rock carving of Tava|right|thumb]]</noinclude>
[[Lore:Tava|Tava]] (the '''Bird God''',{{ref|[[Online:Sai Sahan|Sai Sahan]]'s dialogue during [[Online:The Horn of Ja'darri|The Horn of Ja'darri]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=VOF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith...}}}} also known as the '''Lady of the Air''') is the spirit of the air and goddess of weather in the [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] pantheon, and has dominion over all elements of sun and storm, save only for the stars, which belong to [[Lore:Ruptga|Ruptga]].{{ref|{{Cite Book|The Unveiled Azadiyeh Answers Your Questions}}}} Tava is most famous for leading the Yokudans to the isle of [[Lore:Herne (island)|Herne]] after the destruction of their homeland. She has since become assimilated into the mythology of [[Lore:Kynareth|Kynareth]]. She is still very popular in [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]] among sailors, and her shrines can be found in most port cities.{{ref|name=VOF}} In the mid-[[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], the city of [[Lore:Lainlyn|Lainlyn]] was called "Tava's Blessing" before being renamed after the Lainlyn family.{{Ref|name=AUA|[http://www.elderscrollsonline.com/en-us/news/post/2013/09/23/ask-us-anything-lore Ask Us Anything: Lore]}}<noinclude>

The redtail [[Lore:Hawk|hawk]] is considered sacred to Tava, as is the goshawk.{{ref|name=ONWritingQuill|[[Online:Contraband R#Royal Redtail Hawk Writing Quill|Royal Redtail Hawk Writing Quill]] Item Description}}{{ref|name=TWAHD|{{Cite Book|The Worthy Ar-Azal, His Deeds}}}} Conversely, [[Lore:Harpy|harpies]] are seen as abominations by her faithful.{{ref|name=ONHarpyEgg|[[Online:Listens-to-Sea#Distracting Harpy Egg|Distracting Harpy Egg]] Item Description}}

==Worship==
The second [[Lore:Ra Gada#The Warrior Waves|Warrior Wave]], or "Tavan" wave, so-called because it was spearheaded by the [[Lore:Hattu Mountain|Hattu]] Metropoles brigade, which revered Tava greatly, had their arms and armor repleted with bird imagery.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 28: Ra Gada Style}}}}{{ref|name=Contraband R|[[ON:Contraband R#Ra Gada Helm Ornament|Ra Gada Helm Ornament]] item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:High Isle (Chapter)|High Isle]]''}}

One of [[Lore:Hegathe|Hegathe]]'s most iconic landmarks is the Shrine of Tava, a place of veneration for both the [[Lore:Crowns|Crowns]] and the [[Lore:Forebears|Forebears]]. It is highlighted by the ''Fresco of the [[Lore:Goshawk|Goshawk]]'', a mural depicting [[Lore:Tava|Tava]] nestled together with his mates, which was meant to symbolize the union of the two factions.{{Ref|name=TWAHD}}

Priests of Tava are known to bless astrolabes for Redguard high rank royal naval officers.{{ref|name=ContrabandB|[[ON:Contraband B#Blessed Astrolabe|Blessed Astrolabe]] item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
