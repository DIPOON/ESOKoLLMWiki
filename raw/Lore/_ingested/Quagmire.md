</noinclude>[[Lore:Quagmire|Quagmire]], or the '''Dreamstride''',{{ref|{{Cite Book|Dreamwalkers}}}} is a realm of [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]], created and ruled over by the [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Prince]] of Nightmare, [[Lore:Vaermina|Vaermina]]. It is a realm of horrors, where reality shifts upon itself in seemingly impossible ways. Every few minutes, lightning flashes and the realm morphs into a terrifying scene, each one more frightening than the last.{{ref|name=TDOO}} Mortal sleepers often slip into this realm without any help at all.{{ref|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Imperial Census of Daedra Lords|ns_base=GEN}}}}

From her citadel in the center of the realm, Vaermina reaches out into [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]] and collects the memories of sleeping mortals, leaving nothing but visions of horror and despair.{{ref|name=WN}}{{ref|name=Erandur|[[Skyrim:Erandur|Erandur]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} These experiences, known as nightmares, involve the mind of a mortal traveling to Quagmire. Through magical teleportation, it is possible for a mortal to enter Quagmire while awake, as was done by the mage [[Lore:Morian Zenas|Morian Zenas]].{{ref|name=TDOO|{{Cite Book|The Doors of Oblivion}}}} It is inhabited by [[Lore:Omen|Omens]], daedric servants of Vaermina, who sends them to haunt the inhabitants of [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]].{{Ref|name=TBOD|{{Cite Book|The Book of Daedra}}}}{{ref|name=TOD|{{Cite Book|The Omen of Deception}}}} Wasps inhabiting the realm weave their nests from nightmares.{{Ref|name=DW|[[ON:Dreamharrow Wasp|Dreamharrow Wasp]]'s pet descripion in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

==History==
A cult of wealthy Daedra worshippers were once rewarded by Vaermina with a small pocket of Quagmire. Using a mining operation at {{Lore Link|Hozzin's Folly}} on [[Lore:Bleakrock Isle|Bleakrock Isle]] as cover, the family successfully created a portal to the realm and consecrated an "Unspeakable Sigil" within, seemingly to maintain the pocket. Work began on constructing a temple to Vaermina within the realm, but the pocket remained much smaller than the worshippers had expected and [[Lore:Scamp|scamp]]s began to appear both inside the realm and within the mine on [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]].{{ref|{{Cite Book|Dark Ministrations}}}} After a disastrous ritual, Hozzin's Folly was left abandoned, and the pocket realm was destroyed in {{Year|2E 582}} when the portal was reopened and the sigil smashed.{{ref|name=ESOHF|Events of [[Online:Hozzin's Folly (quest)|Hozzin's Folly]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}  

Quagmire and Mundus have been known to partially merge where Vaermina's influence is strong, usually in the area surrounding one of [[Lore:Vaermina#Artifacts|her artifacts]]. The island of [[Lore:Skald's Retreat|Skald's Retreat]] briefly merged with the realm in 2E 582, sending its inhabitants into a deep sleep.{{ref|name=ESORIFT|Events of [[Online:Guard the Knowledge|Guard the Knowledge]] and [[Online:Song of Awakening|Song and Awakening]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

In {{Year|3E 433}}, the [[Lore:Orb of Vaermina|Orb of Vaermina]] was stolen by the wizard {{Lore Link|Arkved}}, who brought it to his tower in the [[Lore:Nibenay Basin|Nibenay Basin]]. The interior of the tower seemed to enter Quagmire, with reality becoming warped inside.{{ref|name=OBVaermina|Events of [[Oblivion:Vaermina|Vaermina]] in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

In {{Year|4E 201}}, the town of [[Lore:Dawnstar|Dawnstar]] was plagued by continuous nightmares due to the close proximity of the [[Lore:Skull of Corruption|Skull of Corruption]].{{ref|name=WN|Events of [[Skyrim:Waking Nightmare|Waking Nightmare]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}
