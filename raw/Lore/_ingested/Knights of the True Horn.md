The [[Lore:Knights of the True Horn|Knights of the True Horn]] (also known as the '''Host of the True Horn'''){{Ref|name=DFHost|[[Daggerfall:Host of the True Horn|Host of the True Horn]] faction in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} was a [[Lore:Knightly Orders|knightly order]] founded by Lord [[Lore:Kain|Kain]] of [[Lore:Lainlyn (barony)|Lainlyn]] and Lord [[Lore:Harth|Harth]] of the [[Lore:Dragontail Mountains|Dragontail Mountains]]. The knights are followers of Lord Kain, who sought to end the tyranny of his older brother, Baron [[Lore:Shrike|Shrike]] and claim the throne for his own.{{Ref|name=LKW|{{Cite Book|Lord Kelvyn's Will}}}}<noinclude>
{{NewLeft}}

==History==
Baron Shrike ruled over Lainlyn during the late-[[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], around the early years of the fifth century. Lord Kain founded the knighthood with fellow nobleman Lord Harth sometime around {{Year|3E 405}}, and would slowly gather steam from across the [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]] in [[Lore:High Rock|High Rock]],{{Ref|name=TDC|{{Cite Book|The Daggerfall Chronicles|ns_base=BK}}}}{{rp|100}} specifically somewhere in the Barony of [[Lore:Phrygias|Phrygias]].{{Ref|name=DFKain|[[Daggerfall:Lord Kain|Lord Kain]] NPC in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} At this point in time, they had no permanent home and were not accepting recruits; however, they were known to employ mercenaries into their ranks. While some of these knights believed in Kain's cause,{{Ref|name=LJJ|{{Cite Book|Lord Jaren's Journal}}}} some realized that they would not win the war in their lifetime, but hoped that their children would take on their quest. Their rival faction, the [[Lore:Host of the Horn|Host of the Horn]] would mock the True Horn among themselves.{{Ref|name=TDC}}{{rp|100}}

[[File:OB-place-Frostfire Glade Statue.jpg|thumb|left|Garridan's Fate]]
[[File:OB-creature-Lord Kain.jpg|thumb|right|The undead Lord Kain]]
Eventually, the Knights of the True Horn gathered their arms and staged a coup d'etat on Baron Shrike. Kain fought with Baron Shrike. After a failed battle, Lord Kain ordered them to split into small groups across the realms so that they may regroup one day. However, not long after the battle, Lord Kain succumbed to his injuries and passed away. It is unknown where each of the groups went, but one traveled southeast into the province of [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]], where they would build [[Lore:Battlehorn Castle|Battlehorn Castle]], an impressive stronghold outside of [[Lore:Chorrol|Chorrol]].{{Ref|name=LKW}}

Over time, the order continued on with their business, often making trips to the nearby city. One night, Lord Kain arrived in Battlehorn Castle with an infamous necromancer from Lainlyn, {{Lore Link|Arielle Jurard}}. At the time, it was only Lord {{Lore Link|Jaren Aethelweald}} and Lord [[Lore:Garridan Stalrous|Garridan Stalrous]] in the castle, and when they learned about Kain's true fate and Arielle's scheme, they decided to seal them both in the grotto underneath the castle, never to speak of it again.{{Ref|name=LJJ}}

Over time, members of the Knights of the True Horn had either left or passed away. Garridan Stalrous had became the Knight-Errant of {{Lore Link|Farmantle Glens}}, a farming community in the [[Lore:Jerall Mountains|Jerall Mountains]]. When he noticed the withered fields, he spent all of his money to help the crops and gather wisdom from sages, but his month-long effort was fruitless. One day, a sage told him about the [[Lore:Everflow Ewer|Everflow Ewer]], an artifact that provides an endless source of water. He and Jaren traveled for days until they came across the {{Lore Link|Frostfire Glade|ewer sanctuary}}, and found it in the middle of the glade. When they touched the ewer, the [[Lore:Frost Atronach|guardians of the glade]] emerged and attacked the knights. Unable to fight back, the guardian broke the ewer and spilled frozen water everywhere, even on Lord Garridan. Everything around them froze over, and Garridan cried as he realized he failed his mission. Lord Jaren escaped back to Farmantle Glens to break the news, but found that a strange bluish-glowing rain had fallen in town and invigorated the crops. [[Lore:Garridan's Tears|Garridan's Tears]] froze as well, and emitted the same bluish glow found on the rain that saved the town.{{Ref|name=KF|{{Cite Book|Knightfall}}}}

By the waning years of the Third Era, Lord Jaren, his son Kelvyn, and some men-at-arms were the last members of the True Horn. Jaren eventually passed away in an untimely fashion and Lord Kelvyn became the sole proprietor of Battlehorn Castle. He was still waiting for Lord Kain's message, after all those years, without realizing the truth. He spent much of his life maintaining the castle and honoring the memory of his comrades{{Ref|name=LKW}} until in {{Year|3E 433}}, when bandit marauders attacked the castle. Kelvyn died in battle, but the marauders were defeated by a [[Lore:Hero of Kvatch|traveler]], who became the new owner of Battlehorn Castle. With help from Kelvyn's friend, {{Lore Link|Nilphas Omellian}}, the adventurer was able to restore Battlehorn Castle.{{Ref|name=OBBattlehorn|[[Oblivion:Battlehorn Castle (quest)|Battlehorn Castle]] quest in [[Oblivion:Fighter's Stronghold|Fighter's Stronghold]]}} The adventurer also discovered the grotto underneath the castle and defeated Arielle Jurard.{{Ref|name=OBUnwanted|[[Oblivion:Unwanted Tenants|Unwanted Tenants]] quest in [[Oblivion:Fighter's Stronghold|Fighter's Stronghold]]}}

==Known Members==
[[File:DF-npc-Lord Harth (face).png|thumb|right|Lord Harth of the Dragontails]]
*[[Lore:Kain|Kain]]: Co-Founder of the True Horn and noble of Lainlyn. 
*[[Lore:Harth|Harth]]: Co-Founder of the True Horn and noble of the Dragontail Mountains. His intentions of overthrowing the Baron are personal. 
*{{Lore Link|Arielle Jurard}}: an infamous necromancer of Lainlyn 
*[[Lore:Garridan Stalrous|Garridan Stalrous]]: One of the members that founded Battlehorn Castle. He later became the Knight-Errant of Farmantle Glens before he froze in Frostfire Glade.
*{{Lore Link|Jaren Aethelweald}}: One of the members that founded Battlehorn Castle. He was the last member of the original knighthood that lived in the castle, before his untimely death.
*{{Lore Link|Kelvyn Aethelweald}}: The son of Lord Jaren and last known member of the knightly order. He was the sole proprietor of Battlehorn Castle before it was sacked by marauders.
