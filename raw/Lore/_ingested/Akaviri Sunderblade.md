The [[Lore:Akaviri Sunderblade|Akaviri Sunderblade]], also shortened to just '''Sunderblade''' is a legendary katana that belonged to the first leader of the [[Lore:Akaviri|Akaviri]] [[Lore:Dragonguard|Dragonguard]], [[Lore:Ghelin-Brol|Ghelin-Brol]]{{ref|name=GGB}} who were sworn to the protection of the [[Lore:Tamrielic Emperors|Emperor]] [[Lore:Reman Cyrodiil|Reman Cyrodiil]].{{ref|name=Sunderblade|[[Blades:Sunderblade|Sunderblade]]'s description in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}} It was known for its potent enchantment that could disintegrate weapons and armors of the foes of the wielder.{{ref|name=OBL|[[Oblivion:Unique Items#Akavari Sunderblade|Akaviri Sunderblade]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} Numerous superb katanas were fashioned to look like the legendary Sunderblade and were used for centuries by high-ranking [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] [[Lore:Blades|Blades]] before the order was disbanded.{{ref|name=AB|[[Blades:Akaviri Blade|Akaviri Blade]]'s description in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}}

As of {{Year|3E 433}} the legendary blade was available for purchase in the Three Brothers Trade Goods shop in the [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]] from Imperial merchant Sergius Verus. It is unknown whether or not [[Lore:Hero of Kvatch|Hero of Kvatch]] purchased the blade or not, however.{{ref|name=SV}}
