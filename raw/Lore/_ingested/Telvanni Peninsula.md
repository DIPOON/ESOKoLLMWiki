The [[Lore:Telvanni Peninsula|Telvanni Peninsula]] was a region in the eastern part of mainland {{nbsl|Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind}}, and is bracketed by the [[Lore:Inner Sea|Inner Sea]] to the west and the [[Lore:Padomaic Ocean|Padomaic Ocean]] to the east.{{ref|name=TSAI|{{Cite Book|The Second Akaviri Invasion}}}} It was composed of two regions: the fertile river valleys of the [[Lore:Fungal Lowlands|Fungal Lowlands]], and the rocky volcanic cliff coastlines of the [[Lore:Padomaic Crest|Padomaic Crest]].{{Ref|name=VG:TP|{{Cite Book|Visitor's Guide: Telvanni Peninsula}}}} The land south of the region is made up of the coastal [[Lore:Bal Foyen|Bal Foyen]] and fertile [[Lore:Deshaan|Deshaan Plain]] areas,{{ref|name=PGE1M}} while the land north of it is made up of badlands and arid desert plateaus,{{Ref|name=ARTerrain}}{{ref|name=TPMorrowindMoonlight|group=UOL}} whose inhabitants wear Dust Adept clothing to adapt to its dry environment.{{Ref|name=TDA}}{{Ref|name=CA}} As the [[Lore:House Indoril|Indoril]] presently control the heartlands and entire east coast,{{Ref|name=GHoM}} and the [[Lore:House Telvanni|Telvanni]] lost all holdings on the mainland, it is unknown what it is now named or if the region still exists.{{ref|name=NelothDB}}<noinclude>

==Geography==
The Telvanni Peninsula borders [[Lore:Bal Foyen|Bal Foyen]] and [[Lore:Deshaan|Deshaan]] to the south. It shares the Inner Sea waters of [[Lore:Zafirbel Bay|Zafirbel Bay]] with Vvardenfell, separated by the Vvardenfell Rift.{{ref|name=RoKZ}}{{Ref|name=MWConceptMap|group=UOL}} The land north of Necrom is separated by a ridge and controlled by Indoril, with [[Lore:Firewatch|Firewatch]] being the largest city in the region. The northern extremities are very dry, while the southern reaches are more temperate.{{Ref|name=ARTerrain|[[:File:AR-map-Terrain.png|Arena terrain]] map}} The landscape consists of fertile plains in the east, and badlands in the west.{{Ref|name=MWConceptMap|group=UOL}} In particular, the north has arid desert plateaus, mesas, and high steppes.{{ref|name=TPMorrowindMoonlight|group=UOL|[[General:Ted Peterson|Ted Peterson]]'s posts in [[General:Loranna's RP#Campaign 10: Morrowind Moonlight|''Morrowind Moonlight'']]}} Inhabitants of the northern peninsula wear "Dust Adept" clothing to adapt to its dry environment.{{Ref|name=TDA|[[Morrowind:Telvanni Dust Adept Helm|Telvanni Dust Adept Helm]] in ''[[MW:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{Ref|name=CA|[[:File:MW-concept-Telvanni Dust Adept.jpg|Mainland holdings Dust Adept]]s art}} In the Padomaic Ocean, the [[Lore:Northeast Archipelago|isles]] surrounding the palace of [[Lore:Port Telvannis|Port Telvannis]]{{ref|name=LAHT|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive - House Telvanni}}}}{{Ref|name=MWMap|[[:File:LO-map-Morrowind (TES3).jpg|Map of Morrowind – ''The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind'']]}}{{Ref|name=TXGL|group=UOL|[[General:The Xal-Gosleigh Letters|The Xal-Gosleigh Letters]]}} are found across the [[Lore:Sea of Ghosts|Sea of Ghosts]] to the northeast.{{Ref|name=MorrowindMap|[[:File:LO-map-Morrowind (TES3).jpg|Map of Morrowind Codex]]}} The archipelago is a mix of rocky, rugged terrain and flat plains.{{Ref|name=MorrowindDialogue|[[Morrowind:Generic Dialogue G|Geography of Morrowind dialogue option]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{Ref|name=MWElone|[[Morrowind:Elone|Elone]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{ref|name=ConceptMap|group=UOL|[[:File:MW-map-Morrowind Concept.jpg|Concept map of Morrowind, released during the Tenth Anniversary of TES]]}} The island of [[Lore:Vounoura|Vounoura]] is found roughly a month's voyage away from Tel Aruhn,{{ref|name=2920ES|{{Cite Book|2920, Evening Star (v12)}}}} but it is unknown whether it is near the Peninsula. The peninsula is visible from [[Lore:Stonefalls|Stonefalls]] across the waters of the Inner Sea.{{Ref|name=ESO|Appearance in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

===Fungal Lowlands===
The [[Lore:Fungal Lowlands|Fungal Lowlands]] are a spacious tract of wilderness and river valleys making up the western half of the peninsula. It is composed of flatlands along the river, with a number of secluded clearings between the road and the coast.{{Ref|name=VG:TP}}

Its main settlement is [[Lore:Ald Isra|Ald Isra]], where the Mouths of the Telvanni Magisters convene. The [[Lore:Dwemer Ruins|Dwemer ruin]] of [[Lore:Kemel-Ze|Kemel-Ze]] is found along the coast, with easy access to it from boats and nearby mainland villages, such as {{Lore Link|Marog}}.{{ref|name=RoKZ|{{Cite Book|Ruins of Kemel-Ze}}}}

[[File:ON-place-Padomaic Crest.jpg|thumb|left|Padomaic Crest]]
===Padomaic Crest===
The [[Lore:Padomaic Crest|Padomaic Crest]] is a volcanic range making up the eastern half of the peninsula. The eastern end of the formation creates a secondary peninsula where the city of [[Lore:Necrom|Necrom]] lies.{{Ref|name=VG:TP}}

The eastern coast of Morrowind is a coastline of treacherous cliffs, especially in the region around Necrom.{{ref|name=RoKZ|{{Cite Book|Ruins of Kemel-Ze}}}}

The island of [[Lore:Gorne|Gorne]] is found north of Necrom, in the bay between the Padomaic Crest and the northern peninsula.{{ref|name=FOTN|{{Cite Book|Father of the Niben}}}}

==History==
In {{Year|3E 121}}, King [[Lore:Uriel III|Uriel Mantiarco]] of [[Lore:Solitude (kingdom)|Solitude]] coordinated a three-way attack. In his second attack against the Empire, Uriel's forces targeted multiple Imperial garrisons on coastal islands in Eastern Morrowind. While the [[Lore:Elder Council|Elder Council]] was occupied with the battle in Morrowind and with the first attack in the [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]], the King captured the [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]] and proclaimed himself Emperor Uriel Septim III. One of the Empire's casualties here was Empress [[Lore:Kintyra II|Kintyra II]]'s consort, Kontin Arynx.{{Ref|name=BHOTE1|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 1}}}} By the time the [[Lore:War of the Red Diamond|War of the Diamond]] took place, all of northern Morrowind supported Emperor Uriel III and [[Lore:Potema|Potema]], the Wolf Queen of Solitude.{{Ref|name=BHOTE2|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 2}}}}

==Politics==
Despite the name, [[Lore:House Telvanni|House Telvanni]]'s control over the peninsula has waxed and waned over the centuries. During the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]] of the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], House Telvanni cared little for what lies outside of their sanctuary on the coast and controlled a number of strongholds (such as [[Lore:Ald Isra|Ald Isra]], Tel Baro, Tel Dreloth, Tel Hlurag Ven, and Tel Rendys) across the length and breadth of the peninsula.{{ref|name=LAHT}}{{ref|name=TGHaTU|{{Cite Book|The Great Houses and Their Uses}}}}{{Ref|name=RubyShalk|[[Online:Ruby Shroom Shalk|Ruby Shroom Shalk]] pet description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} The main exclusion to this was the City of [[Lore:Necrom|Necrom]], which was under control of the [[Lore:Keepers of the Dead|Keepers of the Dead]] (an arm of the [[Lore:Tribunal Temple|Temple]]){{ref|name=CG|[[ON:Curate Gadayn|Curate Gadayn]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]&#58; ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}{{ref|name=PF|[[ON:Prelate Faram|Prelate Faram]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]&#58; ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}} and remained independent from any great houses,{{Ref|Events in [[Online:Keeper of the Fate|Keeper of the Fate]]}} and the island of Gorne which was administrated by [[Lore:House Indoril|House Indoril]].{{Ref|Events in [[Online:In a Troubled House|In a Troubled House]]}} The Temple also administered [[Lore:Sailenmora|Sailenmora]].{{Ref|name=Sailenmora|[[ON:Sailenmora|Sailenmora]] [[ON:Loading Screens|loading screen]]}} [[Lore:House Dres|House Dres]] had already began carving territory in the southwest, and the [[Lore:Camonna Tong|Camonna Tong]] head quartered themselves in a den called Camonnaruhn just south of the Dres.{{Ref|name=Camonnaruhn|[[ON:Camonnaruhn|Camonnaruhn]] [[ON:Loading Screens|loading screen]]}} Few [[Lore:Ashlanders|Ashlanders]] make their home on the Peninsula.{{ref|name=ONNusum|[[Online:Nusum|Nusum]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
  
By the end of the Second Era, House Telvanni's territory on the peninsula was limited solely to the hills and islands of the extreme northeast, who preferred to tend their herds of giant insects amid the rocky arid hills and islands of that corner.{{ref|name=PGE1M|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Morrowind}}}}{{Ref|name=ESOLive|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|ESO Live Lawrence Schick Interview|ns_base=General}}}} By that time the [[Lore:House Indoril|Indoril]] controlled most of it, particularly the east coast up to Necrom and west coast all along the Inner Sea.{{Ref|name=GHoM}}

In the [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], the Indoril control much of the central highlands, eastern coast and western coast, while House Dres controlled the southeast coast surrounding [[Lore:Alavelis|Alavelis]].{{Ref|name=GHoM|{{Cite Book|Great Houses of Morrowind}}}} House Telvanni's holdings on the peninsula once consisted of Tel Aedrin, [[Lore:Velothis Haven|Velothis Haven]],{{ref|name=MWFaluraLlervu|[[Morrowind:Falura Llervu|Falura Llervu]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{ref|name=TPMorrowindMoonlight|group=UOL}} and [[Lore:Llothanis Heights|Llothanis Heights]].{{Ref|name=Arena|[[Arena:Morrowind|Morrowind]] locations in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}} Baldaras,{{ref|name=TPPlayingHouses|group=UOL}} [[Lore:Greenheights|Greenheights]], [[Lore:Helnim Wall|Helnim Wall]], [[Lore:Sailen Vulgate|Sailen Vulgate]], and [[Lore:Draloris|Draloris]] lie within the borders of the [[Lore:House Indoril|Indoril District]]. [[Lore:House Indoril|House Indoril]] also controlled the ancient fortress of Bal Tereg, found near Firewatch on the inlet of Ouada Gla'amnir.{{ref|name=TPPlayingHouses|group=UOL}}

The [[Lore:Bathogorgen|Bathogorgen]], who have the power of shapeshifting into virtually anything, are the dominant [[Lore:Vampire|vampire]] clan of the region.{{ref|name=TPPlayingHouses|group=UOL|[[General:Ted Peterson|Ted Peterson]]'s posts in [[General:Loranna's RP#Campaign 12: Playing Houses|''Playing Houses'']]}} The [[Lore:Dusksaber|Dusksaber]] vampires were active in the peninsula during the Second Era.{{Ref|Events in [[Online:Eye of Fate|Eye of Fate]]}}

As of the [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]], the Telvanni have no territory on the peninsula or anywhere else on the mainland; their properties are all on Vvardenfell and [[Lore:Solstheim|Solstheim]].{{ref|name=NelothDB|[[Skyrim:Neloth|Neloth]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]: [[Skyrim:Dragonborn|Dragonborn]]''}}
