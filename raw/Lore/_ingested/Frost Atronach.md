[[Lore:Frost Atronach|Frost Atronachs]] (sometimes known as '''[[Lore:Frost Daedra|Frost Daedra]]''', '''Ice Atronachs''', or '''Water Atronachs'''){{ref|[[Daggerfall:Enchantment_Power#Soul_Bound_Table|Trapped Water Atronach souls]] in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}}{{ref|name=ATRQUEST|Dialogue in [[Daggerfall:Background_Information|Background Information]] quest in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}}{{ref|name=MonsterExperts|[[Morrowind:Vala Catraso|Vala Catraso]] and [[Morrowind:Smokey Morth|Smokey Morth]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}} are [[Lore:Elemental Daedra|Elemental Daedra]] native to the coldest reaches of [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]].{{ref|Official description of ''[[Call to Arms:Releases#2023|Frost Atronach]]'' in ''[[Call to Arms:Call to Arms|Call to Arms]]''}} Seemingly made entirely of ice, they can be found unbound in the wild or can be conjured by those with magical aptitude to serve as guards, laborers, or otherwise obey their conjurers. Summoning of Frost Atronachs is usually performed through [[Lore:Koron|Koron's]] [[Lore:Koron's Peremptory Summons|Peremptory Summons]], which can be altered by conjurers to alter the minutiae of the summons (such as plane to summon from, daedra to summon, and form in which the daedra can be conjured).{{ref|name=ESO CSCCSC|[[Lore:Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase/Crown Crates|Crown Crate Showcase]]}} They are a source of [[Lore:Alchemy F#Frost Salts|Frost Salts]], an alchemical ingredient which has varied utility. They are conventionally summoned and found in the form of bulky humanoids. Frost Atronachs are found in many [[Lore:Oblivion|Daedric Realms]], including those of prominent princes and minor planes. While possessing some intelligence, Frost Atronachs are largely an unorganized and chaotic group, with individuals being largely indistinguishable from one another. Many frost atronach [[Lore:Names#Frost Atronach|names]] begin with the word "Nomeg".<noinclude>

==Variants==
Aside from the commonly found humanoid forms, there are many other types of frost atronach found in [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]] and [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]].
