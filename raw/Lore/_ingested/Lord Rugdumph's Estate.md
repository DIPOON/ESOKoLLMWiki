[[Lore:Lord Rugdumph's Estate|Lord Rugdumph's Estate]] (historically called '''Shurgak Clan Estate''') is a settlement located in {{Lore Link|White Fall Valley}}, in the [[Lore:Valus Mountains|Valus Mountains]] of [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]]. It is connected to [[Lore:Cheydinhal|Cheydinhal]] to the south via minor roads.

In the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], Shurgak Clan Estate was a wealthy [[Lore:Orc|Orc]] stronghold, possessing both a traditional fortified encampment and a [[Lore:Breton|Breton]]-style manor house. During the [[Lore:Alliance War|Alliance War]] of {{Year|2E 582}}, the estate was initially within the territory controlled by the [[Lore:Ebonheart Pact|Ebonheart Pact]], although the area changed hands many times over the course of the war. The estate was ruled by Warchief Azgarub, and was an entirely Orcish settlement. The Shurgak Clan was hostile to outsiders, and even servants would attack trespassers on sight. Azgarub was killed that year, and a raider named Krodak planned to loot the manor for treasure.{{ref|name=ESO|Events of [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

By {{Year|3E 433}}, the estate had been inherited by an Orc noble named Lord {{Lore Link|Rugdumph gro-Shurgak}}, from whom the estate got its current name. It consisted of a farm and servants quarters, as well as a manor house built in the Cheydinhal style, with influences from [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]] architecture. That year,  Rugdumph's daughter Lady {{Lore Link|Rogbut gra-Shurgak}} was kidnapped by [[Lore:Ogre|ogre]]s. She was rescued by the local Cheydinhal [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]] branch.{{ref|Events of ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}<noinclude>
