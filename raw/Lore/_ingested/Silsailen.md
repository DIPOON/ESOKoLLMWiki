[[Lore:Silsailen|Silsailen]] or '''Silsailen Point'''{{Ref|name=Arena|[[Arena:Silsailen Point|Silsailen Point]] location and rumors in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}} is a little farming town located in southern [[Lore:Auridon|Auridon]], over the hills of [[Lore:Iluvamir|Iluvamir]], northeast of [[Lore:Vulkhel Guard|Vulkhel Guard]] and south of [[Lore:Phaer|Phaer]].<noinclude>

{{NewLeft}}

==Layout & Geography==
Silsailen's landscape is bucolic, surrounded by farms and barns.{{ref|name=Tendil|[[Online:Captain Tendil|Captain Tendil]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Elen|[[Online:Elenwen|Elenwen]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The unwalled town{{ref|name=SilsailenArch|[[ON:Silsailen|Silsailen]]'s architecture and layout in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} overlooks the Eastshore river delta, where a multitude of inlets and coves are open to the sea separating southeastern Auridon from the [[Lore:Drowned Coast|Drowned Coast]] in [[Lore:Greenshade|Greenshade]].{{ref|name=Ent|[[Online:Eastshore Islets Camp|Eastshore Islets Camp]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=LES|{{Cite Book|Life in the Eagle's Shadow}}}} It was located next to several settlements, including [[Lore:Skywatch|Skywatch]] in the east, and [[Lore:Vulkhel Guard|Vulkhel Guard]] in the south.{{Ref|name=MapArena|[[:File:AR-map-Summurset Isle towns.png|Map of Summurset Isle – ''The Elder Scrolls: Arena'']]}}

==History==
[[File:ON-npc-Auridon Marine.jpg|thumb|left|First Auridon Marine helping rebuild Silsailen]]
It is unclear when Silsailen was founded&mdash;possibly as early as the Early [[Lore:Merethic Era|Merethic Era]], when [[Lore:Torinaan|Torinaan]] first landed in Auridon, to as late as the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], when Silsailen was undoubtedly a well-established [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]] settlement.{{ref|name=AuExChXII|{{Cite Book|Auridon Explored, Chapter XII}}}}{{ref|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} But circa {{Year|1E 2150}},{{ref|name=LAS|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive - Scribing}}}}{{Ref|name=MGC|{{Cite Book|Mages Guild Charter}}}} at least a small mining settlement had already been established at that location.{{Ref|name=FOTI|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Online|Fable of the Indrik (Annotated)}}}}

In {{Year|2E 582}}, Silsailen was under the administration of {{Lore Link|Canonreeve Valano}}, who began to secretly work with the [[Lore:Veiled Heritance|Veiled Heritance]].{{ref|name=TJ|{{Cite Book|Teldur's Journal|ns_base=ON}}}} [[Lore:Estre|High Kinlady Estre]], the Veiled Queen, promised Valano would become Canonreeve of the larger city of [[Lore:Skywatch|Skywatch]] in return for his loyalty and assistance.{{ref|name=BN|{{Cite Book|Burnt Note|ns_base=ON}}}} Before [[Lore:Ayrenn|Queen Ayrenn]] visited the town during her tour of Auridon, Valano ordered the Heritance to raze Silsailen while they were under the guise of the {{Lore Link|First Auridon Marines}} stationed there, imprisoning the real marines in a cellar. Many villagers died during the attack, while others fled and the settlement almost burned to the ground entirely.{{ref|name=Reminder|{{Cite Book|Reminder|ns_base=ON}}}}{{ref|name=Valano|[[Online:Canonreeve Valano|Canonreeve Valano]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Resc|[[Online:Silsailen Rescue|Silsailen Rescue]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} An [[Lore:Hero#Soulless One|Eye of the Queen]] managed to free the captive Marines and save some of the villagers.{{ref|name=Real|[[Online:Real Marines|Real Marines]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} In the meantime, the Canonreeve tried to escape with help from Advisor Norion, a high-ranking member of the Heritance, but was captured before he could.{{ref|name=GL|{{Cite Book|Gilded Letter|ns_base=ON}}}}{{ref|name=Teldur|[[Online:Teldur's End|Teldur's End]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Ultimately, the Heritance was forced from Silsailen and Valano was captured by the actual First Auridon Marines.{{ref|name=Teldur}}

After the settlement burned and the rebellion was defeated, the First Auridon Marines began to rebuild the town alongside the surviving townsfolk under the guidance of their new Canonreeve, Elenwen, the daughter of the former one.{{ref|name=Elen}}{{ref|name=Teldur}}

During the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]], Silsailen was still thriving as a town, led by Lord Soliron. It had a rivalry with [[Lore:Karndar Watch|Karndar Watch]].{{Ref|name=Arena}}

==Government==

[[File:ON-place-Silsailen 02.jpg|thumb|right|Silsailen Canonreeve's Manor]]

Silsailen has always been an integral part of the realm of the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset Isles]] since its foundation. A canonreeve is tasked to rule the settlement.{{ref|name=Valano}}

==Culture==

Silsailen is primarily a farming town, worked by local Altmer,{{ref|name=Tendil}}{{ref|name=Elen}} as opposed to its northern neighbor Phaer, which mainly uses foreign manpower.{{ref|name=Minique|[[Online:Minique|Minique]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} A unique breed of cat, the Silsailen Tortie-Calico, originated here.{{ref|name=Cat|[[ON:Silsailen Tortie-Calico Cat|Silsailen Tortie-Calico Cat]] description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

The many secluded inlets and coves in the region are used by criminals as hideouts, allowing them easy access to the nearby Silsailen.{{ref|name=LES}}
