The [[Lore:Abyss|Abyss]] is a mysterious realm of [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]] consisting of a never-ending series of rooms and corridors, both real and unreal at the same time, which together form an endless dungeon.{{ref|name=Theodor|[[BL:Theodor Gorlash|Theodor Gorlash]]'s dialogue in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}} Few have survived exploring the infinite reaches of the Abyss, and none have conquered it.{{ref|[[Blades:Abyssal Hourglass|Abyssal Hourglass]] description in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}} It is said to be linked to the [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]] [[Lore:Lich|lich]] king, [[Lore:Celemaril Light-Bringer|Celemaril Light-Bringer]]. However, Celemaril is not responsible for its creation.{{ref|[[Blades:Abyssal Altar|Abyssal Altar]] description in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}}<noinclude>

Circa {{Year|4E 180}},{{ref|[[Blades:Junius the Elder|Junius the Elder]]'s dialogue in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}} Celemaril was released from his magical imprisonment in the Ayleid ruins beneath [[Lore:Rivercrest|Rivercrest]]. Soon thereafter, a portal to the Abyss had appeared in the entrance of an old crypt just outside of Rivercrest.{{ref|Default [[Blades:Town|Town]] name in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}}. A madman named Theodor Gorlash (seemingly Lord [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]] in disguise) took an interest in the Abyss. He coined the title "the Abyss" and encouraged the [[Lore:The Warrior (person)|Warrior]] to explore its depths. The Warrior entered this portal and embarked upon expeditions deeper and deeper into the Abyss, fighting through waves of enemies and returning to the surface with many treasures, including a number of legendary [[Lore:Artifacts|artifacts]].{{ref|name=Theodor}}{{ref|name=BL|Events of ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}}

The rooms within the Abyss vary in appearance, with each floor of the dungeon containing a different architectural style and increasingly more powerful creatures. The rooms can take on the appearance of natural caves, ancient Ayleid ruins, and [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] forts. These rooms are inhabited by an ever-changing number of various dangerous creatures and foes, including mortals, [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]], [[Lore:Goblin|goblin]]s, [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]]ic [[Lore:Bestiary|animals]], [[Lore:Undead|undead]], and even [[Lore:Dragon|dragon]]s.{{ref|name=BL}}
