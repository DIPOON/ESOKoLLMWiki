[[Lore:Banekin|Banekin]] are a small lesser [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]] resembling a winged humanoid with mottled gray skin, a tail full of ridged spikes, pointed ears and horns on their heads. Banekin are associated with [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]] and use powerful shock spells in combat. They can speak Tamrielic, and are sometimes used as [[Lore:Familiar|familiars]] by powerful wizards,{{ref|name=ESOValFam|[[Online:Valeyn's Familiar|Valeyn's Familiar]]'s appearance during [[Online:Vision Quest|Vision Quest]]}} though they are unreliable due to their forgetfulness, as well as their inclination for cruelty and destruction.{{ref|name=DTTG|group=UOL|Description in [https://www.theguardian.com/technology/gallery/2014/mar/21/elder-scrolls-online-exclusive-concept-art-gallery Elder Scrolls Online – exclusive concept art gallery – in pictures] [[Wikipedia:The Guardian|The Guardian]] article}} Banekin are agile daedra. Aside from being able to use their claws and tails full of ridged spines, they can use spells to weaken their foes as well as make them nauseous.{{ref|name=KCAHC|[[Books:Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion|Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion]]; High Rock, Banekin}}

They are not well treated by larger daedra, such as [[Lore:Dremora|Dremora]], who torture loose banekin,{{Ref|group=UOL|name=Schick2015|{{Cite Book|Lawrence Schick's Posts|ns_base=General}} [https://www.imperial-library.info/content/posts-schick-2015 from 2015 - ''What is a Daedrat? (03/11/15)'']}} and [[Lore:Daedroth|Daedroths]], who are known to eat them alive.{{Ref|name=G-Nazey|[[ON:General Nazenaechar|General Nazenaechar]] idle activities}} There has been one documented case of a Dremora in [[Lore:Fargrave|Fargrave]] keeping a banekin in a crib, akin to a baby.{{Ref|name=Foshaz|[[ON:Foshaz|Foshaz]]'s banekin in [[ON:Foshaz's House|Foshaz's House]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>
