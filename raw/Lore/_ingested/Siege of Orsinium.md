The [[Lore:Siege of Orsinium|Siege of Orsinium]] (also called the '''Great Siege of Orsinium'''){{ref|name=TGSOO|{{Cite Book|The Great Siege of Orsinium}}}} was a long drawn out conflict between the combined forces of [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] and [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] rulers and the [[Lore:Orc|Orcish]] clans and [[Lore:Goblin-ken|goblin-ken]] inhabitants of the first [[Lore:Orsinium|Orsinium]] (now called Old Orsinium). In response to the Orcish threat to the Kingdom of Wayrest, a Breton–Redguard–Yokudan alliance was formed which, after a campaign of more than 30 years, destroyed the First [[Lore:Orsinium|Orsinium]] in the [[Lore:Wrothgarian Mountains|Wrothgarian Mountains]].{{ref|name=PGE1WR|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|The Wild Region}}}}<noinclude>

==Background==
King [[Lore:Golkarr|Golkarr]] of the Orcs amassed an army and was determined to seize control of the [[Lore:Bjoulsae River|Bjoulsae River]],{{ref|{{Cite Book|The Great Siege of Orsinium}}}}{{ref|name=PGE1WR}} upon which villages and the nomadic Breton [[Lore:Bjoulsae River Tribes|Bjoulsae River Tribes]] lived.{{ref|{{Cite Book|The Mirror}}}} They intended to force Wayrest to pay to regularly use the river for travel and trade.{{ref|name=PGE1WR}} Naturally, this did not settle well with their neighboring kingdoms, especially the Kingdom of [[Lore:Daggerfall (kingdom)|Daggerfall]]. In {{Year|1E 948}}, [[Lore:Joile|King Joile]] of Daggerfall sent a letter to [[Lore:Gaiden Shinji|Gaiden Shinji]] of the [[Lore:Order of Diagna|Order of Diagna]], which proposed a joint attack on Orsinium. Agreeing to provide aid, an alliance was formed between the Order of Diagna, the armies of Daggerfall (High Rock), and [[Lore:Sentinel (kingdom)|Sentinel]] (Hammerfell). The Bjoulsae River Tribes also agreed to lend their aid.{{ref|name=ONABS|[[Online:Ancestral Breton Style|Ancestral Breton Style]] antiquity entries in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=TRHOKE|{{Cite Book|The Royal House of King Eamond}}}} The armies amassed and began a campaign for the destruction of Orsinium.{{ref|name=WJOTB|{{Cite Book|Wayrest, Jewel of the Bay}}}}{{ref|name=PGE3O|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Orsinium}}}} Two years later, in {{Year|1E 950}} the siege began.{{Ref|name=WOBN|{{Cite Book|The War of Betony (Newgate)}}}}{{Ref|name=TPC|{{Cite Book|The Pig Children}}}} [[Lore:Guthrag Stone-Talker|Guthrag Stone-Talker]]'s diplomatic prowess was acknowledged as one of the factors in postponing the siege for more than a decade.{{Ref|name=GM|{{Cite Book|Guthrag's Mask}}}} The siege was seen as a response to the Bjoulsae Raids on Breton villages carried out by the [[Lore:Bagrakh Clan|Bagrakh Clan]].{{ref|name=ESOTrystan|[[Online:Lord Trystan|Lord Trystan]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
