[[Lore:Abah's Landing|Abah's Landing]], the '''Jewel of Khefrem''',{{Ref|name=Zabani|[[ON:Zabani|Zabani]]'s dialogue}} is a harbor city on the eastern coast of [[Lore:Hew's Bane|Hew's Bane]] in southern [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]]. It is a major port on the [[Lore:Abecean Sea|Abecean Sea]].{{ref|[[ON:Abah's Landing|Abah's Landing]] in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

During the period of [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokudan]] invasions of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] in the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]] known as the [[Lore:Ra Gada#The Warrior Waves|Tavan Wave]], Prince [[Lore:Hubalajad|Hubalajad]] became the first to attempt to settle Hew's Bane (then known as Khefrem's Boot), seeing strategic value in its naturally protected harbor.{{ref|name=IDoPH|{{Cite Book|In Defense of Prince Hubalajad}}}} There, he established a small settlement. Originally little more than a soldier's camp, it would eventually grow into the walled city of Abah's Landing.{{ref|[[ON:Hew's Bane|Hew's Bane]] loading screen in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} It was named after [[Lore:Ra Abah|Ra Abah]], a warrior who arrived there with Prince Hubalajad, who fought local Nedes to protect attacked noble, and was the first person to land.{{Ref|name=TCOH|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Online|The Conquests of Hubalajad}}}}

By the mid-[[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], royal power in Abah's Landing had been entirely supplanted by a group of criminal cartels known collectively as the "Merchant Lords". The hinterlands of Hew's Bane were populated primarily by smuggler crews and [[Lore:Maormer|Maormer]] pirates, with whom the Lords actively did business.{{ref|name=MFDL|[[Online:Magnifica Falorah|Magnifica Falorah]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=v1|{{Cite Book|Abah's Landing Merchant Lords, V. 1}}}} The city-state of Abah's Landing thus developed a deep rivalry with the kingdom of [[Lore:Taneth|Taneth]] to the north, whose traders usually bore the brunt of this thievery they harbored.{{ref|name=ESOTG|[[ON:Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]] storyline in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}

A local criminal gang known as the [[Lore:Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]] was founded in Abah's Landing in the Second Era, and ultimately grew to become a Tamriel-wide organization.{{ref|name=Zeira|[[ON:Zeira|Zeira]]'s dialogue in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|[[ON:The Vault of Moawita|The Vault of Moawita]] quest in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|[[Arena:Factions#The_Thieves_Guild|Thieves Guild]] in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}
