# Lore:Ring of Namira

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

<noinclude>{{Lore Artifacts Trail}}__NOTOC__
</noinclude>The [[Lore:Ring of Namira|Ring of Namira]] is a [[Lore:Daedra|Daedric]] [[Lore:Artifacts|artifact]] bestowed by [[Lore:Namira|Namira]] upon those who do her bidding.{{ref|name=Daggerfall|[[Daggerfall:Namira's Quest|Namira's Quest]] in [[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|''Daggerfall'']]}}{{ref|name=Oblivion|[[Oblivion:Namira|Namira]] quest in [[Oblivion:Oblivion|''Oblivion'']]}}{{ref|name=Skyrim|[[Skyrim:The Taste of Death|The Taste of Death]] quest in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|''Skyrim'']]}} The ring has been known to, at times, reflect damage back onto an attacker,{{ref|name=DFRing|The [[Daggerfall:Ring of Namira|ring]]'s effects in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}}{{ref|name=OBRing|The [[Oblivion:Ring of Namira|ring]]'s effects in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} while at other times being able to increase a person's stamina and restore their health if the wearer cannibalizes a corpse.{{ref|name=SRRing|The [[Skyrim:Ring of Namira|ring]]'s effects in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}<noinclude>

In {{Year|3E 405}}, an [[Lore:Agent|agent of the Blades]] was awarded the ring by Namira in return for killing a [[Lore:Vampire|vampire ancient]] that had fallen out of favor with the Prince.{{ref|name=Daggerfall}} In {{Year|3E 433}}, the [[Lore:Hero of Kvatch|Hero of Kvatch]] was tasked by Namira to kill a group of priests of [[Lore:Arkay|Arkay]] who were attempting to convert her followers in the ancient [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]] ruin of [[Lore:Anga|Anga]]; once the task was complete, Namira awarded the Hero her ring.{{ref|name=Oblivion}} In {{Year|4E 201}}, the [[Lore:Last Dragonborn|Last Dragonborn]] was awarded the ring for bringing a priest of Arkay to Namira's shrine in [[Lore:Reachcliff Cave|Reachcliff Cave]], and then killing and eating him.{{ref|name=Skyrim}}
