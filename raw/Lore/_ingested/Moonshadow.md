</noinclude>[[Lore:Moonshadow|Moonshadow]] is a realm of [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]] created and ruled over by the [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Prince]] [[Lore:Azura|Azura]], Queen of Dawn and Dusk.{{ref|name=TDOO|{{Cite Book|The Doors of Oblivion}}}}{{ref|name=MH|{{Cite Book|Modern Heretics}}}} The realm is said to hold "too much beauty", so much so it can render mortal visitors "half-blind". It contains flowers, waterfalls, pink trees,{{Ref|name=RF|[[ON:Contraband R#Rose Foundation|Rose Foundation]] description}} and a city of silver. Azura herself resides in a rose palace, and is welcoming to mortal travelers.{{ref|name=TDOO}}

To the [[Lore:Khajiit|Khajiit]], Moonshadow is where [[Lore:Azurah|Azurah]] tends the Gates of the Crossing—the bridge between Nirni and the afterlife beyond.{{Ref|name=ONMM|[[ON:Mazza-Mirri|Mazza-Mirri]]'s dialogue}}{{Ref|name=ONSR|[[ON:Shando-ri|Shando-ri]]'s dialogue}} It is there where they are guided to the [[Lore:Sands Behind the Stars|Sands Behind the Stars]] if they are worthy.{{ref|name=Book|{{Cite Book|The Elder Scrolls: The Official Survival Guide to Tamriel|ns_base=Books}}}}<noinclude>{{rp|152}}</noinclude>

According to myth, as Azurah wept in the [[Lore:Void|Great Darkness]] after the death of her [[Lore:Fadomai|mother]], the light from her tears chased away the Darkness and eventually left a "place of moonlight and shadow".{{ref|name=TFDOF|{{Cite Book|The Favored Daughter of Fadomai}}}} Khajiiti mythology also writes that when [[Lore:Magrus|Magrus]] fled from [[Lore:Boethra|Boethra]] and [[Lore:Lorkhaj|Lorkhaj]] after the former had plucked his eye out,{{ref|name=TWS|{{Cite Book|The Wandering Spirits}}}} he fell into the Moonshadow. There, Azurah judged him as too fearful to rule a sphere, and tore out his other eye. The blinded Magrus fled to the [[Lore:Aetherius|heavens]], and Azurah made of his eye a "stone to reflect the {{Lore Link|Varliance Gate}}".{{Ref|name=TSS|{{Cite Book|The Sky Spirits}}}}<noinclude>

During the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], the [[Lore:Ashlander|Ashlander]] inhabitants of [[Lore:Ald'ruhn|Ald'ruhn]] had come into possession of several Crimson Shards of Moonshadow, large red crystals which glow with a powerful red light.{{ref|[[ON:Ald'ruhn|Ald'ruhn]] daily quests in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Rose foundation is made from the pink petals of flowering trees in the realm and imported to Tamriel.{{Ref|name=RF}} Several pieces of armor of Daedric quality found in [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] are thought to have originated from Moonshadow; they are enchanted with a strong magical light enchantment, but are otherwise unremarkable.{{ref|name=OBArtifacts|Moonshadow [[Oblivion:Moonshadow Gauntlets|gauntlets]] and [[Oblivion:Moonshadow Shield|shield]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

==History==
During [[Lore:Morian Zenas|Morian Zenas]] and [[Lore:Divayth Fyr|Divayth Fyr]]'s experiments to travel to Oblivion between {{Year|2E 418}} to {{Year|2E 431}}, they managed to figure out a ritual to open a gate to Oblivion. Zenas decided to try going to Moonshadow as it was a safe option and visited the realm,{{Ref|name=CM:8|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 8: Orc Style}}}} but then went on to various other realms before settling in [[Lore:Apocrypha|Apocrypha]].{{ref|name=TDOO}}{{Ref|name=CM:10|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 10: Imperial Style}}}} He described Moonshadow to his assistant, [[Lore:Seif-ij Hidja|Seif-ij Hidja]].{{Ref|name=CM:14|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 14: Daedric Style}}}}

A rift to Moonshadow opened within [[Lore:Scrivener's Hall|Scrivener's Hall]] on 2nd Hearthfire, {{Year|2E 571}}, and closed two years later on 25th Evening Star, {{Year|2E 573}}.{{Ref|name=MRT|{{Cite Book|Mazandi's Rift Tracking|ns_base=ON}}}} 

Following [[Lore:Vivec|Vivec]]'s indecent acts toward Azura at Hogithum Hall in the [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]], Azura had made the regular gateways to the realm inaccessible by {{Year|3E 432}}.{{Ref|name=ICDL|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Imperial Census of Daedra Lords|ns_base=GEN}}}}

==Notes==
* "Moonshadow" is also another name for Azura. 
* Mortals born under the sign of [[Lore:The Shadow|The Shadow]] are known to have the ability to turn invisible. This ability is called "Moonshadow", although it is unclear if there is any connection to Azura's realm. 
* In a speech to the [[Lore:Hero of Kvatch|Hero of Kvatch]], [[Lore:Mankar Camoran|Mankar Camoran]] incorrectly names [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]]'s [[Lore:Mephala's Realms|realms]] the "ten Moonshadows".{{ref|name=OBMankar|[[Oblivion:Mankar Camoran|Mankar Camoran]]'s dialogue in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}
