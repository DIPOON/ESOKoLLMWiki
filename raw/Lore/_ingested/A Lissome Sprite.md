The lilting laughter of a lissome {{Lore Link|sprite}}<br>
Goes meandering through the watchful trees<br>
To kiss stone and brook in secret delight<br>
A loving caress that no other sees.

Across fair green hills where {{Lore Link|the Telling Stone}}<br>
Remembers holy [[Lore:Druids|druid]] dreams of yore<br>
Unseen this playful sprite flits to the throne<br>
Of {{Lore Link|Ivyhame}}, then lingers by the shore.

The laughter of this playful sprite calls me<br>
Drawn from the day's cares I do not resist<br>
I cast aside my chores to go dance free<br>
In sun-drenched glens amid the sacred mists.

I walk in {{Lore Link|Galen}}; there is none but me<br>
And my longing for woods, and hills, and sea.
{{Book End}}
