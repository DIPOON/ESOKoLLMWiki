[[Lore:Mirmulnir|Mirmulnir]] is a [[Lore:Dragon|dragon]] whose name means "Allegiance Strong Hunt" (or more accurately "Loyal Mortal Hunter"){{Ref|name=TESVSPOGG|{{Cite Book|The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide|ns_base=Books}}}} in the [[Lore:Dragon Language|Dragon Language]].

Mirmulnir was one of many dragons to survive the [[Lore:Dragon War|Dragon War]], and one of the few who successfully avoided persecution by the [[Lore:Akavir|Akaviri]] [[Lore:Dragonguard|Dragonguard]]. By {{Year|2E 373}}, he was still purportedly alive, having last been sighted by the [[Lore:Blades|Blades]] in the [[Lore:The Reach|Reach]] in {{Year|2E 212}}.{{ref|name=AOD|{{Cite Book|Atlas of Dragons}}}}

It is unknown if Mirmulnir was slain before the return of his master [[Lore:Alduin|Alduin]] in {{Year|4E 201}}. Upon his return, Alduin began the process of resurrecting the many dead dragons buried throughout Skyrim in ancient times by the [[Lore:Dragon Cult|Dragon Cult]]. Mirmulnir was one of the first dragons to make a reappearance, when he attacked a watchtower west of [[Lore:Whiterun|Whiterun]], killing several guardsmen. The Jarl of Whiterun, [[Lore:Balgruuf the Greater|Balgruuf the Greater]], sent a group of soldiers to deal with the dragon. Among them was the [[Lore:Last Dragonborn|Last Dragonborn]], whose Dragonblood was revealed when Mirmulnir was slain and his soul absorbed. This event was noticed by the [[Lore:Greybeards|Greybeards]], and the Dragonborn was summoned to [[Lore:High Hrothgar|High Hrothgar]]. Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Mirmulnir was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.{{ref|name=SRDragonRising|Events of [[Skyrim:Dragon Rising|Dragon Rising]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}<noinclude>
