# Lore:Leki

{{Lore Gods Trail}}__NOTOC__[[File:RG-misc-Leki.jpg|thumb|right|Illustration of Leki from the [[Lore:Saintsport|Saintsport]] wayshrine{{ref|name=RH}}{{ref|name=RGCompanion|{{Cite Book|The Redguard Companion|ns_base=Books}}}}]]{{about|the goddess|the Ansei warrior|Makela Leki}}<onlyinclude>
[[Lore:Leki|Leki]], also known as the '''Saint of the Spirit Sword'''{{ref|name=VOF}}{{ref|name=RH|''[[Redguard:Redguard History|Redguard History]]'' description in ''[[Redguard:Redguard|Redguard]]''}}{{ref|name=RGCompanion|{{Cite Book|The Redguard Companion|ns_base=Books}}}} is the goddess of aberrant swordsmanship and divine daughter of [[Lore:Ruptga|Tall Papa]],{{ref|name=VOF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith...}}}} who is said to reside in the [[Lore:Far Shores|Far Shores]].{{ref|name=ONRada|[[Online:Rada al-Saran|Rada al-Saran]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} She is said to be one of the most popular gods in [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]].{{ref|name=PGE1Ham|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Hammerfell}}}}

The Na-Totambu of [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokuda]] warred to a standstill during the mythic era to decide who would lead the charge against the [[Lore:Lefthanded Elves|Lefthanded Elves]]. Their swordmasters, though, were so skilled in the Best Known Cuts as to be matched evenly. Leki introduced the Ephemeral Feint (a [[Lore:Sword-singers|Sword-Singing]] technique, acording to some stories);{{Ref|name=VivecMeeting|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|ns_base=GEN|Lord Vivec's Sword-Meeting With Cyrus the Restless}}}} afterwards, a victor emerged, and the war with the Elves began.{{ref|name=VOF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith...}}}} 

According to legend, one of Leki's miracles resulted in the founding of a settlement in the [[Lore:Alik'r|Alik'r]]. [[Lore:Leki's Blade|Leki's Blade]] was established after she dueled with [[Lore:Rada al-Saran|Rada al-Saran]], a great warrior who claimed to match the gods in the way of the sword.{{ref|name=ONMF|[[Online:Rahama|Rahama]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} [[Lore:Sai Sahan|Sai Sahan]] came from a long line of Redguard swordmaster nobles, and was "something more than mortal man", descending from Leki the Yokudan Sword-Saint.{{ref|name=Varen|[[Online:The Prophet|The Prophet]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}</onlyinclude>
A lock of hair supposedly belonging to Leki the Sword-Saint was found preserved inside small, exquisitely carved [[Lore:Jadeite|jadeite]] box and kept as a relic.{{ref|name=ContrabandJ|[[ON:Contraband J#Jadeite Saint Leki Reliquary|Jadeite Saint Leki Reliquary]] item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
