# Lore:Acharyai

<noinclude>__NOTOC__{{Lore Gods Trail}}</noinclude>
The [[Lore:Acharyai|Acharyai]] is the name of the supposed first spirit or spirits in existence, the distant ancestor or ancestors of the traditionally worshipped [[Lore:Gods|gods]]: the [[Lore:Aedra|Aedra]] and [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]]. They are acknowledged by the [[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic Order]] and considered fundamental to their complex system of [[Lore:Ancestor Worship|Ancestor Worship]], known to them as the Old Ways. As the foundation of their faith, the nature and existence of the Acharyai is one of the first things any neophyte of [[Lore:Artaeum|Artaeum]] will learn.{{ref|name=TOW|{{Cite Book|The Old Ways}}}} [[Lore:Centaur|Centaurs]] practice the same devout system as the Psijics, presumably including the Acharyai.{{ref|name=DUG|{{Cite Book|Daggerfall User's Guide|ns_base=Books}}}}<noinclude>

The concept of anything existing before the Daedra has been considered a paradoxical impossibility, even to those in contact with the Psijic Order.{{ref|name=GMFR|{{Cite Book|Grand Maestro Forte's Research}}}}

==Notes==
*What relation, if any, the term "Acharyai" has to "Alcharyai" (a term used to refer to [[Lore:Aldmer|Aldmer]] in some dialects) is unknown.{{Ref|name=PoM|{{Cite Book|People of Morrowind|ns_base=GEN}}}}
*[[Lore:Anu|Anu]] and [[Lore:Padomay|Padomay]] are sometimes called the parents of the et'Ada,{{ref|name=VoFiT|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith in Tamriel}}}} but their relation to the Acharyai is unknown.
*[[Lore:Hermaeus Mora|Hermaeus Mora]] has been referred to as 'elder than Ada' in obscure texts.{{ref|name=|{{Cite Book|Fragmentae Abyssum Hermaeus Morus}}}}
