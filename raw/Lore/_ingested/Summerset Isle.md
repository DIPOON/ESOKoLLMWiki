[[Lore:Summerset Isle|Summerset]]{{intnote|nb1|[nb 1]}} (also referred to as: '''Summerset Isle''', the '''Eternal Isle''',{{ref|name=RGGerrick|[[Redguard:Gerrick|Gerrick]]'s dialogue in ''[[Redguard:Redguard|Redguard]]''}} and '''Blessed Isle'''){{ref|name=PGE3S|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Summerset}}}}{{ref|name=ESOProxyQueen|[[ON:Proxy Queen Alwinarwe|Proxy Queen Alwinarwe]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} is a large island to the southwest of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]]'s mainland. It is the largest of the three main islands in the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset Isles]], which encompasses over a dozen more smaller islands. ==Geography==
[[File:ON-place-Summerset 02.jpg|The landscape of Summerset Isle circa {{Year|2E 582}}|thumb|left]]
The landscape of Summerset Isle is similar to that of its sister island of [[Lore:Auridon|Auridon]], with a warm climate, colorful forests, idyllic meadows, and craggy mountains. Most of the southern part of the island and a central region in the north are covered with mountains, with a central pass linking the western and eastern parts of the island.{{Ref|name=SummersetESO|[[ON:Summerset|Summmerset]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} The highest mountain on the island is [[Lore:Eton Nir|Eton Nir]], located in the northern region. [[Lore:Coral|Coral]] is abundant on the shores of Summerset Isle; the [[Lore:Coral Forest|Coral Forest]] on the eastern shore is one of the best-known coral formations.{{Ref|name=CF|[[ON:Coral Forest|Coral Forest]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} Coral formations are sometimes found far from the sea, which suggests original occupation by the [[Lore:Sload|Sload]].{{ref|name=PGE3S}} A river with two tributaries named [[Lore:Clott River|The Clott]] flows into the northeastern shore.{{ref|[[:File:RG-map-West Tamriel-1024x768.png|Map of West Tamriel]]}}

The isle is home to the city of [[Lore:Alinor (city)|Alinor]], the capital of the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset Isles]] and the seat of the High Elven rulers since time immemorial. The city of [[Lore:Cloudrest|Cloudrest]] sits atop Eton Nir, the highest mountain. Other major cities include [[Lore:Lillandril|Lillandril]], [[Lore:Shimmerene|Shimmerene]], [[Lore:Sunhold|Sunhold]], and [[Lore:Dusk|Dusk]]. The [[Lore:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]], one of the eight mythical [[Lore:The Towers|focal points]] of [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]], was once located in the northern mountains. Like Auridon, Summerset is also dotted with [[Lore:Aldmer|Aldmer]]i ruins.{{ref|name=ESOSummerset|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}{{ref|name=PGE3S}}{{NewLeft}}

==History==
[[File:ON-place-Crystal Tower.jpg|thumb|left|The Crystal Tower]]
Summerset Isle was colonized along with the other island in the archipelago by the Aldmer when they came from [[Lore:Aldmeris|Aldmeris]] in the [[Lore:Merethic Era|Merethic Era]]. The ancestors of the modern [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]] formed a caste-based society and established the city of Alinor as their capital. The [[Lore:First Era|First Era]] was a period of unrest, with internal conflicts between the city-states, as well as [[Lore:Sload|Sload]] and [[Lore:Maormer|Maormer]] invasions. In the third millennium of the First Era, Summerset Isle was struck by the [[Lore:Thrassian Plague|Thrassian Plague]], which depopulated whole towns such as [[Lore:Corgrad Wastes|Corgrad]]. Since early times, Summerset Isle had been off-limits to almost all non-Altmer. In their isolation, the elves of the Isle developed a unique architectural style, more similar to the [[Lore:Falmer|Falmer]]i style than to the architecture of Auridon.{{ref|name=ESOWastingAway|[[Online:Wasting Away|Wasting Away]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}{{ref|name=PGE3S}}{{ref|name=EGTSummersetIsles|{{Cite Book|EGT|Summerset Isles}}}} The population on the Summerset Isle was three to four times larger in the late First Era, than it was in the mid Second Era.{{Ref|group=UOL|[https://www.twitch.tv/videos/312931886 Lawrence Schick - Summerfall Event Special: 19:15]}}
[[File:ON-place-Alinor.jpg|thumb|right|Alinor, the capital]]
In the sixth century of the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], during the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]], Queen [[Lore:Ayrenn|Ayrenn]], daughter of King [[Lore:Hidellith|Hidellith]], formed the first [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]], an alliance with [[Lore:Valenwood|Valenwood]] and [[Lore:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]] whose goal was to conquer [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] and seize the {{Lore Link|Ruby Throne}}. Ayrenn chose [[Lore:Elden Root|Elden Root]] in Valenwood rather than Alinor to be the capital of the Dominion, and her cousin [[Lore:Alwinarwe|Alwinarwe]] ruled Summerset on her behalf. Around {{Year|2E 582}}, Ayrenn issued a {{Lore Link|An End to Isolation|decree}} that opened the borders of Summerset Isle for outsiders, which resulted in an influx of immigrants.{{ref|name=ESOSummerset}} It remained open until roughly {{Year|2E 814}} when foreign traders were only allowed at its ports.{{Ref|name=PGE1AD}} 

The Second Era also saw the {{Lore Link|Abyssal Cabal}}'s plot to sink Summerset Isle. The Cabal were a group of [[Lore:Sea Sload|Sea Sload]] who wanted to reclaim the island for their underwater kingdom of [[Lore:Ul'vor Kus|Ul'vor Kus]]. They joined forces with the [[Lore:Court of Bedlam|Court of Bedlam]], an alliance of the cultists of [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]], [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]], and [[Lore:Clavicus Vile|Clavicus Vile]] who attempted to gain control over the Crystal Tower around 2E 582. Both groups were defeated by the combined efforts of the [[Lore:The Vestige|Vestige]] and the [[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic Order]].{{ref|name=ESOSummerset}}. At the end of the Second Era, the island was conquered by [[Lore:Tiber Septim|Tiber Septim]] in his unification of Tamriel under the [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]].

In the [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], Summerset Isle, along with the rest of the province, was officially incorporated into Tiber Septim's Empire. In {{Year|3E 110}}, the island was almost lost to the [[Lore:Maormer|Maormer]] in the [[Lore:War of the Isle|War of the Isle]].{{ref|name=PGE3S}} In the late fourth century of the Third Era, Summerset Isle was visited by the [[Lore:Eternal Champion|Eternal Champion]], who retrieved one of the pieces of the [[Lore:Staff of Chaos|Staff of Chaos]] from the Crystal Tower.{{ref|name=Arena|Events of [[Arena:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]] in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}} In {{Year|3E 433}}, the island was devastated by the [[Lore:Oblivion Crisis|Oblivion Crisis]], with the hordes of the [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]] managing to topple the Crystal Tower. The forces of the [[Lore:Thalmor|Thalmor]] contributed greatly to repelling the Daedra, which later allowed them to gain control of the whole province, and ultimately form the Third Aldmeri Dominion, the dominant force in [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] in the early [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]].{{ref|name=RT|{{Cite Book|Rising Threat}}}}

The apprentice of [[Lore:Balagog gro-Nolob|Balagog gro-Nolob]], [[Lore:Urzhag gro-Larak|Urzhag gro-Larak]], visited the Summerset Isles several times around the end of the Second Century of the Fourth Era during his travels across Tamriel which saw him learn from a variety of races to improve his cooking skills. During one of these visits, he got permission with two other outsiders to research with some limitations in the College of Sapiarchs. Though the Isles were opened up, many of the native inhabitants were still hesitant to outsiders.{{ref|name=Cookbook2|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Books|The Elder Scrolls: The Official Cookbook Vol. 2}}}}

==Architecture==
[[File:ON-place-Cloudrest.jpg|thumb|left|The city of Cloudrest atop Eton Nir, the highest mountain on Summerset Isle]]
The architectural style of many cities in Summerset Isle is unique from its counterparts on [[Lore:Auridon|Auridon]]. White marble construction, ornate glass and metalwork, and enormous elegant towers are a signature found across the island.{{ref|name=ESOSummerset}} According to some, Altmer build such vast towers because of the belief that they were once gods, and so they aspire to reach the heavens once more.{{ref|name=twitch1|group=UOL|[[General:ESO Live Summerset Story and Lore|ESO Live Summerset Story and Lore]]}} 

The modern architectural style of Summerset has been refined for thousands of years, as is evident by the numerous ruins that dot the island sharing a similar style. Altmer architects are said to look into the past for inspiration and add new layers of refinement, elegance, and detail to their structures. This discipline of continual improvement is in line with the overarching cultural concept known as the "Path to Alaxon", where many Altmer dedicate their long lives striving for perfection in their life or work.{{ref|name=ESOOlnewil|[[ON:Olnewil|Olnewil]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}{{ref|name=twitch1|group=UOL|[[General:ESO Live Summerset Story and Lore|ESO Live Summerset Story and Lore]]}} 

Although human traders to the island have famously described the architecture of Summerset as being made from glass or insect wings,{{Ref|name=PGE1AD|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Aldmeri Dominion}}}} this is believed to not be entirely accurate and was simply the viewpoint of outsiders attempting to explain what they saw.{{ref|name=twitch1|group=UOL|[[General:ESO Live Summerset Story and Lore|ESO Live Summerset Story and Lore]]}}

==Fauna==
[[File:ON-creature-Gryphon.jpg|thumb|left|A gryphon]][[File:ON-creature-Indrik.jpg|thumb|right|An indrik]]
Summerset Isle was originally inhabited by species such as [[Lore:Gheatus|Gheatus]], [[Lore:Welwa|welwa]], and [[Lore:Illyadi|Illyadi]], which were exterminated by the [[Lore:Aldmer|Aldmer]] when they colonized the island (although the welwa were reintroduced into the island's ecosystem in the Second Era{{ref|name=RW|{{Cite Book|Restoring the Welwas}}}}). The most iconic Summerset animals are [[Lore:Gryphon|gryphon]]s and deer-like [[Lore:Indrik|indrik]]s.{{ref|name=PGE3S}}{{ref|name=ESOSummerset}} Additionally, the [[Lore:Canah|canah]] is the exotic bird of Summerset Isle, bred for its beautiful feathers.{{ref|name=RGGerrick}}

==Notable Places==
[[File:LO-map-Summerset Isle (Oblivion Codex).jpg|thumb|right|A map of the whole province]]
;[[Lore:Alinor (city)|Alinor]]: The capital of the Summerset Isles. It is located on the western shore, along the Oleander Coast.

;[[Lore:Sollicich-on-Ker|Sollicich-on-Ker]]: Birthplace of [[Lore:Vanus Galerion|Vanus Galerion]].
;[[Lore:Sunhold|Sunhold]]: A city with the largest port on the Isle, located on the southern shore. The main road to Sunhold winds its way through the Eastern Pass.
