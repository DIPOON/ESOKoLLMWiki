[[Lore:Brindle Home|Brindle Home]], formerly '''Castle Brindle''', is a settlement located far north of [[Lore:Skingrad|Skingrad]], deep in the [[Lore:Great Forest|Great Forest]] of [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]].<noinclude>

==History==
</noinclude>During the [[Lore:Three Banners War|Three Banners War]], Castle Brindle was an expansive and strategically important fortress. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was considered to be in the [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]]'s territory.{{ref|name=ESO|[[ON:Castle Brindle|Castle Brindle]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

By the late [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], nothing remained of the keep. Brindle Home consisted of a small village and stables. For a time, an [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] warrior named {{Lore Link|Melus Petilius}} served as the town's guardian, killing off hordes of [[Lore:Goblin|goblin]]s when they attacked the settlement and clearing out a nest of [[Lore:Minotaur|minotaur]]s single-handedly. However, Petilius swore never to raise a weapon again when his wife died of a fever while he was away fighting, and he built a cabin outside of town to be closer to her grave. His piousness disgusted [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]], who sent the [[Lore:Hero of Kvatch|Hero of Kvatch]] to taunt him at his wife's grave in {{Year|3E 433}}. Enraged, Petilius killed the Hero with a cursed mace, damning himself and allowing Bal to claim his [[Lore:Soul|soul]]. The Hero was later returned to life by the Prince to torment Petilius further.{{ref|Events of [[OB:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]]'s quest in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}<noinclude>
