The [[Lore:Bloodmoon|Bloodmoon]] is a phenomenon occurring when [[Lore:Hircine|Hircine]]'s [[Lore:Great Hunt|Great Hunt]] is hosted on [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]]. During the Great Hunt, [[Lore:Secunda|Secunda]] turns a deep shade of red and becomes the Bloodmoon. The hunt is believed to end when the Bloodmoon disappears,{{ref|name=KWEBM}} and Secunda turning red is considered a calling for the chase.{{Ref|name=J'Kier|[[Skyrim:J'Kier|J'Kier]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} The Bloodmoon occurs naturally in the [[Lore:Hunting Grounds|Hunting Grounds]],{{Ref|name=The Great Hunt|Events of [[ON:The Great Hunt|The Great Hunt]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=HG|[[ON:Hunter's Glade|Hunter's Glade]] appearance in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} as the Great Hunts are constantly held there.{{ref|name=Hanu|[[ON:Hanu|Hanu]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

==Culture==
The [[Lore:Bloodmoon Prophecy|Bloodmoon Prophecy]] is a legend of the [[Lore:Skaal|Skaal]], a native group of [[Lore:Nord|Nords]] from [[Lore:Solstheim|Solstheim]]. It foretells an event which happens every era, when Hircine walks Nirn with his Hounds to collect mortals that shall become quarry in the [[Lore:Great Hunt|Great Hunt]]. The selected are either taken to the [[Lore:Hunting Grounds|Hunting Grounds]],{{ref|name=BMKorst|[[Bloodmoon:Korst Wind-Eye|Korst Wind-Eye]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon|Bloodmoon]]''}} or preyed upon on Nirn.{{ref|name=BMHunt|Events of [[Bloodmoon:Hircine's Hunt|Hircine's Hunt]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon|Bloodmoon]]''}}

The Bloodmoon is said to symbolize the blood of the Hunter's Prey, and those wishing to join the Great Hunt craft rounded bone pendants that are stained with blood to symbolize it.{{ref|name=KWEBM|[[Bloodmoon:Korst Wind-Eye|Korst Wind-Eye]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon|Bloodmoon]]''}}{{ref|name=FPI|[[ON:Contraband B#Bloodmoon Pendant|Bloodmoon Pendant]] item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Allegedly, the light of the Secunda during a Great Hunt can be used to infuse oils to create a red colored substance, known as the Bloodmoon Oil, with unknown alchemical properties.{{ref|name=Contr|[[ON:Contraband B#Bloodmoon Oil|Bloodmoon Oil]] item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
