The [[Lore:Imperial City Archives|Imperial City Archives]] (also referred to as the '''Imperial Archives'''){{Ref|name=Blades|[[Blades:Gaius Platorius|Gaius Platorius]]' dialogue in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}}{{ref|name=AT|[[ON:Abnur Tharn|Abnur Tharn]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=EJ|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Skyrim|Eranya's Journal}}}} is an [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] institution based in the [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]]. It served as a repository of valuable Imperial records, such as deeds to holdings and estates within the Empire's bounds,{{Ref|name=DTBM}}{{Ref|name=DTFS}} court proceedings for a military tribunal,{{Ref|name=Blades}} diplomatic marriage agreements between kingdoms,{{ref|name=TWQ1}} and records of the Imperial noble houses.{{ref|name=AT}}

One of its branches, the Documents Division, handled matters regarding documents of ownership. Bearers of appropriate ownership documents could amend them to rename the official names of their residence by submitting the proper forms and payments to the appropriate Construction Charter (such as the Anvil Construction Charter or Cyrodiil Construction Charter) and by filing duplicate forms with the Documents Division.{{Ref|name=DTBM|{{Cite Book|Deed to Benirus Manor|ns_base=Oblivion}}}}{{Ref|name=DTFS|{{Cite Book|Deed to Frostcrag Spire|ns_base=Oblivion}}}} It is unknown when the institution was founded, but it was active during the [[Lore:Second Era|Second]],{{ref|name=AT}} [[Lore:Third Era|Third]],{{Ref|name=DTBM}}{{Ref|name=DTFS}} and [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]].{{Ref|name=Blades}}{{ref|name=EJ}}<noinclude>
