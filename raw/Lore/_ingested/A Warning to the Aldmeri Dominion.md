The overlords of the {{Lore Link|Aldmeri Dominion}} are hereby put on notice: renounce your ambitions of aggression and return to your islands and jungles, or the wrath of the {{Lore Link|Daggerfall Covenant}} shall fall upon you like a hammer of the {{Lore Link|Divines}}.

We are well aware that the Aldmeri plan nothing less than a return to the Elven domination of the other races, particularly Men and {{Lore Link|Orc}}s. They wish to overturn the legacies of the {{Lore Link|Alessian Empire|First}} and {{Lore Link|Second Empire}}s and wipe them from history. This we shall not allow. Never again will free Men and Orcs submit to the tyranny of Elven oppression!

The arrogant High Elves of {{Lore Link|Summerset Isles|Summerset}} are clearly the driving force behind the Dominion. We of the Covenant have shown, through our acceptance of the {{Lore Link|Direnni}}, that we can live with the Elves in peace, but Queen {{Lore Link|Ayrenn}} insists on war. Her invasion of the continent of {{Lore Link|Tamriel}} is nothing but naked aggression. The Dominion has no territorial rights in {{Lore Link|Cyrodiil}}. If the Altmer do not return to Summerset, their invading armies will be destroyed.

The {{Lore Link|Bosmer|Wood Elves}} and the {{Lore Link|Khajiit}} have traded with our {{Lore Link|Iliac Bay}} kingdoms for ages, but they've made a mistake in allying themselves with the perfidious {{Lore Link|Altmer}} of Summerset. Though we of the Covenant wish them no ill will, if they maintain their alliance with the Dominion, they will suffer the same fate at our hands as the High Elves. They must reconsider, or they must face our wrath.
