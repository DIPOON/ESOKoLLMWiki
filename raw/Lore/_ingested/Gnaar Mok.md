[[Lore:Gnaar Mok|Gnaar Mok]] was a financially deprived fishing village located on an island in the [[Lore:Bitter Coast|Bitter Coast]] region of [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]].{{Ref|name=GtV|{{Cite Book|Guide to Vvardenfell}}}} The local [[Lore:House Hlaalu|Hlaalu]] manor dominated the village; the [[Lore:Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]] also operated out of a dilapidated hut in town. The road to Gnaar Mok from [[Lore:Ald'ruhn|Ald'ruhn]] was a long and exceptionally unpleasant walk, made perilous by the lack of trails or marked routes and casual travelers being ill-equipped for the attacks of wild beasts and brigands, making it preferable for them to instead keep to the roads and travel services.{{Ref|name=GTA|{{Cite Book|Guide to Ald'ruhn}}}}

During the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], Gnaar Mok was not a settlement but rather the site of a '''Arenim Manor''', consisting of a mansion and a warehouse; however, an area known as Gnaar Mok was a stop for [[Lore:Silt Strider|Silt Strider]] caravaneers during this time.{{Ref|{{Cite Book|Silt Strider Caravaner's Log|ns_base=ON}}}} In {{Year|2E 582}}, Councilor Dolvara of [[Lore:House Redoran|House Redoran]] resided in the manor, and was later assassinated in it by the [[Lore:Morag Tong|Morag Tong]].{{Ref|name=APW|[[ON:A Purposeful Writ|A Purposeful Writ]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

In {{Year|3E 410}}, Sixth House bases were founded near Gnaar Mok, with its operatives exploiting smuggler organizations and communications to spread their influence among victims unbalanced by [[Lore:Dagoth Ur|Dagoth Ur]]'s dream sendings.{{Ref|name=DUP|{{Cite Book|Dagoth Ur's Plans}}}} By {{Year|3E 427}}, a number of huts had been built around the manor, turning it into the settlement of Gnaar Mok. That year the [[Lore:Boots of Blinding Speed|Boots of Blinding Speed]] were seen in the possession of a [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] by the name of Peminie, who asked the [[Lore:Nerevarine|Nerevarine]] for safe passage to Gnaar Mok. The Nerevarine complied, and received the boots upon leading her there. The Nerevarine was then informed by locals that Peminie was in fact a wanted felon; upon interrogation, she became hostile and had to be killed.{{ref|name=Morrowind|Events of the [[Morrowind:Pemenie and the Boots of Blinding Speed|Pemenie and the Boots of Blinding Speed]] quest in ''[[MW:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}<noinclude>

==Notes==
* "Gnaar Mok" shares a phonetic similarity to "[[Lore:Noormoc|Noormoc]]", a location on mainland Morrowind mentioned in the ''[[Lore:36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 5|36 Lessons of Vivec]]''. It is unknown if there is any connection between the two locations, as Noormoc was in existence prior to the splitting of Vvardenfell from the mainland, which the Sermons say occurred due to the activation of the [[Lore:Numidium|Numidium]].
