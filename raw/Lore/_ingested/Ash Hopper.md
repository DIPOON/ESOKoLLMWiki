[[Lore:Ash Hopper|Ash Hopper]]s are moderately large insects comparable to [[Lore:Grasshopper|grasshoppers]] found on [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]], southern [[Lore:Solstheim|Solstheim]], and the [[Lore:Systres|Systres Archipelago]]. Ash Hoppers thrive in volcanic environments.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Bolga's Guide to Galen Beasts}}}} Somehow, they even found their way into the [[Lore:Deadlands|Deadlands]], and are able to survive there comfortably. They have since become quite common in the realm.{{ref|name=FOTD|{{Cite Book|Food of the Deadlands}}}}<noinclude>

The Amber Ash Hopper is native to the upper Ashlands and the slopes of [[Lore:Red Mountain|Red Mountain]] and can be taken in as low maintenance pets.{{ref|name=ONAmberAshHopper|[[Online:Amber Ash Hopper|Amber Ash Hopper]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}  The Deep-Moss Ash Hopper is native to the fungal-forested uplands and the coastal marshes of southern Vvardenfell. They are the mascot bugs of the [[Lore:Buoyant Armigers|Buoyant Armigers]] due to their admiration of their green carapaces.{{ref|name=ONDeepMossAshHopper|[[Online:Deep-Moss Ash Hopper|Deep-Moss Ash Hopper]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} 

The Ash Hoppers of Solstheim differ in size, their head shape is somewhat similar to that of a [[Lore:Scrib|Scrib]]. Unlike their docile cousins on Vvardenfell, Solstheim's hoppers are hostile to travelers and they can carry [[Lore:Droops|droops]].{{ref|[[Skyrim:Ash Hopper|Ash Hopper]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} Their meat and jelly is used in cooking and alchemy,{{ref|[[Skyrim:Food|Food]] in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]'': [[SR:Ash Hopper Leg|Ash Hopper Leg]], [[SR:Ash Hopper Meat|Ash Hopper Meat]]}}{{ref|[[Online:Ash-Hopper Dumplings on Scathecraw|Ash-Hopper Dumplings on Scathecraw]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|[[Skyrim:Ash Hopper Jelly|Ash Hopper Jelly]] as an alchemy ingredient in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} and their chitin can be used in creating traditional [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]] armor types. Their shells can also be made into lava-resistant overshoes.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Naryu's Journal/Vvardenfell}}}}
