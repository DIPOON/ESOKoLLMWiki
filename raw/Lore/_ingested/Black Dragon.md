[[Lore:Black Dragon|The Black Dragon]] was a mysterious individual who worked for the [[Lore:Order of the Hour|Order of the Hour]] as their First Sword, hunting down members of the [[Lore:Dark Brotherhood|Dark Brotherhood]] in the [[Lore:Gold Coast|Gold Coast]] around {{Year|2E 582}}. Initially, she was an [[Lore:Imperial|Imperial]] assassin named '''Lyra Viria''' who served as a Silencer of the Brotherhood, stationed in {{Lore Link|Xith-Izkul Sanctuary}} until its purification. Following that, she defected to the Order of the Hour under the guidance of Primate [[Lore:Artorius Ponticus|Artorius Ponticus]].<noinclude>

==Biography==
===Early Life===
[[File:ON-place-Ruins of Xith-Izkul.jpg|thumb|right|Lyra's original sanctuary, Xith-Izkul]]
:''"She was an assassin. She killed on command and with impunity, leaving a graveyard full of corpses in her wake. She's a member of the Dark Brotherhood. That is, she was. Until they pushed her too far."'' ―Lyra Viria{{ref|name=ONTBD}}
The Black Dragon was originally a member of the Dark Brotherhood known as Lyra Viria. Lyra was stationed at the Xith-Izkul Sanctuary, located between [[Lore:Blackwood|Blackwood]] and Western Argonia.{{ref|name=OnlineDB|Events of [[ON:Online|ESO]]'s [[Online:Dark Brotherhood (DLC)|Dark Brotherhood DLC]]}} She was friends with many of the assassins in the sanctuary, including {{Lore Link|Green-Venom-Tongue}}.{{ref|name=ONGVT|[[Online:Green-Venom-Tongue|Green-Venom-Tongue]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Venom and Lyra had joined the Brotherhood around the same time, and were told horror stories by the veteran members of the group. Among them was the story of Brombar, an assassin who betrayed his brother. Brombar was mortally wounded by his sister, who left him so that the [[Lore:Wrath of Sithis|Wrath of Sithis]] could claim his soul while he was fully aware of what was happening. Venom said that he and Lyra couldn't sleep soundly for a week after that.{{ref|name=VSM|{{Cite Book|Venom's Sanctuary Marginalia|ns_base=Online}}}}

===The Purification===
[[File:ON-quest-A Ghost from the Past.jpg|thumb|left|Lyra purifies her sanctuary, killing many of the people she was close to in the process]]
Before long, Lyra reached the title of {{Lore Link|Silencer}}, a high rank in the Brotherhood that was granted to those who showed promising skill and devotion.{{ref|name=OnlineDB}} Green-Venom-Tongue noted her as the most skilled of any assassin in the sanctuary.{{ref|name=ONGVT}} For unknown reasons, the {{Lore Link|Black Hand}} ordered a Purification on the Xith-Izkul Sanctuary. As silencer, Lyra was ordered to kill the members of the sanctuary, a difficult task as she was close with many of them.{{ref|name=OnlineDB}} Green-Venom-Tongue was spared, having been on a special mission and therefore no part in the matter, and was led to believe Lyra died in the purification.{{ref|name=ONGVT}}

Once the deed was complete, Lyra prayed at the Statue of Sithis, questioning him if all the deaths were what was truly right for the Dark Brotherhood. Speaker Arawen arrived, assuring Lyra purification of the sanctuary was by Sithis' will. Almost immediately, she assigned Lyra the task of assassinating [[Lore:Jonas Coventine|Jonas Coventine]], the Primate of Akatosh. Lyra requested time to grieve for her fallen brothers and sisters, though Arawen scolded her for it, saying that she was a weapon and such bonds were weakness.{{ref|name=OnlineDB}}

===Becoming the First Sword===
[[File:ON-quest-A Ghost from the Past 06.jpg|thumb|right|Lyra speaking to Artorius]]
:''"Before I donned the armor of the Black Dragon, I was simply a Silencer FOR the Dark Brotherhood. Now I'm the Black Dragon, Silencer OF the Dark Brotherhood and First Sword of the Order of the Hour."'' ―The Black Dragon{{ref|name=TBDJ|{{Cite Book|The Black Dragon's Journal}}}}
Lyra traveled to the Cathedral of Akatosh in Kvatch, where she killed Primate Jonas.{{ref|name=ONTBD}} Immediately after Jonas' murder, Artorius Ponticus confronted Lyra. Artorius told her that he performed the Black Sacrament that brought her to Kvatch to assassinate his superior, Primate Jonas, and that his true goal was to meet with Lyra. Seeking his own personal champion, Artorius used Lyra's disillusionment with the Brotherhood to try to convince her that Akatosh wanted them to join forces and that the only way to atone for her sins and achieve redemption was through him.{{ref|name=ONPA|[[Online:Primate Artorius|Primate Artorius]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Dark Brotherhood (DLC)|Dark Brotherhood]]''}}{{ref|name=ONGFTP|[[Online:A Ghost from the Past|A Ghost from the Past]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Dark Brotherhood (DLC)|Dark Brotherhood]]''}} This worked, and Lyra pledged her blade and life to him and the dragon god.{{ref|name=ONGFTP}} She became known as the Black Dragon and became the First Sword of the [[Lore:Order of the Hour|Order of the Hour]].{{ref|name=ONGFTP}}{{ref|name=TBDJ}}

Lyra became the Black Dragon, and no longer considered herself Lyra Viria. Being part of the Order was not the same as being part of the Brotherhood. Lyra considered her fellow assassins as family, and the members of the Order instead respected and feared her. They showed love for no one except Akatosh, and perhaps Primate Artorius. The Black Dragon was cocksure of her actions in the Brotherhood for most of her time there, and originally in the Order, but began to feel discontent.{{ref|name=TBDJ}}

===Hunting the Brotherhood===
[[File:ON-quest-Dark Revelations 03.jpg|thumb|left|Lyra Viria kills Mirabelle Motierre]]
As the First Sword, Primate Artorius assigned the Black Dragon the task of hunting down Brotherhood members and finding the Sanctuary operating within the Gold Coast. The Black Dragon killed a number of members, both new initiates and veteran assassins, including two named Van{{ref|name=ONMM|[[Online:Mirabelle Motierre|Mirabelle Motierre]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} and Symdul.{{ref|name=VSM}}

The Black Dragon continued hunting members, and eventually captured an assassin named Cimbar. After capturing Cimbar, she took him to Grand Sermonizer Fithia to torture him for information.{{ref|name=ONTBD|[[Online:Lyra Viria|Lyra Viria]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Cimbar refused to reveal the location, and perished from the torture. She later went to the Enclave of the Hourglass, and met [[Lore:The Vestige|the Vestige]] under the name of Lyra. She questioned the Vestige on whether they had regrets, saying Primate Artorius believed everyone was worthy of redemption, and led her to the Black Dragon's chambers. Mirabelle Motierre, seeking to exact revenge from the Black Dragon for killing Cimbar, waited in her chambers in order to murder her. Mirabelle accidentally revealed her plan to Lyra, who swiftly killed Mirabelle, and threatened the Vestige.{{ref|name=OnlineDB}}

The Black Dragon later found a Brotherhood member named Brema, and had her reveal the location of the sanctuary before slitting her throat. She told Primate Artorius of the sanctuary's location,{{ref|name=ONTBD}} and set out to Knightsgrave after the Enclave of the Hourglass began to feel to confining for her.{{ref|name=TBDJP2|{{Cite Book|The Black Dragon's Journal, Part 2}}}}

===Seclusion in Knightsgrave===
[[File:ON-quest-The Wrath of Sithis 02.jpg|thumb|left|Lyra kills Venom in the halls of Knightsgrave]]
:''"I sought out the ruins of Knightsgrave after the Enclave of the Hourglass began to feel too confining, too full of life. I don't deserve to mingle with so many vibrant, living beings. Death has become more than my vocation—it's a vital part of my life. So why not linger in this place of the dead?"'' ―The Black Dragon{{ref|name=TBDJP2}}
Lyra traveled to Knightsgrave, an ancient headquarters for the Order of the Hour, now in ruins. She researched the founders and their statues, admiring their actions, specifically the original First Sword Justia Desticus. She later discovered the braziers served as a key into the inner parts of Knightsgrave, and quickly deciphered how to activate them. She found many Minotaurs within, taking careful measures to ignore them. The Black Dragon then came across the Paragon's Crown, a large circle with pillars surrounding it, and prayed to Akatosh.{{ref|name=TBDJP2}}

Further into the ruins she found the grotto of Knightsgrave, which contained a large statue of Akatosh built by the Alessian Order in the First Era. The many cages suggested the Alessian Order performed darker rites than the Order of the Hour did in Kvatch. The Black Dragon then defected from the Order of the Hour, hating the Primate and the Dark Brotherhood for turning her into what she became.{{ref|name=ONTBD}}{{ref|name=TBDJP2}}
[[File:ON-quest-The Wrath of Sithis.jpg|thumb|right|The Wrath of Sithis rips Lyra's soul from her body and brings it to the Void]]
The Dark Brotherhood then discovered Lyra was hiding out in Knightsgrave. Astara Caerellius sent Green-Venom-Tongue and the Vestige to kill the Black Dragon. Lyra sent many shadows to communicate to them, warning them to go back. They ignored her pleas, and after they had been separated, she impaled Green-Venom-Tongue with a sword, mortally wounding him before he said his last words to the Vestige.{{ref|name=OnlineDB}}

:''"And here we are. I'm ready to die. Let's see if you're good enough to kill me."'' ―The Black Dragon to the Vestige{{ref|name=ONTBD}}

The Vestige pursued the Black Dragon, while she sent more shadows to communicate to them. At the Paragon's Crown, she sent multiple shadows with different tactics to attack and kill the Vestige, evaluating them defeat the shadows and discovering more of the Vestige's weaknesses. Finally, at Knightsgrave Grotto, the Black Dragon dueled the Vestige to the death. The Black Dragon finally perished at the Vestige's hand, and the Wrath of Sithis shackled Lyra's soul, taking her to the [[Lore:Void|Void]].{{ref|name=OnlineDB}}
