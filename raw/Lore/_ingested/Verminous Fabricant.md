[[Lore:Verminous Fabricant|Verminous Fabricants]] are [[Lore:Fabricant|Fabricants]] created by [[Lore:Sotha Sil|Sotha Sil]] that resemble lean bipedal [[Lore:Reptile|reptiles]]. They have long necks, long tails, a horn on their noses, and various metallic limbs and spikes, which they utilize to attack their prey.{{ref|name=ASoF|{{Cite Book|A Study of Fabricants}}}}{{ref|name=ONVFA|[[Online:Verminous Fabricant#Skills and Abilities|Verminous Fabricant's Skills and Abilities]] in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}} Sotha Sil created them as imitations of [[Lore:Verminous|extinct creatures]] of the same name that once roamed across [[Lore:Velothi|Velothi]] [[Lore:Morrowind|lands]], to serve as a reminder of a thing that once was. He sought to perfect them (in his eyes) with an amalgam of flesh and metal.{{ref|name=LACW|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive - The Clockwork City‎}}}} Aside from being quick, agile, and being able to fit into small spaces,{{ref|name=ASoF}} they also are capable of spinning into their target, generating shockwaves, and as a last resort, self-destructing.{{ref|name=ONVFA}} They are powered by an [[Lore:Animo Cores|animo core]],{{ref|[[Online:Clockwork City Quest Items|Fabricated Animo Core]] description from [[Online:In Search of a Sponsor|In Search of a Sponsor]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Clockwork City (DLC)|Clockwork City]]''}} and can be contained within fabrication tanks.{{ref|[[Online:Active Fabrication Tank|Active Fabrication Tank]] furnishing description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Verminous Fabricants dwell in [[Lore:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]],{{ref|name=ASoF}} but have in occasion made it to [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]].{{ref|name=TRAttackMournholdQuest|[[Tribunal:An Attack on Mournhold|An Attack on Mournhold]] quest in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''}}<noinclude>

==History==
[[File:TR-interior-Clockwork City.jpg|Fabricant Machine (Tribunal)|thumb|left]]
Before their extinction and subsequent mechanomagical recreation by Sotha Sil, the original reptiles the fabricants were based on, were known simply as the "Verminous" and they dwelled in ancient Velothi lands.{{ref|name=LACW}}

Verminous fabricants were one of Sotha Sil's earlier creations. As part of the preliminary work on his lifelong project, the Clockwork City, Sotha Sil stored some of his early memories in Mnemonic Fragments within the [[Lore:Dwemer Ruins|Dwarven ruin]] of [[Lore:Kemel-Ze|Kemel-Ze]]. A wing there was gifted to him as a sign of the alliance between the [[Lore:Chimer|Chimer]] and the [[Lore:Dwemer|Dwemer]].{{ref|name=Armas|[[Online:Thoryn Armas|Thoryn Armas]]' dialogue in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Protus|[[Online:Protus|Protus]]' dialogue in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}} One of the memories here showcases his work on an early Verminous Fabricant, where he comments that it eventually will wear down and crumble, but at that moment it was the definition of perfection. And so, self-repair was how he would "circumvent inevitable deterioration".{{ref|name=Protus}}

[[File:ON-prerelease-Clockwork City 06.jpg|A pack of Verminous Fabricants (ESO)|thumb|right]]
Circa {{Year|2E 582}}, a portal to the Clockwork City inexplicably opened up inside the [[Lore:Abanabi Caves|Abanabi Caves]] when Divayth Fyr undertook to extend [[Lore:Tel Fyr|Tel Fyr]] there, spilling out Refabricants and Fabricants from the {{Lore Link|Halls of Fabrication}}. The Hunter-Killer Positrox and Negatrix were a pair of formidable Verminous Fabricants encountered there. The Fabricant threat was neutralized when a large group of [[Lore:Undaunted|Undaunted]] accompanied Divayth Fyr into the cave and entered the Clockwork City portal, defeated the Assembly General, and disabled the Fabricants' production. Divayth then studied the portal.{{ref|[[Online:Forging the Future|Forging the Future]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}} During this year, Verminous Fabricants along with other creatures across space and time also manifested within the Dwarven ruin of Nchuthnkarst in [[Lore:Blackreach|Blackreach]],{{ref|[[Online:Nchuthnkarst#Normal Enemies|Nchuthnkarst Normal Enemies]] in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}} due to the temporal and spatial distortions caused by a malfunctioning [[Lore:Tonal Architecture|Dwemer device]] there.{{ref|name=Thaddeus Cosma|[[ON:Thaddeus Cosma|Thaddeus Cosma]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

When the [[Lore:Nerevarine|Nerevarine]] came to [[Lore:Mournhold|Mournhold]] after [[Lore:Dagoth Ur|Dagoth Ur]]'s fall in {{Year|3E 427}}, Almalexia sought to trick, entrap, and destroy them as part of this plot. She first turned on Sotha Sil and slew him in his [[Lore:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]], then unleashed its mechanical inhabitants into her own city{{ref|name=TRAttackMournholdQuest|[[Tribunal:An Attack on Mournhold|An Attack on Mournhold]] quest in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''}} in order to frame her old friend, among them the Verminous Fabricants. Her ruse lured the Nerevarine to Sotha Sil's city, where she hoped that the Nerevarine would be killed by the inhabitants. When the Nerevarine persevered, Almalexia tried to finish the job herself. But her powers failed her, and she died at the Nerevarine's hand in the Clockwork City.{{ref|name=TRAlmalexia|[[Tribunal:Almalexia|Almalexia]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''}}{{ref|[[Tribunal:The Mad God|The Mad God]] quest in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''}}

The Clockwork City and its people, creatures, and constructs were still around in the Fourth Era. Circa {{Year|4E 201}}, a battle occurred between the [[Lore:Forgotten Hero|Forgotten Hero]] and [[Lore:Mecinar|Mecinar]], and the fate of the city and its inhabitants remains unknown. Some stories claim that the [[Lore:Forgotten Hero|Forgotten Hero]] destroyed the [[Lore:Mechanical Heart|Mechanical Heart]] that powered the Clockwork City, and its inhabitants were forced to abandon it as it ceased to function.{{ref|name=LRTCCTCF|[[Legends:The City Falls|The City Falls]] in [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}} Another account holds that the hero used the Heart to harness its power and fought Mecinar on equal ground, allowing the Heart to live on along with the city.{{ref|name=LRTCCKJE|[[Legends:Journey's End|Journey's End]] in [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}}{{ref|name=RCWC|Events of [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}}

==Uses==
Verminous Fabricants have been miniaturized for keeping as pets.{{Ref|[[Online:Microtized Verminous Fabricant|Microtized Verminous Fabricant]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The artifact known as [[Lore:Barilzar's Mazed Band|Barilzar's Mazed Band]] and an iteration of the [[Lore:Imperfect|Imperfect]] had the ability to summon them.{{Ref|[[Tribunal:Unique Items#Barilzar's Mazed Band|Barilzar's Mazed Band]] item ability in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''}}{{ref|[[Online:The Imperfect#Skills and Abilities|The Imperfect's Skills and Abilities]] in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}} Verminous Fabricant Elixirs, extracted from the remains of the creatures, give an increase to one's speed.{{Ref|[[Tribunal:Potions#Verminous Fabricant Elixir|Verminous Fabricant Elixir]] item ability in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''}}
