# Lore:Calendar

{{Trail|Appendices}}{{incomplete}}[[File:ON-quest-Ritual of Change.jpg|thumb|right|Argonian calendar at the [[Lore:Xinchei-Konu Monument|Xinchei-Konu Monument]]]]{{TOClimit|2}}
The main calendar that is used across the Elder Scrolls Series is the one that shares similarities to [[wikipedia:Gregorian calendar|the Gregorian calendar]]. However, there is another that is specifically followed by the Argonians, see [[Lore:Argonian#Culture|the Argonian racial article.]] The months listed below include these as well, but do not describe their in-depth meanings.

==Era==
{{Lore:Eras}}

==Year==
Each year is uniquely expressed by its era and its ordinal numeration with respect to the first year of the era, in the case of the Second{{ref|{{Cite Book|2920, Evening Star (v12)}}}} and Fourth,{{Ref|name=SkyrimLoad|[[Skyrim:Loading Screens#By Era|Loading screen]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} whereas the First{{ref|name=BAM|{{Cite Book|Before the Ages of Man}}}} and Third{{ref|{{Cite Book|The Third Era Timeline}}}} progress from their respective zeroth years. The Merethic Era is dated backwards, starting at {{Year|ME 2500}}, the [[Lore:Convention|Convention]] of the [[Lore:Divines|Divines]] at the [[Lore:Adamantine Tower|Adamantine Tower]], to {{Year|ME 1}}, the year before King [[Lore:Eplear|Eplear]] established the [[Lore:Camoran Dynasty|Camoran Dynasty]].{{ref|name=BAM}}

==Month==
The '''Tamrielic Calendar''' is thus used to keep track of the date and month of the year.{{ref|name=DFC|[[Books: The Daggerfall Chronicles| ''The Daggerfall Chronicles'']]}} Each year is composed of twelve distinct months (sometimes called "Seasons"{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}}), each around thirty days in length.{{ref|name=SR|[[Skyrim:Calendar|Calendar]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} Each month has an associated [[Lore:Constellations|constellation]] which is typically in the sky throughout its duration.

===Morning Star===
'''Morning Star''' (also spelled '''Morningstar'''{{ref|name=ARguide|[[Books:Arena Player's Guide|Arena Player's Guide]]''}} and known as '''Vakka''' in [[Lore:Jel|Jel]], meaning [[Lore:Sun|Sun]]){{ref|name=TSoA|{{Cite Book|The Seasons of Argonia}}}} is the first month of the year. It is a winter month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 31 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Ritual.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}}

===Sun's Dawn===
'''Sun's Dawn''' (known as '''Xeech''' in Jel, meaning Nut){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the second month of the year. It is a spring month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It seemingly has 28 days,{{ref|name=SR}} though some sources place a 29th day in the month.{{ref|{{Cite Book|2920, Sun's Dawn (v2)}}}}{{intnote|Leap|[nb 1]}} Its associated birthsign is the Lover.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}}

===First Seed===
'''First Seed''' (known as '''Sisei''' in Jel, meaning Sprout){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the third month of the year. It is a spring month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 31 days.{{ref|name=SR}}. Its associated birthsign is the Lord.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}}

===Rain's Hand===
'''Rain's Hand''' (known as '''Hist-Deek''' in Jel, meaning [[Lore:Hist|Hist]] Sapling){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the fourth month of the year. It is a spring month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 30 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Mage.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 

===Second Seed===
'''Second Seed''' (known as '''Hist-Dooka''' in Jel, meaning Mature Hist){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the fifth month of the year. It is a summer month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 31 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Shadow.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 

===Midyear===
'''Midyear''' (also spelled '''Mid Year'''{{ref|name=RJ2|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Online|Redfur Journal, v. 2}}}} and '''Middle Yarr''',{{ref|name=SOP}} and known as '''Hist-Tsoko''' in Jel, meaning Elder Hist){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the sixth month of the year. It is a summer month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 30 days.{{ref|name=SR}} In the [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]] version of the calendar the Mid Year was fifty-seven days long and lasted "all across summer", before it was split by [[Lore:Pelinal Whitestrake|Pelinal Whitestrake]].{{ref|name=SOP|{{Cite Book|The Song of Pelinal, v 10}}}} Its associated birthsign is the Steed.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 

===Sun's Height===
'''Sun's Height''' (known as '''Thtithil-Gah''' in Jel, meaning Egg-Basket){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the seventh month of the year. It is a summer month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 31 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Apprentice.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 

===Last Seed===
'''Last Seed''' (known as '''Thtithil''' in Jel, meaning Egg){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the eighth month of the year. It is a fall month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 31 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Warrior.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 

===Hearthfire===
'''Hearthfire''' (also known as '''Heartfire''',{{ref|name=TF}} sometimes spelled '''Hearth Fire'''{{ref|name=2920HF|{{Cite Book|2920, Hearth Fire (v9)}}}} or '''Heart Fire''',{{Ref|name=AC|[[Arena:Calendar|Calendar]] in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}{{ref|name=CS|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Books|Codex Scientia}}}} and known as '''Nushmeeko''' in Jel, meaning [[Lore:Lizard|Lizard]]){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the ninth month of the year. It is a fall month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 30 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Lady.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} The ''[[Lore:Book of the Leaves|Book of the Leaves]]'' places an emphasis on the Hearthfire, highlighting its significance within certain [[Lore:Druid|Druidic]] customs and rituals.{{ref|name=OE|[[ON:Oldefire Elk|Oldefire Elk]]'s description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=HL|[[ON:Harvestleaf Lynx|Harvestleaf Lynx]]'s description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

===Frostfall===
'''Frostfall''' (also spelled '''Frost Fall'''{{ref|name=LTKM|{{Cite Book|Letter to King Maxevian}}}} and known as '''Shaja-Nushmeeko''' in Jel, meaning Semi-Humanoid Lizard){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the tenth month of the year. It is a fall month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 31 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Tower.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 

===Sun's Dusk===
'''Sun's Dusk''' (known as '''Saxhleel''' in Jel, meaning Argonian){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the eleventh month of the year. It is a winter month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 30 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Atronach.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 

===Evening Star===
'''Evening Star''' (known as '''Xulomaht''' in Jel, meaning The Deceased){{ref|name=TSoA}} is the twelfth month of the year. It is a winter month.{{ref|name=ARguide}} It has 31 days.{{ref|name=SR}} Its associated birthsign is the Thief.{{ref|name=TF|{{Cite Book|The Firmament}}}} 


==Week==
There are seven days in each week. A month usually lasts around four weeks, but may span up to five.{{ref|name=SR}} The days are arranged in the following order:{{ref|name=ARguide}}
*'''Morndas'''
*'''Tirdas'''
*'''Middas'''
*'''Turdas'''
*'''Fredas'''
*'''Loredas'''
*'''Sundas'''

==Day==
Each day is 24 hours.{{ref|name=TESVSPOGG|{{Cite Book|The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide|ns_base=Books}}}} Some days have special significance as [[Lore:Holidays|holidays]].

==Notes==
*The Elder Scrolls calendar shares many similarities with the [[wikipedia:Gregorian calendar|Gregorian calendar]].
** {{LNote|Leap|^|Potentially due to the observance of [[wikipedia:Leap year|leap years]].}}
*Morning Star was not available in unmodified ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]'' due to a bug in the game.  This was caused by an "off-by-one error": although the "GameMonth" value counted months from 0 to 11, the developers incorrectly set it to month 1 after reaching the end of the year, assuming the months would count from 1 to 12.  On the PC version, it is possible to change the current month to Morning Star manually by using the console command "set GameMonth to 0".
* The ancient [[Lore:Faun|Faun]]s were known to have months with their own variation of names, such as "Hare's Leap" and "Fawn's Cry" which correspond to unknown months.{{ref|name=Here|{{Cite Book|Ancient Hunter's Journal}}}}
