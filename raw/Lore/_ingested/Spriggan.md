[[Lore:Spriggan|Spriggan]]s, often called '''Nature's Guardians''',{{Ref|name=OBRumours|[[Oblivion:Rumors#Wilderness|Wilderness rumors]] in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} are [[Lore:Nature Spirits|nature spirits]] that  typically take the form of [[Lore:Flora|tree]]-like female humanoids,{{ref|name=FGTS|{{Book Link|Field Guide to Spriggans}}}}{{ref|name=OBPrima|{{Cite Book|The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion Official Game Guide|ns_base=BK}}}}{{rp|55}} though some also take animal-like forms.{{Ref|name=SB}} They have also been described as woodland [[Lore:Faerie|fairykin]].{{ref|name=DUG|[[Books:Daggerfall User's Guide|Daggerfall User's Guide]]}} They possess moderate intelligence, but despite being revered they are usually hostile towards travelers. They are usually found protecting secluded glades and groves all over [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]], often blending in with the plant life and catching trespassers unaware. Spriggans attack with their long, sharp fingers as well as with their teeth, and have the ability to poison enemies. They have incredible regenerative abilities, resulting in adventurers often having to defeat them several times before they remain dead. If threatened, they can call nearby woodland animals to aid them in battle, and some even have the ability to magically summon [[Lore:Black Bear|black bears]].{{ref|name=OBlivion|[[OB:Spriggan|Spriggans]] in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}{{ref|name=Skyrim|[[SR:Spriggan|Spriggans]] in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} They also often utilize swarms of insects to defend themselves.

According to Nordic myth, Spriggans are immortal, and become larger and more powerful each time they are slain.{{ref|name=DUG|[[Books:Daggerfall User's Guide|Daggerfall User's Guide]]}}
==Origins and Religion==
Spriggans are considered by Nords to be creations of the goddess [[Lore:Kyne|Kyne]]. Spriggans are also associated with [[Lore:Kynareth|Kynareth]], and regard the [[Lore:Crusader's Relics#Boots of the Crusader|Boots of the Crusader]] as sacred.{{ref|[[Oblivion:Knights of the Nine|Knights of the Nine]]}} They oftentimes serve as the guardians of ancient trees, such as the [[Lore:Eldergleam|Eldergleam]] and the {{Lore Link|First Tree}}. According to [[Lore:Skaal|Skaal]] legend, the Spriggans of [[Lore:Solstheim|Solstheim]] came into being when the [[Lore:All-Maker|All-Maker]] breathed life into the land, and his Breath blew through the trees.{{ref|[[Bloodmoon:Skaal Village|Skaal Village]] residents' dialogue about [[Bloodmoon:Creatures#Spriggans|Spriggans]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon|Bloodmoon]]''}}

==Behavior and Culture==
[[File:ON-npc-Spriggan Venombow.jpg|A Spriggan using a natural bow (ESO)|thumb|left]]
Spriggans are known to form hierarchies, with powerful elder Spriggans such as Spriggan Matrons and the even more powerful Spriggan Earth Mothers acting as Matriarchs.{{ref|[[ON:Jafelma|Jafelma]]'s dialogue during [[ON:Land Dispute|Land Dispute]] quest in [[ESO:ESO|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=SkyrimLead|[[SR:Spriggan Matron|Spriggan Matrons]] an [[SR:Spriggan Earth Mother|Spriggan Earth Mother]]s in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} Though the scholar [[Lore:Phrastus of Elinhir|Phrastus of Elinhir]] was convinced that Spriggans are incapable of such intelligent behavior, and that they only act according to instinct.{{ref|name=FGTS}} 

Spriggans have their own unique [[Lore:language|language]], which can be learned by scholars and used to calm the creatures.{{ref|name=Language|[[DF:Spriggan|Spriggan (skill)]] language skill in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} Some Spriggans are even capable of speaking [[Lore:Tamrielic|Tamrielic]], and are capable of negotiating with others.{{ref|name=Hunt}}

Spriggans are known to to grow from seeds, which eventually sprout into saplings.{{ref|name=Hunt}}

==Variants and Related Creatures==
There are several varieties of Spriggan. Regular Spriggans appear as female humanoids made of wood, sometimes covered in foliage or emitting magical green light. Some scholars have speculated that the color of their floral fixtures change to match the current season of the year, accounting for "the so-called spring, summer, autumn, and winter" variants found amidst Tamriel's deciduous forests.{{ref|name=FGTS}}

===Animals===
Spriggans occasionally take the shapes of animals, such as bears and [[Lore:Senche-Cat|senche-cats]].{{Ref|name=SB|[[ON:Spriggan Bear|Spriggan Bear]]s and [[ON:Spriggan Senche|Spriggan Senche]]s in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

They are similar in appearance to [[Lore:Wild Hunt|Wild Hunt]] animals, creatures that were caught in the proximity of Bosmeri Wild Hunts that have forgotten their Y'ffre-taught shapes and shift into unnatural forms.{{Ref|name=WHC|[[ON:Wild Hunt Crate|Wild Hunt Crate]] description}}

===Burnt Spriggans===
Spriggans have occasionally been altered by certain firey stimulus, such as the firey Ashen Spriggans found during volcanic activity in the Systres Archipelago.{{ref|[[ON:Ashen Spriggan|Ashen Spriggan]]s in [[ESO:ESO|ESO]]}} After the [[Lore:Red Year|Red Year]], the [[Lore:Hirstaang Forest|Hirstaang Forest]] on Solstheim was scorched by the eruptions from [[Lore:Red Mountain|Red Mountain]], which gave rise to mysterious [[Lore:Burnt Spriggan|Burnt Spriggans]]. These twisted creatures glow from eternally-smouldering embers, and have the ability to attack travelers with magical fire. They wander the ashlands of southern Solstheim and are the subject of research for the [[Lore:Telvanni|Telvanni]] of [[Lore:Tel Mithryn|Tel Mithryn]].{{ref|[[SR:Burnt Spriggan|Burnt Spriggans]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]: [[Skyrim:Dragonborn|Dragonborn]]''}}

==Uses==
The bodies of Spriggans provide [[Lore:Taproot|Taproot]] (also called Supple Roots), a valuable alchemical ingredient. They can also occasionally be harvested for [[Lore:Belladonna|Belladonna]] berries or rare [[Lore:Heartwood|Heartwood]]. [[Lore:Spriggan Sap|Spriggan Sap]], [[Lore:Spriggan's Ear|Spriggan Ears]],{{ref|Events of ''[[Redguard:Redguard|Redguard]]''}} [[Lore:Spriggan Bark|Spriggan Bark]], [[Lore:Primal Sproutling|Primal Sproutlings]],{{ref|name=ESO|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} and [[Lore:Burnt Spriggan Wood|Burnt Spriggan Wood]] can all be obtained from Spriggans, and some can be of alchemical use. Once a year, Spriggans shed branches and leaves known as a Spriggan's Veil. These are prized by herbalists and wizards, and are believed to have symbolic meaning in [[Lore:Druid|Druidic]] practices.{{ref|{{Item Link|Spriggan's Veil|questid=7889|quality=1}} quest item in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|[[Online:Isobel_Veloise#Oaken_Order_Task|Isobel Veloise's dialogue]] during [[ON:Tournament of the Heart|Tournament of the Heart]] quest in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=Contraband S|[[ON:Contraband S#Spriggan's Veil|Spriggan's Veil]] item description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} The [[Lore:Glenmoril Wyrd|Glenmoril Wyrd]] are thought to use [[Lore:Spriggan|spriggan]] sap in their tattoo ink, so that the wearer may form a more intimate bond with nature.{{ref|[[ON:Glenmoril Wyrd Body Markings|Glenmoril Wyrd Body Markings]] item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Witches sacrifice Spriggans and use their defiled body parts in their rituals. The artifact [[Lore:Nettlebane|Nettlebane]] was made by witches for this purpose.{{ref|[[Skyrim:Danica Pure-Spring|Danica Pure-Spring]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}
