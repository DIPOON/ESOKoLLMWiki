# Lore:Conjuration

'''Conjuration''' magic is the art of summoning creatures or items from [[Lore:Planes of Existence|another plane]]. It is one of the six colleges of [[Lore:Magic|Magic]], but is not considered to be one of the "great schools" by the [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]],{{ref|name=MGC|{{Cite Book|Mages Guild Charter}}}} and only became mainstream after [[Lore:Warp in the West|Warp in the West]]. Despite this, it has been practiced for centuries by [[Lore:witches|witch covens]]  and wizards throughout Tamriel. Clan [[Lore:Direnni|Direnni]]'s sorcerers were some of the first to formalize the rituals, chants, and incantations used to open a connection with the plane of [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]], many of which are still standard centuries later.{{ref|name=EGTSummersetIsles|{{Cite Book|EGT|Summerset Isles}}}}

This school can be used to summon a wide range of [[Lore:Daedra|daedric creatures]], [[Lore:Weapons|weapons]] and armor. Spells used to dominate creatures and humanoids were once considered to be part of this school, but they are now part of the college of [[Lore:Illusion|Illusion]]. A further type of spell grants power over the [[Lore:Undead|undead]], until those were reassigned to the school of [[Lore:Restoration|Restoration]].

* Summoned creatures typically fall into three groups. There are spells that call a daedra, from weak creatures such as [[Lore:Scamp|scamp]]s to more powerful ones like [[Lore:Dremora|dremora]]. Others summon undead servants such as [[Lore:Skeleton|skeleton]]s or [[Lore:Wraith|wraith]]s. Lastly, some spells summon natural creatures such as [[Lore:Bear|bear]]s and [[Lore:Wolf|wolves]]. Any type of summoned creature can be used as a mage's [[Lore:Familiar|familiar]].

* Summoned weapons and armor are daedric in nature, although often not as strong as normal daedric items. 
 
* Turn Undead effects cause undead creatures to become afraid of the caster, from whom they will run as long as the effect lasts. 

* Some [[Lore:Necromancy|necromantic]] rituals, particularly the reanimation of corpses, overlap with this school. 

In {{Year|3E 431}}, the Council of Mages decided that Conjuration magic in [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] was to become the responsibility of the guildhall in the town of [[Lore:Chorrol|Chorrol]].{{ref|name=MGC}}

Some conjuration practices utilize parts of the creature, such as Daedra skin for summoning Daedra. [[Lore:Scrolls|Scrolls]] of summoning are always based on the Law of Similarity. [[Lore:Milyn Faram|Milyn Faram]] devised techniques that allow to conjure Daedra with scrolls that only have an image of the Daedra. They were noted to yield almost as good results as the techniques that required parts of a Daedra for conjuration. His research was based on Dwemer machinery.{{ref|name=MF|[[MW:Milyn Faram|Milyn Faram]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}

Summoning a creature in its natural habitat is more straightforward because there are more options available. Additionally, when the creature doesn't have to cover long distances, the summoning process consumes less energy.{{ref|name=Aramril|[[ON:Aramril|Aramril]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
