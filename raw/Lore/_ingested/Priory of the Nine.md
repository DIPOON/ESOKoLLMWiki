The [[Lore:Priory of the Nine|Priory of the Nine]] (originally the '''Abbey of the Eight''') is a settlement in the [[Lore:West Weald|West Weald]] of [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]].

{{NewLeft}}
==History==
During the time of the [[Lore:Three Banners War|Three Banners War]], the Abbey of the Eight was a center of worship of the [[Lore:Eight Divines|Eight Divines]]. It was led by an abbess and home to several initiates, along with an order known as the [[Lore:Knights of the Eight|Knights of the Eight]]. By {{Year|2E 582}}, it was within the territory controlled by the invading [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]].{{ref|Events of [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}

The settlement was renamed the Priory of the Nine after the apotheosis of [[Lore:Tiber Septim|Tiber Septim]] and the creation of the [[Lore:Nine Divines|Nine Divines]]. In {{Year|3E 111}}, it became home to the newly-founded [[Lore:Knights of the Nine|Knights of the Nine]], an order led by [[Lore:Amiel Lannus|Sir Amiel Lannus]] and dedicated to the recovery of the [[Lore:Crusader's Relics|Crusader's Relics]]. The knights saw many early successes and great fame, but began to dwindle away following the [[Lore:War of the Red Diamond|War of the Red Diamond]]. Sir Amiel, the last of his order, met his fate in {{Year|3E 153}} while embarking on a final quest to [[Lore:Vanua|Vanua]]. The location of the abandoned priory was eventually lost to history.{{ref|{{Cite Book|The Knights of the Nine}}}}

In {{Year|3E 433}}, [[Lore:Umaril the Unfeathered|Umaril the Unfeathered]] returned to [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]] with an army of [[Lore:Auroran|Auroran]]s on a quest to destroy the worship of the Nine. The [[Lore:Hero|Divine Crusader]], a reincarnation of [[Lore:Pelinal Whitestrake|Pelinal Whitestrake]], was led to the priory by the corpse of Sir Amiel. With the guidance of the Nine and the spirits of the old knights, the Divine Crusader recovered all of the Crusader's Relics in order to destroy Umaril. Word soon spread, and the Knights of the Nine were refounded as volunteers flocked to the restored priory. The order ultimately defeated Umaril in [[Lore:Garlas Malatar|Garlas Malatar]], and had plans to expand across [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]].{{ref|Events of ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]: [[Oblivion:Knights of the Nine|Knights of the Nine]]''}} An {{Lore Link|Oblivion Gates|Oblivion Gate}} opened near the priory during the [[Lore:Oblivion Crisis|Oblivion Crisis]].{{ref|Events of ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

It is unknown what effect the [[Lore:White-Gold Concordat|White-Gold Concordat]] had on the settlement. In {{Year|4E 201}}, the Crusader's Relics were stolen by a group of bandits from [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]] and taken to [[Lore:The Reach|the Reach]]. They were later reclaimed by the [[Lore:Last Dragonborn|Last Dragonborn]].{{ref|[[Skyrim:Relics of the Crusader|Events]] of the [[SR:Divine Crusader|Divine Crusader]] [[SR:Creation Club|Creation]]}}<noinclude>
