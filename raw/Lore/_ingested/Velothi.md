'''Velothi''' may refer to the following subjects:
* The [[Lore:Chimer|Chimer]], who were also known as the Velothi
* [[Lore:Ashlanders|Ashlanders]] that renounced their nomadic culture to settle with the House Dunmer, known now as the Velothi
* The teachings, followers, or anything related to Saint [[Lore:Veloth|Veloth]] (e.g. the Velothi Prophecies or the Velothi Exodus)
