The {{Lore Link|All Flags Navy}} returned to {{Lore Link|High Isle}} and received a hero's welcome, but the joy of victory was tempered by remorse. Nearly half of the armada's contingent was lost in the battle to wreak vengeance upon the {{Lore Link|Sload}}.

After the all-out assault on Thras, Baron-Admiral {{Lore Link|Bendu Olo}} and the leaders of the various groups who had come together to assemble the largest allied naval force in {{Lore Link|Tamriel}}ic history decided to establish a monument to honor and remember those who had fought and died to defeat the {{Lore Link|Thras}}sian threat.

Under the supervision of Master Builder {{Lore Link|Tobin Moorcroft|v=1}}, {{Lore Link|Monument Islet|v=1}}, a very small island at the very center of {{Lore Link|High Isle}}, was selected as the site of the memorial. Construction began almost immediately on the key tributes honoring the {{Lore Link|All Flags Navy}}. These included:

{{Lore Link|All Flags Castle|v=1}}, {{Lore Link|Monument Lighthouse|v=1}}, {{Lore Link|Memorial Garden|v=1}}, and {{Lore Link|Monument Inn|v=1}}.

The memorial complex was completed in {{Year|1E 2271}}. Baron-Admiral Olo, the leaders of the allied groups, and many of the surviving captains were on hand when the memorial was dedicated to the memory of the lost vessels and their crews, as well as to the victory over the Sload.
