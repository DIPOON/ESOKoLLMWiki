[[Lore:Aegilief|Aegilief]] was a [[Lore:Nord|Nord]] chieftain of [[Lore:Thirsk|Thirsk]] during the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]] in the fourth century of the [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]]. She became Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying Oddny the Unfaithful and presenting her head as a battle trophy. She was chieftain for eight years.{{ref|name=TAH|{{Cite Book|Thirsk, a History}}}}<noinclude>{{NewLeft}}
