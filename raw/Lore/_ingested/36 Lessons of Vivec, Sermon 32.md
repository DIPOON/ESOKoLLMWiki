The Scripture of the Mace, First:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The pleasure of annihilation is the pleasure of disappearing into the unreal. All those that would challenge the sleeping world will seek membership in this movement. I denounce the alienation of the Cloven Duality with a hammer.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Second:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}Take from me the lessons as a punishment for being mortal. To be made of dirt is to be treated as such by your jailers. This is the key and the lock of the [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]]. Why do you think they escaped the compromise?{{nst|'|ON="}}

Third:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}[[Lore:Chimer|Velothi]], your skin has become the [[Lore:Dunmer|pregnant darkness]]. My brooding has brought this on. Remember that [[Lore:Boethiah|Boethiah]] asked you to become the color of bruise. How else to show yourselves people of the exodus into the vital: pain?{{nst|'|ON="}}

Fourth:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The sage who is not an anvil: a conventional sentence and nothing more. By which I mean dead, [[Lore:CHIM|the fourth walking way]].{{nst|'|ON="}}

Fifth:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}A proper comprehension of the virtues: stage-managed and to be murdered.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Sixth:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}In the end, rejoice as a hostage released from drumming torment but that savors his wound. The drum breaks and you find it to be a nest of hornets, which is to say: your sleep is over.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Seventh:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The suspicious is spectacle and the lie is only a theoretical inspiration.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Eighth:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}But then why, you ask, do the Daedra wish to meddle with the [[Lore:Aurbis|Aurbis]]? It is because they are the radical critique, essential as all martyrs. That some are more evil than others {{nst|{{sic|in|is}}|ON=is}} not an illusion. Or rather, it is a necessary illusion.{{nst|'|ON="}}

The ending of the words is [[Lore:Almsivi|ALMSIVI]].
