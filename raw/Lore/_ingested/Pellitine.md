==History==
In the early [[Lore:First Era|First Era]], Elsweyr was known to consist of sixteen independent realms that cooperated with each other for their mutual protection and economic benefit.{{Ref|name=PGE3}} Pellitine was one of the ports that traded with the burgeoning [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]].{{Ref|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Cyrodiil}}}}

The devastating [[Lore:Thrassian Plague|Thrassian Plague]] of {{Year|1E 2260}} disrupted Elsweyr's delicate political balance, and when it subsided the original sixteen realms had consolidated into two survivors: Pa'alatiin and [[Lore:Anequina|Ne Quin-al]], or Pellitine and Anequina as they were known in Cyrodilic.{{Ref|name=PGE3}} The two kingdoms fell into a bitter, often violent feud that stalemated for centuries,{{Ref|name=PGE1}} even as both kingdoms were incorporated into the Empire of [[Lore:Reman Cyrodiil|Reman Cyrodiil]];{{Ref|Name=Redmane|{{Cite Book|A Legionary's History of Fort Redmane}}}} Anequina had developed a disciplined warrior culture, but Pellitine had wealth enough to hire mercenaries thanks to its fertile lands and the cultivation of [[Lore:Moon Sugar|Moon Sugar]] derived from the [[Lore:Tenmar Forest|Tenmar Forest]].{{Ref|name=PGE3}}{{Ref|name=PGE1}} The feud finally ended in {{Year|2E 309}}, as the kingdoms' rulers, [[Lore:Keirgo|Keirgo]] of Anequina and [[Lore:Eshita|Eshita]] of Pellitine, married and united their realms into the Elsweyr Confederacy. While this move sparked a tribal revolt in Anequina that threatened to sunder the two kingdoms once more, [[Lore:Mane|Mane]] [[Lore:Rid-Thar-ri'Datta|Rid-Thar-ri'Datta]] quelled the unrest by instituting a power-sharing system between the tribes and the nobility based on the phases of the moons [[Lore:Masser|Masser]] and [[Lore:Secunda|Secunda]].{{Ref|name=PGE1}}{{Ref|name=PGE3}}

By the time of the late [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], the Khajiit of southern Elsweyr were considered settled city dwellers with ancient mercantile traditions and a stable agrarian aristocracy based on sugarcane and saltrice plantations. Compared to the northern nomadic tribes clinging to traditions, the settled south had been quick to adopt Imperial ways.{{Ref|name=MorrowindDialogue|[[Morrowind:Generic Dialogue E|'''Elsweyr''' dialogue option]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}

In {{Year|4E 115}}, the [[Lore:Thalmor|Thalmor]] backed a coup that dissolved the Elsweyr Confederacy. In its place, the kingdoms of Anequina and Pelletine were reestablished and made client states of the third [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]].{{Ref|name=TGW|{{Cite Book|The Great War (book)}}}}

==Flora and Fauna==
The region is home to unique mammalian fauna such as [[Lore:Glyptodon|glyptodons]], giant armored herbivores, and [[Lore:Addax|addaxes]], a species of [[Lore:Antelope|Antelope]] with distinctive horns.
