The [[Lore:Great Forest|Great Forest]] is an immense crescent-shaped woodland region in [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] that extends around the [[Lore:Heartlands|Heartlands]] on three sides. The forest covers the area from just northwest of the city of [[Lore:Bravil|Bravil]], around the western border of the Heartlands, to just west of [[Lore:Cheydinhal|Cheydinhal]]. The outer edge of the Great Forest is bordered by the [[Lore:West Weald|West Weald]] to the south, the [[Lore:Colovian Highlands|Colovian Highlands]] to the west, and the [[Lore:Jerall Mountains|Jerall Mountains]] to the north. The city of [[Lore:Chorrol|Chorrol]] lies along the western border of the Great Forest and is the only city in this region. The terrain consists of rolling hills, and the region is dotted with caves, mines, abandoned forts, [[Lore:Ayleids|Ayleid]] ruins and Daedric shrines.{{ref|[[OB:Great Forest|Great Forest]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

Some parts of the Great Forest were cleared for agriculture around [[Lore:Lake Rumare|Lake Rumare]].{{ref|name=OBGeneric|[[Oblivion:Generic Dialogue|Generic wilderness dialogue]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} The isolated farming settlement of [[Lore:Harm's Folly|Harm's Folly]] is one such example.{{ref|name=OB|[[Oblivion:Harm's Folly|Harm's Folly]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} Outside of this, the region is mostly wilderness.{{ref|name=OBGeneric}}<noinclude>

[[Lore:Hircine|Hircine]] is said to appear in the Great Forest during the month of [[Lore:Rain's Hand|Rain's Hand]] in the form of the [[Lore:Great Dark Ram|Great Dark Ram]], a large black [[Lore:Goat|goat]] with red eyes.{{ref|[[ON:Great Dark Ram|Great Dark Ram]] mount in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}

Circa {{Year|2E 582}}, the effects of the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]] were evident in parts of the Great Forest with at least one known dolmen being present in the area.{{ref|[[ON:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]] zone in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}
