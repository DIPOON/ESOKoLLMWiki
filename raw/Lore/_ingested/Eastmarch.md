</noinclude>[[Lore:Eastmarch|Eastmarch]] is the eastern [[Lore:Hold|hold]] of {{nbsl|Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim}}, and one of the four known collectively as the [[Lore:Old Holds|Old Holds]]. As its name suggests, it lies against the province's eastern border, close to the [[Lore:Dunmeth Pass|Dunmeth Pass]], the only passage to and from [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]] in the area due to the high and rugged [[Lore:Velothi Mountains|Velothi Mountains]]. 

[[Lore:Windhelm|Windhelm]] is the only sizable city in the otherwise rural province due to the expansive hot springs which dominate the hold.{{ref|name=PGE1S|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Skyrim}}}} According to legend, these hot springs were created when the hero {{Lore Link|Wuunding}} fought a mighty Daedra and defeated it with a flaming hammer. The hammer's blows melted the Daedra, leaving behind the numerous hot springs.{{ref|name=AMOD|[[Online:Antique Map of Eastmarch|Antique Map of Eastmarch]] codex entry in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=WAT|{{Cite Book|Wuunding and Tumult}}}}

[[Lore:Darkwater River|Darkwater River]] winds north from Lake Geir into this treacherous hot spot, where it meets the [[Lore:White River|White River]] which then drains into the [[Lore:Sea of Ghosts|Sea of Ghosts]] as it passes Windhelm. [[Lore:River Yorgrim|River Yorgrim]] also joins with the White River before it ends at the Sea of Ghosts. It drains [[Lore:Lake Yorgrim|Lake Yorgrim]], which lies west in the hold known as [[Lore:The Pale|the Pale]].{{ref|name=Skyrim|Events of ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}<noinclude>
