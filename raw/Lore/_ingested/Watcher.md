[[Lore:Watcher|Watchers]] (also called ''Doom-Truths''){{ref|name=KCAC|[[Books:Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion Characters and Summary #Summerset|Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion, Summerset: Watcher]]}} are flying tentacled [[Lore:Daedra|Daedra]] with a large eye in the center of their body.{{ref|Appearance in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Watchers are quite nimble, and can shoot beams of energy from their large eye.{{ref|name=ESOBeh|[[ON:Behelir|Behelir]]'s description of Watchers during [[ON:Lost in Study|Lost in Study]]}}{{ref|Watchers' [[Online:Watcher#Skills and Abilities|abilities]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} They may be the most intelligent of the Daedra, and are known to have hierarchical ranks.{{Ref|group=UOL|[[ON:Cut Content|ESO Cut Content]] - [[ON:Apocrypha's Gate|Apocrypha's Gate]] dialogue}}  They are knowledge gatherers and minions found in the service of [[Lore:Hermaeus Mora|Hermaeus Mora]], modeled after the Prince's appearance. He often employs them as overseers and guardians, with them tending to 
his important concerns.{{ref|name=DoA}} However, they are known to serve other [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Princes]], such as [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]],{{ref|name=ESOYgg|Appearance of [[ON:Yggmanei|Yggmanei]] and other Watchers serving Molag Bal in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} [[Lore:Vaermina|Vaermina]],{{ref|name=ESOVaer|Appearance of [[ON:Vaermina's Watcher|Vaermina's Watcher]] and [[ON:Omen of Betrayal|other]] [[ON:Omen of Fear|Watchers]] serving Vaermina in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]],{{ref|name=ESOZili|Appearance of [[ON:Zilipif|Zilipif]]}} and [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]].{{ref|[[Online:Amaranthine Pocket Watcher|Amaranthine Pocket Watcher]] pet description in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}
Locking eyes with a Watcher is dangerous; a mortal's soul can be eaten away if one stares too long into their large central eye.{{ref|name=KCAC}} Some Watchers have a necrotic gaze, causing the skin on a mortal's face to tear away if they stare too long.{{ref|name=IPDR|{{Cite Book|Imperial Prison Discipline Records}}}} They are able separate the lies from the truth, which comes in handy to Mephala, adding to her powers of deception.{{Ref|name=Keshargo|[[ON:Keshargo|Keshargo]]'s dialogue}}
<noinclude>[[File:ON-creature-Cres.jpg|thumb|left|A green Watchling (ESO)]]</noinclude>
The term '''Watchling''' refers to watchers that are far smaller in size than the average watcher. Some of them consider the term an insulting diminutive. They apparently have a name for themselves, but it is not as commonly known.{{ref|name=ONScruut|[[Online:Scruut|Scruut]]'s dialogue in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}} Another term used to describle small watchers is '''Pocket Watcher'''. These are occasionally kept as pets or assistants by some [[Lore:Warlocks|warlocks]].{{Ref|name=PocketWatcher|[[Online:Pocket Watcher|Pocket Watcher]] pet description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}  

Watchlings or Pocket Watchers are no bigger than a human's fist and are not the offspring of Watchers. They are described as runts of their kind and curiosities as opposed to terrors. It is unknown whether they keep their form forever or eventually transform into the larger versions.{{ref|name=FAFOTS|{{Cite Book|Flora and Fauna of the Sever}}}} Hermaeus Mora frequently employs Watchlings as messengers and recorders, tasking them with obscure clerical duties in the hidden bureaucracy that quietly administers [[Lore:Apocrypha|Apocrypha]].{{ref|name=DoA|{{Cite Book|Denizens of Apocrypha}}}}<noinclude>

Most watchers, particularly those in service to Mora,{{Ref|name=TFG|Watchers in [[ON:The Flooded Grove|The Flooded Grove]] quest}} are varying shades of green with orange or cloudy eyes.{{ref|name=ESOBrol|Appearance of [[ON:Lord Brol|Lord Brol]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=CEPW|[[ON:Cipher's Eye Pocket Watcher|Cipher's Eye Pocket Watcher]] pet in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} The watchers that serve Molag Bal are typically grey with blue eyes.{{ref|name=ESOYgg}} Amaranthine Pocket Watchers are vibrantly hued white/purple watchers with purple eyes that once inhabited the ruins of {{Lore Link|Zaintiraris}} until an unfortunate explorer set them free. Given their color and origin, [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]] is suspected to hold some sway over them.{{Ref|name=APW|[[Online:Amaranthine Pocket Watcher|Amaranthine Pocket Watcher]] pet description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Powerful watchers can also be red with green eyes.{{ref|name=ESOAO|Appearance of [[ON:The Ancient One|The Ancient One]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Dagonic Watchers serve Mehrunes Dagon and are red in appearance with yellow eyes and nerves, similar in color to lava, and shoot searing beams of lava.{{ref|name=ESODW|Appearance of [[ON:Dagon's Witness|Dagon's Witness]]}} Those in service to Mephala are black with red eyes and highlights.{{Ref|name=Keshargo}}
