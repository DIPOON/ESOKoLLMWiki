Curse the {{Lore Link|Ebonheart Pact|Pact}} and its stupid rules! And curse that stiff-necked {{Lore Link|Dunmer|Dark Elf}} protector at the temple. You'd think we were offering to urinate on their precious {{Lore Link|Elder Scrolls|Elder Scroll}}, the way {{Lore Link|Protector Vodryn|Vodryn|v=1}} shrieked at us to get out.

Is offering quality goods at a fair price now a crime in {{Lore Link|Cyrodiil}}? True, there is some markup from the prices in the big city markets, but considering the travel time, dangers of the road, costs of guards, and lost time with our families—these goods are a bargain.

If the Pact doesn't want them, the {{Lore Link|Daggerfall Covenant|Covenant}} will surely buy them and be grateful. No doubt they'd also appreciate information on the disposition of Pact troops en route to Covenant-held territory. That might bring in more gold than our goods ever would. Must not get caught though.

Oh, for the days of the {{Lore Link|Empire}}. Curse these invaders!
