[[Lore:Phrygias|Phrygias]] was a barony on the western coast of [[Lore:High Rock|High Rock]], in the [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]] region.{{ref|name=Map|[[:File:DF-map-Iliac Bay (labeled).png|Map of Iliac Bay – ''The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall'']]}} It existed in the late [[Lore:First Era|First Era]],{{ref|name=2920Book|{{Cite Book|2920, The Last Year of the First Era}}}} as well as in the late [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]].{{ref|name=DF|Events of ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} It was surrounded by the [[Lore:Wrothgarian Mountains|Wrothgarian Mountains]] to the east, [[Lore:Dwynnen|Dwynnen]] and [[Lore:Urvaius|Urvaius]] to the south, and [[Lore:Ykalon|Ykalon]] to the west.{{ref|name=Map}} 

The [[Lore:Garlythi|Garlythi]] clan was the predominant [[Lore:Vampire|vampire]] bloodline, and the regional deity was [[Lore:Stendarr|Stendarr]]. The provincial seat was the city of Phrygias.{{ref|name=DF}} Phrygias was one of the primary members of a coalition that fought and defeated the forces of the [[Lore:Camoran Usurper|Camoran Usurper]] in the massive [[Lore:Battle of Firewaves|Battle of Firewaves]].{{ref|name=TFOTU|{{Cite Book|The Fall of the Usurper}}}}<noinclude>

The events of the [[Lore:Warp in the West|Warp in the West]] led to most of the forests in Phrygias being burned down.{{Ref|name=WITW|{{Cite Book|The Warp in the West}}}}
