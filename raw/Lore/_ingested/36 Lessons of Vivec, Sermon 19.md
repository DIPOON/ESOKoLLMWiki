[[Lore:Vivec|Vivec]] put on his armor and stepped into a non-spatial space filling to capacity with mortal interaction and information, a canvas-less cartography of every single mind it has ever known, an event that had developed some semblance of a divine spark. He said, {{nst|ON="|'}}From here I shall launch my attack on the eight monsters.{{nst|ON="|'}}

Vivec then saw the [[Lore:Imperial Legion|moths]] that would come from the [[Lore:Cyrodiil|starry heart]], bringing with them dust more horrible than the ash of [[Lore:Red Mountain|Red Mountain]]. He saw the twin head of a ruling king who had no equivalent. And [[Lore:Eight Divines|eight imperfections rubbed into precious stones]], set into a [[Lore:Amulet of Kings|crown that looked like shackles]], which he understood to be the twin crowns of the two-headed king. And a river that fed into the mouth of the two-headed king, because he contained multitudes.

Vivec then built the Provisional House at the Center of the Secret Door. From here he could watch the [[Lore:Third Era|age to come]]. Of the [[Lore:House of Troubles|House]] is written:

Cornerstone one has a finger<br>
Buried under, pointing through<br>
Dirt, slow low in the ground<br>
North cannot be guessed,<br>
And yet it is spirit-free

Cornerstone two has a tongue,<br>
And even dust can be talkative,<br>
Listen and you will see the love<br>
The ancient libraries need

Cornerstone three has a bit of string,<br>
Shaped like your favorite color,<br>
A girl remembers who left it there<br>
But she is afraid to dig it out,<br>
And see what it is attached to

Cornerstone four has nine bones,<br>
Removed carefully from a black cat,<br>
Arranged in the fashion of this word,<br>
Protecting us from our enemies

Your house is safe now<br>
So why is it{{nst|ON=&mdash;|--}}<br>
