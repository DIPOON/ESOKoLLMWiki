[[Lore:Flame Atronach|Flame Atronachs]] (sometimes known as '''Fire Atronachs''' or '''[[Lore:Fire Daedra|Fire Daedra]]'''){{ref|name=FAESO|[[ON:Fire Atronach|Fire Atronach]]s in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=MonsterExperts|[[Morrowind:Vala Catraso|Vala Catraso]] and [[Morrowind:Smokey Morth|Smokey Morth]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{ref|name=ATRQUEST|Dialogue in [[Daggerfall:Background_Information|Background Information]] quest in ''[[DF:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} are [[Lore:Elemental Daedra|Elemental Daedra]] native to [[Lore:Infernace|Infernace]], a collective realm full and made of lava and fire. Seemingly made entirely of flame, they can be found unbound in the wild or can be conjured by those with magical aptitude to serve as guards, laborers, or otherwise obey their conjurers. Summoning of Flame Atronachs is usually performed through [[Lore:Koron|Koron's]] [[Lore:Koron's Peremptory Summons|Peremptory Summons]], a spell which usually summons Flame Atronachs in their conventional forms but which can be altered by conjurers to alter the minutiae of the summons (such as plane to summon from, daedra to summon, and form in which the daedra can be conjured).{{ref|name=ESO CSCCSC|[[Lore:Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase/Crown Crates|Crown Crate Showcase]]}} <noinclude>

They are conventionally summoned and found in the form of vaguely feminine flaming floating humanoids; however they do appear in other forms—including a smaller form called the Flame Pixie. Their summoning was first perfected by the [[Lore:Direnni|Direnni]], especially [[Lore:Corvus Direnni|Corvus Direnni]] and [[Lore:Peregrine Direnni|Peregrine Direnni]], who discovered that they could be bound into different forms by their conjurers.{{ref|name=ESOFCC|[[Online:Online|ESO]]'s [[Online:Flame Atronach Crown Crate|Flame Atronach Crown Crate]] descriptions}} Flame Atronachs can be shaped by their conjuror to take the form of common animals to serve as mounts. Known varieties include bears, horses, guar, camels, senche-tigers and wolves.{{ref|Appearance and mount descriptions of [[Online:Flame Atronach Bear|Flame Atronach]] mounts in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The [[Lore:Laumont|Laumonts]], a Breton family line, is rumored to have originated from a union of [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] and Flame Atronach.{{ref|name=LudLauHireling|[[ON:Ludovic Laumont|Ludovic Laumont's hireling correspondence #203]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}. Similarly, it isn't unheard of for some conjurers to be attracted to Flame Atronachs.{{ref|name=LATFMDD|[[ON:Melyn Drad|Melyn Drad]]'s dialogue during [[ON:Love Among the Fire|Love Among the Fire]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]<nowiki>:</nowiki> ''[[Online:Blackwood (Chapter)|Blackwood]]''}}

Flame Atronachs are found in many Daedric Realms, including those of prominent princes and minor planes. While possessing some intelligence, Flame Atronachs are largely an unorganized and chaotic group, with individuals being largely indistinguishable from one another. They are a source of [[Lore:Alchemy F#Fire Salts|Fire Salts]], an alchemical ingredient which has varied utility.

Their inversions, [[Lore:Cold-Flame Atronach|Cold-Flame Atronachs]], originate in [[Lore:Fourth Sinus of Takubar|Fourth Sinus of Takubar]], an inversion of Infernace where all flame is cold.{{ref|name=DDCFA}}

===Potentially related===
There are some creatures which share many traits with Flame Atronachs. They have not been confirmed to be variants of atronachs or entirely different creatures altogether, leaving their nature ultimately unclear.
* Blazeborn Senche-lions are elemental felines that take a molten fiery appearance. They are described as being "born in flame".{{Ref|name=BBL|[[ON:Blazeborn Senche-lion|Blazeborn Senche-lion]] mount in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
* Dragonfire Wolves are colossal flaming wolves ridden by [[Lore:Dragonknight|Dragonknights]] into battle. It is speculated that they could be mystically imbued with [[Lore:Dragon#Uses|Dragonfire]] or are flame atronachs conjured in the form of wolves.{{Ref|name=Dragonfire Wolf|[[ON:Dragonfire Wolf|Dragonfire Wolf]] mount description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
* Flame Hounds are elemental wolves that are composed entirely from a lava-like substance. Much like Flame Atronachs, they are constantly burning and use fire-based attacks in combat. They also can be found in Daedric realms such as the Deadlands.{{ref|[[ON:Flame Hound|Flame Hounds]] in [[ESO:ESO|ESO]]}}

==Infernace==
'''[[Lore:Infernace|Infernace]]''', the collective realm of the Flame Atronachs, is described as mundane and repetitive, apparently being the aggregate of all of its inhabitants. It, much like the Flame Atronachs, is largely constructed of magma and flame, and is apparently the plane where most Flame Atronach summonings call. It has an inversion known as the Fourth Sinus of Takubar, which is apparently much like Infernace except totally devoid of heat.{{ref|name=DDCFA|{{Cite Book|name=DDCFA|Daedra Dossier: Cold-Flame Atronach}}}}
