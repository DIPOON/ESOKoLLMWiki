[[Lore:Ehlnofex|Ehlnofex]] is the ancient [[Lore:Linguistics|language]] of [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]], spoken by the [[Lore:Ehlnofey|Ehlnofey]]. It is used in magical incantations{{ref|name=JoaSM|{{Cite Book|Journal of a Stranded Mage|ns_base=ESO}}}} in uppercase letters. Several [[Lore:Elder Scrolls|Elder Scrolls]] were named after Ehlnofex words.{{intnote|CAPS|[nb 1]}}

==Usage, Language Family, and Genetic Relationship==
According to legend, before the creation of [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]], the Ehlnofey lived on one of the twelve worlds of Creation.{{ref|name=TAA|{{Cite Book|The Annotated Anuad}}}} Ehlnofex was related to the language of the [[Lore:Umbriel#Inhabitants|Umbrielans]], with speakers of the two tongues being able to converse with one another.{{ref|name=TIC|{{Cite Book|The Infernal City|ns_base=BOOKS}}}}{{rp|67}} The tongue was introduced to Nirn in the prehistoric, non-linear days of the [[Lore:Dawn Era|Dawn Era]].{{ref|name=MK|group=UOL}} The original Ehlnofex has been described as a dangerous language with characters that constantly distort as one looks at them.{{Ref|name=Thief|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|More on the Psijic Endeavor|ns_base=General}}}}

It was passed on to the mortal races, eventually evolving into [[Lore:Aldmeris (language)|Aldmeris]] and every subsequent [[Lore:Mer|Elvish]] tongue.{{ref|group=UOL|Brad Derrick, [https://forums.elderscrollsonline.com/en/discussion/comment/4160500/#Comment_4160500 SoundMaster on the official ESO forums], May 25, 2017}} It influenced [[Lore:Nordic|Atmoran]]—with the two tongues sharing the word "[[Lore:Talos|Talos]]"—and the subsequent tongues of [[Lore:Men|Men]],{{ref|name=PGE1C}} such as [[Lore:Nordic|Nordic]].{{ref|name=TAH|{{Cite Book|The Arcturian Heresy}}}}

Ehlnofex and the [[Lore:Dawn Era|Dawn Era]] magics of the [[Lore:Ehlnofey|Ehlnofey]] were preserved by the early [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleids]] in the [[Lore:Merethic Era#Middle Merethic Era|Middle Merethic Era]], when they separated from the [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]], allowing them to retain ancient runes and magic which the other elves had began to forget.{{ref|name=BtAoM|{{Cite Book|Before the Ages of Man}}}}{{ref|name=Descent|{{Cite Book|Ahzidal's Descent}}}} However, even their preservation eventually evolved into a new tongue known as [[Lore:Ayleidoon|Ayleidoon]].{{ref|[[ESO:Eld Angavar|Eld Angavar]] loading screen in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}} By the [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]], the language, while still studied, was considered dead.{{ref|name=TIC}}{{rp|47}}

[[Lore:Ulfsild the Evergreen|Archmage Ulfsild]]'s library beneath [[Lore:Eyevea|Eyevea]], rediscovered by the [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] around {{Year|2E 582}}, was theorized to hold texts in some previously unknown dialects of Ehlnofex.{{ref|name=TLttMG|{{Cite Book|Third Letter to the Mages Guild, Draft Copy|ns_base=ON}}}}
