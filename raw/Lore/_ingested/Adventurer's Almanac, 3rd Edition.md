Another day, another {{Lore Link|Gold|drake}}. That's the way I look at it and you should too if you've got any sense. So listen up, papa Skordo's got more wisdom to sell along with his sword. Here are my latest leads for the discerning mercenary. 

{{Lore Link|Repeatable_Quests#Summerset|Summerset|v=1}}

The {{Lore Link|Altmer|High-and-Mighty}} [[Lore:Mer|Elves]] of {{Lore Link|Summerset}} have finally {{Lore Link|An End to Isolation|deigned}} to let us mere mortals set foot on their unspoiled soil, but don't believe all the sugarcoated twaddle—the place has a heap of troubles. The {{Lore Link|Divine Prosecution|Justiciar}}s in {{Lore Link|Alinor (city)|ONlink=ON:Alinor}} are practically begging for competent mercenaries to come show'em how it's done. 

{{Lore Link|Repeatable_Quests#Murkmire|Murkmire|v=1}}

You ever heard of {{Lore Link|Cyrodilic Collections}}? Me neither. Not until they started throwing gold around like {{Lore Link|Jorunn|King Jorunn}} in a bawd house. I don't know where they're getting their {{Lore Link|Kassandra|financing|v=1}}, but they pay their dues on time. When you get to {{Lore Link|Lilmoth}}, tell them Skordo sent you. I want my cut. 

If running errands for a bunch of mudpanners doesn't excite you, the {{Lore Link|Dead-Water Tribe}} is organizing {{Lore Link|Death Hunts|v=1}} for some of the nastiest things in {{Lore Link|Murkmire}}. It'll get your blood pumping. Out of your neck if you're not careful. 

Maybe the idea of wading through a bog getting eaten alive by bugs doesn't appeal. Can't imagine why. You can still get your dainty hands on some of that easy Cyrodilic Collections gold. You can find an {{Lore Link|Argonian}} organizing supplies for their expeditions. Goes by {{sic|{{Lore Link|Jee-Lar|Jee Lar|v=1}}|Jee-Lar}}. He'll talk your ear off if you let him, but he's always got {{Lore Link|Repeatable_Quests#Cyrodilic_Collections|something|v=1}} that needs doing. 

{{Lore Link|Repeatable_Quests#Northern_Elsweyr_Defense_Force|Dragon|v=1}}s 

Finally, there're tusking {{Lore Link|Dragon}}s raging across {{Lore Link|Northern Elsweyr|Elsweyr}}. If you're just getting this news from me, get out from under the rock you call home and get to work! The {{Lore Link|Northern Elsweyr Defense Force|v=1}} is recruiting any help they can get for their war effort against the scaly bastards. They've got people stationed in {{Lore Link|Daggerfall}}, {{Lore Link|Davon's Watch}}, and {{Lore Link|Vulkhel Guard}}. 

Now I ain't heard much more than rumors about Dragon hunting, but you can bet there's someone out there itching to bag a Dragon and willing to offer a reward. Personally, you couldn't pay me enough to tangle with one of those flying murder-lizards—though you're welcome to try. 

There you have it. Plenty of work for an enterprising adventurer-for-hire. Remember papa Skordo when you strike it rich. And if you strike it dead, go haunt the sorry sack who taught you to fight.
