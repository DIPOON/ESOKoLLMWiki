[[Lore:Morthal|Morthal]], supposedly named after the great Nord hero [[Lore:Morihaus|Morihaus]],{{Ref|name=LoadingScreens|Skyrim [[Skyrim:Loading_Screens#Morthal|loading screen]]}}{{ref|name=Banner|[[Blades:Morthal Banner|Morthal Banner Decoration]] in ''[[BL:Blades|Blades]]''}} is the capital of [[Lore:Hjaalmarch|Hjaalmarch]]. It is a fairly small, humble town of little economic or strategic importance besides its small lumber mill.{{Ref|name=THOS|{{Cite Book|The Holds of Skyrim}}}} The town's symbol is a white [[Lore:Triskelion|triskelion]] on a field of gray reminiscent of its misty marshes. The triskelion's meaning, whether it represents the harmony of the mind, body, and spirit, or another concept entirely, was a topic of scholarly discussion.{{ref|name=Banner}}{{Ref|name=MBH|[[ON:Morthal Banner, Hanging|Morthal Banner, Hanging]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]<nowiki>:</nowiki> ''[[Online:Greymoor|Greymoor]]''}} Tattoos featuring the Morthal triskelion were reserved solely for individuals who have valiantly defended Morthal and its inhabitants.{{Ref|name=MCBM|[[ON:Morthal Champion Body Markings|Morthal Champion Body Markings]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Sitting on the southern edge of the [[Lore:Drajkmyr|Drajkmyr marsh]],{{ref|[[Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide|The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide]]}} dry ground can be hard to come by in Morthal. It is often shrouded in a thick fog. Ominous twisted trees grow from the surrounding marsh, giving it a foreboding appearance to approaching travelers. The torches of the town glowing in the fog often give the impression of wisps at a distance. Many choose to steer clear of the place altogether.{{Ref|Rumors in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}<noinclude>

Morthal's small population and relatively remote location have kept it distant from most major conflicts in its history.{{Ref|name=LoadingScreens}} The citizens of Morthal are known to be reclusive and cautious of outsiders. Many alchemists travel to Morthal and its surrounding marshes to make use of the plethora of rare and useful ingredients available. Bull Mastiffs from Morthal are fearless and tireless hunters inured to the hardships of the frozen marshes, and are owned as pets by many across the land.{{Ref|name=ONMorthalMastiff|[[Online:Black Morthal Mastiff|Black]], [[Online:Gray Morthal Mastiff|Gray]], and [[Online:Tan Morthal Mastiff|Tan Morthal Mastiff]] descriptions}}

Residents of Morthal have passed down the legend of the Pale Lady for untold years.{{Ref|name=LL|{{Cite Book|Lost Legends}}}} They tell of a dangerous ghost woman who prowls the marshes to the north of town seeking her lost daughter, who is thought to be linked to "Aumriel" (mentioned in ancient records as a foe defeated by the descendants of [[Lore:Ysgramor|Ysgramor]]).{{Ref|name=LL}} As the legend goes, the Pale Lady steals daughters because she lost her own. Some believe that if they carve wooden statues beautiful enough, she might return their lost children to them.{{Ref|name=Skalferth|[[ON:Skalferth|Skalferth]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Greymoor|Greymoor]]''}}
