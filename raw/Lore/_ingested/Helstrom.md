[[Lore:Helstrom|Helstrom]] is one of the eight major cities in the province of [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]],{{Ref|name=ARMap|[[:File:AR-map-Black Marsh towns.png|Map of Black Marsh – ''The Elder Scrolls: Arena'']]}} located in the reputedly inhospitable center of the province called [[Lore:Middle Argonia|Middle Argonia]]. Many consider Helstrom the immortal capital of the [[Lore:Argonians|Argonians]].{{Ref|name=ONAntiCirclet|[[Online:Blackwood Antiquities#Ritual Circlet|Ritual Circlet]] antiquity codex entries in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Greymoor|Greymoor]]''}} Located in the interior of Black Marsh, which is generally held to be "impenetrable" by non-Argonian armies,{{Ref|name=PGE3E|{{Cite Book|Pocket Guide to the Empire, 3rd Edition/Argonia}}}} the city itself is notorious for its perilous reputation and the city's guard is reportedly dangerous.{{Ref|name=AR|[[Arena:Helstrom|Helstrom]] location and rumors in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}<noinclude>

==Layout and Geography==
[[File:ON-pet-Helstrom Ancestor Lizard.jpg|thumb|right|The Ancestor Lizard of Helstrom]]
The marsh of Helstrom is very dense and heavily consumed by Heart-Mud, which is reportedly very dark in color.{{Ref|name=NN|{{Cite Book|Neelo's Notes|ns_base=ON}}}} Some creatures in the region have their names attributed to the city, such as the Helstrom [[Lore:Ancestor Lizard|Ancestor Lizard]], which some scholars speculated to be the progenitors of the Argonian race,{{Ref|name=ONLizard|[[Online:Helstrom Ancestor Lizard|Helstrom Ancestor Lizard]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} as well as the Helstrom [[Lore:Crocodile|Crocodile]], an incredibly large variant that went extinct during the [[Lore:Great Burn|Great Burn]] of {{Year|1E 2828}} in the Blackwater War. Their skulls were used by the [[Lore:Dead-Water Tribe|Dead-Water Tribe]] as a trap for either animals or people.{{Ref|name=ONAntiSkull|[[Online:Murkmire Antiquities#Naga Skull Trap|Naga Skull Trap]] antiquity codex entries in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Greymoor|Greymoor]]''}} Its alleys are considered both wonderful and dangerous.{{Ref|name=TA|{{Cite Book|The Alik'r}}}} Helstrom is neighbored by several settlements, including Alten Markmont to the southeast, Greenspring to the southwest, and [[Lore:Tenmar Wall|Tenmar Wall]] to the north.{{Ref|name=ARMap}}

==History==
Helstrom as a city has been reportedly active since at least the late [[Lore:First Era|First Era]], concurrent to the [[Lore:Second Empire|Second Empire]]'s invasion of Argonia, known as the [[Lore:Blackwater War|Blackwater War]].{{Ref|name=TBW1|{{Cite Book|The Blackwater War, Volume 1}}}} The [[Lore:Battle of Argonia|Battle of Argonia]] in {{Year|1E 2811}} was a one-sided conflict between the [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial Legion]] and the Argonians, the latter suffering severe casualties. The defending forces retreated to the interior of Black Marsh toward Helstrom, during which the Empire refused to follow nor ever intended to siege. The Empire was content to incorporate the border and coastal regions into its territory rather than the interior.{{Ref|name=SCOS|{{Cite Book|Stormhold, City of Shadowfen}}}} What followed after was the Blackwater War proper, where the Empire eventually managed to take even portions of the interior swamp with the temporary assistance of pirates recruited off the coasts near Lilmoth and Archon.{{Ref|name=TBW1}} Though the Empire's progress from Gideon in the west eventually ground to a halt outside the core of Black Marsh in 1E 2834, in 1E 2836 the Argonians abruptly stopped fighting with no explanation, and by {{Year|1E 2837}} the entire region was claimed by the Empire and formally founded as the Imperial Province of Black Marsh.{{Ref|name=TBW7|{{Cite Book|The Blackwater War, Volume 7}}}}

The city is mentioned in passing in a book called ''[[Lore:The Alik'r|The Alik'r]]'', which was written by the western scribe, [[Lore:Enric Milres|Enric Milres]] in the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]].{{Ref|name=TA}} Helstrom became a fully incorporated city-state of the [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]] and settlements were built deep in the interior by the fourth century of the Third Era. During the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]] in the late [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], the city-state of Helstrom was an active settlement. It was ruled by King Germanus and had a rivalry with [[Lore:Stormhold|Stormhold]].{{Ref|name=AR}}
