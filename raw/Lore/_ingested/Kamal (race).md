# Lore:Kamal (race)

The [[Lore:Kamal (race)|Kamal]] (or '''Kamali'''){{ref|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 18: Akaviri Style}}}} are a race of "snow demons" who live in [[Lore:Akavir|Akavir]].{{Ref|name=PGTTE3OL|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Other Lands}}}} The word means "Snow Hell".{{Ref|name=MA|{{Cite Book|Mysterious Akavir}}}} According to ''[[Lore:Mysterious Akavir|Mysterious Akavir]]'', the snow demons spend the winter frozen solid, but, when summer comes, they thaw out and attack [[Lore:Tang Mo (place)|Tang Mo]], always unsuccessfully.{{Ref|name=MA}}<noinclude>

==History== 
</noinclude>
In {{Year|2E 572}},{{Ref|name=GTTEP}} the Kamal invaded [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] under their king, [[Lore:Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal|Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal]],{{Ref|name=PGTTE1S|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Skyrim}}}} reportedly searching for a person or object they called the "[[Lore:Ordained Receptacle|Ordained Receptacle]]".{{ref|name=TSAI|{{Cite Book|The Second Akaviri Invasion}}}} The Kamal fleet bypassed [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]] entirely and landed near [[Lore:Windhelm|Windhelm]] in Eastern [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]], catching the [[Lore:Nord|Nord]]s off-guard and sacking the city.{{ref|name=JtSK|{{Cite Book|Jorunn the Skald-King (book)}}}} Oddly, the Kamal army then headed south but ignored [[Lore:Riften|Riften]], marching into the [[Lore:Stonefalls|Stonefalls]] region of Morrowind. There, on a beach just to the west of [[Lore:Ebonheart|Ebonheart]] that would later be named [[Lore:Vivec's Antlers|Vivec's Antlers]],{{ref|name=ESO|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} they were surrounded by a [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]] army led by [[Lore:Almalexia|Almalexia]] and a Nord army led by Prince [[Lore:Jorunn|Jorunn]].{{ref|name=TSAI}}{{ref|name=JtSK}} The Kamal fought ferociously, hoping to last long enough for their fleet to arrive and evacuate them, but, at the last moment, a phalanx of [[Lore:Argonian|Argonian]]s joined the fray. With the aid of a tidal wave summoned by [[Lore:Vivec (god)|Vivec]],{{ref|name=ESO}} the Kamal were obliterated by the three unlikely allies.{{ref|name=TSAI}}{{ref|name=JtSK}}{{ref|name=GTTEP|{{Cite Book|Guide to the Ebonheart Pact}}}}

This was not the last Tamriel would see of the Kamal, however. Sometime before {{Year|2E 812}}, remnants of the Kamal invasion force who had somehow survived the slaughter at Vivec's Antlers resurfaced in [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]], seizing the Ruby Throne from the successors of the warlord [[Lore:Attrebus (warlord)|Attrebus]]. The Kamal were soon joined by the [[Lore:Akaviri|Akaviri]] of [[Lore:Rimmen|Rimmen]], who had previously fled to [[Lore:Elsweyr|Elsweyr]] to avoid Attrebus' persecution. The Kamal and Rimmen Akaviri attempted to rebuild the [[Lore:Empire|Empire]], but the effort was doomed to failure.{{Ref|name=PGTTE1E|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|The Elsweyr Confederacy}}}}{{FMI}}<noinclude>
