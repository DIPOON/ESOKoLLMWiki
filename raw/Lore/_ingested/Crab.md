[[Lore:Crab|Crab]]s are crustaceans that possess two front-facing appendages with claws on the end. There are several known species of crab, with the most prevalent being the [[Lore:Mudcrab|mudcrab]]. Other crabs are typically considered lesser than mudcrabs in aspects such as ferocity.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Corpse Preparation}}}} Most crabs have hard shells, but softshell varieties also exist.{{ref|[[Online:Xukas#Deepmire|Xukas]]' dialogue at the Hall of Memories during [[Online:The Swamp and the Serpent|The Swamp and the Serpent]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Murkmire (DLC)|Murkmire]]''}} Crabs of all species can grow to absurdly large sizes.{{ref|Appearance of the [[Online:Queen of the Reef|Queen of the Reef]] (a giant coral crab) in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}{{ref|Appearance of [[Online:Titanclaw|Titanclaw]] (a giant mudcrab) in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|Appearance of [[Online:Shellcracker|Shellcracker]] (a giant blue sandcrab) in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

===Sessile Crab===
Sessile crabs are crustaceans that lived in the submerged sections of [[Lore:Umbriel|Umbriel]]'s Marrow Sump. They have squat, thorny bodies, with venomous claws on the end of twenty-foot-long yellow and viridian tentacles.  They do not locomote, and instead attach themselves to the bottom of the body of water they inhabit, moving their long tentacles slowly through the water to try and catch prey. They prey upon quick [[Lore:Fish N#Nickfish|nickfish]], which the toxins in the sessile crab's claws can kill almost instantly.{{ref|name=TIC|{{Cite Book|The Infernal City|ns_base=Books}}}}{{rp|189}}
