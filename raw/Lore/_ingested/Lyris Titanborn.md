'''Lyris Titanborn''' was a [[Lore:Nord|Nord]] with [[Lore:Giant|giant]] ancestry{{ref|name=ESOLF|[[Online:Gjalder|Gjalder]]'s (Lyris' father) dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=ESOMurthin|[[ON:Captain Murthin|Captain Murthin]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} and the personal bodyguard of Emperor [[Lore:Varen Aquilarios|Varen Aquilarios]]. She easily towers over other members of her race and dwarfs even the tallest race in Tamriel, the High Elves.

Lyris was born to Alyr Golden-Hair and the giant-blooded Nord, Gjalder. Her mother died during childbirth, leaving her father to raise his considerably large infant daughter alone.{{ref|name=MTCLT|{{Cite Book|Meet the Character - Lyris Titanborn}}}} At the age of fifteen, she ran away from home and joined the [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial Legion]]. She faced endless ridicule from her peers due to her large stature.{{ref|name=MTCLT}}{{ref|name=DOG|Events of [[ON:Daughter of Giants|Daughter of Giants]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} She was hated and feared by some, but she endured, and eventually met Varen Aquilarios.{{ref|name=MTCLT}} She subsequently aided him in his revolution, and joined him, [[Lore:Mannimarco|Mannimarco]], [[Lore:Abnur Tharn|Abnur Tharn]] and [[Lore:Sai Sahan|Sai Sahan]] as one of the [[Lore:Five Companions|Five Companions]] in search of the [[Lore:Amulet of Kings|Amulet of Kings]].{{ref|name=MTCLT}}

She was imprisoned in [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] following the calamity known as the [[Lore:Soulburst|Soulburst]]. With Varen's disappearance during the event, she and Sai Sahan were suspected of murdering him and stealing the [[Lore:Amulet of Kings|Amulet of Kings]].{{ref|name=TCC|{{Cite Book|The Chorrol Crier}}}} She and Sai had romantic feelings for one another, with Sai giving her the nickname "'''Snow Lily'''".{{Ref|name=ESOSai|[[Online:Sai Sahan#The Dragonguard's Legacy|Sai Sahan]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

In Coldharbour, she led the [[Lore:Soul Shriven|Soul Shriven]] in an uprising against their captors, and together with [[Lore:The Vestige|the Vestige]], they fought their way to save Lyris' friend and ally, the Prophet. She offered herself in place of the Prophet to enable him and the Vestige escape from the realm.{{ref|name=SSIC|Events of [[ON:Soul Shriven in Coldharbour|Soul Shriven in Coldharbour]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} They eventually returned to save her, and the three banded together in a quest to save [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] from the invasion of [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]].{{ref|name=DOG}}{{ref|name=ESO|Events of [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

When the time came to power the Amulet of Kings, Lyris offered to sacrifice herself for the ritual. It is unknown whether she was chosen, but if she had died, she woke up in a cold foggy location outside of Tamriel (she also jokingly said she was sent back to Coldharbour, and had to break out of the realm once again). She went inside the warm warrior-filled longhouse that she believed was the {{Lore Link|Hall of Valor}} in [[Lore:Sovngarde|Sovngarde]], staying there for an unknown amount of time, before waking up one day and finding herself in Skyrim.{{Ref|name=ESOLyris|[[Online:Lyris Titanborn#The Coven Conspiracy|Lyris Titanborn]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} After Molag Bal's defeat, Lyris briefly stayed at the Abbey of Blades to help Sai Sahan rebuild it. Sai hoped she would join him in his efforts to reclaim the wisdom of the Dragonguard, but Lyris felt another calling pull her away, back to Skyrim.{{Ref|name=ESOSai}} Lyris worked as an agent for [[Lore:Jorunn|King Jorunn]],{{ref|name=ESOGM}} and served as one of the strongest of his sword-thanes,{{ref|name=Arvnir|[[ON:Arvnir|Arvnir]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} helping to investigate the vampiric threat of the Icereach Coven and their associates in the [[Lore:Haafingar|west]].{{ref|name=ESOGM|Events of the [[ON:Greymoor|Greymoor]] story quests in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
