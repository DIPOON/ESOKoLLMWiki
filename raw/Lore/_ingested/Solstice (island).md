[[Lore:Solstice (island)|Solstice]] (or '''Oztet-Ta''' ''"Sprouting Root"'' in [[Lore:Jel|Jel]]){{Ref|name=TAECS|{{Cite Book|The Amber Egg and Cerulean Sea}}}}{{Ref|name=SST|[[ON:Stone-Shaper Tozka|Stone-Shaper Tozka]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} is an isolated tropical island within the [[Lore:Southern Sea|Southern Sea]],{{Ref|name=JusticeTheFallen|[[ON:Justice for the Fallen|Justice for the Fallen]] quest in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=Jubilee2025|[[ON:Jubilee Cake 2025|Jubilee Cake 2025]] description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=TaS|{{Cite Book|The Turtle and the Sloth|ns_base=ON}}}} located south of [[Lore:Murkmire|Murkmire]] in [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]].{{Ref|group=UOL|name=ESODirect2025|[[General:2025 ESO Direct|2025 ESO Direct]] - [https://youtu.be/6lSVd1cR8w0?t{{=}}1794 29:54]}} The island has a sizeable [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]] population who largely descended from settlers that arrived with the [[Lore:Corelanya Clan|Corelanya Clan]].{{ref|name=HoCC|{{Cite book|History of Clan Corelanya}}}}<!--Could use a more specific source--> The [[Lore:Argonian|Argonians]] that inhabit the island came before the [[Lore:Duskfall|Duskfall]] period occurred.{{ref|group=UOL|[https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2466390916 Twitch Stream of ''Solstice Walkthrough w/ Devs''], time stamp 49:19-50:50}} It has been described as a "paradise".{{Ref|name=Jubilee2025}}<noinclude>

In the aftermath of the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]], the [[Lore:Worm Cult|Worm Cult]] was reported to be amassing forces on the island of Solstice in preparation for a massive attack. Though its location was unknown to the population of Tamriel at the time, the [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] vowed to locate the island and stop them.{{Ref|name=JusticeTheFallen}}
