# Lore:Skooma

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

<noinclude>{{Lore Item Trail|Drug}}[[File:MW-item-Moon Sugar and Skooma.jpg‎|thumb|right|Moon sugar, a skooma pipe and skooma (Morrowind)]]{{TOCright}}</noinclude>
[[Lore:Skooma|Skooma]] is a hallucinatory narcotic refined from [[Lore:Moon Sugar|moon sugar]].{{ref|name=EGTE|{{Cite Book|EGT|Elsweyr}}}} Extremely addictive, its victims are left in constant, alternating states of delusional euphoria and lethargy.{{ref|name=PGE1Elsweyr|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Elsweyr}}}} Sustained use of the drug seemingly results in permanent confusion and mental deterioration,{{ref|[[OB:Atraena|Atraena]]'s dialogue in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}{{ref|name=OBSkoomaDen|[[Oblivion:Skooma Den|Skooma Den]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}{{ref|[[SI:Caldana Monrius|Caldana Monrius]]' dialogue in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]: [[Shivering:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]]''}}as well as loss of teeth.{{ref|[[Online:Pacrooti]]'s shipment notes in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

The drug can be smoked through a water pipe or consumed in liquid form; some of the apparatus used can be quite elaborate.{{ref|name=PGE1Elsweyr}}{{ref|name=OBSkoomaDen}} When poor quality ingredients—like moldy moon sugar—are used in its production, the resulting "tainted" skooma has more extreme side effects including death. As spoiled moon sugar is cheaper to obtain, this practice is regrettably common.{{ref|name=ONKahiri|[[Online:Guard Captain Kahiri|Guard Captain Kahiri]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Elsweyr|ESO]]}} Regardless of the quality of the skooma consumed, withdrawal from the drug can have mental effects with violent consequences.{{ref|name=SFK|[[Skyrim:Elder Knowledge#The Lost Expedition|Elder Knowledge]] quest in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}<noinclude>

==History, Culture, and Mythology==
[[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]], [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Prince]] of Madness, is thus known as the [[Lore:Skooma Cat|Skooma Cat]] among the [[Lore:Khajiit|Khajiit]], "for what is crazier than a cat on skooma?"{{ref|name=WCMA|{{Cite Book|Words of Clan Mother Ahnissi}}}} Some stories about the Mad God claim that skooma is the medium which the Prince speaks through the protection of the [[Lore:Lunar Lattice|Lunar Lattice]].{{ref|name=ONHaduras|[[ON:Priest Haduras|Priest Haduras]]' dialogue in [[Online:Elsweyr|ESO]]}} Because of the prevalence of moon sugar in Khajiiti culture, skooma addiction has proven to be a particular menace for Khajiit with a "sweet tooth".{{ref|[[ON:Azbi-ra|Azbi-ra]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{fact}} However, the "sacred sugar" as it is also referred to, does have use in certain Khajiit ceremonies, where it is strictly controlled by the Moon Bishops.{{ref|name=EGTE}} The visions seen during these rituals are seen as messages from the [[Lore:Jode|Twin]] [[Lore:Jone|Moon]]s, but the user runs the risk of attracting Sheogorath's attention.{{ref|name=ONSizenza|[[ON:Moon-Bishop Sizenza|Moon-Bishop Sizenza]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Elsweyr|ESO]]}}{{ref|[[Online:The Lunacy of Two Moons|The Lunacy of Two Moons]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

In the [[Lore:Fourth Era|Fourth Era]], it is said that the drug was originally developed by the [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]].{{ref|name=SKWujeeta|[[Skyrim:Wujeeta|Wujeeta]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} The drug has been banned throughout history by both the [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]] and the [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]].

Skooma is typically smuggled along with moon sugar.{{ref|name=WSSH|{{Cite Book|Wayrest Sewers: A Short History}}}} However, a seemingly skooma-specific smuggling route existed between [[Lore:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]] and [[Lore:Vivec (city)|Vivec]] in the [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]].{{ref|name=CDJ1|{{Cite Book|Cap'n Dugal's Journal, Part 1}}}} The traffic of skooma has led to violent criminal conflicts both individual and organized.{{ref|name=SKJiub|[[Skyrim:Jiub|Jiub]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}{{ref|name=OBCJ|{{Cite Book|Cicero's Journal}}}}

==Refinement and Effects==
The liquids used to refine skooma from moon sugar are highly flammable.{{ref|name=LW|{{Cite Book|Laboratory Warning: Be Careful!|ns_base=ON}}}} Skooma is used to hide the taste of [[Lore:Redmaiden|Redmaiden]], a drug for [[Lore:Nirn|Nirnborn]] mortals to keep themselves sane for a short time in the daedric realm of [[Lore:Fargrave|Fargrave]].{{Ref|name=IOTS|{{Cite Book|Intoxicants of the Shambles}}}}

Although addiction to skooma is commonly thought to be incurable,{{ref|name=PGE1Elsweyr}} cures have been achieved through both alchemy and the elimination of the addict's own self-hatred.{{ref|name=SKHH|[[Skyrim:Helping Hand|Helping Hand]] quest in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}{{ref|name=CSE|{{Cite Book|Confessions of a Skooma-Eater}}}} Some medical practices, such as the application of numbing agents, are said to relieve the symptoms of withdrawal.{{ref|name=NumbingNeedles|[[ON:Contraband N#Numbing Needles|Numbing Needles]] item description}}

===Variants===
Balmora Blue is a highly illegal variety made primarily from Moon Sugar, with many other ingredients added later in the process to increase its potency. When [[Lore:Balmora|Balmora]] was in its prime before the [[Lore:Red Year|Red Year]], Balmora Blue was a lucrative underworld commodity, and its value became exponentially higher when the city was destroyed.{{Ref|name=MyBuddyErikur|[[Skyrim:Erikur|Erikur]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}

In the [[Lore:Fourth Era#Third Century|third century]] of the Fourth Era, Redwater Skooma, a far more potent form of skooma, was refined using water from the [[Lore:Redwater Spring|Bloodspring of Lengeir's Feast]] in [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]]. It was used by [[Lore:Vampire|vampires]] to exploit skooma addicts for blood.{{ref|name=SKVVR|{{Cite Book|Venarus Vulpin's Research}}}}
