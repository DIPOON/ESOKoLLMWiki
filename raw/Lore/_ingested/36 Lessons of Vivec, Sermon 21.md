The Scripture of the Wheel, First:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The Spokes are the [[Lore:Eight Divines|eight components]] of [[Lore:Aurbis|chaos]], as yet solidified by the [[Lore:Akatosh|law of time]]: static change, if you will, something the lizard gods refer to as the Striking. That is the reptile wheel, coiled potential, ever-preamble to the never-action.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Second:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}They are the lent bones of the [[Lore:Aedra|Aedra]], the Eight gift-limbs to [[Lore:Sithis|SITHISIT]], the wet earth of the new star our home. Outside them is the [[Lore:Aurbis|Aurbis]], and not within. Like most things inexplicable, it is a circle. Circles are [[Lore:Satakal|confused]] [[Lore:Sep|serpents]], striking and striking and never given leave to bite. The Aedra would have you believe different, but they were givers before liars. Lies have turned them into biters. Their teeth are the proselytizers; to convert is to place oneself in the mouth of falsehood; even to propitiate is to be swallowed.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Third:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The enlightened are those [[Lore:Satakal|uneaten]] by the world.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Fourth:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The [[Lore:Planes of Oblivion|spaces between the gift-limbs]] number sixteen, the signal shapes of the [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Demon Princedoms]]. It is the key and the lock, series and manticore.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Fifth: 

:{{nst|'|ON="}}Look at the majesty sideways and all you see is {{Lore Link|the Towers|the Tower}}, which [[Lore:Aldmer|our ancestors]] made idols from. Look at [[Lore:Mundus|its center]] and all you see is the begotten hole, [[Lore:Lorkhan|second serpent]], womb-ready for the Right Reaching, exact and without enchantment.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Sixth:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The [[Lore:Heart of Lorkhan|heart]] of the second serpent holds the secret triangular gate.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Seventh:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}Look at the secret triangular gate sideways and you see the secret Tower.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Eighth:

:{{nst|'|ON="}}The secret Tower within the Tower is the shape of the only name of God, [[Lore:CHIM|I]].{{nst|'|ON="}}

The ending of the words is [[Lore:Almsivi|ALMSIVI]].
