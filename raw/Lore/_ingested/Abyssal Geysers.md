# Lore:Abyssal Geysers

<noinclude>{{Trail|Magic}}__NOTOC__[[File:ON-place-Corgrad Abyssal Geyser.jpg|thumb|right|An Abyssal Geyser being opened by an acolyte of the Court of Bedlam]]</noinclude>
[[Lore:Abyssal Geysers|Abyssal Geysers]] were a magical phenomenon that occurred on the coasts of [[Lore:Summerset Isle|Summerset Isle]] during its invasion by the [[Lore:Court of Bedlam|Court of Bedlam]] and their [[Lore:Sea Sload|Sea Sload]] allies, the Abyssal Cabal, in {{Year|2E 582}}. By [[Lore:Alchemy|alchemically]] treating and applying magical sigils to sea gems called Abyssal Pearls, the Sload were able to conjure unnatural spouts of water from the sea, which lured [[Lore:Yaghra|Yaghra]] and various forms of undead.{{ref|name=Indirim|{{Cite Book|Indirim's Journal, Assembled}}}}{{ref|name=PRN|{{Cite Book|Pearl Research Notes, Log 3}}}} [[Lore:Altmer|High Elf]] acolytes of the Court of Bedlam communicated with the Cabal,{{ref|name=Indirim}} and would carry the Pearls to particular sites to begin the process of opening the Geysers.{{ref|[[Online:Acolyte of Bedlam|Acolytes of Bedlam]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} The Sload hoped to use the Geysers to sink Summerset itself into the sea.{{ref|name=Indirim}}{{ref|name=Grog|[[ON:Grog|Grog]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}<noinclude>

==History==
A group of adventurers and [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]] marines dispatched to investigate rumors of a Sea Sload incursion in Summerset made the initial discovery of the Geysers. This expedition was attacked by Yaghra and the marines were slain.{{ref|name=Miriya|[[ON:Miriya|Miriya]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} One of them had compiled notes on the phenomenon, however, which were subsequently reassembled and retrieved.{{ref|name=Indirim}}{{ref|name=TAC|Events of [[ON:The Abyssal Cabal|The Abyssal Cabal]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} With the bulk of the Dominion's forces occupied in the [[Lore:Three Banners War|Three Banners War]], the proxy government in [[Lore:Alinor (city)|Alinor]] relied on the help of adventurers and the local garrison to suppress the Geysers.{{ref|name=Tanerline|[[ON:Battlereeve Tanerline|Battlereeve Tanerline]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}

The Geysers were initially overseen by the Sload K'Tora, but were later directed by [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]] after K'Tora's death at the hands of the [[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic Order]].{{ref|[[ON:K'Tora|K'Tora]] and [[ON:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]]'s dialogue at [[ON:Abyssal Geysers|Abyssal Geysers]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}{{ref|name=BM|Events of [[ON:Buried Memories|Buried Memories]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} Another Sload, the alchemist [[Lore:B'Korgen|B'Korgen]],{{ref|name=Farowel|[[ON:Justiciar Farowel|Justiciar Farowel]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} saw to the preparation of the Pearls.{{ref|name=Indirim}}
