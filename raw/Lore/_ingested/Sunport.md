[[Lore:Sunport|Sunport]] is the [[Lore:Clan Corelanya|Corelanyan]] capital city on the eastern coast of [[Lore:Sunport Bay|Sunport Bay]]. It is said to rival the metropolises of the [[Lore:Tamriel|mainland]] and [[Lore:Pyandonea|Pyandonea]]. Sunport was originally a far-flung trading outpost of the clan, but was built up into a city after they lost their holdings in [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]] and were exiled from [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset]], becoming the home of the remaining Corelanya Clan. 

The city was built upon '''Tluthik-Tzel''',{{Ref|name=SST|[[ON:Stone-Shaper Tozka|Stone-Shaper Tozka]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} a temple belonging to the [[Lore:Argonian|Argonian]]s who already lived on the island, which can still be seen throughout the city to this day. During the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]], Sunport was under the rulership of [[Lore:Karinwe Corelanya|Regent Karinwe]], who ruled from [[Lore:Vinutilmo Palace|Vinutilmo Palace]] within the Palace District on the eastern side of the city.

The Sunport Docks are outside the western walls, and are the transportation hub for passage off Solstice. The roads going north from the city lead to [[Lore:Deetra Grotto|Deetra Grotto]] and the [[Lore:Li-Xal Pass|Li-Xal Pass]].{{Ref|name=VGTWS|{{Cite Book|Visitor's Guide to Western Solstice}}}}{{Ref|name=HoCC|{{Cite Book|History of Clan Corelanya}}}}<noinclude>

==Layout and Geography==
[[File:ON-place-Sunport 02.jpg|thumb|left|Sunport gates]][[File:ON-place-Palace District.jpg|thumb|right|Palace District]]
Open square, plazas, and gardens fill Sunport which feature an impressive array of architecture: Altmer construction atop ancient Argonian foundations. Artisans and crafters are everyone, working and selling from open-air stalls and inside quaint shops distributed throughout the city. 

The Cathedral of the Three Queens serves as the Sunporter house of worship, and shrines inside the holy building are dedicated to not only the revered [[Lore:Azura|Azura]], [[Lore:Meridia|Meridia]], and [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]], but to various [[Lore:Divines|Divines]] as well.

On the Eastern side of the city is the Palace District, where one can gaze upon the impressive Vinutilmo Palace, seat of power of Regent Karinwe Corelanya and her advisors. The temporary headquarters of Fighters and Mages Guilds can be found in this section of the city.{{ref|name=VGTWS}}

==History==
===Early History===
[[File:ON-load-Sunport Palace District.png|thumb|right|The Palace District under attack ca. {{Year|2E 582}}]][[File:ON-place-Sunport Docks.jpg|thumb|left|Sunport Docks]]
Before the arrival of the Corelanyan elves, the Argonians of the island had built a temple known as Tluthik-Tzel (''"where the sun rests and the stones hold its warmth through the night"'') by the bay.{{Ref|name=SST}} When the Corelanyans arrived in the region, Sunport was originally founded as a small Corelanyan outpost during Iniel Corelanya's first attempt at subjugating the island on top of the temple's ruins.{{Ref|name=HoCC}}

After they were no longer welcome in domains under the control of the high king of [[Lore:Summerset|Summerset]], Lady Earlenque Corelanya and her clan retreated to the distant island of Solstice to establish a new Corelanya realm. In the fall of 1E 909, her fleet dropped anchor in the bay of Sunport. By the time of the arrival of the Altmer, the region had become deserted. The more numerous and advanced tribes, the Stone-Nest People, lived in stone cities on the eastern side of the island, while most of the Argonians residing in western Solstice were Tide-Born, simple villagers who lived along the outer coast. Earlenque Corelanya took for herself the title Kinlady of Sunport and set about establishing a new Corelanya realm.

Solstice was considered the perfect refuge for the clan. It offered a defensible location against their enemies in the west, provided an excellent harbor, and featured a small Corelanya outpost from the days when Kinheir Iniel had attempted to subjugate the island in the early years of the eighth century. The rough waters around the island and the legends of curses also made Solstice a true safe haven for the exiled High Elves. While Iniel never subdued the native Argonians, she had laid the foundations for a fortified haven on Solstice, and Corelanya merchant ships still visited the island from time to time to engage in trade with a wary Argonian populace.

Wary of rousing the hostility of their more numerous neighbors, Kinlady Earlenque took care to limit settlement to Sunport and its immediate environs. She focused on building up the city's defenses and giving her people a chance to grow strong again. Solstice's Argonians, a pragmatic people, were content to live and let live. There was little friendship between the groups, but neither was there open hostility.{{ref|name=HoCC}}

===Second Era===
Sunport got opened up to trade, diplomacy, and was used as a staging ground by the Stirk Fellowship during the Second Era. The Palace District was attacked by the Worm Cult during this time.
