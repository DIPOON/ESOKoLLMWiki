The [[Lore:Empire|Empire]] is a contemporary term referring to the realms under the rule of [[Lore:Men|Men]] on the continent of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]]. There have been three such Empires in recorded history, and the rule of any given Empire spans centuries. While all these Empires reigned over great swaths of Tamriel, [[Lore:Tiber Septim|Tiber Septim]]'s [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]] is the only empire which united all of Tamriel under one banner.

The Empire should not be confused with [[Lore:Empires|other empires]], such as the [[Lore:First Empire of the Nords|First Empire of the Nords]] or the [[Lore:Kingdom of Alinor|Kingdom of Alinor]].
