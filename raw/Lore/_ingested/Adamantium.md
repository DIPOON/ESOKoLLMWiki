# Lore:Adamantium

[[Lore:Adamantium|Adamantium]] (also called '''Adamantine''') is a rare and ageless{{Ref|{{Cite Book|Tower of Adamant}}}} metal used for making valuable armor and [[Lore:Weapons|weapons]]. It is typically seen in a lustrous silvery color dubbed "Adamantine Silver",{{ref|name=ASDye|[[Online:Adamantine Silver|Adamantine Silver]] dye color in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} though adamantium equipment can also have a stone blue hue{{Ref|name=AR|[[Arena:Items|Adamantium Item]]'s appearance in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}} or a dark grey coloration.{{Ref|name=DF|[[Daggerfall:Items|Adamantium Item]]'s appearance in ''[[DF:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} The ore is very heavy and can be used in alchemy.{{Ref|name=MW|[[Tribunal:Adamantium Ore|Adamantium Ore]] and [[Tribunal:Adamantium Armor|Armor]]'s appearance in ''[[MW:Morrowind|Morrowind:]] [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]}} Adamantium armor can appear as either a high-quality heavy weight armor or medium weight armor. It is above the quality of [[Lore:Mithril|Mithril]]{{Ref|name=DF}} or [[Lore:Orichalcum|Orichalcum]],{{Ref|name=MW}} but is inferior to [[Lore:Stalhrim|Stalhrim]]{{Ref|name=MW}} and [[Lore:Ebony|Ebony]].{{Ref|name=DF}} The armor has a high potential for enchantment.{{Ref|name=MW}} Adamantium weapons can harm magical creatures that resist normal weapons.{{Ref|name=DF}}<noinclude>

The oldest structure on [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]], the [[Lore:Adamantine Tower|Adamantine Tower]], was created by the [[Lore:Aedra|Aedra]] using the material around {{Year|ME 2500}},{{Ref|name=PGE1|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|High Rock}}}} and it was intended to be a place for discussing the creation of [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]].{{Ref|name=PGE3|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|All the Eras of Man}}}} Known for using the techniques of both [[Lore:Man|men]] and [[Lore:Mer|mer]] in crafting her exceptional wares, the [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] [[Lore:Asterie Bedel|Asterie Bedel]] created the legendary claymore [[Lore:Chrysamere|Chrysamere]] in the [[Lore:Merethic Era|Merethic Era]], which is one of the oldest mortal-made objects forged using Adamantium.{{ref|name=LAIA|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive - Infinite Archive}}}}{{ref|name=AR/DF|[[Lore:Chrysamere|Chrysamere]]'s ''[[Arena:Chrysamere|Arena]]/[[Daggerfall:Chrysamere|Daggerfall]]'' in-game description}} The [[Lore:Staff of Towers|Staff of Towers]] which was constructed during the [[Lore:Alessian Slave Rebellion|Alessian Slave Rebellion]], features an adamantine shard for the Adamantine Tower for one of its eight segments.{{ref|name=ESOAO|[[ON:Augur of the Obscure|Augur of the Obscure]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} The [[Lore:Adamantium Helm of Tohan|Adamantium Helm of Tohan]] is an ancient artifact of obscure origins with immensely high potential for enchantment.{{ref|name=MWHT|[[MW:Helm of Tohan|Helm of Tohan]] plugin for [[Morrowind:Morrowind|''Morrowind'']]}}

Residing in [[Lore:Mournhold|Mournhold]] is the [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]] Indalen family, who have been adamantium crafters for many generations. There are small deposits of Adamantium that can be found in the ruins of [[Lore:Old Mournhold|Old Mournhold]].{{ref|name=MWBI|[[Tribunal:Bols Indalen|Bols Indalen]]'s dialogue in [[Morrowind:Morrowind|''Morrowind'']]}} The statue of [[Lore:Ephen|Ephen]] in his tomb underneath Mournhold depicts him wielding a hammer and poised on an adamantium anvil.{{Ref|name=TTBVIII|{{Cite Book|The True Barenziah, Part VIII}}}}

==Notes==
*Differing in only one letter, Adamantine is not to be confused with [[Lore:Adamantite|Adamantite]], a widely available green material used to forge arms and armaments in [[Lore:Altmer|Altmeri]] style during the mid-[[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]].{{ref|name=ESOAdamantite|[[Online:Style_Materials#Adamantite|Adamantite]] material in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=ESOCM1|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 1: High Elf Style}}}}
*Adamantine is also not to be confused with [[Lore:Dwarven Adamant|Dwarven Adamant]], which is a white-hued alloy of ores of unknown origin, believed to be the most durable material ever made. It is among the rarest materials on [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]].{{ref|name=ADW|[[ON:Adamant Dwarven Wolf|Adamant Dwarven Wolf]] mount in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=ADH|[[ON:Adamant Dwarven Horse|Adamant Dwarven Horse]] mount in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=ADS|[[ON:Adamant Dwarven Senche|Adamant Dwarven Senche]] mount in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}
