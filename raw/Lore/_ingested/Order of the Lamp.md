The [[Lore:Order of the Lamp|Order of the Lamp]], sometimes called the '''Lamp Knights''',{{Ref|name=MKOW|{{Cite Book|Mannimarco, King of Worms}}}} was an order of [[Lore:knights|knights]] that acted as the protectors of the [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]]. It was formed a year after the Mages Guild itself was formed, as hired guards were not enough to protect the guild in dangerous territories.{{Ref|name=OotM|{{Cite Book|Origin of the Mages Guild}}}}<noinclude>
==Organization==
[[File:ON-item-armor-Order of the Lamp Style.jpg|thumb|left|Order of the Lamp Armor]]
Lamp Knights were given appropriate rights to certain services of the Mages Guild. A knight's rank within the Order approximated the individual's rank within the Guild, in terms of rights and privileges. The Order only recruited those who had already demonstrated fighting skills and were in good standing with the Mages Guild.{{Ref|[[Daggerfall:Order of the Lamp|Guild description]] in [[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|''Daggerfall'']]}} These knights were generally not spellcasters, and they traveled to and from various Mages Guild branches as needed.{{ref|{{Cite Book|The Daggerfall Chronicles|ns_base=Books}}}}{{rp|101}} Each chapter of the Order of the Lamp is led by the Palatinus{{Ref|name=OotM|{{Cite Book|Origin of the Mages Guild}}}} or Master Palatinus{{Ref|name=OotMESO|[[ON:Origin of the Mages Guild|Origin of the Mages Guild (Varennian Edition)]] — [[Lore:Books by Author#The Archmage Salarth|The Archmage Salarth]]}}, who was commanded directly by the Archmagister.{{Ref|name=MKOW}} The position of Votary Commander, with seniority over all Lamp Knights, did exist, however.{{Ref|name=VN|[[ON:Votary Nahlia|Votary Nahlia]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Gold Road|Gold Road]]''}} A contingent of the order was led by [[Lore:Vanus Galerion|Vanus Galerion]] to confront [[Lore:Mannimarco|Mannimarco]] and they participated in the resulting battle.{{Ref|name=MKOW}} After he became Archmagister, [[Lore:Hannibal Traven|Hannibal Traven]] commanded the Order of the Lamp to arrest Magister [[Lore:Ulliceta gra-Kogg|Ulliceta gra-Kogg]] of [[Lore:Orsinium|Orsinium]] for necromancy but she escaped.{{Ref|{{Cite Book|The Black Arts On Trial}}}} The current status of the Order in the aftermath of the collapse of the Mages Guild is unknown.<noinclude>

The Order trains a variety of mounts for various situations. Their horses selectively bred for generations for improved stamina and agility, and no expenses are spared for their training. Their steed's armor are equipped with equipment to rival other knights throughout [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]].{{ref|[[Online:Order of the Lamp Destrier|Order of the Lamp Destrier]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Jackals are utilized for their cunning and speed as well as their tendency to finding quick shortcuts and exits.{{ref|[[Online:Order of the Lamp Destrier|Order of the Lamp Jackal]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} A tale of a knight of Palatinus rank on top of a lion smashing through the enemies ranks to save a squad of Mages Guild members exists.{{ref|[[Online:Order of the Lamp Lion|Order of the Lamp Lion]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Vvardvark mounts were adopted by the Order due to their loyalty, surefootedness, tracking skills, as well as the belief that they can smell magicka.{{ref|[[Online:Order of the Lamp Vvardvark|Order of the Lamp Vvardvark]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
{{NewLine}}

By 3E 405, the [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]]'s Order of the Lamp was still around, under the purview of the Archmagister.{{Ref|[[Daggerfall:Order of the Lamp|Guild description]] in [[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|''Daggerfall'']]}}. A renegade member of the Mages Guild had gone on a rampage and had stolen magical items before fleeing. Described by the common folk as "too official an organization to deal with the mage", the Order had instead contracted [[Lore:Dark Brotherhood|the Dark Brotherhood]] to assassinate the mage in their stead. [[Lore:The Agent|A fate-driven assassin]] was sent out to commit the "accounting". {{Ref|[[Daggerfall:A Renegade Mage|A Renegade Mage]] quest in [[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|''Daggerfall'']]}}
