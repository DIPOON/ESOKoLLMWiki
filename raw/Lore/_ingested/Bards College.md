The [[Lore:Bards College|Bards College]] is an institute found in the city of [[Lore:Solitude|Solitude]], within the province of [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]]. The Bards College is well known throughout the province as a prominent institute of music and history for the aspiring bard, or the ''Skald'' as they are known in Skyrim. Students of the Bards College are known to be well-trained and successful in their business. Graduates of the Bards College are often employed in various noble households; some have even entertained the [[Lore:Elder Council|Elder Council]] of the [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]].{{Ref|name=PGE1E|{{Cite Book|Pocket Guide to the Empire, 1st Edition/Skyrim}}}} The college is marked by the ''Flame of Callisos'', which is found at the entrance. As long as the flame remains lit, the College will stand.{{Ref|name=WtWXI|{{Cite Book|Walking the World, Vol XI}}}}<noinclude>

==Further Information==
The Bards College has two known departments: History and Lute (Music). The Bards College, along with many poets and historians, write poems and stories in the [[Lore:Poetic Edda|Poetic Edda]], a collection of endless poems that depict Skyrim's extended history.{{Ref|name=SRGiraud|[[SR:Giraud Gemane|Giraud Gemane]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} The [[Lore:Ateia|Ateia]] family is a recurring contributor to the Bards College, since their earliest members, {{Lore Link|Parteron Ateia}}, who donated his last flute to the Bards College,{{Ref|name=AF|{{Cite Book|Ateian Fife}}}} all the way to {{Lore Link|Pantea Ateia}}, who was a dean at the college during the Skyrim Civil War.

==History==
[[File:ON-place-Bards College.jpg|thumb|left|The Bards College circa {{Year|2E 582}}]][[File:SR-quest-Tending the Flames 03.jpg|thumb|right|The Burning of King Olaf circa {{Year|4E 201}}]]
The Bards College was founded when Skyrim was a part of the [[Lore:Alessian Empire|First Empire]], roughly during the twilight years of Ysgramor's dynasty, and the years following.{{Ref|name=PGE1E}} At one point, Solitude was sacked by [[Lore:Olaf One-Eye|Olaf One-Eye]] in the late years of the [[Lore:War of Succession|War of Succession]], and sometime later, Solitude and the Bards College would make a yearly tradition based on Olaf's exploits. The College's students flood the markets to celebrate for a week and end it with the ''Burning of King Olaf'', where everyone gathers at the college courtyard to burn an effigy that depicts King Olaf One-Eye.{{Ref|name=KOV|{{Cite Book|King Olaf's Verse}}}}{{Ref|name=SRTending|Events of [[SR:Tending the Flames|Tending the Flames]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} This tradition would continue for many centuries, to celebrate Olaf's victory over [[Lore:Numinex|Numinex]] and his ascension to the throne. More and more famous bards and scholars would come from the Bards College, such as [[Lore:Callisos|Callisos]] and [[Lore:Morachellis|Morachellis]].{{Ref|name=PGE1E}} The now famous Flame of Callisos was named after the alumni.{{Ref|name=WtWXI}}

Many years later, during the [[Lore:Skyrim Civil War|Skyrim Civil War]] in {{Year|4E 201}}, the Bards College was administrated by Headmaster [[Lore:Viarmo|Viarmo]], who chose to keep the politics of the civil war away from his institute.{{Ref|name=SRViarmo|[[SR:Viarmo|Viarmo]]'s dialogue in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} At this time, Jarl [[Lore:Elisif the Fair|Elisif the Fair]] had canceled the annual Burning of King Olaf, as she considered it in poor taste after the assassination of her husband, High King [[Lore:Torygg|Torygg]]. To persuade her to change her mind, Viarmo had sent a [[Lore:Last Dragonborn|student]] of the college to find a lost verse of the Poetic Edda, [[Lore:King Olaf's Verse|King Olaf's Verse]], written by the First Era skald, [[Lore:Svaknir|Svaknir]]. The student found themselves at [[Lore:Dead Man's Respite|Dead Man's Respite]], the burial place of High King Olaf One-Eye. The student had brought back King Olaf's Verse and presented it to Jarl Elisif. The burning of the effigy would commence that year, and continue to do so.{{Ref|name=SRTending}}
