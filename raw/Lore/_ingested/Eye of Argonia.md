# Lore:Eye of Argonia

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

<noinclude>{{Lore Artifacts Trail}}</noinclude>
The [[Lore:Eye of Argonia|Eye of Argonia]] is a treasured artifact described as the "priceless king's jewel of ancient [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]]".{{ref|[[RG:Dreekius|Dreekius]]' dialogue in ''[[RG:Redguard|Redguard]]''}} 

Little is known of the eye, but it has captured the imagination of treasure hunter's throughout all of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] as to what it is capable of.{{ref|name=MWGazetteer|[[Betrayal:Betrayal of the Second Era|Betrayal of the Second Era]] ''Morrowind Gazetteer''}}{{rp|21}} Some theories included this invaluable gem being a key the "lost city of Black Marsh",{{ref|name=Origin|{{Cite Book|The Origin of Cyrus!|ns_base=Books}}}} or that it could serve as a gateway to undiscovered lands.{{ref|name=MWGazetteer}}{{rp|21}}

At some point during the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]] of the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]] Lark of [[Lore:Rosgard|Rosgard]], a mysterious masked thief sought to obtain the Eye.{{ref|name=MWGazetteer}}{{rp|21}} Years later [[Lore:Cyrus|Cyrus]] the [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] and the Cyro-Nordic captain [[Lore:Tobias|Tobias]] both wished to attain the prized artifact as well, although it's unknown if either of them ever succeeded in doing so.{{ref|name=Origin}}{{ref|[[Redguard:Tobias|Tobias]]' dialogue in ''[[Redguard:Redguard|Redguard]]''}} An archeologist and author by the name of Rolard Nordssen aspired to claim the fabled eye, a potential accomplishment which he considered even greater than his prior feats.{{ref|name=ROKZ|{{Cite Book|Ruins of Kemel-Ze}}}} Circa {{Year|3E 427}}, the [[Lore:Nerevarine|Nerevarine]] mentioned the legendary jewel as a means to cover for his true intentions to infiltrate the chambers of the Common Tongue press printers.{{ref|name=TRTrels|[[TR:Trels Varis|Trels Varis]]' dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]: [[Tribunal:Tribunal|Tribunal]]''}} There exists dubious tales about the jewel's discovery.{{ref|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|The Eye of Argonia|ns_base=GEN}}}}{{ref|name=TiberMeeting|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|ns_base=General|Tiber Septim's Sword-Meeting with Cyrus the Restless}}}}
