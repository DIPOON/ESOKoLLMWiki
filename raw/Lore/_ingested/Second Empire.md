The [[Lore:Second Empire|Second Empire]], also known as the '''Cyrodilic Empire''',{{ref|name=PGE1C|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Cyrodiil}}}}{{ref|name=Origin|{{Cite Book|The Origin of Cyrus!|ns_base=Books}}}} the '''Empire of Cyrodiil''',{{ref|name=HOTFG|{{Cite Book|History of the Fighters Guild}}}} the '''Empire of Tamriel''',{{ref|name=2920LSv8|{{Cite Book|2920, Last Seed (v8)}}}}{{ref|name=TOAM3|{{Cite Book|Triumphs of a Monarch, Ch. 10}}}} the '''Tamrielic Empire''',{{Ref|name=TDC|{{Cite Book|The Daggerfall Chronicles|ns_base=Books}}}}{{rp|6}}{{ref|name=THOTE|{{Cite Book|True Heirs of the Empire}}}} the '''Reman Empire''',{{ref|name=THOTE|{{Cite Book|True Heirs of the Empire}}}} '''Reman's Imperium''',{{Ref|{{Cite Book|Crafting Motif 26: Daggerfall Covenant Style}}}}  and the '''Second Empire of Men''',{{ref|name=PGE1C}} was a human-led empire that, at its height, controlled every region of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] with the exception of [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]].{{Ref|name=PGE1C}} The history of the Second Empire is divided into two main periods: the reign of the [[Lore:Reman Dynasty|Reman Dynasty]] that witnessed the final two centuries of the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]], and the subsequent [[Lore:Akaviri Potentate|Akaviri Potentate]] during the first four hundred thirty years of the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]].<noinclude>

==Reman Dynasty==
[[File:CT-Events-Imperial Generic (Large).png|left|thumb|Reman-era Imperial]]
The Second Empire and the [[Lore:Reman Dynasty|Reman Dynasty]] were founded by [[Lore:Reman|Reman Cyrodiil]] after he defeated the [[Lore:Akaviri|Akaviri]] at the Battle of [[Lore:Pale Pass|Pale Pass]] in {{Year|1E 2703}}. Until that point, [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] had been divided into several small kingdoms, but once Reman had brought the armies of [[Lore:Colovia|Colovia]] and the [[Lore:Nibenay|Nibenay]] together to defeat the invasion, he was able to unite all of Tamriel's human inhabitants in the face of possible aggression from the [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]].{{Ref|name=PGE1C|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Cyrodiil}}}}

The Battle of Pale Pass established Reman I as the leading power in Tamriel, but many parts of the continent refused to acknowledge his rule. Valenwood was not subdued until {{Year|1E 2714}}.{{Ref|name=PGE3V|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Valenwood}}}} In {{Year|1E 2804}}, [[Lore:Reman II|Reman II]] deposed Emperor [[Lore:Kastav|Kastav]], marking the beginning of what many consider the golden age of the Second Empire.{{ref|name=RIITLoA|{{Cite Book|Reman II: The Limits of Ambition}}}} In {{Year|1E 2811}}, the Empire under Reman II invaded [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]], and the [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial Legion]] quickly won a great and decisive victory in the Battle of Argonia, as well as in several other clashes, inflicting significant casualties on the [[Lore:Argonian|Argonians]].{{ref|name=TBW|{{Cite Book|The Blackwater War}}}}{{Ref|name=PGE3A|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Argonia}}}} The conflict, known as the [[Lore:Blackwater War|Blackwater War]], continued until {{Year|1E 2837}}, when the Argonians abruptly buried their weapons and went back to farming, fishing, and weaving, and Reman II declared the official annexation of Black Marsh.{{ref|name=TBW}}{{Ref|name=PGE1WR|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|The Wild Region}}}}

In {{Year|1E 2840}}, the Empire invaded [[Lore:Morrowind|Morrowind]], the only province outside of Imperial control, in what became known as the [[Lore:Four-Score War|Four-Score War]].{{ref|name=PGE3M|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Morrowind}}}} It was during the war in {{Year|1E 2843}} that Reman II was killed in battle against the [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]].{{ref|name=RIITLoA}}{{ref|name=R2|{{Cite Book|Tomb of Reman Cyrodiil II}}}} Reman II was succeeded by his illegitimate son [[Lore:Brazollus Dor|Brazollus Dor]], who continued the war until his death in {{Year|1E 2877}}.{{ref|name=TEotV|{{Cite Book|The Emperor of the Villa}}}}{{ref|name=HKEAYQ|{{Cite Book|High King Emeric Answers Your Questions}}}} Brazollus Dor's successor, [[Lore:Reman III|Reman III]], continued the war, but progress was slow. The three living gods of Morrowind's [[Lore:Tribunal|Tribunal]]—[[Lore:Vivec|Vivec]], [[Lore:Almalexia|Almalexia]], and [[Lore:Sotha Sil|Sotha Sil]]—proved to be capable leaders. By {{Year|1E 2920}}, both sides were nearing exhaustion after the Empire captured the fortress of Ald Marak; thus, the two sides agreed to a truce to bring an end to the war.{{Ref|name=TLYOTFE|{{Cite Book|2920, The Last Year of the First Era}}}}

Before the truce could be formally agreed on, Reman III was assassinated,{{ref|name=PGE3C|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Cyrodiil}}}} bringing an end to the Reman Dynasty and instigating the period of the Second Empire known as the [[Lore:Akaviri Potentate|Akaviri Potentate]].{{Ref|name=TLYOTFE}}

==Akaviri Potentate==
===First Era===
After the assassination of Reman III, Potentate [[Lore:Versidue-Shaie|Versidue-Shaie]], who is believed to have ordered the Emperor's assassination, and Vivec agreed to the [[Lore:Cervant Truce|Cervant Truce]], ending the Four-Score War. On 22 Evening Star, 1E 2920, Versidue-Shaie proclaimed that he was assuming leadership of the Empire but retaining his title of Potentate. He also declared the end of the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]] and the start of the [[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]].{{Ref|name=2920ES|{{Cite Book|2920, Evening Star (v12)}}}}

===Second Era===
After becoming the ruler of the Empire, Versidue-Shaie began persecuting those who opposed his rule and remained loyal to the Reman Dynasty, a period known as the Reman Purges.{{ref|name=Systres|{{Cite Book|Systres History: Volume 6}}}}{{ref|name=ONBlackmarrow|[[Online:Blackmarrow Harvester|Blackmarrow Harvester]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Dragon Bones|Dragon Bones]]''}} By {{Year|2E 283}}, Versidue-Shaie was faced with a disintegrating empire. The vassal kingdoms throughout Tamriel had reached a new height of rebellion, openly challenging Imperial rule by refusing to pay taxes and leading sorties against Imperial garrisons. To suppress the rebellious armies,  Versidue-Shaie declared martial law in 2E 283, initiating the War of Subjugation, which finally ended in Imperial victory in {{Year|2E 320}}.{{Ref|name=HotFG|{{Cite Book|History of the Fighters Guild}}}} The immense cost of the War of Subjugation resulted in the impoverishment of both the Empire and its constituent kingdoms. To maintain order without incurring the expense of a standing army, Versidue-Shaie agreed in {{Year|2E 321}} to a proposal from his kinsman Dinieras-Ves, which established what would later be known as the [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]].{{Ref|name=FGC|{{Cite Book|Fighters Guild Charter}}}}

In {{Year|2E 324}}, Versidue-Shaie was assassinated by the [[Lore:Morag Tong|Morag Tong]] in the city of [[Lore:Senchal|Senchal]]{{Ref|name=BoD|{{Cite Book|The Brothers of Darkness}}}}{{Ref|name=FAD|{{Cite Book|Fire and Darkness}}}} and succeeded by his son, [[Lore:Savirien-Chorak|Savirien-Chorak]].{{Ref|name=BoD}} The Morag Tong were outlawed across the continent and reformed secretly as the Dark Brotherhood.{{ref|name=TDC}}{{rp|6}} In {{Year|2E 400}}, Savirien-Chorak held a celebration to commemorate four centuries of potentate rule.{{ref|name=PCoV|{{Cite Book|The Poet-Champion of Vastyr}}}} Savirien-Chorak granted Orsinium provincial status during his reign.{{Ref|name=PGE1WR}} Savirien-Chorak and all his heirs were assassinated by the Dark Brotherhood on a night in the month of Sun's Dawn {{Year|2E 430}}—within a fortnight the Akaviri Potentate crumbled.{{Ref|name=BoD}}{{ref|name=KRP|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Ken Rolston's Posts|ns_base=GEN}}}} By {{Year|2E 431}}, civil wars and insurrection had dissolved the Empire, ushering in the [[Lore:Interregnum|Imperial Interregnum]].{{ref|name=TDC}}{{rp|6}}{{Ref|name=BoD}}{{Ref|name=PGE1C}}

During the early years of the Interregnum, the Empire survived as the [[Lore:Empire of Cyrodiil (Interregnum)|Empire of Cyrodiil]], a rump state ruled by non-[[Lore:Dragonborn|Dragonborn]] emperors that was mostly confined to the [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Imperial Province]].

==Administrative Titles==
The exact hierarchy of ranks, the nuances between them, and the trajectory of advancement has not been spelled out, but can generally be understood as categorized below.

*'''[[Lore:Cyrodilic Emperors|Emperor]]''', the ruler of the Empire.

*'''[[Lore:Akaviri Potentate|Akaviri Potentate]]''', leader of the Akaviri faction on the Elder Council and Chief Advisor to the Emperor.{{ref|name=HKEAYQ|{{Cite Book|High King Emeric Answers Your Questions}}}}

*'''[[Lore:Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel|High Chancellor]]''' (sometimes Imperial Battlemage), the head of the Elder Council.
**'''Councilor''', a member of the Elder Council.

*'''Provincial Governor''', A Governor of an Imperial Province.{{Ref|name=PGE1p|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Prologue}}}}

*'''Governor''', A Governor of a region within an Imperial Province.{{ref|name=TFoS|{{Cite Book|The Founding of Southpoint}}}}
