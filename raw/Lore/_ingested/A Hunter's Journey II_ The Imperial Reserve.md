I first discovered my love for hunting in my own backyard, so to speak. I grew up in the {{Lore Link|Colovian Highlands}}, and the grasslands of the {{Lore Link|Imperial Reserve}} were a mere half-day's walk from my father's house. From the time I was old enough to string a bow, I used to slip away to roam the Reserve and try my luck. {{Lore Link|Deer}}, {{Lore Link|antelope}}, {{Lore Link|pheasant}}, and the occasional {{Lore Link|West Weald}} {{Lore Link|boar}} abounded in the finest hunting ground between {{Lore Link|Cyrodiil}} and the {{Lore Link|Gold Coast}}.

Given the abundant game, I came to appreciate why the old emperors kept the place for themselves. My father told me that Emperor [[Lore:Brazollus Dor|Brazollus Dor]] used to love hunting in these lands. In fact, he loved it a lot more than sitting on the Ruby Throne and trying to run the empire, so he told his potentate to deal with business in Cyrodiil while he built himself a villa near {{Lore Link|Weatherleah}}. His only official act was to declare a vast swath of the plains his own personal game reserve.

For a century or so, no one but the occasional poacher dared to take the Emperor's deer. But the [[Lore:Akaviri Potentate|Potentate]]s who followed the [[Lore:Reman Dynasty|Reman Emperor]]s weren't interested in paying to guard herds they never personally hunted. The Imperial Reserve gradually became just a name on a map in the {{Lore Link|White-Gold Tower}}. Soon, the ordinary people of {{Lore Link|Colovia}}, including my father, and myself, quietly resumed hunting in the area. And why not?

I've hunted all over {{Lore Link|Tamriel}}. I've tracked {{Lore Link|mammoth}}s in {{Lore Link|Skyrim}}, speared {{Lore Link|Nix-Ox|nix-oxen}} in {{Lore Link|Morrowind}}, and skinned {{Lore Link|wamasu}} in {{Lore Link|Black Marsh}}. But I come back to the Imperial Reserve in Colovia at least once a year for the simple pleasure of a good, clean hunt in the land where I was born. Especially in the fall, when the air is crisp and the bucks are in the rut.

I'll see you on the hunt!
