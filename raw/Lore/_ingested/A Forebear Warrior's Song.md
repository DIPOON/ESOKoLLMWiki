{{Lore Link|Akatosh}}, my strength comes from your hands,<br>
My enemies fall before my sword.<br>
Can you hear their cries?<br>
Death, they cry.<br>
Death, I sing.<br>
But death is theirs, not mine.

Wipe the blade of their color.<br>
Wipe the shield of their color.<br>
Victory is ours!

{{Lore Link|Stendarr}}, grant my [[Lore:Weapons|weapon]] fall only thus:<br>
For justice, for the right ways.<br>
Can you hear my plea?<br>
Justice, they pray.<br>
Justice, I sing.<br>
Justice is mine, and also theirs.

Wipe the blade of their color.<br>
Wipe the shield of their color.<br>
Victory is ours!

For the way of the righteous is mercy,<br>
And compassion for the weak.<br>
Can I wield your {{Lore Link|One Hand and Shield|sword and shield}}?<br>
Mercy, they beg.<br>
Mercy, I give.

Swift death to the unjust!<br>
Slow death to the merciless!<br>
Wipe the blade of their color.<br>
Wipe the shield of their color.

Victory is ours!
{{Book End}}
