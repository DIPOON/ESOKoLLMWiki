[[Lore:Abomination|Abominations]] are mechanomagical lifeforms that were created by the former [[Lore:Clockwork Apostles|Clockwork Apostle]], [[Lore:Mecinar|Mecinar]], and are a perversion of [[Lore:Sotha Sil|Sotha Sil's]] goal of creating the perfect lifeform.{{ref|name=MDRTCB|[[Legends:Mecinar|Mecinar]]'s dialogue in the mission [[Legends:The Betrayal|The Betrayal]] in [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}} Early variants consisted of individuals modified against their will that were melded with steel and sorcery similar to [[Lore:Fabricants|Fabricant]]s, but are a step further and are merged with beasts.{{ref|name=LRTCCD|[[Legends:Sotha Sil|Sotha Sil]]'s dialogue in the mission [[Legends:The Betrayal|The Betrayal]] in [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}} The creations led Sotha Sil to exile Mecinar out of the [[Lore:Clockwork City|Clockwork City]].<noinclude>

In {{Year|4E 201}}, long after Sotha Sil's death, Mecinar enacted a plan to gain access to the Clockwork City and obtain the divine power of the [[Lore:Mechanical Heart|Mechanical Heart]]. He was successful and now had the power to enact his vision. With the machines of the Clockwork City now bent to his will, the denizens of the Clockwork City were forcibly converted into abominations. He discovered that he could turn the [[Lore:Undead|undead]] into mechanical slave abominations with the aid of the divine powers.{{ref|name=SANAARCC|[[Legends:Swims-at-Night|Swims-at-Night]]'s Abomination appearance in the mission [[Legends:Return to The Inner Curiosity|Return to The Inner Curiosity]] in [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}}{{ref|name=LAARCC|[[Legends:Laaneth|Laaneth]]'s Abomination appearance in the mission [[Legends:Return to The Inner Curiosity|Return to The Inner Curiosity]] in [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}} The [[Lore:Forgotten Hero|Forgotten Hero]] entered the Clockwork City, slayed Mecinar, and stopped the remaining inhabitants from being forcibly converted.{{ref|name=RCWC|Events of [[Legends:Return to Clockwork City|Return to Clockwork City]]}}
