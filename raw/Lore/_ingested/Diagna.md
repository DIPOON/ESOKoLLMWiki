[[Lore:Diagna|Diagna]], (also known as the '''Orichalc God of the Sideways Blade''') was an avatar of the [[Lore:HoonDing|HoonDing]] (the Yokudan God of Make Way) that achieved permanence as a [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] sword-god.{{ref|name=EGTHammerfell|{{Cite Book|EGT|Hammerfell}}}}{{Ref|name=ASE|[[ON:Wrothgar Antiquities#Armless Stone Effigy|Armless Stone Effigy Antiquities Codex]] from [[ON:Greymoor|ESO]]}} His "hoary thuggish cult" originated in [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokuda]] during the Twenty Seven Snake Folk Slaughter.{{ref|name=VOF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith...}}}} He was instrumental to the defeat of the [[Lore:Lefthanded Elves|Lefthanded Elves]], as he brought [[Lore:Orichalcum|orichalc]] [[Lore:Weapons|weapons]] to the Yokudan people to win the fight.{{ref|name=VOF}} According to one myth, Diagna crashed his fist against the HoonDing Gong and swords fell from the walls of the Orichalc Tower.{{ref|name=LAB|[[Online:Lefthander's Aegis Belt|Lefthander's Aegis Belt]] codex entry}}

In [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]], he led a very tight-knit group of followers, called the [[Lore:Order of Diagna|Order of Diagna]], against the [[Lore:Orc|Orcs]] of [[Lore:Orsinium|Orsinium]] during the height of their ancient power, but then faded into partial obscurity. He is described as now being little more than a local power spirit of the [[Lore:Dragontail Mountains|Dragontail Mountains]]{{ref|name=VOF}} but he is still worshipped as of the [[Lore:Fourth Era|4th Era]],{{ref|[[Legends:Alik'r Bandit|Alik'r Bandit]]'s dialogue in ''[[Legends:Legends|Legends]]''}} being part of the Eight of the [[Lore:Crowns|Crowns]].{{Ref|name=VoFCR|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith, Crown Redguards}}}}

High Kings of Hammerfell are crowned on the Throne of [[Lore:Hegathe|Hegathe]] by wearing the [[Lore:Diadem of Diagna|Diadem of Diagna]].{{ref|name=TWAAHD|{{Cite Book|The Worthy Ar-Azal, His Deeds}}}} Warriors of the Order of Diagna carried stone effigies of Diagna during the siege.{{Ref|name=ASE}} The [[Lore:Daggerfall Covenant|Daggerfall Covenant]]'s alliance of the Orcs and Redguards was alleged by the Unveiled Azadiyeh to have inflamed Diagna's very sword-arm with the Crimson Rash of Betrayal.{{ref|name=PfOE|{{Cite Book|Plea for Open Eyes}}}}<noinclude>
==Notes==
* The description of the Cobalt Sep Adder originally stated that Diagna was said to have created the lightning-blue Sapphire Adder by passing his orichalc sword over a common [[Lore:Sep Adder|Sep Adder]] when he needed a symbol of Redguard ferocity during the first Siege of Orsinium.{{ref|name=Cobalt|[[Online:Cobalt Sep Adder|Cobalt Sep Adder]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} It was later changed to remove all mentions of Diagna.
