[[Lore:Pinnacle Rock|Pinnacle Rock]] is the home of the [[Lore:Mazken|Mazken]] in the [[Lore:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]], located in [[Lore:Dementia (place)|Dementia]] at the southern tip of the Madgod's Boot. This is the Mazken's most sacred place, as it contains the Wellspring of the Mazken. The souls of the fallen are called from the Waters of [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]] to the Font of Rebirth by use of ceremonial chimes.{{Ref|name=DM|Dialogue of the [[Shivering:Mazken|Mazken]] in the [[Shivering:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]].}} The stronghold was a gift from Lord [[Lore:Sheogorath|Sheogorath]], and usually no one—not even Sheogorath himself—are permitted inside its sacred halls.{{Ref|name=DM}}<noinclude>

In {{Year|3E 433}} Pinnacle Rock was invaded by Order. This was rumored to have been caused by the subterfuge of Syl, a previous Duchess of Dementia who had been converted to a Priestess of Order in the coming [[Lore:Greymarch|Greymarch]],{{Ref|name=ESI|Events of the [[Shivering:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]].}} although this conflicts with the rumors of her assassination.{{Ref|name=AltSI|Alternate events of the [[Shivering:Shivering Isles|Shivering Isles]].}} The effects of this infiltration was devastating on the realm's army. Order crystals covered the Wellspring, drying up its waters and turning the Mazken to stone. Sheogorath's [[Lore:Hero of Kvatch|Champion]], who had been granted access to the stronghold to fight the invading [[Lore:Knight of Order|Knights of Order]], was able to shatter the crystals around the Wellspring and restore the Mazken, who were then able to defeat the remaining forces of Order.{{Ref|name=ESI}}
