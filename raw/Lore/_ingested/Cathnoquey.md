[[Lore:Cathnoquey|Cathnoquey]] is an archipelago that lies somewhere between [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] and [[Lore:Akavir|Akavir]]. It was conquered by [[Lore:Uriel Septim V|Uriel Septim V]] in {{Year|3E 276}},{{Ref|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 3}}}} and was still an inhabited [[Lore:Empire|Imperial]] territory and trade hub circa {{Year|3E 331}}.{{ref|group=UOL|name=PG2}} According to dubious Imperial records, its main inhabitants are the [[Lore:Chimeri-quey|Chimeri-quey]], a tribal remnant of [[Lore:Chimer|Chimer]] composed of fisherfolk who live on the coastlines in grand flotilla-townships of advanced design, and the [[Lore:Keptu-quey|Keptu-quey]], [[Lore:Men|men]] with tan brown skin said to have a strange obsession with drawing [[Lore:Bull|bulls]] and living in leaderless societies in military fortresses.{{ref|group=UOL|name=PG2}}{{Ref|name=MKP|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Michael Kirkbride's Posts|ns_base=General}}}} One of its major exports is wasabi,{{ref|group=UOL|name=PG2}} a substance that has been popular in [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]] since ancient times.{{ref|name=500COTOYTR|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|The Five Hundred Mighty Companions or Thereabouts of Ysgramor the Returned|ns_base=GEN}}}}{{ref|name=SFA|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|The Seven Fights of The Aldudagga|ns_base=GEN}}}}<noinclude>

==Notes==
* In [[General:Michael Kirkbride|Michael Kirkbride]]'s descriptions of the archipelago, the inhabitants of Cathnoquey were inspired by the culture of the [[wikipedia:Native Hawaiians|Native Hawaiians]].{{ref|group=UOL|name=PG2}} Kirkbride had spoken about how during his time on series, he wished he had introduced more diverse peoples on Nirn that physically resembled real-world ethnic groups that otherwise had no analogues in the setting, such as [[wikipedia:Pacific Islander|Pacific Islanders]].{{Ref|name=MKP|group=UOL}}
