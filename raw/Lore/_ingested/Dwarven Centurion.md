'''Dwarven Centurions''' (also known as '''Dwemer Centurions''' and '''Steam Centurions''') are heavily armored [[Lore:Dwemer Animunculi|constructs]] with either an extendable mace or hammer on one arm and a spring-loaded blade in the other. Some can emit a scalding blast of steam.{{ref|name=SkyrimCent|Combat capabilities of centurions in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]] and [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} They are not as common of a sight as lesser constructs in Dwarven ruins. ''[[Lore:Ruins of Kemel-Ze|Ruins of Kemel-Ze]]'' implies that the centurions have a weakness to frost and magic attacks, due to the steam that drives them, although they are highly resistant against fire, frost, and shock. They are sometimes found attached to a charging gantry when not in use.{{ref|Appearance of Dwarven Centurions in [[Skyrim:Alftand|Alftand]]'s deepest fathoms}} 

While they are typically taller than most men, they can vary in size. Many of the Steam Centurions found in Morrowind are actually shorter than men.{{ref|[[MW:Steam Centurion|Steam Centurion]]s in ''[[MW:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}

==Unique Dwarven Centurions==
===Dwarven Mechanical Giant===
A massive version of the centurion, the '''Dwarven Mechanical Giant''' (or '''Steam Golem'''), has an extendable mace on one arm. It is very deadly and can only be killed if attacked on its heel. A Mechanical Giant was located in [[Lore:Bthzark|Bthzark]], but was defeated by [[Lore:Cyrus|Cyrus]] in {{Year|2E 864}} when he attacked its only weak point.{{ref|Events of ''[[Redguard:Redguard|Redguard]]''}}
