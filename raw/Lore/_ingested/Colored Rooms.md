</noinclude>The [[Lore:Colored Rooms|Colored Rooms]] are a many-faceted realm of [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]], created and ruled over by [[Lore:Meridia|Meridia]], known as the [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Prince]] of Infinite Energies.{{Ref|name=TCR|[[ON:The Colored Rooms|The Colored Rooms]] [[Online:Loading Screens|loading screen]]}} From what little has been seen of the Colored Rooms, they seem to resemble a cross between a [[Lore:Coral|coral reef]] and a vast field of floating stones, strewn with colorful trails of dust or cloud. The "ground" between the stones looks like luminescent water, but is solid enough to walk on. The skies are described as floating effluvium oceans of crimson.{{ref|name=Online|Events of [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=ETLR|[[General:ESO Twitter Lyranth Roleplay|ESO Twitter Lyranth Roleplay]]}} It has been suggested that the "colors" of the realms are a reference to the different schools of [[Lore:Magic|magic]].{{Ref|name=GT|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Gary Noonan's Posts|ns_base=General}}}}

[[Lore:Auroran|Aurorans]] and their warhorses are the spawn of Meridia that reside in the Colored Rooms, being made from pure light and have skin tones of virtually any hue.{{ref|name=ONCostumes|[[Lore:Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase/Costumes|Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase]]}}{{ref|name=ONWarhorse|Flavor text for [[Online:Auroran Warhorse|Standard]], [[Online:Auroran Twilight Warhorse|Twilight]], and [[Online:Auroran Zenith Warhorse|Zenith]] Auroran Warhorse mounts}} [[Lore:Aureal|Golden Saints]] are also purported to reside in the realm. The Colored Rooms have been described by those souls who have been there as "pretty boring" aside from Meridia's lectures (which are described as "pretty boring" as well).{{ref|name=ONDarien|[[ON:Darien Gautier#Summerset|Darien Gautier]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}}<noinclude>

==History==
The Colored Rooms were formed when Meridia fled [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]] with her fellow [[Lore:Magna Ge|Magna-Ge]]. However, instead of fleeing to [[Lore:Aetherius|Aetherius]], Meridia went into the [[Lore:Void|Void]], where she was chided by the Daedric Princes for her trespass. Bending the light of [[Lore:Magnus (god)|Magnus]], Meridia then carved her own sphere from the chaos of the Void by an act of "sheer divine will", creating a new [[Lore:Planes of Oblivion|plane of Oblivion]].{{Ref|name=TCR}}{{ref|name=EOMN|{{Cite Book|Exegesis of Merid-Nunda}}}}<noinclude>

The half-Ayleid king [[Lore:Umaril the Unfeathered|Umaril the Unfeathered]] was defeated by [[Lore:Pelinal Whitestrake|Pelinal Whitestrake]] in the [[Lore:First Era|First Era]] and was cast adrift in the "waters of Oblivion", having made an alliance with Meridia granting him immortality. He spent many thousands of years reforming in the Colored Rooms before he could return to Mundus.{{ref|name=KOTN|Events of ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]: [[Oblivion:Knights of the Nine|Knights of the Nine]]''}} 

A rift to the Colored Rooms opened within [[Lore:Scrivener's Hall|Scrivener's Hall]] on 1st Hearthfire, {{Year|2E 570}}, and closed a year later on 1st Hearthfire, {{Year|2E 571}}.{{Ref|name=MRT|{{Cite Book|Mazandi's Rift Tracking|ns_base=ON}}}} 

During an earlier confrontation with [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]] in the {{Lore Link|Planar Vortex}}, [[Lore:Darien Gautier|Darien Gautier]] was caught up in the magical spell of a {{Lore Link|Light of Meridia}} and was transported to the Colored Rooms.{{ref|name=ONDarien}} When the [[Lore:The Vestige|Vestige]] defeated [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]] in [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]] circa {{Year|2E 582}}, Meridia briefly brought them to the Colored Rooms to reclaim their soul, where Molag Bal addressed them and stated the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]] was over.{{ref|name=Online}} Darien later returned to Tamriel as Meridia's "Golden Knight", a vessel of her power,{{ref|name=ONDarien}} and after he was abducted by [[Lore:Mephala|Mephala]], [[Lore:The Triad|The Triad]] invaded the Colored Rooms.{{ref|name=ONMeridia|[[ON:Meridia|Meridia]]'s dialogue in [[ON:The Dreaming Cave|The Dreaming Cave]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[ON:Summerset (Chapter)|Summerset]]''}} When the invasion was over, enemy combatants were rounded up and [[Lore:Purified|purified]], with some being banished to Nirn.{{ref|name=LusDae|[[ON:Lustrated Daedrat|Lustrated Daedrat]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} [[Lore:Narilmor|King Narilmor]], reincarnated as the Symphony of Blades, fought off a group of Undaunted in the realm but was defeated, resulting in the retrieval of the right half of the [[Lore:Wrathstone|Wrathstone]].{{ref|[[ON:The Guiding Light|The Guiding Light]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Wrathstone|Wrathstone]]''}}
