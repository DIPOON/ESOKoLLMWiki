[[Lore:Abelle Chriditte|Abelle Chriditte]] was a renowned [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] master [[Lore:Alchemy|alchemist]] from [[Lore:High Rock|High Rock]]. She was also a [[Lore:Witches|witch]],{{Ref|name=TCATD|{{Cite Book|The Cake and the Diamond}}}} who specialized in [[Lore:Conjuration|Conjuration]] and [[Lore:Mysticism|Mysticism]], and practiced [[Lore:Alteration|Alteration]].{{Ref|name=AC|[[MW:Abelle Chriditte|Abelle Chriditte]] skills and services in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}<noinclude>

==History==
[[File:MW-place-Valenvaryon.jpg|thumb|left|Valenvaryon]]
Little is known about Abelle's past. She hailed from the province of High Rock. She was regarded as a witch and learned her craft in the wilds rather than under the tutelage of the [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]]. She was known for her unorthodox methods, and her skill was so renowned that even the [[Lore:Dunmer|Dunmer]] of [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]] were familiar with her reputation of a master alchemist.{{Ref|name=TCATD}}{{ref|name=TMP|{{Cite Book|The Morrowind Prophecies|ns_base=Books}}}}{{rp|235}}

Sometime before {{Year|3E 427}}, Chriditte traveled to the island of Vvardenfell, already advanced in age by that time. During her stay in the province of Morrowind, she resided for a while in [[Lore:Ald'ruhn|Ald'ruhn]], frequenting the Rat and the Pot inn. There, she spoke openly with many patrons before approaching [[Lore:Athyn Muendil|Athyn Muendil]] and his companions, seeking financial assistance to secure passage to [[Lore:Ald Redaynia|Ald Redaynia]].{{Ref|name=TCATD}} 

After being recognized as a famed alchemist, Chriditte offered to sell potions in exchange for [[Lore:Currency|gold]]. A bargain was eventually struck involving a stolen [[Lore:Diamond|diamond]] belonging to Athyn's group, which she proposed to use as an alchemical reagent. Despite their suspicions, she allowed Athyn to observe her work firsthand. During the exchange, Chriditte outwitted the group by exploiting her alchemical skill, employing both invisibility and mental influence to escape with the diamond. She vanished and neither she nor diamond were ever found by Athyn Muendil. Whether she ultimately completed her journey to Ald Redaynia was unknown. Athyn Muendil later recorded the incident in his published account, ''[[Lore:The Cake and the Diamond|The Cake and the Diamond]]''.{{ref|name=TCATD}}

In 3E 427, she resided in [[Lore:Valenvaryon|Valenvaryon]], where she lived alongside a group of [[Lore:Orcs|Orcs]]. During this time, she provided services as an alchemical vendor and was willing to share her alchemical knowledge, as well as offer training in Mysticism and Conjuration.{{Ref|name=AC}}{{Ref|name=Valenvaryon|[[MW:Valenvaryon|Valenvaryon]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}
