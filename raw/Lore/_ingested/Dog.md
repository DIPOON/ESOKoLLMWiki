{{Lore Bestiary Trail}}{{TOCright}}[[File:ON-prerelease-Gifting 02.jpg|thumb|left|Various dogs in a home]]
'''Dogs''' are domestic animals found across Tamriel. They are often held as pets, guarding and protecting their owner's property. Bandits will often train their dogs to protect camps and attack intruders. Hunters may bring a dog along to protect them while they retrieve their kills.{{ref|name=BiscuitESO|[[Online:Biscuit Bear-Dog|Biscuit Bear-Dog]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} Dog meat is edible.{{ref|name=DogMeat|[[SR:Dog Meat|Dog Meat]]'s appearance and edibility in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} Dogs attack primarily by biting, and can carry [[Lore:Witbane|Witbane]].{{ref|name=OBDog|[[Oblivion:Animals#Dogs|Dog]] abilities in ''[[OB:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

Domesticated dogs are related to [[Lore:Wolf|wolves]], and the two can be [[Lore:Sex#Fauna|bred]] to produce [[Lore:Wolfdog|wolfdog]]s.{{ref|name=OBHjalti|[[OB:Hjalti|Hjalti]] in [[OB:Fighter's Stronghold|Fighter's Stronghold]] download for ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

===Anthorbred Avalanche Dog===
Large, loyal dogs with very thick coats that help them brave the cold mountains of Skyrim. They are trained to help mortals navigate through the most treacherous of avalanches in the Pale Pass, and are capable of pulling fallen climbers out from under large piles of fallen snow and detritus.{{ref|[[Online:Anthorbred Avalanche Dog|Anthorbred Avalanche Dog]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}}

===Bear-Dog===
The Bear-Dog is a fierce, thick-furred breed used to hunt [[Lore:Bear|bears]] in western [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]]. They track, harry and corner the bear until the hunters arrive with spears.{{ref|[[Online:Markarth Bear-Dog|Markarth Bear-Dog]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} Black Mask Bear-Dogs were popular with the [[Lore:Markarth|Markarth]] [[Lore:Markarth Watch|Watch]] (and the outlaws who hoped to evade them) during the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]].{{ref|[[ON:Black Mask Bear-Dog|Black Mask Bear-Dog]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} Copperback Bear-Dogs are a mischievous breed known for its responsiveness to training. Copperback Bear-Dogs are heterochromic.{{ref|[[ON:Copperback Bear-Dog|Copperback Bear-Dog]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} Karth Winter Hounds are Bear-Dogs whose coats are white year-round for hunting in Skyrim's snowy peaks. Some Reach clans only allow them to be owned by chieftains.{{ref|[[ON:Karth Winter Hound|Karth Winter Hound]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} The Bear-Dogs of Morthal have spotted coats and playful dispositions.{{ref|[[Online:Morthal Bear-Dog|Morthal Bear-Dog]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} The Biscuit Bear-Dog possesses golden fur, serving as a guard and loyal companion to hunters.{{ref|name=BiscuitESO}}

===[[Lore:Death Hound|Death Hound]]===
Death Hounds are monstrous, undead canines who can often be found in the company of [[Lore:Vampire|vampires]].{{ref|name=EOTDH|{{Cite Book|Ecology of the Death Hound}}}} They have a bite as cold as the grave, which freezes its prey's flesh.{{ref|name=EOTDH}} Their meat is edible, and they often wear valuable collars.{{ref|name=SRDH|Appearance and loot of [[SR:Death Hound|Death Hounds]] in ''[[SR:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} Death hounds are voracious, indiscriminate predators that raze ecosystems where they are present.{{ref|name=EOTDH}} They eviscerate and consume prey, not for survival, but due to their temperament.{{ref|name=EOTDH}} Death hounds will leave nothing left alive if their populations are left unchecked.{{ref|name=EOTDH}} Despite how harmful they can be to the environment, their presence can be vital to the ecology of the area they inhabit.{{ref|name=EOTDH}}

===Mastiff===
Mastiffs are dogs with droopy jowls that make loyal companions and excellent guard dogs. During the [[Lore:Three Banners War|Three Banners War]], mastiffs were bred by the [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]], [[Lore:Daggerfall Covenant|Daggerfall Covenant]], and [[Lore:Ebonheart Pact|Ebonheart Pact]]. They wore armor specially forged for them, and often served as aides to the military police.{{ref|[[ON:Alliance War Dog|Alliance War Dog]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} The black mastiffs of [[Lore:Morthal|Morthal]] are popular with mages and alchemists during the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]]. while tan mastiffs were popular with scouts, hunters, and trackers.{{ref|[[ON:Black Morthal Mastiff|Black Morthal Mastiff]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}}{{ref|[[ON:Tan Morthal Mastiff|Tan Morthal Mastiff]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} During this same time period, Morthal's gray mastiffs were popular with rogues, thieves, and, oddly, priests. Morthal's titular mastiffs are considered to be fearless and tireless.{{ref|[[ON:Gray Morthal Mastiff|Gray  Morthal Mastiff]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} White mastiffs are commonly used by the [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial Legion]] in battle. The white mastiff is the mascot of the [[Lore:Second Legion|Second Legion]], which is headquartered near [[Lore:Bruma|Bruma]] in peacetime.{{ref|[[ON:Imperial War Mastiff|Imperial War Mastiff]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} Mastiffs can be outfitted in a variety of fashionable and practical gear.{{ref|[[ON:Dwarven War Dog|Dwarven War Dog]] appearance and description in {{g|ESO}}}}

===Wolfdog===
Wolfdogs are a crossbreed produced by mating a dog with a wolf. They closely resemble wolves, but exhibit doglike behavior and are less resistant to frost. Like dogs, they can also carry Witbane.{{ref|name=OBHjalti}}

===Wolfhound===
Wolfhounds are a shaggy breed used as hunting companions by the [[Lore:Nord|Nords]] of Skyrim. Whiterun Wolfhounds have gray fur, are reportedly brave enough to face [[Lore:Sabre Cat|sabre cats]] for their masters.{{ref|[[Online:Whiterun Wolfhound|Whiterun Wolfhound]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} [[Lore:Windhelm|Windhelm]] Wolfhounds have tawny coats and are said to be descended from royal hunting dogs.{{ref|[[Online:Windhelm Wolfhound|Windhelm Wolfhound]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}} Winterhold Wolfhounds have lighter, fluffier coats, as they live at high elevations where the snow never melts. They are used to hunt wolves, and their howls resemble that of their canine quarry.{{ref|[[ON:Winterhold Wolfhound|Winterhold Wolfhound]] pet description in {{g|ESO}}}}
