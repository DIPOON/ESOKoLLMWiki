[[Lore:Uriel I|Uriel Septim I]] was the fourth [[Lore:Cyrodilic Emperors|emperor]] of the [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]] and [[Lore:Septim Dynasty|Septim Dynasty]], reigning for eleven years, from {{Year|3E 53}} to {{Year|3E 64}}. He was preceded by his mother, [[Lore:Kintyra I|Kintyra I]], and was succeeded by his son, [[Lore:Uriel II|Uriel II]].<noinclude>{{ref|name=BHOTE1|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 1}}}}

==Biography==
Uriel Septim was the son of [[Lore:Kintyra I|Kintyra Septim]] (later Empress Kintyra I).{{ref|name=BHOTE1}}{{ref|name=DC|Tamriel's Timeline, ''[[Books:The Daggerfall Chronicles|The Daggerfall Chronicles]]'' — Ronald Wartow}} At some point, he had a son, [[Lore:Uriel II|Uriel]].{{ref|name=BHOTE1}}{{ref|name=DC}} In {{Year|3E 53}}, Kintyra I died, so Uriel was crowned Emperor of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]].{{ref|name=DC}}{{ref|name=BA|{{Cite Book|Battlespire Athenaeum|ns_base=Books}}}}{{rp|16}}

Uriel I was the great lawmaker of the Septim Dynasty, and a promoter of independent organizations and guilds. The [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]] and the [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] increased in prominence throughout Tamriel during his reign. During his reign, Uriel I became known as a kind and tender ruler but also a firm one.{{ref|name=BHOTE1}} He was the first of seven emperors to use the name Uriel, a practice that ended with [[Lore:Uriel VII|Uriel VII]]'s death in {{Year|3E 433}}.{{ref|name=BHOTE1}}{{ref|name=TTET|{{Cite Book|The Third Era Timeline}}}}{{ref|name=BA}}{{rp|16}} In {{Year|3E 64}}, Uriel I died and was succeeded by his son, Uriel II.{{ref|name=BHOTE1}}{{ref|name=DC}}{{ref|name=TTET}} His son's reign witnessed the beginning of the [[Lore:Third Empire|Empire]]'s disunification, and it would not return to the level of unity seen during Uriel I's reign until the days of [[Lore:Pelagius IV|Pelagius IV]].{{ref|name=BHOTE4|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 4}}}}
