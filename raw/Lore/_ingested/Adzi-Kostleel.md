# Lore:Adzi-Kostleel

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

<noinclude>{{Lore Factions Trail}}[[File:LO-map-Black Marsh (Anthology).jpg|thumb|right|Black Marsh, the homeland of Adzi-Kostleel]]</noinclude>
The [[Lore:Adzi-Kostleel|Adzi-Kostleel]] are one of the many tribes of [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]]. They are known for their oral history, surrounding the creation of [[Lore:Mundus|Mundus]]. Their version of the [[Lore:Argonian|Argonian]] creation story was written down by [[Lore:Books by Author#Solis Aduro|Solis Aduro]], titled ''[[Lore:Children of the Root|Children of the Root]]''.{{ref|name=COTR|{{Cite Book|Children of the Root}}}}<noinclude>

Records of their existence date back to before {{Year|2E 582}}. By that time, the text written by Solis Aduro was already available to the public. The creation story of Adzi-Kostleel documented by Solis Aduro, gathered from their oral tradition, is not corroborated by any other known sources.{{ref|name=COTR}}

According to this creation story, their religion included deities like [[Lore:Atak|Atak]], [[Lore:Kota|Kota]], and [[Lore:Atakota|Atakota]].{{ref|name=COTR}} The Shadow of Atakota is also acknowledged by at least some of the [[Lore:Nisswo|Nisswo]].{{Ref|name=Walks-Under-Shadow|[[ON:Walks-Under-Shadow|Walks-Under-Shadow]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]&#58; ''[[Online:Blackwood (Chapter)|Blackwood]]''}}
