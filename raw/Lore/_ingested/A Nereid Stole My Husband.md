A {{Lore Link|Nereid}} stole my husband<br>
My husband a Nereid stole<br>
Beware the maids of the seastrand<br>
Lest you be the next we console.


We strolled blithely along the shore<br>
Gathering shells and turning stones<br>
When came the voice I now deplore<br>
A voice, a song, such soaring tones!


At once, my husband quickly sped<br>
"Wait!" I cried, "'Tis a Nereid!"<br>
But faster still he pressed ahead<br>
Her sweet call could not be gainsaid.


Too late, alas, alas, too late<br>
I found him swaying, deep in thrall<br>
My worst fear sent to me by fate<br>
He followed the Nereid's call.


And she, beautiful, cruel, and vain<br>
Swam to her sisters, calling this back,<br>
"Your husband was no prize to gain;<br>
He's yours once more, Lady Crookback!"


A Nereid stole my husband<br>
Returning him without delay<br>
Poor me, near free from his demands<br>
Stuck instead with him since that day!
