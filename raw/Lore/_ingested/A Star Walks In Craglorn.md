He fell from the sky, a star descended from {{Lore Link|Aetherius}} to walk among the commonfolk. Some called him Celestial. Others, the Warrior. But me? I called him "my love."

I met my love on a desolate road, not far from the town of {{Lore Link|Dragonstar}}. I was moving quickly through the darkness, trying to avoid the notice of the {{Lore Link|Iron Orcs}} and {{Lore Link|Scaled Court}} soldiers who seemed to be around every turn and bend. It was around just such a bend in the landscape that I literally ran into the tall, powerfully built man in the ancient armor.

He caught me before I fell, steadying me with a strong hand that literally sparkled like the stars in the sky. "Where do you run to in such a hurry, fair maiden," he said. His voice was deep and resonate, sending shivers throughout my body.

Damn it! This is terrible! Perhaps I should stick to writing true accounts of the happenings in this {{Lore Link|Craglorn|wartorn region}}. I never was very good at pure fiction. Oh, wait! I have another idea. I wonder if the Warrior is fond of peacock confit and aurum potabile?

Maybe I will keep writing and see how this turns out after all.
