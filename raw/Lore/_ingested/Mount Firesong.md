[[Lore:Mount Firesong|Mount Firesong]] is an active volcano that towers above [[Lore:Y'ffelon|Y'ffelon]], often producing clouds of smoke and steam from its lofty summit. The entire [[Lore:Systres|Systres]] archipelago itself is volcanic, hence the many steam vents, hot springs, and other such features in the island's back country.{{Ref|name=VGTHI|{{Cite Book|Visitor's Guide to High Isle}}}}
