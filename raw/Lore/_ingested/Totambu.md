[[Lore:Totambu|Totambu]] is an inland fiefdom of [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]], south of the [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]].{{ref|name=Map|[[:File:DF-map-Iliac Bay (labeled).png|Map of Iliac Bay]]}} It was named after [[Lore:Na-Totambu|Na-Totambu]], the ruling class of [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokuda]] and progenitors of the Hammerfell [[Lore:Crowns|Crowns]] faction.{{ref|name=PGE3Ham|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Hammerfell}}}}

Totambu borders [[Lore:Lainlyn (barony)|Lainlyn]] to the northwest, [[Lore:Satakalaam (county)|Satakalaam]] to the north, [[Lore:Mournoth (fiefdom)|Mournoth]] to the northeast, the [[Lore:Dragontail Mountains|Dragontail Mountains]] to the south, and [[Lore:Kozanset (barony)|Kozanset]] to the west.{{ref|name=Map}} The main deity of this [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]]-dominated region is [[Lore:Zenithar|Zenithar]]. The military force of Totambu is the [[Lore:Order of the Scarab|Order of the Scarab]]. They also served as the official guard for the royal family of Totambu, based in the city of Totambu. The [[Lore:Khulari|Khulari]] clan is the predominant [[Lore:Vampire|vampire]] bloodline in the region.{{Ref|name=Daggerfall|Events of ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}}

After the [[Lore:Warp in the West|Warp in the West]], Totambu came under the dominion of the Kingdom of [[Lore:Sentinel (kingdom)|Sentinel]] ruled by the [[Lore:Forebears|Forebears]] faction.{{ref|name=PGE3Ham}}{{Ref|name=TWINTW|{{Cite Book|The Warp in the West}}}} Later, however, it became a site of a [[Lore:Crowns|Crown]] rebellion against [[Lore:Lhotun|King Lhotun]] of Sentinel. Totambu declared independence from Sentinel and pledged allegiance to the Crown leader {{Lore Link|Ayaan-si}}, High Prophet of [[Lore:Elinhir|Elinhir]]. Even though Lhotun's kingdom surrounded Totambu on all four sides, its people kept holding their resistance.{{ref|name=PGE3Ham}} The Knights of the Scarab remained a military force in the region even after the Warp.{{ref|[[Oblivion:Lathon|Lathon]]'s dialogue in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}<noinclude>
