[[Lore:Yneslea|Yneslea]] is a region of land that lies somewhere between [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] and [[Lore:Akavir|Akavir]].<noinclude>{{Ref|name=RDAI|{{Cite Book|Report: Disaster at Ionith}}}}</noinclude> It was conquered by [[Lore:Uriel Septim V|Uriel Septim V]] in {{Year|3E 279}} during the opening stages of his campaign against the Dragon Land.<noinclude>{{Ref|[[Lore:Brief History of the Empire v 3|Brief History of the Empire v 3]], ''[[Lore:Books by Author#Stronach k'Thojj III|Stronach k'Thojj III]], Imperial Historian''}}</noinclude> By {{Year|3E 288}}, unrest had broken out, causing the emperor to divert two legions that had been intended as reinforcements to pacify the inhabitants instead, ultimately contributing to the failure of the invasion.<noinclude>{{Ref|name=RDAI}}
