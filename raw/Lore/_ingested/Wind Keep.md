[[Lore:Wind Keep|Wind Keep]] is a settlement found at the base of the Cumberland Falls, in the province of [[Lore:High Rock|High Rock]]. The town's people are well-known across the province for being expert clothiers and tailors, while producing the finest [[Lore:Light Armor|light armor]] around. Many [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] families reside in Wind Keep and live up to its prestige, such as the Garments, the Habiliments, the Raiments, and the one who presides over all of them, [[Lore:House Manteau|House Manteau]].{{Ref|name=CLAB|{{Cite Book|Clothier: Light Armor Basics}}}}<noinclude>

Wind Keep is neighbored by several settlements such as Black Wastes to the north-northeast and Vermeir Wastes to the southwest.{{Ref|name=ARMap|[[:File:AR-map-High Rock towns.png|Map of High Rock – The Elder Scrolls: Arena]]}}

==History==
[[File:ON-npc-Countess Ilise Manteau.jpg|thumb|right|Countess Ilise Manteau ca. 2E 582]]
When Wind Keep was founded is unknown. House Manteau has existed since the early-mid [[Lore:First Era|First Era]]. They made their name at this time by tailoring coats for King [[Lore:Joile|Joile]] of [[Lore:Daggerfall (kingdom)|Daggerfall]] and his court.{{Ref|name=LEAYQ|{{Cite Book|Lady Eloisse Answers Your Questions}}}} Wind Keep was also the name of the fort south of town. When King [[Lore:Aphren Gardner|Aphren Gardner]] of [[Lore:Wayrest (kingdom)|Wayrest]] took it over, he changed its name to [[Lore:Aphren's Hold|Aphren's Hold]] while the settlement kept the original name.{{Ref|name=ONMathias|[[Online:Mathias Raiment|Mathias Raiment]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} 

Sometime in the mid-[[Lore:Second Era|Second Era]], Wind Keep was ruled by an unnamed count, who married a young Lady Ilise of House Manteau. At some point, the count passed away and Ilise Manteau decided to take leadership. Ever since she became a widow, suitors all over [[Lore:Stormhaven|Stormhaven]] came to town to court her, but for years, she never [[Lore:Marriage|re-married]].{{Ref|name=ONMathias}}{{Ref|name=ONIlise|[[Online:Countess Ilise Manteau|Countess Ilise Manteau]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} By {{Year|2E 582}}, Wind Keep was a territory of the [[Lore:Daggerfall Covenant|Daggerfall Covenant]],{{Ref|name=ONGuard|[[Online:Covenant Guard|Covenant Guards]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} and trouble across the surrounding region halted traffic to the town in the last few months, like the [[Lore:Supernal Dreamers|Supernal Dreamers]] in [[Lore:Menevia|Menevia]] and the Ironhand [[Lore:Ogre|Ogres]] in [[Lore:Gavaudon|Gavaudon]].{{Ref|name=ONGerbert|[[Online:Gerbert Lateur|Gerbert Lateur]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

In 2E 582, more suitors came to court the Countess, each looking to elevate their status, one of these people was Sirion Guerat, a member of the Supernal Dreamers trying to overthrow her. His gift to her was a cursed mirror that changed her disposition and turned her into a reclusive shell of her former self. After she called all her servants and dismissed them from work, she shut herself inside the manor, leaving everyone wondering why.{{Ref|name=ONMathias}} 

One of these servants, Mathias Raiment asked an [[Lore:The Vestige|adventurer]] to solve this issue and they found the Countess enchanted by the mirror. The adventurer entered its mirrored realm, saving the Countess from the reflective spirit that trapped her.{{Ref|name=ONMirror|[[Online:A Look in the Mirror|A Look in the Mirror]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Next came the investigation of the culprit but as the adventurer saved the Countess, Mathias Raiment was killed. Lady Ilise went to mourn him at his grave but was saved again just before Sirion Guerat could kill her.{{Ref|name=ONGift|[[Online:Gift from a Suitor|Gift from a Suitor]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

During the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]] in the late [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], the village of Wind Keep was ruled by Lord Uthistyr and had a rivalry with Black Wastes and [[Lore:Norvulk Hills|Norvulk Hills]].{{Ref|name=AR|[[Arena:Wind Keep|Wind Keep]] location and rumors in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}

===Known Rulers===
[[File:ON-place-Manteau Manor.jpg|thumb|right|House Manteau's manor]]
Per the traditions of noble-ranking customs in High Rock,{{Ref|name=NRAT|{{Cite Book|Noble Ranks and Titles}}}} Wind Keep under the rule of its late duchess,{{Ref|name=ONTreasuresF|[[Online:Treasures F#Framed Duchess Portrait|Framed Duchess Portrait]] item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} was the seat of a duchy that only answered to the authority of a King, however, who this ruler or what their kingdom was is never stated. When it came under Countess Manteau's rule, the surrounding land became a county and she would have answered to a Duke or Duchess.{{Ref|name=NRAT}} Who these people were is also never made clear.
