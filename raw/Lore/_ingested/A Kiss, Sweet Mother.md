{{LetterPic|S}}o you wish to summon the {{Lore Link|Dark Brotherhood}}? You wish to see someone dead? Pray, child. Pray, and let the {{Lore Link|Night Mother}} hear your plea.

You must perform that most profane of rituals{{nst| - |ON=&mdash;}}the Black Sacrament. 

Create an effigy of the intended victim, assembled from actual body parts, including a heart, skull, bones and flesh. Encircle that effigy with candles. 

The ritual itself must then commence. Proceed to stab the effigy repeatedly with a dagger rubbed with the petals of a {{Lore Link|Nightshade}} plant, while whispering this plea:

"Sweet Mother, sweet Mother, send your child unto me, for the sins of the unworthy must be baptized in blood and fear." 

Then wait, child, for the Dread Father {{Lore Link|Sithis}} rewards the patient. You will be visited by a representative of the Dark Brotherhood. So begins a contract bound in blood.
