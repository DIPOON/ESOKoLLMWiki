# Lore:Yoku

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

{{Trail|Linguistics}}[[File:RG-comic-Page 23-Speaking Yoku.jpg|thumb|right|[[Lore:Cyrus|Cyrus]]' father speaking Yoku, referring to him as "Sura"]][[File:RG-misc-StarstoneRunes.png|thumb|right|Yokudan runes written upon a Yoku Starstone]][[File:RG-misc-RaGadaRunes.png|thumb|right|Ra Gada runes found in the catacombs of [[Lore:Port Hunding|Stros M'Kai Palace]]]]__NOTOC__
[[Lore:Yoku|Yoku]], sometimes called '''Old [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]]''',{{ref|name=Ghr|{{Cite Book|Ghraewaj}}}} '''Old Yokudan''',{{ref|name=THFOS|{{Cite Book|The Horse-Folk of Silverhoof}}}} or '''High-Yokudan''',{{ref|name=SisHis|{{Cite Book|Systres History: Volume 2}}}} was the ancient native language of [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokuda]]. After the Yokudan migration to [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]] around {{Year|1E 808}}, the settlers began to assimilate into the larger Tamrielic theater and the language was almost entirely replaced to help stabilize foreign trade.{{ref|name=PGE1Ham|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Hammerfell}}}} However, certain groups of Yokudans maintained their language and traditions outside of mainstream Redguard society.{{ref|name=RG|Events of ''[[Redguard:Redguard|Redguard]]''}} The famous Redguard poet, [[Lore:Weltan|Weltan]] of [[Lore:Sentinel|Sentinel]] wrote all of his work in Yoku despite also being fluent in Tamrielic.{{ref|name=TA'r|{{Cite Book|The Alik'r}}}} By the late [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], the language was still spoken, albeit rarely.{{ref|name=LOS|{{Cite Book|Lord of Souls}}}} There is a regional variant of Yoku known as the Banthan dialect. It derives from the eponymous region of [[Lore:Bantha|Bantha]].{{ref|name=Ghr}}

Yoku has 48 different versions of the word "honor", which all trace their roots to ugak-ta.{{ref|group=UOL|name=LVSM|[[General:Lord Vivec's Sword-Meeting With Cyrus the Restless|Lord Vivec's Sword-Meeting With Cyrus the Restless]]}} In the Yoku language, [[Lore:Chrysocolla|chrysocollas]] held a strong association with defense. Interestingly, in Yoku, the term for defense is simply a different tense of the word for offense, rather than being a distinct word.{{Ref|name=LAB|[[ON:Lefthander's Aegis Belt|Lefthander's Aegis Belt]] antiquity codex entries in {{G|ESO|high isle}}}}
  
Despite the geographical proximity of the [[Lore:Lefthanded Elf|Lefthanded Elves]], Yoku and the Sinestral language diverge considerably.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Cries from Empty Mouths}}}}

==Yokudan Holidays==
Many of the holidays extant in modern Hammerfell are said to be direct translations of older Yokudan festivals before their migration to Hammerfell.{{ref|name=HotIB}}

* [[Lore:Aduros Nau|Aduros Nau]]: The villages in the Bantha celebrate the baser urges that come with Springtide on Aduros Nau.
* [[Lore:Baranth Do|Baranth Do]]: Celebrated by the Redguards of the [[Lore:Alik'r Desert|Alik'r Desert]]. Pageants featuring demonic representations of the old year are popular, and revelry to honor the new year is everywhere.
* [[Lore:Chil'a|Chil'a]]: The blessing of the new year in the barony of [[Lore:Kairou|Kairou]], is both a sacred day and a festival. The archpriest and the baroness each consecrate the ashes of the old year in solemn ceremony, then street parades, balls, and tournaments conclude the event.
* [[Lore:Drigh R'Zimb|Drigh R'Zimb]]: Held in the hottest time of year in [[Lore:Abibon-Gora|Abibon-Gora]], is a jubilation held for the sun [[Lore:Daibethe|Daibethe]] itself.
* [[Lore:Ghraewaj|Ghraewaj]]: A festival of the graces the gods have granted the people of [[Lore:Lainlyn|Lainlyn]] over the centuries.
* [[Lore:Koomu Alezer'i|Koomu Alezer'i]]: A tradition in [[Lore:Sentinel|Sentinel]] for thousands of years. The people of Sentinel solemnly thank the gods for their bounty, and pray to be worthy of the graces of the gods.
