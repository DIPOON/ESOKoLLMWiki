The [[Lore:Aalto|Aalto]] (or '''Alto'''),{{ref|name=SRWarrior}}{{ref|name=AltoWine|[[Skyrim:Food#Alto Wine|Alto Wine]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} also known as the '''Eastmarch Geysers''',{{ref|[[SR:Through a Glass, Darkly|Through a Glass, Darkly]] quest journal entries in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} is a geological formation that makes up the central area of [[Lore:Eastmarch|Eastmarch]], in the province of [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]].{{ref|name=SRWarrior|{{Cite Book|Warrior}}}}{{ref|name=HGTS|{{Cite Book|Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim}}}} The volcanic tundra{{ref|name=HGTS}}{{Ref|name=DM|Internal names for [[SR:Dragon Mounds|Dragon Mounds]]}} interior of Eastmarch is a no-man's-land of bubbling sulfur pools, giant camps, crests, slopes, and mines.<ref>Appearance in [[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]</ref><ref>Appearance in [[Online:Online|ESO]]</ref> The entire Hold is dominated by sulfur pools where very little grows, as the volcanic terrain limits farming and development, and seems sunken in compared to the higher elevations of the Rift and the towering Throat of the World.{{Ref|name=TESVSPOGG|{{Cite Book|The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim: Prima Official Game Guide|ns_base=Books}}}}<noinclude>

[[Lore:Alcoholic Beverages#Alto Wine|Alto Wine]] is a common wine found across Skyrim. It is made from [[Lore:Jazbay|Jazbay grapes]], which grow in the Aalto,{{ref|name=SRWarrior}} and it was once considered treason to pick one of these grapes without express permission from the [[Lore:Emperor|Emperor]] himself.{{ref|name=HGTS}} Reven's ''[[Lore:Warrior|Warrior]]'' mentions a privately owned vineyard in the Aalto, which produced wine. Alto Wine was at one point rare and extremely expensive, and the Emperor required the [[Lore:Elder Council|Elder Council]]'s permission to have a glass of it once a year.{{ref|name=SRWarrior}} Although growers in Skyrim were successful in improving the fruit's survivability, it came at the cost of flavor.{{ref|name=HGTS}}

==Notes==
*The location of the Aalto has been retconned slightly: in the original ''[[Lore:Warrior|Warrior]]'' book shipped with ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion]]'', Jazbay Grapes were said to only be able to grow in the Aalto, everywhere else they wither and die.{{ref|name=SRWarrior}} The Aalto was intially described as having been located in "north-central" Skyrim in a volcanic valley of glaciers, but ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim]]'' later retconned the regional placement where Jazbays grow to the volcanic tundra of Eastmarch.{{ref|name=SRWarrior}}{{ref|name=HGTS}}
