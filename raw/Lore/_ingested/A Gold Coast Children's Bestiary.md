{{Lore Link|Goblin}}<br>
Goblins from beyond the heath,<br>
Wear necklaces of children's teeth!<br>
Their arrows with the poison laced,<br>
From nixad guts—ground into paste.

{{Lore Link|Minotaur}}<br>
The minotaur are warriors born.<br>
They hang dead children from their horns!<br>
Don't think them cows; they never "moo."<br>
The meat they most prefer is you!

{{Lore Link|Nixad}}<br>
The nixad flit about like flies.<br>
Their favorite meal is children's eyes!<br>
These sugar fiends are cruel and vain.<br>
They take delight in children's pain.

{{Lore Link|River Troll}}<br>
Every child with half a mind,<br>
Knows river trolls are most unkind.<br>
Those who don't will soon endure,<br>
Their childish minds in troll manure!
