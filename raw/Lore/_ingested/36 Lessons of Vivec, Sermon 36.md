For these were the days of [[Lore:Resdayn|Resdaynia]], when {{Lore Link|Chimer}} and {{Lore Link|Dwemer}} lived under the wise and benevolent rule of the [[Lore:Almsivi|ALMSIVI]] and their champion the [[Lore:Nerevar|Hortator]], though the Dwemer had become foolish and challenged their masters.

Out of their fortresses they came with golden {{Lore Link|Dwarven Ballista|ballistae}} that walked and mighty [[Lore:Dwemer Animunculi|atronachs]] and things that spat flame and things that made killing songs. Their king was [[Lore:Dumac|Dumac]] Dwarf-Orc, but their high priest was [[Lore:Kagrenac|Kagrenac]] the Blighter.

Under mountains and over them the [[Lore:War of the First Council|war]] with the Dwemer was raged, and then came the {{Lore Link|Nord|northern men}} to [[Lore:Five Songs of King Wulfharth#The Ash King|help Kagrenac]] and they brought [[Lore:Wulfharth|Ysmir]] again.

Leading the armies of the Chimer was the slave that would not perish, the Hortator Nerevar, who had traded his axe for the Ethos Knife. He slew Dumac at {{Lore Link|Red Mountain}} and saw the [[Lore:Heart of Lorkhan|heart bone]] for the first time.

Men of brass destroyed the eleven gates of the {{Lore Link|Mournhold|Mourning Hold}} and behind them came the Dwemeri architects of tone. {{Lore Link|Almalexia|Ayem}} threw down her cloak and became the Face-Snaked Queen of the [[Lore:Tribunal|Three in One]]. Those that looked upon her were overcome by the meanings of the stars.

Under the sea, {{Lore Link|Sotha Sil|Seht}} stirred and brought the army he had been working on in the castles of glass and coral. Clockwork {{Lore Link|dreugh}}s, mockeries of the {{Lore Link|Dwemer Animunculi|Dwemeri war machines}}, rose up from the seas and took their counterparts back beneath, where they were swallowed forever by the sea.

Red Mountain exploded as the Hortator went too far inside, seeking the {{Lore Link|Dagoth Ur (god)|Sharmat}}.

Dwemeri high priest Kagrenac then revealed that which he had built in the image of {{Lore Link|Vivec|MWlink=Morrowind:Vivec (god)}}. It was a [[Lore:Numidium|walking star]], which burnt the armies of the Triune and destroyed the [[Lore:Vvardenfell|heartland]] of [[Lore:Morrowind|Veloth]], creating the [[Lore:Inner Sea|Inner Sea]].

Each of the aspects of the ALMSIVI then rose up together, combining as one, and showed the world the sixth path. Ayem took from the star its fire, Seht took from it its mystery, and Vehk took from it its feet, which had been constructed {{Lore Link|36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 12|before the gift}} of {{Lore Link|Molag Bal}} and destroyed in the manner of truth: by a great hammering. When the soul of the Dwemer could walk no more, they were removed from this world.

Resdaynia was no more. It had been redeemed of all the iniquities of the foolish. The ALMSIVI drew nets from the Beginning Place and captured the ash of Red Mountain, which they knew was {{Lore Link|the Blight}} of the Dwemer and that would serve only to infect the whole of the [[Lore:Tamriel|middle world]], and ate it. ALTADOON {{Lore Link|Dunmer|DUNMERI}}!

The beginning of the words is ALMSIVI. I give you this as Vivec.
