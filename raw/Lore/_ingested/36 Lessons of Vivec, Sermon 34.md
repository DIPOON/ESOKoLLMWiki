Then {{Lore Link|Vivec|MWlink=Morrowind:Vivec (god)}} left the {{Lore Link|Ministry of Truth}} and went back to the space that was not a space. From the Provisional House he looked into the middle world to find the eighth and final and mightiest monster, called GULGA MOR JIL and more. The wise must look {{Lore Link|36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 12|elsewhere}} for this string of power.

Vivec called to his side the Hortator and this was the first time that [[Lore:Nerevar|Nerevar]] had ever been to the Provisional House. He had the {{Lore Link|36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 19|same vision}} that Vivec had so many years ago: that of the [[Lore:Talos|two-headed ruling king]].

:{{nst|'|ON="}}Who is that?{{nst|'|ON="}} he wondered.

Vivec said, {{nst|'|ON="}}The [[Lore:Amulet of Kings|red jewel]] of [[Lore:Third Empire|conquest]].{{nst|'|ON="}}

Nerevar, perhaps because he was frightened, became vexed at his lord's answer. {{nst|'|ON="}}Why are you always so evasive?{{nst|'|ON="}}

Vivec told the Hortator that to be otherwise was to betray his nature.

Together they moved into the middle world, to a village near where Vivec had been found by {{Lore Link|Almalexia|Ayem}} and {{Lore Link|Sotha Sil|Seht}}. The eighth monster was there, but he did not act much like a monster. He sat with his legs in the ocean and with a troubled look on his face. When he saw his mother-father, he asked why he should have to die and return to {{Lore Link|oblivion}}.

Vivec told the eighth monster that to be otherwise was to betray his nature. Since this did not seem to satisfy the monster and Vivec still had a touch of {{Lore Link|36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 3|Ayem's mercy}} he said:



:{{nst|'|ON="}}The fire is mine: let it consume thee,

:And make a secret door

:At the altar of [[Lore:Padomay|Padhome]],

:In the House of {{Lore Link|Boethiah|Boet-hi-Ah}}

:Where we become safe

:And looked after.{{nst|'|ON="}}



The monster accepted Muatra with a peaceful look and his bones became the foundation for the [[Lore:Necrom|City of the Dead]], anon {{sic|{{Lore Link|Narsis}}|Necrom}}.

Nerevar put away his axe, which he had at the ready, and frowned.

:{{nst|'|ON="}}Why,{{nst|'|ON="}} he said, {{nst|'|ON="}}did you ask me to come if you knew the eighth monster would give in so easily?{{nst|'|ON="}}

Vivec looked at the Hortator for a long time.

Nerevar understood. {{nst|'|ON="}}Do not betray your nature. Answer as you will.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Vivec said, {{nst|'|ON="}}I brought you here because I knew the mightiest of my issue would succumb to Muatra without argument, if only I gave him consolation first.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Nerevar looked at Vivec for a long time.

Vivec understood. {{nst|'|ON="}}Say the words, Hortator.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Nerevar said, {{nst|'|ON="}}Now I am the mightiest of your children.{{nst|'|ON="}}

Let this sermon be consolation to those who read it that are destined to die.

The ending of the words is [[Lore:Almsivi|ALMSIVI]].
