A [[Lore:Daedra Lord|Daedra Lord]] (also spelled '''Daedric Lord'''{{ref|name=ONTelbarilOran|[[Online:Telbaril Oran|Telbaril Oran]]'s dialogue during [[Online:A Son's Promise|A Son's Promise]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} or '''Daedroth Lord'''){{ref|name=MWGenericF|[[MW:Generic Dialogue F|Generic dialogue regarding Farvyn Oreyn]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}}{{intnote|DaedricLord|[nb 1]}} is a greater [[Lore:Daedra|Daedric]] being who occupies a rarefied space near the top of Daedric society, just one rung down from the Daedric Princes themselves,{{ref|name=LAIA|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive - Infinite Archive}}}} maintaining great power and hierarchy over [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]] that is matched only by the [[Lore:Demiprince|Demiprinces]],{{ref|name=LHDR|{{Cite Book|Lord Hollowjack's Dread Realm}}}} and exceeded only by the [[Lore:Daedric Prince|Daedric Princes]].{{ref|name=ESOLive1|group=UOL|[[General:ESO Live Funny Lore Bits with Lawrence Schick and Zach Bush#Is Hircine the Daedric Prince of Fishing?|Is Hircine the Daedric Prince of Fishing?]] on [[General:ESO Live|ESO Live]]}} The powers of the Daedra Lords are varied in scope, with some embodying their own individual spheres, and others being servants of the Daedric Princes who help maintain sub-spheres.{{ref|name=ESOLive1|group=UOL}} Many of the more powerful Lords also rule over their own realms and demi-planes within the infinite swirling void of Oblivion.{{ref|name=LHDR}}{{ref|name=LAIA}}

Daedra Lords are one of the most powerful Daedra to be commonly seen in [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]], and are to be feared by all but the most experienced adventurers. They are known to be powerful fighters and spell casters, and can only be harmed by [[Lore:mithril|mithril]] or better [[Lore:Weapons|weapons]].{{ref|[[Daggerfall:Daedra Lord|Daedra Lord]]s in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}} [[Lore:Mehrunes Dagon|Mehrunes Dagon]] has many Daedra Lords in his service.{{ref|[[BS:Daedra Lord|Daedra Lords]] in ''[[BS:Battlespire|Battlespire]]''}}{{Ref|name=BS}} <noinclude>

While only "a few handfuls" of Daedric Princes are known to mortal scholars, there are dozens of known Daedra Lords whose identities have been recorded, and a potentially infinite amount more within Oblivion, who simply haven't interacted with mortals or been recorded doing so yet.{{ref|name=LAIA}}

==Known Daedra Lords==
*The {{Lore Link|Bad Man}} is a minor Daedric Lord of [[Lore:Bad Man's Hallows|Bad Man's Hallows]], a pocket realm centered around his form, a tree that channels dark energy.{{ref|name=EV|Dialogue with [[ON:Eveline Vette|Eveline Vette]]}} He took the form of a [[Lore:Dremora|Dremora]] Lord masquerading as the [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] God [[Lore:Gods_S#Sheor|Sheor]], who is the true Bad Man.{{ref|name=EV}}{{ref|name=ONLBMH|[[Online:Bad Man's Hallows|Bad Man's Hallows loading screen]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
*The {{Lore Link|Crow Mother}}, [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]]'s "Nether-Niece" that has taken the form of a [[Lore:Hagraven|Hagraven]], who rules over the [[Lore:Crow's Wood|Crow's Wood]] pocket realm.{{ref|name=ONCrowMother|[[Online:Crow Mother|Crow Mother]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=ONTelbarilOran}}
*[[Lore:Faydra Shardai|Faydra Shardai]] is the leader of the [[Lore:Shardai Clan|Shardai Clan]] and is associated with Mehrunes Dagon,{{Ref|name=BS}} who considers her his adoptive daughter.{{ref|name=BSAthenaeum|{{Cite Book|ns_base=Books|Battlespire Athenaeum}}}}{{rp|156,159}}
*[[Lore:Hollowjack|Hollowjack]], a [[Lore:Pumpkin Spectre|Pumpkin Spectre]], is the Daedra Lord of mortal fears who rules over the demi-plane [[Lore:Detritus|Detritus]].{{ref|name=LHDR}}
*[[Lore:Imago Storm|Imago Storm]] is the leader of the [[Lore:Dremora Clan|Dremora Clan]] and served under Mehrunes Dagon for a time.{{Ref|name=BS|Events of [[Battlespire:Battlespire|''Battlespire'']]}}
*{{Lore Link|Menthyx}} is a powerful Daedra Lord who was defeated by the Dremora Xynaa and the Ambition Sombren. After his defeat, his essence was bound and he was imprisoned in a dungeon in the Deadlands known as the Spidernest. The [[Lore:Vestige|Vestige]] and Sombren used the skull of Menthyx to recharge his power which undid the curse Xynaa placed upon him, allowing him to reform.{{ref|name=ONSombren|[[Online:Sombren|Sombren]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
*[[Lore:Sumeer Jabran|Sumeer Jabran]] is a Daedra Lord who served under both Mehrunes Dagon and [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]] for a time.{{Ref|name=BS}}{{ref|[[Online:Dremora Travelers|Dremora Travelers]] in [[ESO:Online|ESO]]}}
*[[Lore:Tho'at Replicanum|Tho'at Replicanum]] is a Daedra Lord associated with the [[Lore:Daedric Prince|Daedric Prince]] [[Lore:Ithelia|Ithelia]],{{ref|group=UOL|1=[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JhQeU-Izx1w YouTube Stream of ESO's ''10-Year Anniversary Celebration Livestream''], time stamp 38:53-39:36}} and Tho'at herself resembles the glass-like Shardborn from the realm of [[Lore:Mirrormoor|Mirrormoor]].{{ref|name=LAIA}}{{ref|name=ESO2024GR|[[General:ESO 2024 Global Reveal|ESO 2024 Global Reveal]]}} Tho'at has an affinity toward ink, purportedly being made of it herself. She created the [[Lore:Maligraphy|Maligraphies]], beings made from ink, during her invasion of [[Lore:Apocrypha|Apocrypha]] circa {{Year|2E 582}}.{{ref|name=Malkhest|[[ESO:Master Malkhest|Master Malkhest]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}{{ref|name=LAIA}}
*[[Lore:Velark‎|Velark‎]] is a minor Daedric Lord linked to the incident at the lands of Edrald family.{{Ref|name=KE|[[ON:Krisandra Edrald|Krisandra Edrald]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
*[[Lore:Xivilai Moath|Xivilai Moath]] is the leader of the [[Lore:Xivilai's Clan|Xivilai's Clan]] and is associated with Mehrunes Dagon,{{Ref|name=BS}}{{ref|name=CyrodGazetteer|[[Betrayal:Betrayal of the Second Era|Betrayal of the Second Era]] ''Cyrodiil Gazetteer''}}{{rp|23,44}} who considers him his adoptive son.{{ref|name=BSAthenaeum}}{{rp|156,159}} For reasons unknown, he briefly served [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]] during the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]].{{ref|[[Online: Storming the Garrison|Storming the Garrison]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
*The Daedric Lord of Fishing is a  Daedra Lord of which scarcely anything is known, save that they are a servant of Hircine, and have a distinctive aroma.{{ref|name=ESOLive1|group=UOL}}
*[[Lore:The Impresario|The Impresario]] seems to imply in her writings that she is a Daedra Lord,{{ref|name=LATH}} possibly in reference to claims that she is a [[Lore:Demi-Prince|Demi-Prince]] in mortal disguise.{{ref|{{Cite book|Meet the Character - The Impresario}}}}

==Notes==
*{{LNote|DaedricLord|^|The term "Daedric Lord" is a phrase with connotations of "nobility", denoting the Daedroth in question as a power of importance. However, the usage of this term may lead some to conflate the Princes with Daedra Lords,{{ref|name=MH|{{Cite Book|Modern Heretics}}}}{{ref|{{Cite Book|The True Nature of Orcs}}}}{{ref|[[Oblivion:Aymar Douar|Aymar Douar]]'s dialogue in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} which are a separate kind of Daedra.{{ref|{{Cite Book|Rulantaril's Notes|ns_base=Online}}}} All Daedric Princes are Daedric Lords, but not all Daedra Lords are Daedric Princes.}}
**Demiprinces are also called Daedra Lords,{{ref|name=ONTF|[[Online:Tutor Riparius|Tutor Riparius]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} but like Daedric Princes, All Demiprinces are Daedric Lords, not all Daedra Lords are Demiprinces.
*[[Lore:Lyranth|Lyranth the Foolkiller]] extracted the power of the [[Lore:Four Ambitions|Four Ambitions]] from [[Lore:Mairead|Mairead]] in the aftermath of Dagon's banishment and escaped. As she thereafter possessed a portion of a Greater Daedra's power, she may be considered a Daedra Lord.{{ref|name=ONAE|Events of [[Online:Ambition's End|Ambition's End]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
