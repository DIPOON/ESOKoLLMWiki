# Lore:Horse

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

{{Lore Bestiary Trail}}__NOTOC__
'''Horses''' are quadrupedal animals used for travel. They may occasionally be eaten, but this is considered in bad taste by certain cultures, and their hides can be tanned into leather. It is said that since the dawn of time, wherever there are humans, there have been horses. As such they originate in various continents, such as [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]], [[Lore:Atmora|Atmora]], and [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokuda]].{{ref|{{Cite Book|Lady Honnorah af-Lahreq Answers Your Questions}}}} Scholars believe that when human settlers arrived from Atmora, they brought with them shaggy breeds of horse adapted to the cold climate, but they also discovered the many horses native to Tamriel itself, undomesticated at the time by the Elves, who relied on different beasts.{{Ref|name=ESOMT1|{{Cite Book|Mounts of Tamriel: Volume 1, Horses}}}}

There are many different varieties of horses found across [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]], but cannot adapt to [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]]'s harsh climate.{{ref|name=MWGaro|[[Morrowind:Garothmuk gro-Muzgub|Garothmuk gro-Muzgub]]'s dialogue in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}} They are bred for many purposes, including combat, sport, and labor. Horses can be outfitted in armor.{{ref|name=OBHA|[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]] [[Oblivion:Horse Armor Pack|Horse Armor DLC]]}}{{ref|name=DWH|[[ON:Dwarven War Horse|Dwarven War Horse]] mount appearance and description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} As with many creatures, horses can be reanimated by necromancers.{{ref|name=FLC|[[ON:Fang Lair Courser|Fang Lair Courser]] and [[ON:Skeletal Horse|Skeletal Horse]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Two [[Lore:Septim Dynasty|Septim]] emperors died from falling off a horse. The first was [[Lore:Cephorus I|Cephorus I]] in {{Year|3E 140}} at sixty-three years old.{{Ref|name=TWQ8|{{Cite Book|The Wolf Queen, v8}}}} The second one was [[Lore:Uriel VI|Uriel VI]] in {{Year|3E 320}}, who despite the efforts of the finest Imperial healers, could not be saved.{{Ref|name=BHOTE4|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 4}}}}{{Ref|name=TTET|{{Cite Book|The Third Era Timeline}}}}

===White Horse===
White Horses are a type of horse breed. In [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]], they are associated with [[Lore:Anvil|Anvil]] and considered the strongest breed in the province.{{Ref|name=OBClesa|[[Oblivion:Clesa|Clesa]]'s dialogue in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} The southern [[Lore:Nibenese|Nibenese]] county of [[Lore:Leyawiin|Leyawiin]] uses the Ivory Horse as their coat of arms. But its origins are unknown and is associated with a couple myths. One tradition ties it to the ancient hero, [[Lore:Pelinal Whitestrake|Pelinal Whitestrake]] but one from the [[Lore:Merethic Era|days of myth]] claims that [[Lore:Blackwood|Blackwood]] was the home to a white horse of divine strength and shining beauty that protected the [[Lore:Niben River|Niben River]]'s banks.{{Ref|name=TGTL|{{Cite Book|Traveler's Guide to Leyawiin}}}}

===Yokudan Charger===
The '''Yokudan Charger''' is a breed found in the [[Lore:Alik'r|Alik'r Desert]] of [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]].{{Ref|name=LOTYC|{{Cite Book|Legend of the Yokudan Chargers}}}} They are descendants of the gray Yokudan Warhorses brought from [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokuda]] by the [[Lore:Ra Gada|Ra Gada]].{{Ref|name=ONYokudan|[[Online:Gray Yokudan Charger|Gray Yokudan Charger]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=ACTB|{{Cite Book|A Child's Tamriel Bestiary}}}} These horses are exceptional for their ability to traverse the Alik'r Desert's terrain, unlike other horse breeds. They are also the primary steed of choice for the [[Lore:Gallants of Hammerfell|Gallants of Hammerfell]], the knights of the province. The [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] family in [[Lore:Aswala Stables|Aswala Stables]] breed and exclusively sell Yokudan Chargers.{{Ref|name=LOTYC}} The Galvanic Storm Steed is a breed of Yokudan Charger that is infused with the magic of [[Lore:Storm Atronach|Storm Atronachs]].{{Ref|name=ONGalvanic|[[Online:Galvanic Storm Steed|Galvanic Storm Steed]] mount description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

===Yokudan Warhorse===
The '''Yokudan Warhorse''' was a breed from [[Lore:Akos Kasaz|Akos Kasaz]], the largest island on Yokuda. These horses were brought across the [[Lore:Eltheric Ocean|Eltheric Ocean]] by [[Lore:Yaghoub|Yaghoub]],{{Ref|name=LOTYC}} who later founded [[Lore:Sentinel|Sentinel]].{{Ref|name=STJOA|{{Cite Book|Sentinel, the Jewel of Alik'r}}}} These horses live on through the Yokudan Charger of modern-day Alik'r and because of this, some people call the original warhorses, the Forebear.{{Ref|name=LOTYC}}{{Ref|name=ACTB}}

==Horse-like Beings==
===Frost Mare===
Although horselike in appearance, [[Lore:Forst Mare|Frost Mares]] are composed of solid ice, appearing as if carved from a [[Lore:Skyrim|Skyrim]] glacier. The [[Lore:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] theorized that the creatures originate from the [[Lore:Fourth Sinus of Takubar|Fourth Sinus of Takubar]]. Frost Mares are rarely used by mortals as mounts. They are not to be confused with Frost Atronach Horses, which are just normal [[Lore:Frost Atronach|Frost Atronach]]s compelled to take the shape of a horse by powerful [[Lore:Conjuration|conjuration]] magic.{{ref|[[Lore:Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase|Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase]]}}
