The [[Lore:Azura's Coast|Azura's Coast]] (or '''Telvanni Coast'''){{Ref|name=RubyShalk|[[Online:Ruby Shroom Shalk|Ruby Shroom Shalk]] pet description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} region consisted mainly of the numerous islands to the south and east of [[Lore:Vvardenfell|Vvardenfell]]. It included all of the southern islands east of the Daedric ruin of [[Lore:Ald Sotha|Ald Sotha]], extended north to include the islands up the eastern coast and [[Lore:Zafirbel Bay|Zafirbel Bay]], and three small peninsulas on the southeastern corner of Vvardenfell where a Shrine of Azura could be found.

The area was fairly desolate, and most of the territory was claimed by [[Lore:House Telvanni|House Telvanni]]. It included the towns and towers of [[Lore:Tel Mora|Tel Mora]], [[Lore:Tel Aruhn|Tel Aruhn]], [[Lore:Sadrith Mora|Sadrith Mora]], [[Lore:Tel Fyr|Tel Fyr]], and [[Lore:Tel Branora|Tel Branora]]. It was also home to numerous wizards, sorcerers, necromancers, and [[Lore:Ashlanders|Ashlander]] Mabrigashes. Many of these were based in old Velothi remnants or caves, rather than wizard towers. By no means were all magic-workers in the area Telvanni, but the dominance of the Telvanni did mean that there was little objection to their activities.

As the area was dominated by the Telvanni, it was one of the least subject to the power of either the [[Lore:Third Empire|Empire]] or the [[Lore:Tribunal|Tribunal]], though both had enclaves in the House capital, Sadrith Mora. Since Telvanni lords were usually over a thousand years old and very powerful, they tended to see the "gods" of the Tribunal as peers rather than supreme beings.

Despite the rocky terrain, a variety of plants thrive on the region's regular rainfall.{{Ref|name=MorrowindDialogue|[[Morrowind:Generic Dialogue A|''Azura's Coast'' dialogue option]] in ''[[Morrowind:Morrowind|Morrowind]]''}} However, there were Ashlander camps scattered throughout the region. The area was full of ancestral tombs, and there were several significant Daedric Shrines on the islands. Many bandits preyed on unwary travelers.<noinclude>
