{{Lore History Trail}}[[File:BL-quest-Goblins United.jpg|thumb|right|Tribes of Cyrodiil uniting circa {{Year|4E 180}}]]{{Quote Box|style=float:none; max-width:none|''Goblin tribes are notoriously insular and wary of outsiders, not surprising considering their only interactions with people tend to be at the point of swords.''|Landal Gevont, Scholar of Goblins{{Ref|name=LandalGevont|[[ON:Landal Gevont|Landal Gevont]]'s dialogue in {{g|ESO}}}}}}{{TOCright}}
[[Lore:Goblin|Goblins]] usually live in a tribal society, although they are not always part of a named tribe. Those tribes that do have names are notable for their use of symbols, strong leadership, and distinctive traditions.

==Tribes by Province==
===Black Marsh===
----
====Gnaw-Root Tribe====
The '''Gnaw-Root Tribe''' are found in the [[Lore:Shadowfen|Shadowfen]] region of [[Lore:Black Marsh|Black Marsh]]. They were enslaved by the [[Lore:Ebonheart Pact|Ebonheart Pact]] circa {{Year|2E 582}} to work the [[Lore:Iron|iron]] mines at {{Lore Link|Mud Tree Village}}. However, the tribe revolted and briefly took control of the mine. The Pact managed to subdue them by taking their tribal totem and planned to continue using the goblins as slaves, but the local [[Lore:Argonian|Argonian]]s protested and wanted to release them into the swamp. The ultimate fate of the tribe is unknown.

====Bitterfish Tribe====
The '''Bitterfish Tribe''' are a loosely organized tribe found in the [[Lore:West Weald|West Weald]] of [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]]. They use [[Lore:Slaughterfish|slaughterfish]] as a food source, and ritually carry their [[Lore:Scales|scales]]. They attempted to take over the [[Lore:Ayleid|Ayleid]] ruins of [[Lore:Miscarcand|Miscarcand]] in {{Year|3E 433}}, but were driven back by its undead guardians.{{ref|name=Oblivion}}

====Blood Skull Tribe====
The '''Blood Skull Tribe''' were a Goblin tribe found in the [[Lore:Gold Coast|Gold Coast]]. When the Sharp Rock Tribe tricked the Shatter Bone Tribe into attacking the Blood Skulls, they quickly found themselves on a three-sided war with both the Shatter Bone and the Gut Blade Tribe. The Sharp Rock Tribe then allied with the Eye Stick Tribe to conquer those three tribes, but the allies turned on each other at the eve of their victory. Survivors of all the five tribes banded together and formed the Rock Stick and Sharp Stick Tribes.{{ref|name=GCGT|{{Cite Book|Gold Coast Goblin Tribes}}}}

====Bloody Hand Tribe====
[[File:OB-icon-Goblin-Bloody Hand.png|right]]
The '''Bloody Hand Tribe''' inhabit Cracked Wood Cave in the [[Lore:Nibenay Basin|Nibenay Basin]]. Circa {{Year|2E 582}}, the tribe had invaded and occupied [[Lore:Harlun's Watch|Harlun's Watch]] and was aggressively encroaching on the settlement of [[Lore:Cropsford|Cropsford]], where the villagers feared an attack was imminent. The hostility was evidently too much, as Cropsford was later abandoned. In {{Year|3E 433}}, their totem was stolen by the [[Lore:Rock Biter Tribe|Rock Biter Tribe]], inciting a Goblin war. The war ended after a group of settlers got external help to stop the constant raiding parties preventing them from building a farm at Cropsford.{{ref|name=Oblivion}}

====Rock Stick Tribe====
The '''Rock Stick Tribe''' was a Goblin Tribe of the [[Lore:Gold Coast|Gold Coast]] region. They formed when the survivors of a complicated tribal war between the Eye Stick, Sharp Rock, Shatter Bones, Blood Skull, and Gut Blade Tribes banded together (a separate group of survivors of that war founded the Sharp Stick Tribe). Later, they were slaughtered by the Long Claw and Tooth Rock Tribes when they migrated into the Gold Coast from near [[Lore:Chorrol|Chorrol]]. The remaining members of the three tribes were then known as the Long Tooth Tribe.{{ref|name=GCGT}}

Dithis was then kidnapped and taken to a nearby Goblin camp by the Shadowsilk chieftain Grimmstal, prompting a rescue.{{ref|name=ONAGA|[[Online:A Goblin's Affection|A Goblin's Affection]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

====Toothmaul Tribe====
The '''Toothmaul Tribe''' are found on [[Lore:Auridon|Auridon]] in the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset Isles]]. Originally slaves from the main [[Lore:Summerset Isle|Summerset Isle]], the Goblins escaped from captivity and laid claim to a cave they named Toothmaul Gully. They then formed a tribe, led by Chief Koth.{{ref|name=byz|[[Online:Byz|Byz]]'s dialogue in {{g|ESO}}}} In return for the ability to recruit tribe members into labor and fighting forces, the Heritance provided the Goblins with reserve arms and armor. After discovering the Goblin occupation of the gully, the {{Lore Link|First Auridon Marines}} broke up the alliance and ensured the tribe's survival for their continued cooperation with the [[Lore:Altmer|Altmer]].
