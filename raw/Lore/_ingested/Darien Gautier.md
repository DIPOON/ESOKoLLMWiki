[[Lore:Darien Gautier|Darien Gautier]] was a hero of the [[Lore:Daggerfall Covenant|Daggerfall Covenant]] who discovered his true nature as [[Lore:Meridia|Meridia]]'s '''Golden Knight''' during the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]]. Brought into the world as a [[Lore:Light of Meridia|Light of Meridia]] through uncertain circumstances, Darien eventually became captain of the Camlorn Guard and an officer of the [[Lore:Lion Guard|Lion Guard]]. He would later join up with the [[Lore:Fighters Guild|Fighters Guild]] and helped lead their forces during the coalition invasion of [[Lore:Coldharbour|Coldharbour]], disappearing in the {{Lore Link|Planar Vortex}} in a confrontation with [[Lore:Molag Bal|Molag Bal]]. Darien soon returned to Tamriel during the [[Lore:Daedric War|Daedric War]] as Meridia's vessel, learning that he was no ordinary [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] and possessed many otherworldly powers. He helped defend [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]] from [[Lore:Nocturnal|Nocturnal]]'s plot, sacrificing himself to restore [[Lore:Dawnbreaker|Dawnbreaker]].{{fact}}<noinclude>

==Biography==
Darien was born on the 17th of [[Lore:First Seed|First Seed]] in the sixth century of the [[Lore:Second Era|Common Era]].{{Ref|name=ESOLeadershipAUA|group=UOL|[https://www.reddit.com/r/elderscrollsonline/comments/1nbrmqf/comment/ndb741c/?context{{=}}3 ESO Leadership r/elderscrollsonline Ask Us Anything]}} By the time he reached adulthood, he remembered very little of his childhood, considering it all a big blur. He is known to have had a [[Lore:Breton|Breton]] father, General Gautier, who was a high-ranking member of the Lion Guard and member of the [[Lore:Gautier|Gautier]] noble family of [[Lore:Camlorn|Camlorn]],{{Ref|name=ESOLoad|[[Online:Ebon Crypt (place)|Ebon Crypt]] loading screen in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} but had never met his mother, nor had any recollection of her. Darien would later discover that he was a Champion of [[Lore:Meridia|Meridia]], being brought into Tamriel as her vessel and last light.{{Ref|name=DG|[[ON:Darien Gautier|Darien Gautier]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

Before the Planemeld begun, Darien began having strange premonitions of Dark Anchors and Daedra invading Tamriel, each one ending with a strange light guiding him to safety. In {{Year|2E 582}}, his nightmares became reality when the Dark Anchors started to fall and the Planemeld proper had started.{{ref|{{Cite Book|The Journal of Darien Gautier|ns_base=Online}}}} Opting to keep his prophetic dreams secret, he helped keep the peace in the Daggerfall Covenant the best he could. He defended Camlorn from [[Lore:Faolchu|Faolchu]] and helped thwart the plans of [[Lore:Angof|Angof the Gravesinger]]'s Bloodthorn Cult at [[Lore:Cath Bedraud|Cath Bedraud]], noticing that he was somehow unaffected by the poisonous mists that were unleashed.{{Ref|name=ONFaolchu|[[Online:The Fall of Faolchu|The Fall of Faolchu]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=ESOAngof|[[Online:Angof the Gravesinger (quest)|Angof the Gravesinger]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Darien assisted the Vestige in [[Lore:Rivenspire|Rivenspire]], particularly in liberating Northpoint, where a bright flash of light mysteriously killed bloodfiends attacking him.{{Ref|name=ONLiberation|[[Online:The Liberation of Northpoint|The Liberation of Northpoint]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} In [[Lore:Bangkorai|Bangkorai]] he was also joining forces with both the Vestige, [[Lore:Gabrielle Benele|Gabrielle Benele]], and the Mages Guild to protect Queen Arzhela from the Imperial plot.{{ref|name=ONTP|[[Online:The Parley|The Parley]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
[[File:ON-quest-The Final Assault 05.jpg|The Light of Meridia is unleashed in the heart of the Planar Vortex|thumb|right]]
During the invasion of Coldharbour, Darien chose to join the Fighters Guild in their assault and would prove to be instrumental in the battle against [[Lore:Molag Grunda|Molag Grunda]]'s forces in the Chasm. When the time came to assault the Planar Vortex directly and put an end the Planemeld, Darien fought alongside [[Lore:Laloriaran Dynar|the Last Ayleid King]] to protect the Vestige, Vanus Galerion, and Gabrielle Benele while they trigged the complete destruction of the Planar Vortex by detonating the fabled Lights of Meridia within. When the battle was over, however, both King Dynar and Darien were apparent casualties of the explosion, with the latter having vanished altogether.{{ref|name=ESOTFS|[[ON:The Final Assault|The Final Assault]] quest in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

In spite of his demise, rumors of Darien's survival still persisted. While assisting the people of [[Lore:Orsinium|Orsinium]], the Vestige found a strange note seemingly written by Darien himself, claiming to be trapped within the Colored Rooms.{{Ref|name=APfH|{{Cite Book|A Plea for Help|ns_base=ON}}}} When this note was shown to Gabrielle, she speculated that Meridia somehow saved him from the blast and that he is still alive, as his body didn't return to Coldharbour with them like Dynar's.{{Ref|name=Gabrielle|[[Online:Gabrielle Benele#Anvil|Dialogue with Gabrielle]]}} The Grand Prognosticator [[Lore:Sight|prophecized]] seeing a "golden knight" at the culmination of the {{Lore Link|Daedric War}}.{{Ref|name=TGP|[[Online:The Grand Prognosticator|The Grand Prognosticator]]'s Dialogue in [[ON:Cogs of Fate|ESO]]}}

Gabrielle's theory would prove to be correct during the Daedric invasion of [[Lore:Summerset Isle|Summerset]], where Darien finally returns to Nirn as a Knight of Meridia. He promised to serve Meridia as her champion and stop the plot of the Triad in exchange for his freedom, and would be given [[Lore:Dawnbreaker|Dawnbreaker]] as a part of this deal. Unfortunately, the sword would quickly be stolen and corrupted by Nocturnal, while the Triad invaded Meridia's realm directly to incapacitate her, leaving Darien without orders and without a weapon. Nevertheless, he continued onward with his mission and climbed the [[Lore:Crystal Tower|Crystal Tower]] with the Vestige and the ex-[[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic]] Leythen in a last ditch effort to stop Nocturnal from claiming the Tower's Stone, Transparent Law. As they approached the top, Darien would come to understand his true identity as a Light of Meridia, and sacrificed his remaining energy to purify Dawnbreaker for the Vestige.{{ref|name=Summerset|Events of [[Online:Summerset|Summerset]]}}

Many prominent figures active in the decade following the onset of the Planemeld were immortalized in a painting commemorating their adventures. Among them was Darien, portrayed in conversation with his friends, Gabrielle and Skordo the Knife.{{ref|[[Online:Painting: 10-Year Anniversary|Painting: 10-Year Anniversary]] furnishing depiction in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

===Legacy===
[[File:ON-quest-A New Alliance 04.jpg|right|thumb|Darien's last words]]
Darien's sacrifice was not in vain as the Vestige used the purified Dawnbreaker to vanquish Nocturnal atop the Crystal Tower and end the Daedric War.{{ref|name=Summerset}} After dissipating, Darien found that he had survived and appeared in the [[Lore:Colored Rooms|Colored Rooms]] once more, but this time was different. The light within him was fading fast and Darien thought himself beyond help, and so he used the last of his energy to send a [[Lore:Words of the Fallen|final goodbye]] to the Vestige before, presumably, discorporating.{{Ref|name=WotF|{{Cite Book|Words of the Fallen}}}} Meridia would soon be reported to have been looking for a new knight who would not defy her, using her [[Lore:Meridia's Beacon|beacon]].{{Ref|name=JN|{{Cite Book|J'saad's Note|ns_base=ON}}}} In spite of his letter, Gabrielle believed that Darien may still exist somewhere, and vowed to not give up on bringing him back for good.{{Ref|name=Gabrielle}} The Vestige's encounter with the mage Ylahizu the Magnificent saw the latter briefly summoning a vision of a lost spirit, wielding the Dawnbreaker and wearing the exact same armor as Darien.{{Ref|name=YtM|[[ON:Ylahizu the Magnificent|Ylahizu the Magnificent]] random encounter}}
