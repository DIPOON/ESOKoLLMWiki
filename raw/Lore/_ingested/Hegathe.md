[[Lore:Hegathe|Hegathe]] (also called '''Old Hegathe'''){{Ref|name=PGE1E|{{Cite Book|PGE|1|Hammerfell}}}} is one of the eight major cities in the province of [[Lore:Hammerfell|Hammerfell]],{{Ref|name=ARMap|[[:File:AR-map-Hammerfell towns.png|Map of Hammerfell – ''The Elder Scrolls: Arena'']]}} found in the forests of the tropical western shore landings of the [[Lore:Abecean Sea|Abecean Sea]].{{Ref|name=ARTerrain|[[:File:AR-map-Terrain.png|Arena terrain]] map}}{{Ref|name=PGE1E}}{{Ref|name=TVC|{{Cite Book|The Vanishing Crux}}}} Hegathe was once the capital of Hammerfell, ever since the [[Lore:Ra Gada|Ra Gada]] landed on its shores in {{Year|1E 808}} until it was moved to the north, in the [[Lore:Sentinel (kingdom)|Kingdom of Sentinel]]. It is known for its austere ramparts that harken back to antiquity, and is considered an equal to [[Lore:Wayrest|Wayrest]] in terms of splendor.{{Ref|name=PGE1E}}{{ref|name=GPTv3|group=UOL}} The name originally referred to the wastelands that was later designated as Hammerfell, deriving from the [[Lore:Ehlnofex Languages|Aldmeri]] word for ''"Deathland"''.{{Ref|name=PGE3E|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Hammerfell}}}} Despite the meaning of the name, the entire peninsula that the city itself is found upon is a lush subtropical rainforest biome, reaching as far north as [[Lore:Abibon-Gora|Abibon-Gora]] and as far east as [[Lore:Khefrem|Khefrem]].{{Ref|name=ARTerrain}}{{Ref|name=ClimateMap|[[:File:DF-map-Iliac Bay Climate.png|Climate map of Iliac Bay]]}}<noinclude>

==Layout and Geography==
Hegathe is found on the on the south-westernmost point of the mainland of the province, where the coasts are fertile grasslands, and the peninsula itself is a subtropical rainforest.{{Ref|name=PGE1E}}{{Ref|name=ARTerrain}} There is an island off the coastline of the city.{{Ref|name=LOMap|[[:File:LO-map-Hammerfell (Anthology).jpg|Map of Hammerfell – ''The Elder Scrolls Anthology'']]}} Like other traditional cities in Hammerfell, it is built atop of old ruins of past civilizations, replaced with high domes and flying dew sails that are well-suited for the harsh environment.{{Ref|name=PGE3E}} It also has an impenetrable defense of an antique splendor.{{Ref|name=PGE1E}} It is a city of contrasts with tall, plain stone ramparts found side-by-side with beautiful gold-engraved tents, and a busy modern harbor found near the calm ancient shrines.{{ref|name=GPTv3|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|ns_base=General|Games and Pastimes of Tamriel v. 3}}}} One of the city's most iconic landmarks is the Shrine of Tava, a place of veneration for both the [[Lore:Crowns|Crowns]] and the [[Lore:Forebears|Forebears]]. It is highlighted by the ''Fresco of the [[Lore:Goshawk|Goshawk]]'', a mural depicting [[Lore:Tava|Tava]] nestled together with his mates, which was meant to symbolize the union of the two factions.{{Ref|name=TWAHD}} Thanks to its coastal forest environment, the city has vineyards where they make their signature wine.{{Ref|name=ONSherveen|[[Online:Sherveen|Sherveen]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Hegathe Lemon Kaveh is also a traditional drink named for the city.{{Ref|name=HLK|[[ON:Old Hegathe Lemon Kaveh|Old Hegathe Lemon Kaveh]] drink item}} Hegathe is neighbored by several settlements, including [[Lore:Kelps Yat|Kelps Yat]] to the north and [[Lore:Gilane|Gilane]] to the east.{{Ref|name=ARMap}}

==History==
===Early History===
[[File:LO-misc-Old Hegathe.jpg|left|120px|Old Hegathe|thumb]]
[[File:RG-map-West Tamriel-800x600.png|thumb|right|Map of West Tamriel]]
The name dates farther back than the city itself, or even the [[Lore:Yokudan|Yokudan]] incursion that swept across the land. It referred to the vast desert found between [[Lore:Colovia|Colovia]] and the [[Lore:Iliac Bay|Iliac Bay]], which in Aldmeris is translated to the Deathland. The Tamrielic variant of the word, often used by the [[Lore:Nede|Nedes]], was the name used for the region of Hammerfell.{{Ref|name=PGE3E}} 

When the Ra Gada crossed the [[Lore:Sea of Pearls|Sea of Pearls]] to eventually reach Tamriel, they landed on the harborage that became Hegathe. It was occupied by the [[Lore:Beastfolk|beastfolk]], who the Yokudans quickly displaced. It soon became the staging ground for the Ra Gada movement across the coastline. One of these waves was led by [[Lore:Yaghoub|Yaghoub]], who traveled north along [[Lore:Cape Shira|Cape Shira]] and was the first of the Redguards to discover the Iliac Bay and land on [[Lore:Sentinel|Sentinel]].{{Ref|name=STJOA|{{Cite Book|Sentinel, the Jewel of Alik'r}}}} As the remaining refugees had left [[Lore:Herne (island)|Herne]] to populate the mainland, the Yokudan government, the [[Lore:Na-Totambu|Na-Totambu]] became fully-integrated and made Hegathe its capital.{{Ref|name=PGE1E}}

In {{Year|1E 2200}}, the [[Lore:Thrassian Plague|Thrassian Plague]] broke out and it first appeared on mainland Tamrielic soil through major port cities on the southwest coast. Hegathe was the disease's point of entry in Hammerfell and it caused a significant decrease in the population.{{Ref|name=1TTP|{{Cite Book|1. The Thrassian Plague|ns_base=ON}}}} Many people at the time searched for a cure in vain and unsung heroes like Mazazim al-Hegathe died on their quest.{{Ref|name=SH3|{{Cite Book|Systres History: Volume 3}}}} When [[Lore:Mount Firesong|Mount Firesong]] erupted over in [[Lore:The Systres|the Systres]] in {{Year|1E 2484}}, Hegathe experienced vibrations from the seismic force of the volcano.{{Ref|name=SH4|{{Cite Book|Systres History: Volume 4}}}}

Years later when the province of Hammerfell was annexed into the [[Lore:Second Empire|Second Empire]], two political parties were formed to contribute to [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]]'s administration over the land. The Crowns were created from what was left of the Na-Totambu, who retained their high-ranking status in the Empire and used Hegathe as their capital. From there, they were represented by the [[Lore:Phyllocid Dynasty|Phyllocid Dynasty]], who maintained their relationship with the Empire throughout its lifetime. The Forebears was the other political party founded in Hammerfell, from the warrior-lords that descended from the Ra Gada and governed tribal districts in the south. For the longest time, these groups were sworn enemies that exceeded far longer than the Empire itself.{{Ref|name=PGE1E}}

===Ar-Azal's reign and onward===
[[File:RG-scene-Introduction 07.jpg|thumb|right|King Thassad II]]
It took the ascension of High King [[Lore:Ar-Azal|Ar-Azal]] and his union with the Crowns daughter, [[Lore:Arlimahera|Arlimahera]] and the Forebears daughter, [[Lore:Fereshtah|Fereshtah]], to break the divide between them.{{Ref|name=TWAHD|{{Cite Book|The Worthy Ar-Azal, His Deeds}}}} The [[Lore:Osseous Crux|Osseous Crux]] was discovered on the shores of the Abecean Sea near Hegathe. It was constructed from the bones of some enormous, unknown beast, bound together with twisted fibrous cords. It was later relocated to Sentinel.{{Ref|name=TVC}}

By the [[Lore:Interregnum|Interregnum]], the political duality had reverted back to the monarchy of the Na-Totambu and created political unrest, especially when the capital moved from Old Hegathe to the Forebears city of Sentinel. The line of High Kings that ruled from Sentinel continued with [[Lore:Thassad II|Thassad II]] as the conquests of [[Lore:Tiber Septim|Tiber Septim]] continued to expand and enroach on his domain. The High King passed away in {{Year|2E 862}} and the capital was retaken by the Forebears under the command of Baron [[Lore:Volag|Volag]], ensuing a vicious civil war between the Crowns and Forebears. When the latter enlisted the help of the [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]], victory shifted from the Crowns to the Empire, and eventually, the Crowns lost their territories until [[Lore:Stros M'Kai|Stros M'Kai]] was left. Both factions reconciled a year later to formally establish the Imperial Province of Hammerfell. Sentinel, however, remained as its provincial capital.{{Ref|name=PGE1E}}{{Ref|name=RGIntro|[[Redguard:Introduction|Introduction]] text in ''[[Redguard:Redguard|Redguard]]''}}

During their rule under the Empire, Hegathe became a major trading port with [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset]] to the south.{{ref|name=GPTv3|group=UOL}} During the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]] in the late-[[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], the city-state of Hegathe was an active settlement. It was ruled by Queen Ghapimti and it had a rivalry with Gilane and [[Lore:Sentinel|Sentinel]].{{Ref|name=AR|[[Arena:Hegathe|Hegathe]] location and rumors in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}

In the late-second century, the [[Lore:Great War|Great War]] enveloped Cyrodiil and Hammerfell in total warfare. By {{Year|4E 172}}, the [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]] had moved across Hammerfell's southern coast, leaving Hegathe as one of the remaining cities until in early-{{Year|4E 173}} when the Forebears from Sentinel arrived and broke the siege over the city. The victory over Hegathe brought the reconciliation of the two parties.{{Ref|name=TGW|{{Cite Book|The Great War (book)}}}}
