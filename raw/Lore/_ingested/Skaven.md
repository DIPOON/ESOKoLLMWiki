==Layout and Geography==
Skaven is found in north-central Hammerfell near the [[Lore:Dragontail Mountains|Dragontail Mountains]],{{Ref|name=RGMap|[[:File:RG-map-West Tamriel-1024x768.png|Map of West Tamriel – ''The Elder Scrolls Adventures: Redguard'']]}} where old [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokudan]] traditions continue to flourish by the [[Lore:Crown|Crowns]].{{Ref|name=PGE3E|{{Cite Book|Pocket Guide to the Empire, 3rd Edition/Hammerfell}}}} On the northern outskirts of Ska'vyn lies the Caverns of Wry, which contains herds of [[Lore:Skav Bat|Skav Bats]] that are bred to produce dairy by the locals. Their signature Bat Cheese is supposedly paired well with [[Lore:Pomegranate|Pomegranate]] wine.{{Ref|name=LHLAYQ|{{Cite Book|Lady Honnorah af-Lahreq Answers Your Questions}}}} Northwest of the kingdom, across from the [[Lore:Bjoulsae River|Bjoulsae River]] is the town of Candlemass.{{Ref|name=LRPBlueDawn|group=UOL}} Skaven is neighbored by several settlements, such as [[Lore:Cliff Keep|Cliff Keep]] to the east, [[Lore:Heldorn Mount|Heldorn Mount]] to the northeast, and [[Lore:Verkarth City|Verkarth City]] to the southwest.{{Ref|name=ARMap}}

==History==
Ska'vyn was active since at least the early-mid [[Lore:First Era|First Era]], when it and the surrounding region was under the tyrannical reign of the unholy city-state, [[Lore:Verkarth (Hammerfell)|Verkarth]] and their legion, the [[Lore:Gray Host|Gray Host]]. The city had reportedly ruled over Hammerfell's east-central region for many years, between [[Lore:Elinhir|Elinhir]] to the east and [[Lore:Thorstad Place|Thorstad Place]] to the west. Their power had drawn the ire of Empress [[Lore:Hestra|Hestra]] and her [[Lore:Alessian Empire|Empire]], who took the initiative and moved west for conquest. The Empire had defeated the Gray Host at the gates of the [[Lore:Bangkorai Garrison|Bangkorai Garrison]], with the help of the cleric, [[Lore:Pelin|Pelin]]. Ska'vyn was liberated from the [[Lore:Vampire|vampires]] after Verkarth was completely razed by the [[Lore:Imperial|Imperials]].{{Ref|name=TGHAH|{{Cite Book|The Gray Host: A History Part 1}}}} At some point in {{Year|2E 582}}, the [[Lore:Dragonstar Caravan Company|Dragonstar Caravan Company]], one of the largest trading companies at the time had moved their headquarters from [[Lore:Craglorn|Craglorn]] and into Ska'vyn.{{Ref|name=ONFinemo}} 

During the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]] in the late [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]], the city-state of Skaven was an active settlement. It was ruled by Queen Mijurti and it had a rivalry with [[Lore:Dragonstar|Dragonstar]].{{Ref|name=AR|[[Arena:Skaven|Skaven]] location and rumors in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}} During the [[Lore:Great War|Great War]], the [[Lore:Imperial Legion|Imperial Legion]], under General Decianus had a bloody skirmish with the Third [[Lore:Aldmeri Dominion|Aldmeri Dominion]] outside of Skaven's wall, but the city was captured by the Aldmeri Dominion. However, Lady [[Lore:Arannelya|Arannelya]]'s forces could not move out and continue their campaign. Before the general returned to the [[Lore:Imperial City|Imperial City]] in {{Year|4E 174}}, he discharged many soldiers that later formed the core of fighters that retook Skaven from the Dominion and drove Arannelya's force back across the [[Lore:Alik'r Desert|Alik'r Desert]].{{Ref|name=TGW|{{Cite Book|The Great War (book)}}}}

==Culture and Society==

The First Ska'vyn Camel Scouts were a group that was active sometime in the Second Era. One of their flags was in circulation throughout Tamriel's underworld in 2E 582.{{Ref|name=ONContrabandFlag|[[Online:Contraband L#Looted Redguard Battle Flag|Looted Redguard Battle Flag]] contraband text in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} The Firedancers of central Hammerfell wear scandalously little clothing by local standards, which may be why they protect their modesty—and identities—by hiding their faces behind Flamebrow Fire Veils.{{Ref|name=FFV|[[Online:Flamebrow Fire Veil|Flamebrow Fire Veil]] item description}}
