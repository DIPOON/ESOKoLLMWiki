[[Lore:Pits|The Pits]],{{ref|name=SRPeryite|[[Skyrim:The Only Cure|Peryite's dialogue]] in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}} also known as '''Peryite's Pits'''{{Ref|name=BenedictCartiabatch|[[Online:Benedicta Cartia|Benedicta Cartia]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=ICDL|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Imperial Census of Daedra Lords|ns_base=GEN}}}} or the '''Pits of Pestilence''',{{Ref|name=BenedictCartiabatch}}{{Ref|name=Blades|[[Blades:The Pit of Pestilence|The Pits of Pestilence]] in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}} is a realm of [[Lore:Planes of Oblivion|Oblivion]], created and ruled over by [[Lore:Peryite|Peryite]], the [[Lore:Daedric Princes|Daedric Prince]] of Pestilence. Here, Peryite guards the lowest orders of Oblivion.{{Ref|name=TBD|{{Cite Book|The Book of Daedra}}}}

It is unknown what the realm looks like overall. One part of the realm appears as a rocky cave system filled with a hazy green poisonous fog, floating green embers, and some underground plants. This area of the realm has been home to necromancers and undead.{{Ref|name=Blades}} Another part of the realm resembles the [[Lore:Deadlands|Deadlands]], with lava seas, volcanic islands, and ruined structures. It is also home to many of the same Daedra as the Deadlands.{{ref|name=OBPeryite|[[Oblivion:Peryite|Peryite]] quest in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}} The [[Lore:Imperial Geographical Society|Imperial Geographical Society]] believes that the realm is usually inaccessible to mortals,{{Ref|name=ICDL|group=UOL}} but there have been several exceptions.<noinclude>

==History==
[[File:OB-place-Peryite's Realm.jpg|A portal to Tamriel in Peryite's Realm|thumb|right]]
Circa {{Year|2E 582}}, the Hidden Kindred created "Nightmare Alchemy" mounts by using tinctures derived from the Pits and [[Lore:Quagmire|Quagmire]]. They used reagants such as bones salvaged from the Pits,{{Ref|name=NAE|[[ON:Nightmare Alchemy Elk|Nightmare Alchemy Elk]] mount description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} [[Lore:Tenmar Forest|Tenmar]] plants watered from the Pits,{{Ref|name=NAS|[[ON:Nightmare Alchemy Senche|Nightmare Alchemy Senche]] mount description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} ooze from Pits-born [[Lore:Death Hopper|plague hoppers]],{{Ref|name=NAT|[[ON:Nightmare Alchemy Tharriker|Nightmare Alchemy Tharriker]] mount description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} and slime formed from fungal spores mixed with Pits [[Lore:Minerals|minerals]] to create the key components for the mounts.{{Ref|name=NAV|[[ON:Nightmare Alchemy Vvardvark|Nightmare Alchemy Vvardvark]] mount description in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

In {{Year|3E 433}}, five worshippers of Peryite performed a ritual at the Prince's hidden shrine, located along the banks of the [[Lore:Silverfish River|Silverfish River]] in [[Lore:Cyrodiil|Cyrodiil]]. The spell was intended to summon Peryite, but instead, the souls of the worshippers were sent to the Pits, where they became trapped. The [[Lore:Hero of Kvatch|Hero of Kvatch]] stumbled upon the motionless bodies of the worshippers by the shrine, and was tasked by Peryite to travel to a plane in the Pits and recover their souls. The plane consisted of a volcanic island inhabited by many [[Lore:Daedra|daedra]], with a Blood Well tower and a series of volcanic caves called the Sightless Grotto. The Hero rescued the souls of the worshippers and returned them to Tamriel to earn the Prince's favor.{{Ref|name=OBPeryite}}

In {{Year|4E 201}}, Peryite tasked the [[Lore:Last Dragonborn|Last Dragonborn]] with slaying {{Lore Link|Orchendor}}, a former priest of Peryite who had abandoned the Prince. In death, Orchendor's soul was sent to the Pits, where Peryite punished him for his disobedience.{{Ref|name=SROnly|[[Skyrim:The Only Cure|The Only Cure]] quest in ''[[Skyrim:Skyrim|Skyrim]]''}}
