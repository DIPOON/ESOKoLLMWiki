<noinclude>{{Lore Gods Trail}}__NOTOC__:''"May Onsi strengthen your resolve and guide your blade to the monster's heart."'' {{ref|[[Blades:Azzin|Azzin]]'s dialogue in ''[[Blades:Blades|Blades]]''}}</noinclude>
[[Lore:Onsi|Onsi]] (also known as the '''Boneshaver'''), is a notable warrior god of the [[Lore:Yokuda|Yokudan]] [[Lore:Ra Gada|Ra Gada]] and a part of the [[Lore:Redguard|Redguard]] Pantheon. Onsi is said to have taught Mankind how to pull their knives into swords.{{ref|name=VOF|{{Cite Book|Varieties of Faith...}}}}{{ref|{{Cite Book|Sword-Wisdom of Saikhalar}}}} He is mentioned several times in ''[[Lore:The Book of Circles|The Book of Circles]]'' by [[Lore:Frandar Hunding|Frandar Hunding]], who wrote that those forging a sword should recite the "Oblation of Onsi" upon completion. A tale of King [[Lore:Fahara'jad|Fahara'jad]]'s youth claimed that Onsi personally saved him from a vengeful [[Lore:Hagraven|hagraven]].{{ref|name=TABKJ|{{Cite Book|The All-Beneficent King Fahara'jad}}}}<noinclude>

==Worship==
Priests of Onsi wear magnificent cloaks of iridescent [[Lore:Gooblet|gooblet]] feathers during the Boneshaver's Sword Banquet.{{ref|name=ContCloak|[[Online:Contraband_I|Iridescent Boneshaver Cloak]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} 

Worshippers of Onsi often exclaim ''"By Onsi's bright blade!"''
