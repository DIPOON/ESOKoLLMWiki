[[Lore:Forest Wraith|Forest Wraiths]] (also known as '''dray'hoon''') are evil [[Lore:Nature Spirits|nature spirits]] that are filled with rage and longing, and are said to be embodiments of death.{{ref|[[ON:Elder Pitof|Elder Pitof]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|group=UOL|https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1604662466 Twitch Stream of ''ESO Live Legacy of the Bretons Autumn Event'', time stamp 1:04:46-1:05:10}}{{ref|name=ESOLiveFiresong|group=UOL|[[General:Firesong_DLC_%26_Update_36_Preview#Forest_Wraith|Forest Wraith]] section in [[General:Firesong DLC & Update 36 Preview|Firesong DLC & Update 36 Preview]]}} They cannot be reasoned with, and if one has identified you as a threat, it will not relent.{{ref|name=DG|[[ON:Druid Gastoc|Druid Gastoc]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} They are speculated to be related to the Allwither,{{ref|name=JG|[[ON:Journeys In Galen: A Scholar's Travels|Journeys In Galen: A Scholar's Travels]] in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} the representation of death in [[Lore:Druids|druidic]] culture.{{ref|name=ESODA|[[ON:Druid Audrine|Druid Audrine]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}<noinclude>

Forest Wraiths are thought to have been created after the death of [[Lore:Kasorayn|Druid King Kasorayn]] at the hands of the Allwither Cult,{{ref|name=JG}}{{ref|name=ESODruidKing|[[ON:Druid King Kasorayn|Druid King Kasorayn]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} and were originally guardians but went rogue over the centuries.{{ref|name=ESOLiveFiresong|group=UOL}} Some druids willingly "submit to the Green" to become one, allowing wild spirits to possess them and transform themselves into these wooden specters.{{ref|name=DG}} Some however are forced to undergo the process unwillingly.{{ref|[[Online:A Heart Turned Black|A Heart Turned Black]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Like [[Lore:Moth#Phoenix Moth|Phoenix Moths]] and other creatures that inhabit Galen, they represent echoes of the past, when magic was much wilder.{{ref|name=LATD|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive - Tamriel's Dungeons}}}}
