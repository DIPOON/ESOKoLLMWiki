# Lore:Arcanists

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

<noinclude>{{Lore Factions Trail}}[[File:ON-render-Arcanist.jpg|An arcanist with their tome|thumb|left]]{{TOCright}}</noinclude>
[[Lore:Arcanists|Arcanists]] are [[Lore:Magic|spellcasters]] who wield runic spellforms learned from a tome touched by the forces of [[Lore:Apocrypha|Apocrypha]], the [[Lore:Oblivion|Oblivion]] realm of [[Lore:Hermaeus Mora|Hermaeus Mora]]. This tome is both the source of knowledge from which the arcanist learns of the spells they wield and the vector through which these spells are cast. In other words, their power is derived from the book itself: knowledge alone is one thing, but arcanists are unique in that the tome they wield is integral to their spellcasting. All mages use runes of some kind, but the runework wielded by arcanists is extremely elaborate on top of producing unique feats of spellwork.{{ref|name=WA|{{Cite Book|What's an Arcanist?}}}} Some arcanists have been known to use their power to [[Lore:Sight|scry]] or manipulate the very [[Lore:Fate|threads of fate]]. {{ref|name=AzandarDialogue|[[ON:Azandar al-Cybiades|Azandar al-Cybiades]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}{{ref|name=Leramil|[[ON:Leramil the Wise|Leramil]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=ArcLA}} Scruut, a [[Lore:Watcher|Watchling]] fate auditor in the service of Hermaeus Mora, believed that influencing fate in this manner was a power not meant for mortals, and that it could potentially result in the undoing of the mortal that did so.{{ref|name=Scruut|[[ON:Scruut|Scruut]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

==The Arcanist's Tome==
Every arcanist's tome is unique.{{ref|group=UOL|name=TwitArchive|[[General:Twitter Archive|Twitter Archive (22/05/2023)]]}} The knowledge in each tome is suited to the mind and soul of each individual arcanist: if one arcanist were to attempt to read another's book, they would find it impossible to learn from. Just like the tomes themselves, the process of becoming an arcanist is equally unique&mdash;no arcanist discovers their tome the same way as another, and the bond formed between a tome and its caster is wholly unique.{{ref|name=ArcLA|{{Cite Book|Loremaster's Archive - The Arcanists}}}} Some hypothesize that these tomes pieces or debris of Apocrypha itself, and that this bond is a piece of Oblivion finding him in Nirn, and in a mortal's mind.{{ref|name=WA}} As well, the tomes seemingly have a mind of their own: they communicate with their wielders. For at least one Arcanist, this communication wasn't done through audible words but not all tomes "behave" the same way.{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue|[[ON:Azandar al-Cybiades|Azandar al-Cybiades]]' dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}{{ref|name=ArcLA}}

The contents and context within the tomes often shifts, showing arcanists things they should and shouldn't know respectively. The tomes can also travel of their own accord, occasionally teleporting to different locations.{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue}}{{ref|name=AANote4|{{Item Link|Azandar Arcanist Note # 4|id=197879|quality=1}} item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}} Arcanist tomes are not [[Lore:Black Books|Black Books]],{{ref|name=ESO2023GlobalReveal|group=UOL|[[General:ESO 2023 Global Reveal|ESO 2023 Global Reveal]]}} though Hermaeus Mora himself has compared the two: both are forms of knowledge that he allows into [[Lore:Nirn|Nirn]].{{Ref|name=ETHMR|[[General:ESO Twitter Hermaeus Mora Roleplay|ESO Twitter Hermaeus Mora Roleplay]]}} The Prince of Knowledge is capable of communicating with arcanists through these tomes.{{ref|name=AANote5|{{Item Link|Azandar Arcanist Note # 5|id=197880|quality=1}} item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}

===The Arcanist's Arsenal===
[[File:ON-concept-Arcanist Symbols.jpg|Arcanist's runic symbols|thumb|left]]
Runes and [[Lore:Languages|language]] are the foundation from which an arcanist's power is derived. The arcane spellforms learned from and driven by these tomes enable an arcanist to command runeforms, project beams that scar the world in front of them, conjure nigh impenetrable spell armor matrices and command restorative magics deemed to be inventive by mortal standards. Arcanists who specialize in defensive runeforms are known to refer to themselves as "Soldiers of Apocrypha", whilst restorative and supportive runecrafting is known as "Curative Runeforms". However, most of all, they're best known for their beams of eldritch power.{{ref|name=BahtraHun|[[Online:Bahtra at-Hunding|Bahtra at-Hunding]]'s dialogue in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} These powers may be used in various and distinctive ways by each arcanist but it appears that the notion of etching a rune on reality to modify its form seems to be universal among them.{{ref|name=WA}} Arcanists carved runes representing specific beings to manifest unique spells.{{ref|name=CHakAbil|[[ON:Chakram Shields|Chakram Shields]], [[ON:Chakram of Destiny|Chakram of Destiny]] and [[ON:Tidal Chakram|Tidal Chakram]] [[ON:Arcanist|Arcanist]] skills in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

The runework of an arcanist is believed to be a universal language between the spellcasters, however, this is untrue: an arcanist's runework is wholly unique to that individual despite the shared symbols. They don't have specific, exact meanings like [[Lore:Enchanting#Runestones_and_Glyphs|enchantment runestones]] but instead are symbols for the metamagical subconscious of the arcanist. A sigil that to one arcanist might mean "fire", to another could mean something entirely different, despite using the same rune.{{ref|name=ArcLA}} Arcanists who specialize in runes are sometimes referred to as "rune-carvers".{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue}} 
[[File:ON-prerelease-Arcanist_Tentacles.png|An arcanist summoning tentacles|thumb|right]]

Runes are not all an arcanist wields, as tentacles are just as much of a common sight in their arcane repertoire due to their connection with Mora's realm. These creations are summoned not just for self-defense but also to lash out at foes. They're considered both awe-inspiring and disturbing by some due to their eldritch appearance and memorable auditory and olfactory qualities.{{ref|name=WA}}{{ref|name=ASShields|[[ON:Gibbering Shield|Gibbering Shield]] and [[ON:Abyssal Impact|Abyssal Impact]] [[ON:Arcanist|Arcanist]] skills in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} This kind of spellcasting is sometimes designated as the "tentacular school of thought".{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue}} 

The Abyssal Sea of Mora's realm, with endless secrets drowned in its depths is a powerful source of the eldritch for arcanists, who channel and bend its waters to their will. Those who wield these depths are sometimes derogatorily referred to as "tiresome paddlers"{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue}} The very concept of fate itself is a precise tool in an arcanist's spellbook, and some have reported to seeing them manipulate possibility around them in unique and creative ways, extending even to the fate-manipulation of an arcanist's very soul. This skill is considered a faint echo of the "fate sight" believed to be held by Mora themself.{{ref|name=WA}}{{ref|name=ArcAbilitiesFate|[[ON:Harnessed Quintessence|Harnessed Quintessence]], [[ON:Fated Fortune|Fated Fortune]] and [[ON:Circumvented Fate|Circumvented Fate]] [[ON:Arcanist|Arcanist]] skills in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} [[File:ON-prerelease-Arcanist_Crux.png|An arcanist's "crux" floating around them|thumb|left]]

For the arcanist, spellcasting consists of a good deal of hand-waving. Renowned arcanist Azandar al-Cybiades surmised that the reason arcanists tend to favor sweeping hand-motions is due to the nature of Apocrypha itself. As al-Cybiades described it, magicka flows through an arcanist's mind while spellcasting with a robustness not experienced quite as regularly by other mages. All the extra potentiality generated by the arcanist's eldritch power needs to be directed somewhere: as a result, an arcanist may find themselves inadvertently summoning tentacles if the motions they make to cast a less taxing spell (such as conjuring flame to heat water for a cup of tea) are a little too forceful.{{ref|name=ArcLA}}

When compared to the schools of magic, the arcanist's spellcraft would fall under the schools of [[Lore:Mysticism|Mysticism]] and [[Lore:Conjuration|Conjuration]].{{ref|name=ArcLA}}

==Being an Arcanist==
{{Quote Box|
:''"We use the power of forbidden knowledge ..."<br>"Searching for untold secrets."<br>"But there is a price to be paid when calling upon the unknown."<br>"Sometimes ... the unknown ... answers"<br>"Open your mind."<br>"The knowledge you seek ... is waiting."''|[[General:The Elder Scrolls Online: Necrom - Wield the Power of the Arcanist|The Elder Scrolls Online: Necrom - Wield the Power of the Arcanist]]{{ref|name=WTPOFA|[[General:The Elder Scrolls Online: Necrom - Wield the Power of the Arcanist|The Elder Scrolls Online: Necrom - Wield the Power of the Arcanist]]}}}}[[File:ON-trailer-Arcanist Tome.jpeg|An arcanist's tome|thumb|left]]
One becomes an arcanist when an eldritch tome somehow finds its way to Nirn, ultimately seeking out and binding itself to a mortal [[Lore:Soul|soul]] which perfectly matches its arcane resonance.{{ref|name=ArcLA}} At least one known arcanist has "accidentally" walked this path when an arcanist's tome "bit" him.{{ref|name=ArcLA}} Those who become arcanists are said to align with Apocrypha as easily as one learns to breathe or speak.{{Ref|name=ETHMR}} Despite their innate connection with Hermaeus Mora, not all arcanists are devout followers of the Prince: some arcanists outright hate or distrust him.{{ref|name=WA}}

Arcanists are diverse and unique people with different methods to their path. Some view it as a new way of life, others in a more methodical and scholarly manner. Others still crave knowledge as deeply as the Prince whose realm their power stems from, and some take up an isolated life of scholarship.{{ref|name=ArcLA}} Many see this magic as a means to an end, a way of defending themselves or achieving unique goals, while others pledge themselves fully to the One-Who-Knows in mind, body and soul, resulting in blatant Daedric corruption.{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue}}

Being an arcanist can be a troubling experience at times: they may occasionally experience vivid sensory phenomena, such as feeling the waters of the abyssal sea rushing through their head. Regardless, most arcanists find that the price they pay for eldritch knowledge is worth it for the power they wield.{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue}} It is customary for arcanists to establish sanctums to practice their craft.{{ref|name=AzandarDialogue}}{{ref|name=AzandarSanctum|[[ON:Azandar al-Cybiades|Azandar al-Cybiades]]'s [[ON:Skywatch|Skywatch]] sanctum in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}{{ref|name=MartinaArcanistSanctum|[[ON:Martina Abor|Martina Abor]]'s [[ON:The Underweave|Underweave]] sanctum in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}

==History==
Arcanists gained prominence in the academic world during the Interregnum. In {{Year|2E 582}}, more arcanists than ever before utilized the craft in the public sphere, but they are believed to have existed for much longer.{{ref|name=WA}} Acclaimed scholar and arcanist Azandar al-Cybiades theorized that a change in the fabric of reality caused this influx of new arcanists, with the [[Lore:Planemeld|Planemeld]] being cited as a potential catalyst for the sheer number of tomes that traveled to Nirn in such short time.{{ref|name=ArcLA}} Arcanists in this time were known to style their hair in an uncomplicated crest.{{ref|name=ACrest|[[ON:Arcanist's Crest|Arcanist's Crest]] hairstyle in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}

[[Lore:Morian Zenas|Morian Zenas]] had an influence on the arcanist's craft in the Second Era, with his obscure magics making their way into a common arcanist's repertoire.{{ref|group=UOL|1=[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BCFj3pNIV4A&t Youtube Stream of ''The Elder Scrolls Online: Necrom Chapter Preview''], time stamp 26:22-26:57}}{{ref|name=ASZED|[[ON:Zenas' Empowering Disc|Zenas' Empowering Disc]] [[ON:Arcanist|Arcanist]] skill in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}} Other figures such as the [[Lore:Blind Man|Blind Man‎‎]], [[Lore:Fate Crone|Fate Crone]], and [[Lore:Baron of Breakers|Baron of Breakers]] can be invoked and contacted by arcanists seeking power and knowledge.{{ref|name=CHakAbil}}{{Ref|name=AANote6|{{Item Link|Azandar Arcanist Note # 6|id=197881|quality=1}} item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}{{Ref|name=AANote7|{{Item Link|Azandar Arcanist Note # 7|id=197882|quality=1}} item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}}{{Ref|name=AANote8|{{Item Link|Azandar Arcanist Note # 8|id=197883|quality=1}} item description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Necrom (Chapter)|Necrom]]''}} At some point in history, the [[Lore:Rune Knights of the Purgatory Disquisition|Rune Knights of the Purgatory Disquisition]] wielded and perfected a variety of runic spellcraft which found itself in the skillset of arcanists during the Interregnum.{{ref|name=ArcLA}}{{ref|name=RSWAbil|[[ON:Runespite Ward|Runespite Ward]], [[ON:Impervious Runeward|Impervious Runeward]] and [[ON:Spiteward of the Lucid Mind|Spiteward of the Lucid Mind]] [[ON:Arcanist|Arcanist]] skills in [[ON:Online|ESO]]}}
