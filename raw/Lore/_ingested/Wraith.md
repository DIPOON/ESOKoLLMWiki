# Lore:Wraith

<!-- UESP / ns 130 / dump 2026-03-13 -->

<noinclude>{{Lore Bestiary Trail}}__NOTOC__[[File:ON-creature-Freed Soul.jpg|thumb|right|A Wraith (ESO)]]</noinclude>
'''Wraiths''' are a type of powerful and aggressive [[Lore:Ghost|spirit]] which can be formed naturally from individuals who have suffered from a great injustice or from those that have left important work undone, they are described as the darkest expression of a spirit, formed due to the anger of a life cut short. In addition to forming in response to a [[Lore:Soul|soul's]] negative emotions, wraiths can form due to the actions of [[Lore:Necromancy|necromancers]], who have discovered methods by which to create these hateful specters and enslave them as guardians via forcing a spirit's negative emotions to govern its form,{{ref|name=BtGTU|{{Cite Book|Beyond the Grave: Tamriel's Undead}}}}{{ref|name=MfNM|{{Cite Book|Materials for Novice Necromancers}}}} as well as through soul ensnaring curses.{{ref|name=OBDiary|{{Cite Book|Traitor's Diary}}}}{{ref|name=ONTelenger|[[Online:Telenger the Artificer|Telenger the Artificer]]'s dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Some wraiths are described as being the spirits of long dead mages, trapped into this state of existence either by the circumstances of their death or by being called by a powerful wizard. As an undead creature, wraiths are categorized as returned.{{Ref|name=ONLoadFlyleaf|[[Online:Flyleaf Catacombs|Flyleaf Catacombs]] loading screen text in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Wraiths are often found guarding objects or patrolling certain areas. They are known to cast spells, see invisible foes, and attack those that trespass into their domain.{{ref|name=ARWraith|[[Arena:Wraith|Arena Wraith description]]}} Though wraiths can arise from the souls of mages, being one isn't required for a wraith to form.{{ref|name=ONPraxin|[[Online:Praxin Douare|Praxin Douare]]'s appearance in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=OBGhostship|[[Oblivion:The Ghost Ship of Anvil|The Ghost Ship of Anvil]] quest in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}{{ref|name=OBForlorn|[[Oblivion:The Forlorn Watchman (quest)|The Forlorn Watchman]] quest in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

Wraiths are known by the people of [[Lore:Tamriel|Tamriel]] as beings that haunt tombs and lurk in the dark, being featured even in children stories as creatures that will deliver doom.{{ref|name=CTB|{{Cite Book|A Child's Tamriel Bestiary}}}} The fear of wraiths exists even among the ranks of the [[Lore:Dark Brotherhood|Dark Brotherhood]] due to the [[Lore:Wrath of Sithis|Wrath of Sithis]], an entity that takes the form of a wraith sometimes sent against the members of the organization of assassins who break their Five Tenets, which are the greatest rules of the group. This kind of wraith being sent is said to be a worse punishment than expulsion for the traitors of the Brotherhood, breaking each of the five rules can allow for the Wrath to be sent, and the spirit has been known to take the very souls of its targets.{{ref|name=TFT|{{Cite Book|The Five Tenets}}}}{{ref|name=ESOWrath|[[Online:The Wrath of Sithis|The Wrath of Sithis]] quest in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Dark Brotherhood (DLC)|Dark Brotherhood]]''}} Additionally, the [[LO:Mages Guild|Mages Guild]] was known to utilize wraiths as guardians of their guild halls at night hours during the [[Lore:Imperial Simulacrum|Imperial Simulacrum]].{{ref|name=ArenaWraith|[[Arena:Wraith|Wraiths]] in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}

The soul of an individual on the precipice of death can manifest in a spectral form, similar to a ghost. A soul burdened by strong emotions such as regret can splinter, its parts manifesting simultaneously in multiple locations tied to those emotions.{{ref|name=Jahones|[[Online:Jahones Valain|Jahones Valain]]'s soul fragments in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} Such souls, caught between life and death, can be prevented from moving on to the afterlife through the use of powerful magic. However, even the power of a [[Lore:Luminary|Luminary]] can't prevent a soul from passing on to [[Lore:Aetherius|Aetherius]] indefinitely, and powerful wraiths might eventually arrive to force the transition into Aetherius.{{ref|name=Netch|[[Online:The Netch|The Netch]]'dialogue in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=End|[[Online:Inevitable End|Inevitable End]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

As beings formed due to strong emotions, wraiths are harder for necromancers to control than ghosts.{{ref|name=MfNM|{{Cite Book|Materials for Novice Necromancers}}}} In addition to a normal haunting, wraiths can also be attached to cursed objects, such as a mirror.{{ref|name=ONReflective|[[Online:Reflective Spirit|Reflective Spirit]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

Faded wraiths are a less powerful translucent form of wraith, while gloom wraiths are an especially powerful variety, which can wield swords and project curses that damage one's strength and endurance.  The name "Gloom Wraith" is also used by beings that resemble spectral [[Lore:Spiderkith|Spiderkith]].{{ref|[[Online:Gloom Wraith|Gloom Wraiths]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]: ''[[Online:Shadows of the Hist|Shadows of the Hist]]''}}

Powdered remains of wisps are a key component in the construction of [[Lore:Stonewisp|stonewisps]], the constructs created by the clergy of [[Lore:Julianos|Julianos]].{{Ref|name=SOTL|[[ON:Stonewisp of Truth and Law|Stonewisp of Truth and Law]] pet description in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{Ref|name=PWR|{{Item Link|quality=l|Powdered Wisp Remains|collectid=13084}} in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

==Powers==
Owing to their nature as a form of twisted [[Lore:Ghost|Ghost]] wraiths possess many of the same abilities, typically able to fly, see the invisible and cast a variety of frost spells and draining curses.{{ref|name=DGWraith|[[Daggerfall:Wraith| Wraith]]'s abilities in ''[[Daggerfall:Daggerfall|Daggerfall]]''}}{{ref|name=ONWraith|[[Online:Wraith| Wraith]]'s abilities in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} In addition, some wraiths exhibit invulnerability to weapons that aren't of or above a certain quality, or are enchanted, as well as possessing the ability to silence magic.{{ref|name=OBWraith|[[Oblivion:Wraith| Wraith]]'s abilities in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

Certain wraiths have the ability to cast telekinesis, conjure Coldfire and reanimate the dead,{{ref|name=ONDespoiler|[[Online:Secundinus the Despoiler|Secundinus]]'s abilities in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}{{ref|name=End|[[Online:Inevitable End|Inevitable End]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
others can conjure spectral copies of creatures and cause instant death via curses,{{ref|name=ONPraxin|[[Online:Praxin Douare|Praxin Douare]]'s appearance in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} enter corpses to animate them and open portals,{{ref|name=ONCalion|[[Online:Calion|Calion]] and the [[Online:Possessed Soldier|Possessed Soldier]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} or communicate remotely via visions and corrupt objects to afflict the next person to grasp them with curses.{{ref|name=ONBerich|[[Oblivion:Lathon|Lathon]]'s dialogue and [[Oblivion:The Sword of the Crusader|Lord Vlindrel's curse]] in ''[[Oblivion:Oblivion|Oblivion]]''}}

Some wraiths are known to feed on the life force of the living, they will transport those they encounter to the spirit realm of Aetherius where the dead can drain their essence.{{ref|name=ONThirster|[[Online:Soul Thirster|Soul Thirsters]] and [[Online:Baleful Ghost|Baleful Ghosts]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}

There have been cases of wraiths associated with [[Lore:Daedra|Daedric]] cults possessing the living to afflict them with an engineered plague,{{ref|name=ONZaan|[[Online:Zaan the Scalecaller|Zaan the Scalecaller]]'s wraith in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}} or imprisoning them within a mirror and leaving behind the reflection to take their place in the real world.{{ref|name=ONReflective|[[Online:Reflective Spirit|Reflective Spirit]] in [[Online:Online|ESO]]}}
