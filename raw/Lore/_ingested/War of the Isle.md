The [[Lore:War of the Isle|War of the Isle]] was a conflict in the early-mid [[Lore:Third Era|Third Era]] in which King [[Lore:Orgnum|Orgnum]] of the island-kingdom, [[Lore:Pyandonea|Pyandonea]] attacked the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset Isles]] in their closest attempt to claim the province. The war took place in {{Year|3E 110}},{{Ref|name=PGE3|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Summerset}}}} twelve years into Emperor [[Lore:Antiochus|Antiochus Septim]]'s reign{{Ref|name=BHOTE1|{{Cite Book|Brief History of the Empire v 1}}}} and later involved both the [[Lore:Third Empire|Third Empire]] and the [[Lore:Psijic Order|Psijic Order]] of [[Lore:Artaeum|Artaeum]].{{Ref|name=PGE3}}<noinclude>
{{TOCleft}}{{NewLine}}

==History==
While the [[Lore:Maormer|Maormer]] of Pyandonea have made attempts to invade the Summerset Isles since time immemorial,{{Ref|name=PGE3}} the exact circumstances that led to this event are unknown. Although, the historical fiction, ''[[Lore:The Wolf Queen|The Wolf Queen]]'' written by [[Lore:Waughin Jarth|Waughin Jarth]] provides an account of events before the war broke out. In 3E 110, three months before the war and after the Pyandoneans' declaration of war, Queen [[Lore:Potema|Potema]] arranged an evening dinner meeting with King Orgnum in the [[Lore:Blue Palace|Blue Palace]], while in the morning, she met with the Psijic Order and the Ritemaster, [[Lore:Iachesis|Iachesis]].{{Ref|name=TWQ4|{{Cite Book|The Wolf Queen, v4}}}}{{intnote|Ritemaster|[Note 1]}}

The story perpetuates the idea that Potema and Orgnum initially arranged a deal that, in the outcome the Pyandoneans conquered Tamriel; she would become Empress over her older half-brother, Antiochus. And through her manipulation, convinced the Psijics to take Antiochus' deal and promised an insurrection against her brother.{{Ref|name=TWQ4}} The war took place three months later and a united alliance between the Kings of the [[Lore:Summerset Isles|Summerset Isles]] and the Third Empire stood against the Maormer of Pyandonea.{{Ref|name=BHOTE1}} Despite this unified power, the [[Lore:Imperial Navy|Imperial Navy]] and the royal Summerset navies were unable to withstand Orgnum's fleet. Their victory was at hand when a sudden freak storm appeared off the coast of Artaeum and annihilated the fleet. Legends accredit the storm to the Psijic Order and it has since been agreed upon as such.{{Ref|name=BHOTE1}}{{Ref|name=TWQ4}}

===Legacy===
The war was considered Antiochus' greatest victory{{Ref|name=TWQ4}} and as of {{Year|3E 432}}, it was the Maormer's last documented appearance in Tamriel. The storm was said to be so violent that Orgnum could never muster enough strength to retaliate. However, no word from Pyandonea could confirm this.{{Ref|name=PGE3OL|{{Cite Book|PGE|3|Other Lands}}}} After the war, Orgnum lost his magical [[Lore:King Orgnum's Coffer|coffer]], which provided only him with limitless wealth. With the loss of this source of endless wealth, Orgnum could not afford a fleet as great as he had had, leading to Pyandonea losing much of its military power.{{ref|name=AQD|Dialogue during [[Arena:Artifact Quests#King Orghum's Coffer|King Orghum's Coffer]]'s quest in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}{{ref|name=CS}}{{Rp|128}} The coffer washed up in the Summerset Isles and has become an elusive artifact ever since.{{Ref|name=ARArtifact|[[Arena:Artifact Quests#King Orghum's Coffer|King Orghum's Coffer]] quest in ''[[Arena:Arena|Arena]]''}}{{Ref|name=CS|{{Cite Book|Codex Scientia|ns_base=BK}}}}{{rp|128}}

One of the war's most well-known veterans was [[Lore:Amiel Lannus|Amiel Lannus]], whom after his heroic deeds in the war, founded the [[Lore:Knights of the Nine|Knights of the Nine]], a [[Lore:Knights|knightly order]] that was dedicated to recovering the [[Lore:Crusader's Relics|Crusader's Relics]].{{Ref|name=TKOTN|{{Cite Book|The Knights of the Nine}}}} To write ''The Wolf Queen'', Waughin Jarth interviewed veterans for personal accounts of the war but because he believed some of what he was told was exaggerated, he omitted dubious details for the historical-fiction.{{Ref|name=IWTW|group=UOL|{{Cite Book|Interview With Three Writers|ns_base=GEN}}}} Potema's insurrection hinted in ''The Wolf Queen'' later became a reality with the [[Lore:War of the Red Diamond|War of the Red Diamond]], which came not long after Emperor Antiochus' death.{{Ref|name=TWQ6|{{Cite Book|The Wolf Queen, v6}}}}
